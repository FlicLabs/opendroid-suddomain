Executive Vision

The retail landscape is shifting from volume-based logistics to precision-based intelligence. For PriceSmart, robotics offers a defensive moat against supply chain volatility. Our vision proposes a "Connected Warehouse" where floor-scrubbers capture data and security units assist members, unlocking trapped value in operational inefficiencies.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Inventory Intelligence

Deployment of spectral-imaging robots to detect shelf gaps. Focus on real-time planogram compliance and price verification.

PATHWAY B

Automated Care

Industrial autonomous scrubbers for concrete floor maintenance. Reallocates 15-20 labor hours per week to member services.

PATHWAY C

Service & Security

Roving units for loss prevention patrols during off-hours and member wayfinding assistance during peak operations.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot & Validation
    Deploy Pathway B (Cleaning) + Scanning prototypes in Panama City & San Jose.
  • Year 2: Regional Rollout
    Expand to top 20 revenue clubs. Integrate robotic data into central ERP.
  • Year 3: Full Integration
    Deployment to all 54 locations. Activate "Predictive Restocking" via AI.
  • Year 5: Autonomous Ecosystem
    Maturity phase. Robots handle 90% of floor care and counting tasks.
