Common Ground

Executive Summary

Common Ground is a high-touch crisis intervention agency operating 24/7/365. The primary robotics opportunities lie in shifting from manual coordination to AI-Orchestrated Logistics and from manual medication handling to Automated Dispensing Systems. This strategy directly combats staff burnout and increases clinical capacity without proportional headcount growth.
