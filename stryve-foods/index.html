Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Cobot Rack Loading

Soft-gripper Cobots arranging marinated slabs. Reduces RSI risks and ensures uniform drying spacing.

Pathway B: AMR Transport

Automated Mobile Robots moving heavy racks between marination and drying rooms. Touchless transport.

Pathway C: EOL Palletizing

Vision-guided case packing and palletizing. Ensures 100% seal integrity and maximizes throughput.
