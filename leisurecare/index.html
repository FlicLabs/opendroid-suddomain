Leisure Care

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Executive Summary

Leisure Care is a prominent operator in the senior living sector, managing over 50 communities known for their "Five-Star Fun" philosophy. The organization emphasizes high-end hospitality and active lifestyle programming. Facing industry-wide labor shortages, Leisure Care presents a prime environment for Dining Automation and Autonomous Floor Care. Implementing these technologies shifts staff focus from logistics to high-value resident interaction, driving both efficiency and occupancy.
