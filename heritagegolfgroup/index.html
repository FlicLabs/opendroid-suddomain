Heritage Golf Group

Robotics Opportunity Portfolio & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

Heritage Golf Group's aggressive acquisition strategy of private country clubs and premium daily-fee courses presents a prime opportunity for operational automation. The dominant ROI driver is the Autonomous Turf Maintenance Fleet, addressing critical labor shortages and rising agronomy costs. By shifting from manual labor to AI-driven logistics, Heritage can realize millions in annual portfolio savings while elevating course conditions.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Months 1-6

Validation Pilot

Select 2 diverse properties (Warm vs Cool season grass). Validate turf quality and labor hour reduction metrics.

Phase 2: Months 7-18

Cluster Rollout

Deploy fleets to regional clusters (e.g., Florida) to centralize maintenance support. Launch hospitality pilots.

Phase 3: Months 18-36

Standardization

Full integration into SOPs for all new acquisitions. Centralized fleet monitoring dashboard.
