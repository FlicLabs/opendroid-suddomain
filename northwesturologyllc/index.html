Northwest Urology LLC

Robotics Analysis & ROI Portfolio

Executive Summary: Northwest Urology is positioned to shift from hospital-dependent surgery to a vertically integrated Ambulatory Surgery Center (ASC) model. The dominant ROI driver is the repatriation of robotic cases (Da Vinci) to physician-owned facilities, capturing facility fees that currently go to health systems.
Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: ASC Surgical Robotics

Primary Value Facility Fee Capture

Deploying Da Vinci systems into owned ASCs to bill both professional and technical fees.

High CapExHighest Return

Pathway B: Lab Automation

Primary Value Speed & Cost

Robotic sample processing for urine/PSA to reduce turnaround time and FTE load.

Low RiskOperational

Pathway C: Clinical Focal Therapy

Primary Value Market Differentiation

Aquablation/HIFU robotics for BPH and focal cancer therapy to attract volume.
