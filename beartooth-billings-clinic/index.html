Executive Summary

Strategic Overview

Beartooth Billings Clinic, a Critical Access Hospital in Red Lodge, MT, is positioned to leverage robotics not for industrial automation, but for specialist extension. The primary value proposition lies in "Virtual-Physical Integration" using telepresence robotics to connect rural patients with urban specialists, reducing costly transfers and retaining revenue locally.

Pathway A: Telepresence

Hub-and-Spoke Integration

Mobile robots in ER/Wards allowing specialists from Billings to treat locally.

ROI: £500k/year (Retained Admissions)

Pathway B: Pharmacy Ops

Automated Safety

Automated Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) to reduce waste and nursing travel time.

ROI: £80k/year (Efficiency)

Pathway C: Rehab Tech

Outpatient Growth

Portable robotic gait and hand therapy aids to keep physical therapy local.

ROI: £40k/year (Volume)
