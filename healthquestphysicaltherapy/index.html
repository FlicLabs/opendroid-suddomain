Executive Summary

HealthQuest Physical Therapy operates a network of 30+ outpatient clinics in Michigan. The current manual therapy model faces bottlenecks in administrative throughput and therapist capacity. The recommended strategy focuses on Administrative RPA for immediate margin lift, followed by Clinical Robotics for differentiation and Remote Monitoring for new revenue streams.
