Executive Summary: Industry 4.0 Vision

The strategic pivot for CentroMotion involves moving from high-mix discrete manufacturing to a flexible, data-driven ecosystem. By integrating Cobots and AMRs into the production of hydraulic and electronic components, we target a reduction in changeover times and a significant increase in yield for critical sectors like agriculture and transportation.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots

Deployment of UR10e/Fanuc CRX for machine tending in Power-Packer lines. Focus on safety and precise torque application.

Pathway B: AMRs & Logistics

Autonomous transport for heavy WIP (driveshafts/cylinders). Reduces non-value-added motion and forklift risks.

Pathway C: Vision & Quality

Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) for CrossControl electronics, paired with robotic end-of-line palletizing.
