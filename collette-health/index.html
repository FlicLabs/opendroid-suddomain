Collette Health: Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Collette Health (formerly MedSitter) is positioned to transition from a telepresence provider to an AI-orchestrated safety robotics platform. By integrating Computer Vision and Autonomous Mobile Robotics (AMR), the company can drastically shift the observer-to-patient ratio from 1:10 to 1:16+, unlocking millions in labor savings while reducing hospital fall liabilities.

HIGHEST VALUE

Pathway A: AI Computer Vision

Implementation of predictive algorithms to detect pre-fall behaviors (bed exits, agitation). This reduces false alarms and allows one observer to safely monitor more patients.

STRATEGIC HARDWARE

Pathway B: Autonomous Fleet

Deploying self-driving bases for observation units. Robots auto-navigate to rooms and auto-dock for charging, removing the burden on floor nurses to fetch equipment.

COMPLEMENTARY

Pathway C: RPA Integration

Software bots that automate the transcription of observation notes directly into Epic/Cerner, ensuring compliance and saving 2-5 mins per intervention.
