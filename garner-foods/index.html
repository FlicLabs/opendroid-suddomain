01. Strategic Vision

Garner Foods is positioned to revolutionize the Fast-Casual sector by integrating their legendary CPG heritage with a high-tech dining model. By deploying robotics in both Front-of-House (FOH) and Back-of-House (BOH), the brand can ensure the "Texas Pete Standard" of quality while navigating modern labor challenges.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Automation

SERVING BOTS Deployment of autonomous runners to handle food logistics.

  • Increases server face-time with guests.
  • Reduces physical fatigue for staff.
  • Accelerates table turnover.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

FRY STATIONS Automated frying arms for wings and appetizers.

  • Precise cook times for consistency.
  • Enhanced safety (distance from hot oil).
  • Automated sauce tossing mechanisms.
