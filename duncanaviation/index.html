Executive Summary

Duncan Aviation is positioned to revolutionize the MRO industry by integrating autonomous robotics into cleaning and inspection workflows. This shift addresses critical labor shortages while enhancing the precision and consistency required for high-net-worth business jet clients.

Primary Goal: Transition from labor-intensive manual detailing to "Total Asset Preservation" via automation.

Labor Redistribution

Pathway A: Cabin Automation

Deploying compact vacuuming and wiping bots designed for business jet aisle widths.

  • Reduces interior detailing time by 40%.
  • Allows staff to focus on leather care and woodwork.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sanitation for VIP hygiene assurance using slim-profile emitters.

  • Chemical-free sanitization for sensitive veneers.
  • "Certified Sanitized" seal for charter clients.

Pathway C: Exterior Systems

Semi-autonomous crawler units for exterior washing prior to paint or maintenance.

  • Eliminates fall risks from high tail sections.
  • Critical pre-paint surface preparation.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drone and crawler bots for fuselage scanning upon MRO induction.

  • Micro-fracture and corrosion detection.
  • Digitized, immutable aircraft condition records.
