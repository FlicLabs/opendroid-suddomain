Executive Vision

Green Circuits is positioned to transition from manual-heavy workflows to a data-driven Industry 4.0 model. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport, we project a 25% reduction in lead times while maintaining zero-defect standards in high-mix PCB assembly.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: COBOTS

Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of ISO/TS 15066 compliant Cobots for odd-form component insertion and ICT loading. Eliminates safety cages and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Material Transport

LiDAR-enabled Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle magazine transport between SMT lines and AOI stations. Resolves WIP bottlenecks and aisle congestion.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Palletizing & Quality

Vision-guided delta robots for final cosmetic inspection and boxing. Targeted 30% throughput increase at the shipping stage.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase

Deploy 2 Cobots (Through-Hole) & 1 AMR (SMT Line). Validate safety protocols.

Year 2: Integration

Scale AMR fleet to all SMT lines. Integrate Cobots with In-Circuit Testing.

Year 3: Optimization

AI-driven predictive maintenance and full traceability integration.
