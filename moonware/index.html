Automation represents the next frontier in aviation efficiency. This report outlines a strategic vision for Moonware to leverage autonomous robotics, reducing operational expenditures by 35-45% while elevating hygiene standards and safety protocols. By integrating AI-driven inspection and autonomous cleaning, Moonware will optimize turnaround times and establish a resilient maintenance infrastructure capable of scaling with fleet expansion.
