01. Executive Vision

The City Mission of Schenectady has a unique opportunity to transform its logistics from a manual cost-center into a high-tech "Reverse Logistics Hub." By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and Computer Vision, the Mission can process donations faster, increase resale revenue, and provide high-value technical training to program residents.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Transport)

Replacing manual cart pushing with autonomous robots to move donation bins from intake to sorting stations. Reduces worker fatigue and injury risk.

  • 70% reduction in travel time
  • Scalable fleet (add units as needed)

Pathway C: AI Vision (Grading)

Overhead cameras utilizing AI to identify brands, detect defects, and suggest pricing for donated goods automatically.

  • Standardized pricing logic
  • Increased revenue capture per item
