Executive Summary

Automating Aviation Maintenance

Precision Assembly Technologies P.a.t. is positioned to revolutionize its operational efficiency through robotic integration. By shifting from manual dependencies to autonomous systems, the organization will achieve higher hygiene consistency, drastically reduce turnaround times, and secure long-term financial stability.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-mapped debris removal and vacuuming units for rapid turnarounds.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sterilization bots ensuring 99.9% pathogen elimination.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems reducing water usage and drag for better fuel efficiency.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drone and crawler bots for predictive maintenance and micro-fracture detection.
