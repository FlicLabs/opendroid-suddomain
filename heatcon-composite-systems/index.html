Strategic Automation Research

Executive Summary

This strategic report outlines the integration of robotic automation into Heatcon's operational logistics fleet. By leveraging autonomous cleaning and AI-driven inspection bots, Heatcon can reduce MRO downtime, protect sensitive composite assets, and achieve a projected 40% reduction in operational overhead within 24 months.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LIDAR-guided vacuuming and surface disinfection for the Rapid Response Bombardier fleet, minimizing turnaround time.

B. UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade 254nm UV-C arrays to neutralize pathogens in flight decks without chemical damage to avionics.

C. Exterior Washing

Gantry-free robots providing precision cleaning to prevent environmental corrosion on composite fuselage materials.

D. AI-Powered Inspection

Thermal and ultrasonic drone scanners integrated with Heatcon repair data to detect delamination early.
