02. Robotics Pathways

Path A: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport. Eliminates cart pushing, increasing pick time from 35 to 50 mins/hour.

Path B: Piece Picking & ASRS

Goods-to-Person stations for high-velocity SKUs. Increases accuracy to 99.99% and reduces storage footprint.

Path C: AI Logistics

Predictive analytics for dynamic inventory slotting and "honeycomb" reduction.

03. Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 Foundation & Pilot: Deploy 10 AMRs in Cincinnati Hub.
Year 2 Expansion: Roll out AMRs to secondary sites; install ASRS for top 20% SKUs.
Year 3 AI Integration: Activate dynamic slotting and "Lights Out" 3rd shift.
Year 4 Optimization: Hardware refresh and battery infrastructure review.
Year 5 Maturity: Full fleet autonomy; workforce transitions to supervision.
