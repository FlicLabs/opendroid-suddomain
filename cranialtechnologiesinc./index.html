Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Robotic Fabrication (Highest Value)

Manufacturing

Implementation of 6-axis robotic arms for milling and polishing DOC Bands, replacing manual labor.

  • Key Levers: Robotic trimming via CAD data, automated buffing for safety, 24/7 production capability.
  • Impact: 60-75% reduction in finishing labor costs; 30% throughput increase.

Pathway B: Automated Quality Assurance

Quality Control

Deployment of robotic 3D scanning stations to compare finished bands against Digital Twins.

  • Key Levers: Sub-millimeter tolerance verification, automatic rejection of non-conforming units.
  • Impact: Reduced clinical refit rates and enhanced brand safety.

Pathway C: Intelligent Logistics

Supply Chain

AMRs for material handling and automated packaging for shipment to clinics.
