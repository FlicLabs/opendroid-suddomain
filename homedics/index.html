Executive Summary

Homedics requires a pivot from traditional bulk distribution to a high-velocity omnichannel model. Our research identifies that integrating AMRs for heavy pallet transport (massage chairs, air purifiers) and AI-driven piece picking for consumables (filters, oils) will stabilize operations against seasonal demand spikes.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. AMRs (Transport)

Replacement of manual forklift travel for dock-to-stock operations. Reduces incident rates by 80%.

B. Piece Picking

GTP (Goods-to-Person) systems for high-density storage of small parts and consumables.

C. AI Logistics

Predictive slotting based on weather patterns (e.g., humidifiers prioritized in winter).

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1
    Foundation & Pilot: WMS API Integration and Pilot deployment of 6 AMRs in Michigan HQ.
  • Year 2
    Expansion: Roll out Piece Picking for consumables and expand AMR fleet to West Coast nodes.
  • Year 3
    AI Integration: "Lights Out" replenishment shifts and dynamic weather-based slotting.
  • Year 5
    Full Autonomy: Predictive manufacturing linkage and network-wide standardization.
