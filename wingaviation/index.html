Executive Summary & Fleet Profile

Wing Aviation is positioned to transition from manual operations to a Hybrid Autonomous Operational System. With a fleet of 85 aircraft operating out of Teterboro, Van Nuys, and Dallas, the introduction of robotics addresses critical bottlenecks in turnaround time and hygiene consistency.

Primary Goal: Reduce average ground time while increasing sanitization standards to hospital-grade levels using UV-C technology.

Pathway A: Cabin Automation

Autonomous vacuums and waste collection bots operating during the 20-minute disembarkation window.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

High-intensity UV-C towers to destroy pathogens without chemical residue, ensuring 99.9% sterilization.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Gantry and crawler systems using 90% less water, protecting sensors and reducing corrosion risks.

Drone-based visual inspection for predictive maintenance, reducing AOG time from 4 hours to 45 mins.
