Executive Strategic Vision

California Boiler is positioned to transition from a traditional fabrication model to a digitally enabled smart manufacturing enterprise. By integrating collaborative robotics into welding and parts logistics, the organization can increase capacity by 30% while addressing skilled labor shortages in the high-mix/low-volume sector.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Priority A Cobot Welding

deployment of collaborative robots for repetitive linear welds on boiler skids. Allows master welders to focus on complex joints.

Priority B AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving heavy valves and pumps from warehouse to fabrication staging, reducing non-value travel.

Priority C Auto-Inspection

Automated 3D scanning and ultrasonic testing for quality assurance logs and regulatory compliance.
