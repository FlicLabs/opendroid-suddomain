Consultation Ref: SEA-PINES-2024

Executive Summary

The Sea Pines Resort represents a prime candidate for high-impact robotics integration due to its massive 5,000-acre footprint and labor-intensive landscape requirements. By pivoting from manual groundskeeping to autonomous turf management, and augmenting hospitality staff with service robotics, the resort can realize over $2.5M in annual operational savings while elevating the guest experience for the RBC Heritage and high-net-worth clientele.

Pathway A: Autonomous Turf Management

Scope: Autonomous mowing fleets for 4 golf courses (including Harbour Town) and common grounds.

  • 24/7 Silent Operation
  • 40-50% Labor Reduction
  • Precision GPS Spraying
High ROI Immediate Impact

Pathway B: Hospitality Robotics

Scope: Service robots for The Inn & Club, plus autonomous cleaning for large venues.

  • Room Service Delivery
  • Lobby Vacuum/Scrubbing
  • F&B Runners
Med ROI High Visibility
