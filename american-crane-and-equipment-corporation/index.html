04. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Welding

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of IP67-rated collaborative robots for sub-assembly welding. Allows skilled welders to manage multiple arcs simultaneously, increasing yield by 200%.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for line-side kit replenishment, decoupling material movement from manual labor availability.
