Executive Summary

Fifth Street Gaming (FSG) operates in a high-pressure hospitality environment within Las Vegas. With a focus on the local and Latino markets, the primary operational lever for robotics is labor augmentation in Food & Beverage and Facility Maintenance. By deploying autonomous service units and cleaning fleets, FSG can realize tens of thousands in monthly savings while modernizing the guest experience at flagship properties like Downtown Grand and Silver Nugget.
