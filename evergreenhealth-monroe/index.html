Executive Summary

EvergreenHealth Monroe is a pivotal community hospital serving the Sky Valley region. The facility faces typical challenges: staffing constraints and transfer logistics. The most potent robotics levers are (1) Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for internal logistics, (2) Telepresence for remote specialist access, and (3) Robotic Assisted Orthopedics for case retention. The dominant ROI driver is labor efficiency and reduced patient leakage.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics AMRs

Fleet of autonomous robots for meds, labs, and linen delivery.

  • Reclaims 2,500 nursing hours/year
  • Reduces staff injury claims
  • High ROI efficiency driver

Pathway B: Telepresence

Remote Stroke/ICU robots connecting to Kirkland campus.

  • Reduces unnecessary transfers
  • Immediate specialist access
  • Retains admission revenue

Pathway C: Orthopedics

Robotic arm systems for joint replacement.

  • Market differentiation
  • Standardized outcomes
  • Attracts surgical talent
