Executive Summary

Paumac stands at a pivotal threshold of Industry 4.0 adoption. Transitioning from static HMLV (High-Mix Low-Volume) production to a flexible "Smart Cell" architecture is critical. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous logistics, Paumac can decouple labor hours from production volume, ensuring higher throughput and consistent quality in tube fabrication and assembly.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Machine Tending Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

  • Automated loading for Tube Benders & CNC.
  • Eliminates operator idle time during cycles.
  • Runs through breaks for +15% uptime.
PATHWAY B

AMR Intralogistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

  • Autonomous transport of raw tubing & finished parts.
  • Reclaims 10-15% of skilled labor wasted on walking.
  • Reduces shop floor congestion.
PATHWAY C

Automated Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield

  • Vision-guided end-of-line stacking.
  • Integrated quality checks for bend angles.
  • Reduces heavy lifting and ergonomic risks.
