Executive Summary

Pinnacle Hospital is poised to transform its physician-owned surgical model through strategic robotics. By shifting focus from manual logistics to automated workflows, the hospital can maximize OR utilization and patient throughput.

Primary ROI Driver Surgical Volume & Turnover Speed
Est. Payback Period 18 - 24 Months

Pathway A: Surgical Robotics

Highest Value

Scope: Expansion of robotic-assisted orthopedic and spine platforms.

Impact: 10-15% volume increase, center of excellence status, and reduced revision rates.

Pathway B: Logistics (AMR)

Efficiency

Impact: Saves ~2,000 nursing hours/year, reducing non-clinical labor.

Pathway C: UV-C Disinfection

Throughput

Scope: Automated UV-C robots for OR terminal cleaning.

Impact: Cuts turnover time by 15 mins/case, enabling +1 case per room/day.
