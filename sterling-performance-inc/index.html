Executive Summary

Sterling Performance Inc. faces industry-wide challenges regarding therapist burnout and capped throughput. The integration of therapeutic robotics and automated workflow solutions presents a transformative opportunity. By deploying robotic exoskeleton systems and automated assessment tools, Sterling can decouple revenue growth from linear headcount hiring. The primary ROI drivers are increased patient throughput, high-margin service offerings for elite athletes, and reduced staff turnover.
