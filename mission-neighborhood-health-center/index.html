Executive Summary

Mission Neighborhood Health Center (MNHC) operates in a high-cost, high-need environment. The optimal robotics strategy focuses on operational efficiency: Pharmacy Automation, Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and Sanitation Logistics. This portfolio targets a 15% increase in patient capacity and over $500k in cumulative net operational benefits over 5 years.
