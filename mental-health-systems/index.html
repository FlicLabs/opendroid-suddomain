Mental Health Systems Inc.

Robotics & Automation Portfolio Analysis

Executive Summary

Mental Health Systems Inc. (MHS) is a non-profit organization established in 1968, focused on providing behavioral health and wellness services throughout Southern California. The primary robotics and automation opportunities for MHS exist in enhancing administrative workflows, improving physical security within facilities, and utilizing telepresence technologies to extend the reach of care.

The strongest automation levers are Administrative RPA, Facility AMRs, and Telepresence Robotics. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of administrative burden, allowing clinicians to focus entirely on patient care.
