Executive Summary

This report presents a strategic vision for integrating Open Droids' automation solutions into Pittsburgh International Airport's (PIT) operations. The focus is on achieving a "Touchless Turnaround" protocol, utilizing advanced robotics to sanitize, clean, and inspect aircraft and facilities with unprecedented speed and precision. By leveraging four distinct robotic pathways, PIT can transition from manual labor dependency to a data-driven, automated ecosystem, enhancing passenger confidence and operational throughput.

Organizational Profile

Operational Context

Pittsburgh International Airport serves as a vital regional hub, managing approximately 150-200 daily aircraft movements. The current operational model relies heavily on manual processes for cabin cleaning, exterior washing, and facility inspection.

Key Challenges

  • Labor shortages impacting turnaround times
  • Variable hygiene standards in manual cleaning
  • High water and chemical usage in exterior washing
  • Reactive rather than predictive maintenance

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous robots for vacuuming and surface wiping. Reduces turnaround time by 40%.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

High-intensity UV-C light neutralizes pathogens in hard-to-reach areas within minutes.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems reduce water usage by 30% and improve fuselage aerodynamics.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

LiDAR and visual sensors detect surface damage and cleanliness anomalies instantly.

Operational Analytics

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1: Discovery & Pilot

Procurement of 4 pilot units. Testing in maintenance hangars. Establishing data baselines for efficiency.

YEAR 2: Operational Integration

Deployment of UV-C and Cabin bots for overnight operations. Staff training on fleet management software.

YEAR 3: Full Airport Deployment

Scale to 20+ units. Introduction of exterior washing systems for narrow-body fleets.

YEAR 4: Optimization & AI

Implementation of predictive maintenance. Data-driven scheduling based on flight occupancy and weather.

YEAR 5: Sustainability & Expansion

100% coverage for sanitization. Integration of renewable energy charging for robotic fleets.
