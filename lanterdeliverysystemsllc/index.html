Executive Vision

Lanter Delivery Systems operates in a high-stakes logistics niche: unattended overnight delivery. The strategic vision moves beyond basic storage automation to High-Velocity Intelligent Sortation. By implementing AMRs and AI-driven induction, Lanter can secure its "pre-8 AM" guarantee and mitigate labor volatility.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: AMR Transport
    Replacing manual forklifts with heavy-payload AMRs to move bulk cages from inbound to outbound doors, increasing throughput by 35%.
  • Pathway B: Robotic Sortation
    Deploying modular sorting bots to handle individual parts (filters, belts, pads). This ensures 99.9% accuracy for route-specific bins.
  • Pathway C: AI Logistics
    Computer Vision tunnels to scan inbound freight dimensions instantly, allowing dynamic route planning and truck loading.
