Strategic Executive Summary

Onsite Dental operates in a high-fixed-cost environment where the profitability of every mobile unit is dictated by schedule density. The research identifies Pathway A (Logistics AI) and Pathway C (Voice Charting) as the immediate priorities. Combined, these software-based robotic solutions can effectively create "virtual vans" by unlocking capacity equivalent to adding 10-15 physical units to the fleet without the associated capital expenditure.

Recommendation: Immediate pilot of Voice AI to fund the development of the larger Logistics Engine.
