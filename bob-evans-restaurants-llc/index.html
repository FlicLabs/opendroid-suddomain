01. Executive Summary

Bob Evans represents the heart of American family dining. As the industry faces unprecedented labor shortages, the vision is not to replace the human warmth of "Farm-Fresh" hospitality, but to augment it. By integrating robotics for heavy lifting and AI for logistics, we protect the server-guest relationship.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Goal: Increase table turns and reduce physical strain.

Implementing shelf-style AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots) to run food from the expo line to the dining zone. This allows servers to remain in their sections, increasing beverage sales and guest satisfaction.

Pathway B: BOH Automated Frying

Goal: Consistency and Safety.

Automated baskets for hash browns and fried chicken products ensure consistent cook times and reduce grease-related injuries in the kitchen.

Pathway C: AI Voice Agents

Goal: Upselling and Order Accuracy.

Deploying AI to handle the high volume of carryout phone orders. This system never misses an upsell opportunity and frees up staff to focus on in-store guests.

Implementation Phase 1

Pilot Program (Year 1)
Deploy 2 robots per store in 20 Ohio locations. Establish baseline metrics.

Implementation Phase 2

Regional Rollout (Year 2-3)
Expansion to 150 locations. Integration of Voice AI for holiday ordering.

Implementation Phase 3

System Maturity (Year 4-5)
Full fleet integration, Smart Kitchen communication, and advanced data analytics.
