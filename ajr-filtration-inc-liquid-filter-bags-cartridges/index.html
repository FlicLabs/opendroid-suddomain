Executive Summary

AJR Filtration Inc. is positioned to transition from high-volume batch manufacturing to a flexible, data-driven Smart Factory. This strategic roadmap leverages Industry 4.0 technologies to automate repetitive machine tending, material transport, and palletizing. The initiative aims to elevate the skilled workforce to supervisory roles while securing consistent micron-level precision and reducing lead times for custom filtration solutions.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of collaborative robots for feeding ultrasonic welders and sewing stations. Eliminates human error in seam alignment and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport media rolls and WIP from cutting to assembly. Reduces forklift traffic and optimizes floor safety.

Pathway C: Automated QA

Focus: End-of-Line & Palletizing

High-speed robotic palletizing combined with machine vision for final ring fitment and seam integrity inspection.
