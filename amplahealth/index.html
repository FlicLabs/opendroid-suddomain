AMPLA HEALTH

Robotics & Automation Strategic Portfolio

Executive Summary: Ampla Health is a vital FQHC network in Northern California. The primary robotics opportunity lies not in expensive surgical systems, but in Process Automation, Dental Workflow Robotics, and Telerobotics. These technologies solve critical labor shortages and expand access for underserved populations.
Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Admin Automation

Focus: RPA & AI Scheduling

Deploying software robots to handle referrals, billing coding, and multilingual appointment reminders.

Impact: Reduces no-shows by ~50% and recovers admin time.

Highest ROI

Pathway B: Dental Robotics

Focus: Sterilization Automation

Automated instrument processing centers in high-volume dental clinics to improve safety and turnover.

Impact: Increases chair capacity by 15%.

Pathway C: Telerobotics

Focus: Remote Access Carts

Mobile robotic carts enabling specialists to perform exams in rural/remote clinics.

Impact: Reduced patient leakage and travel barriers.

Implementation Roadmap
Year 1: Foundation

Launch AI-driven scheduling & SMS engagement. Pilot RPA for claims processing.

Year 2: Clinical Support

Upgrade dental sterilization centers. Deploy initial telerobotic units to remote sites.

Year 3: Expansion

Network-wide telerobotics. Predictive staffing analytics for seasonal farmworker influx.

Strategic Goal: Position Ampla Health as a Tech-Enabled Safety Net, ensuring long-term financial sustainability and meeting modern federal grant requirements for innovation.
