01. Executive Summary

Sensys Networks acts as a pivotal player in the Smart City ecosystem. The strategic vision involves a transition from manual-heavy assembly to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). Adopting Industry 4.0 technologies will shift the manufacturing focus from reactive quality control to predictive process assurance, ensuring IP68 standards for all deployed sensors.

02. Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Collaborative Potting

Goal: Safety & Precision.

Deployment of IP-rated Cobots (UR5e/Fanuc CRX) for precise epoxy potting. This eliminates air bubbles and ensures consistent ruggedization for outdoor sensors.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Goal: WIP Flow Efficiency.

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) transport "wet" sensors to curing racks, reducing vibration risks and manual walking time.

Pathway C: Automated EOL Testing

Goal: Throughput & Yield.

A robotic cell places cured sensors into Faraday cages for RF testing, performs visual inspection, and packages the unit.

03. Implementation Roadmap

YR 1 Pilot Phase: Deploy Cobot Dispensing & Validate IP68.
YR 2 Integration: Automated Testing/Packing & ERP Link.
YR 3 Logistics: AMR deployment linked to cure times.
YR 4 Optimization: AI Vision & Speed increases.
YR 5 Autonomy: Lights-out assembly capability.
