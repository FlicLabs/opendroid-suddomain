EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The aviation maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) sector is undergoing a pivotal shift toward automation. For Pegasus Aircraft Maintenance LLC, integrating robotic cleaning and inspection systems represents a strategic leap. Our research indicates that by adopting autonomous cabin cleaning, UV-C disinfection, and automated exterior washing, Pegasus can achieve a 35-45% reduction in OPEX while reallocating human talent to complex maintenance tasks.
