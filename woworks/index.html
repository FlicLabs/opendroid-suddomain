Organizational Profile

Entity: WOWorks (Saladworks, Frutta Bowls, Garbanzo, etc.)

Model: Fast-Casual / Counter Service

Challenge: High ingredient customization vs. speed of service. Need to balance "freshness" with operational efficiency.

Pathway A: FOH Robotics

Automated Bussing & Delivery

Utilization of AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots) to handle "Last 20 Feet" delivery from counter to table, and subsequent tray collection.

  • Eliminates dedicated food runner shifts.
  • Increases sanitation cycles during peak hours.
  • Supports "Fast-Casual Plus" dining experience.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Precision Dispensing

Integration of automated slicers and weight-based dispensing for protein and salad bases.

  • Reduces protein shrinkage/waste.
  • Guarantees nutritional consistency (calorie counts).
  • Speeds up the assembly line bottleneck.
