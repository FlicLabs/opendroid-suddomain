ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Robots performing vacuuming and mopping during overnight layovers. Reduces physical labor and accelerates turn-around times by 50%.

B. UV-C Disinfection

High-intensity UV light neutralizes pathogens on high-touch surfaces in under 15 minutes. Residue-free and bio-safe.

C. Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems with recycled water. Reduces downtime from 6 hours to 90 minutes while improving aerodynamics.

D. AI Inspection Bots

Thermal and visual sensors in wheel wells and nacelles. Shifts maintenance from reactive to predictive.
