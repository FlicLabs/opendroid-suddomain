Executive Vision

For Beef 'O' Brady's and The Brass Tap, the goal is not to remove the human element, but to amplify the neighborhood feel. By deploying automation for high-friction tasks (heavy tray running and wing frying), staff can focus on upsells and guest interaction.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Hybrid Serving Models

Utilization of multi-shelf delivery bots to handle heavy drink trays and wing platters. Reduces server fatigue and keeps staff in the dining zone.


Pathway B: Back-of-House

Automated Frying

Integration of basket-management robotics to ensure consistent wing texture and improve kitchen safety during high-volume sports events.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot & Validation

Deploy runners in 5 high-volume FL locations. Measure ticket times.

Year 2: Regional Rollout

Expand to top 50 revenue stores. Test auto-fryers.

Year 3-5: Full Ecosystem

Full POS integration. System-wide standard for new franchises.
