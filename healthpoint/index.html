01. Executive Summary & Profile

HealthPoint faces critical pressure points in administrative burden and pharmacy bottlenecks. The analysis identifies three high-impact levers: Centralized Robotic Pharmacy (Pathway A), Administrative RPA (Pathway B), and Facility Logistics AMRs (Pathway C).

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Central Fill (Priority)

  • High-speed robotic dispensing for 70% of network volume.
  • Automated inventory management to cut carrying costs.
  • Frees pharmacists for revenue-generating clinical services.

Pathway B: Administrative RPA

  • Software bots for prior auth and claims processing.
  • Reduces claim denials and front-desk load.
  • Low CapEx, high immediate cash flow impact.
