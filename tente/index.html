01. Executive Summary

Tente International faces a pivotal opportunity to transition from discrete legacy manufacturing to a flexible, smart-factory model. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport, Tente can address the high-mix complexity of its mobility solutions catalog while reducing ergonomic strain and increasing throughput.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Assembly & Machine Tending.

Deploying force-sensing cobots for bearing insertion and wheel assembly to ensure zero-defect production.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Intralogistics.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle WIP movement between injection molding and assembly stations.

Pathway C: Palletizing

Focus: End-of-Line.

Heavy-payload arms for boxing and palletizing industrial castors, removing heavy lifting from human operators.
