Executive Summary

Outpatient Imaging Affiliates (OIA) operates a joint-venture model across 60+ locations. The primary robotics opportunity lies in shifting from manual scanner operations to AI-assisted workflow automation. By implementing auto-positioning gantries and remote command centers, OIA can unlock significant latent capacity in existing capital assets.
