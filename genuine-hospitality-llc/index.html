Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Guest Delivery HIGHEST VALUE

Deployment of autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for room amenities and food delivery. Directly addresses runner labor shortages.

  • Impact: Reduces staff trips by 15-20 per day.
  • Benefit: Front desk staff focus on high-value guest interactions.
  • Tech: Elevator integration and secure compartment locking.

Pathway B: Commercial Floor Care

Automated vacuuming and scrubbing for lobbies, corridors, and conference centers.

  • Impact: Consistent 24/7 cleanliness standards.
  • Benefit: Reallocates housekeeping labor to deep cleaning rooms.
