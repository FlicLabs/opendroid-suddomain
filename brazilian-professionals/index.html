02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Machine Tending & Assembly

Focus on safety and precision. Deploying ISO/TS 15066 compliant units to work alongside humans without safety fencing.

PATHWAY B

AMRs

Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle WIP flow efficiency, replacing manual forklifts for line-side replenishment.

PATHWAY C

Automated End-of-Line

Palletizing & Inspection

High-payload arms for palletizing combined with vision systems for quality control, eliminating shipping bottlenecks.
