01. Executive Strategy

GoJet Airlines is positioned to revolutionize regional aviation by integrating robotics into the CRJ-550/700/900 fleet operations. By moving from manual labor dependency to an automated ecosystem, GoJet can secure consistent "Premium Regional" hygiene standards while aggressively cutting turnaround costs. The strategy targets the high-frequency nature of regional hubs (ORD, DEN, EWR, IAD) to maximize robot utilization cycles.

Pathway A: Cabin Robotics

Focus: Turnaround Efficiency. Compact autonomous vacuums designed for narrow CRJ aisles to handle debris collection during 20-minute turns, freeing crew for security checks.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Focus: Health Compliance. Robotic arms utilizing UV-C light to neutralize surface pathogens on tray tables and armrests without degrading interior fabrics with chemicals.

Pathway C: Exterior Systems

Focus: Fuel & Surface. Automated gantries for frequent washing of low-altitude flying aircraft, improving aerodynamics and water conservation by 60%.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Focus: Safety. Drone-based visual inspection during overnight maintenance to predict rivet failures and lightning strike damage before AOG events occur.
