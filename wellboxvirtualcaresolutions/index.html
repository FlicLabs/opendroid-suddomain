Wellbox Virtual Care

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

01. Executive Summary

Wellbox shifts the healthcare labor paradigm by utilizing remote nurses for Chronic Care Management (CCM). The primary opportunity lies in Software Robotics (RPA) to bridge the gap between proprietary platforms and client EMRs, and AI Voice Robotics to handle routine patient triage. Automation can potentially increase nurse capacity by 40-60%, driving millions in incremental margin.


02. Opportunity Pathways
HIGHEST VALUE

Pathway A: RPA Integration

Digital workers (bots) that automate data entry between Wellbox and systems like Epic or Athena.

Impact: Reduces admin time by 30%, unlocking capacity for ~15k new patients.

SCALE DRIVER

Pathway B: Voice AI

Conversational AI agents for routine "medication adherence" and "symptom check" calls.

Impact: Filters 70% of negative checks, allowing nurses to focus on high-acuity needs.

EFFICIENCY

Pathway C: RPM Logistics

Warehouse automation for shipping, tracking, and refurbishing remote monitoring hardware.

Impact: Reduces device shrinkage by 15% and lowers logistics overhead.
