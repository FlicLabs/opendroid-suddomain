Executive Summary

The Indigo Road Hospitality Group faces the dual challenge of rising labor costs and the need for consistent service delivery across a diverse portfolio. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Front-of-House Serving
Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous runners transport heavy trays and bus tables. Reduces server fatigue and increases run efficiency during peak hours.
B. Back-of-House Automation
Focus: Consistency & Safety

Robotic arms for frying and grilling ensure precise cook times, reducing waste and minimizing kitchen injuries from hot oil or grills.
C. AI Ordering Systems
Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

AI Kiosks and Voice Agents handle order intake, eliminating errors and programmatically upselling add-ons with 100% consistency.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase
Deploy serving robots at 2-3 pilot locations (COOP & Oak Steakhouse). Implement AI Kiosks. Establish baseline metrics for labor cost and table turns.
Year 2: Optimization
Refine POS integration. Deploy BOH automated frying stations in high-volume lunch concepts. Adjust staffing models based on pilot data.
Year 3: Standardization
Standardize hardware across similar concepts. Begin rollout to 50% of the portfolio. Implement predictive maintenance analytics.
Year 4: Full Rollout
Expand automation to remaining locations. Integrate advanced AI upselling algorithms across all digital channels.
Year 5: Innovation
Evaluate next-gen technologies (busser bots, inventory drones). Achieve full ROI. Leverage data ecosystem for supply chain optimization.
