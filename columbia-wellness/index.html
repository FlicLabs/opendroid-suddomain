Healthcare Robotics Portfolio

Client: Columbia Wellness

Executive Summary

Columbia Wellness operates in a high-pressure behavioral health environment where administrative friction and safety compliance are critical bottlenecks. Our analysis identifies Robotic Process Automation (RPA) as the primary lever for immediate ROI, automating billing and intake workflows. Secondary opportunities in Automated Medication Dispensing provide critical safety infrastructure for residential units. The strategy shifts focus from manual labor to value-based care delivery.
