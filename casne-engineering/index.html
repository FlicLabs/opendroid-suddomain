Executive Vision

Casne Engineering is positioned to transition from traditional systems integration to Engineered Systems Manufacturing. By adopting flexible robotics, Casne can reduce manual assembly variability, mitigate skilled labor shortages, and deliver "Digital Twin" verified infrastructure.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of Cobots for automated enclosure modification (drilling/cutouts) and torque-critical terminal tightening. Focus on safety and precision.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle WIP flow, moving components from staging to technician build benches to maximize billable engineering time.

Pathway C: Automated Quality Control

End-of-Line Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) to verify wiring schematics and automated palletizing for heavy industrial skids.
