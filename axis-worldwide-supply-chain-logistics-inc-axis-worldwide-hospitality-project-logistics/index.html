02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Heavy-Duty AMRs

Implementation of pallet-transport AMRs to replace manual forklifts for internal dock-to-staging transport. Target: 35% increase in dock throughput.

Pathway B: Automated Dimensioning & VLMs

Utilization of Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for OS&E (linens, small electronics). This recovers 85% of floor space currently wasted on static shelving.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

A predictive logic layer that synchronizes warehouse activity with construction site readiness, automatically prioritizing inventory based on real-time project schedules.
