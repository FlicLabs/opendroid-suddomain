Executive Summary

The wholesale distribution sector is shifting from manual legacy operations to automated fulfillment. For Wiseway Supply, the strategic vision is to maintain regional leadership by integrating a hybrid robotic ecosystem. The objective is to decouple revenue growth from labor dependency, allowing for later order cut-off times and reduced return rates caused by picking errors.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Internal Transport)

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle long-haul transport of pipe, wire, and pallets. Reduces "dead walking time" by keeping associates in their zones.

Pathway B: Piece Picking & ASRS

Implementation of Goods-to-Person systems for small electrical/plumbing components. Increases storage density by 60% and accuracy to 99.9%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive route planning and cross-docking optimization to reduce dock-to-stock time.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Foundation
    Clean WMS data, standardizing SKU metrics, and deploying a 5-unit AMR pilot.
  • Year 2: High-Density Integration
    Installation of ASRS grid for top 20% of small-part SKUs.
  • Year 3: Expansion
    Scaling AMRs to 15 units and integrating AI packing algorithms.
  • Year 4-5: Autonomous Ecosystem
    Moving toward "Dark Warehouse" capabilities for night shifts.
