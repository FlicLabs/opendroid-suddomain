01. Executive Summary

DMC Engineering is positioned to transition from static automation to Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS). Our research indicates that integrating Industry 4.0 technologies—specifically collaborative robotics and autonomous logistics—will solve current throughput bottlenecks. The strategic goal is to augment skilled labor, reducing non-value-added tasks like material transport and manual machine tending.

03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly. Focus on safety and precision (0.03mm repeatability) to work alongside humans without caging.

Pathway B: AMRs

Material Transport. Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle line-side replenishment, reducing WIP stagnation and forklift risks.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Palletizing & Inspection. Automated end-of-line systems with vision integration to ensure 100% yield and continuous throughput.
