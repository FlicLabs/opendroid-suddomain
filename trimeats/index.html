01. Executive Summary

The transition to a Connected Cold Chain Factory is the primary lever for sustainable growth at Tri Meats. By integrating washdown-ready robotics and intelligent transport systems, Tri Meats can mitigate chronic labor shortages in cold environments while exceeding upcoming hygiene regulatory standards.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Deployment of IP69K-rated Cobots for tray loading and thermoform machine tending. Focus on reducing Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI) and ensuring consistent weight in vacuum-sealed products.

PATHWAY B Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

AMRs for intralogistics between Cold Storage and Packaging. Automated transport of "combos" eliminates cross-contamination risks between raw and cooked zones.

PATHWAY C End-of-Line Palletizing

High-speed robotic palletizing with integrated vision systems to inspect seal integrity and label accuracy (OCR) before shipping.
