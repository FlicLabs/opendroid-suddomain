01. Executive Summary

The strategic imperative for Beauty Quest Group (BQG) is agility. Moving beyond traditional contract manufacturing, BQG must transition to a Smart Flexible Factory. By deploying agnostic robotic systems, BQG can reduce changeover times by 40% and stabilize labor costs, securing the Roanoke facility's status as a flagship for North American automation.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of IP66-rated Cobots for automated "pucking" and "de-pucking" of bottles. This increases line speed consistency and allows human operators to focus on QC.

PATHWAY B

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: Material Transport

Utilizing heavy-payload AMRs to transport pallets of empty bottles and WIP. This reduces forklift traffic and ensures filling lines are never starved of materials.

PATHWAY C

Automated Palletizing

Focus: End-of-Line Throughput

Centralized robotic palletizing cells integrated with Vision Systems to verify label placement and lot codes, eliminating heavy lifting injuries.
