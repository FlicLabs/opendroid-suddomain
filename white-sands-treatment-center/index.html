Client: White Sands Treatment Center

Executive Summary

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

White Sands Treatment Center is a premier provider of addiction treatment in Florida. The strongest automation levers are Automated Medication Dispensing, Autonomous Service Robots for facility hygiene, and AI-driven utilization review. These technologies shift the focus from manual logistics to high-touch patient care.

Pathway A

Medication Safety

Implementation of biometric dispensing cabinets in detox units.

  • Eliminates diversion risks
  • Reduces shift-change count times
  • Lowers liability premiums

Pathway B

Service Robotics

Autonomous floor scrubbers and UV-C disinfection for common areas.

  • Consistent sanitation standards
  • 30-40% reduction in housekeeping labor
  • Visual assurance of safety

Pathway C

Administrative RPA

Automated insurance verification and utilization review.

  • Reduces administrative denials
  • Accelerates revenue cycle
  • Replaces manual data entry
