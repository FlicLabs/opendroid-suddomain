ProSmile: Robotics Opportunity Analysis

Strategic portfolio analysis for integrating robotic surgery, automated manufacturing, and process automation into the ProSmile network.

Executive Summary

ProSmile is a premier multi-specialty Dental Support Organization (DSO) operating extensively across the Northeast United States. With a network of over 80 locations and a strong focus on comprehensive care (including the specialized SmartArches dental implant network), ProSmile is uniquely positioned to capitalize on robotic precision in oral surgery.

The dominant ROI driver is the conversion of high-value implant cases and the compression of surgical time, enabling millions in incremental margin.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot & Brand

Deploy surgical robotics in 3 flagship SmartArches locations. Train lead oral surgeons. Establish marketing campaign focused on Robotic Precision.

Year 2: Scale & Production

Expand surgical robotics to top 15 performing locations. Initialize centralized automated lab hub for overnight fabrication of prosthetics.

Year 3: Optimization

Roll out RPA for backend revenue cycle management. Optimize surgical workflows based on data collected from first two years.
