Pathway A: AMRs for Transport

Eliminating picker travel time. Transitioning from 60 UPH to 180+ UPH using Shelf-to-Person technology.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Leveraging ASRS for vertical density, compressing inventory footprint by 60% and reaching 99.99% accuracy.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive inbound/outbound management using computer vision gantries to reduce dock dwell time.
