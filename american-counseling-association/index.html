Client: American Counseling Association

Executive Summary

The American Counseling Association (ACA) serves as the world's largest association exclusively dedicated to professional counseling. With a membership exceeding 58,000 professionals, the organization faces significant operational challenges in continuing education delivery, licensure portability verification, and member administrative support.

The dominant ROI driver is the implementation of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for administrative backend tasks and AI-driven simulation robotics for standardized clinical training, shifting the model from manual verification to automated, tech-enabled membership value.
