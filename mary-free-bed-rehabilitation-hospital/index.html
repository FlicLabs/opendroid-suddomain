Mary Free Bed Robotics Opportunities Portfolio

This document outlines the robotics opportunities for Mary Free Bed Rehabilitation Hospital, focusing on rehabilitation robotics, surgical automation, and operational efficiency.

Executive Summary

Mary Free Bed is a leader in rehabilitation and limb loss care. Key robotics opportunities include expanding rehabilitation robotics, investing in surgical automation, and implementing pharmacy and logistics automation. The primary ROI drivers are increased patient throughput, reduced length of stay, and improved clinical outcomes.

Estimated Annual Benefit (Pathway A)

$12 - $20 Million

Organizational Profile

  • 245-bed rehabilitation hospital
  • 7,500 inpatients and 22,000 outpatient visits annually
  • Leading pediatric and limb loss programs
  • Recent investment in robotic surgery (e.g., neurovascular)

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Rehabilitation Robotics (High Impact)

Expand robotic exoskeletons and upper-limb systems with clinical intelligence software to standardize therapy and increase throughput.

Pathway B: Robotic Surgery

Invest in platforms for orthopedics and neurosurgery to retain high-margin cases and enhance surgical precision.

Pathway C: Logistics Automation

Automate pharmacy dispensing and equipment tracking to reduce errors and improve operational safety.
