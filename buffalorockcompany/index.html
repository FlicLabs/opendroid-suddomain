01. Executive Vision

Buffalo Rock is positioned to transition from a legacy Direct Store Delivery model to a Digitally Orchestrated Fulfillment Network. By integrating heavy-payload AMRs and mixed-case palletizing arms, operations can decouple throughput from labor availability, ensuring resilience during seasonal demand spikes typical in the beverage industry.

PATHWAY A

AMR Transport

Deployment of high-capacity (1000kg+) autonomous mobile robots for internal pallet movement. Eliminates long-travel forklift runs and reduces facility congestion.

PATHWAY B

Robotic Palletizing

Automated mixed-case (Rainbow Pallet) building using robotic arms and buffering towers. Solves the primary ergonomic challenge of manual beverage case picking.

PATHWAY C

Predictive AI

Integration of predictive logic into the WMS. Uses historical seasonality data to pre-position inventory closer to loading docks during peak volume periods.
