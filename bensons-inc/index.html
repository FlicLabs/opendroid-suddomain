Executive Summary

Benson's Inc. stands as a regional leader in commercial facility management. The integration of robotics offers a transformative opportunity to shift from a headcount-based billing model to a performance-based outcome model. The primary automation levers are Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for floor care, IoT sensors for sanitation, and robotic façade cleaning.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Floor Care (AMRs)

Deployment of industrial-grade autonomous scrubbers across sites >50k sq ft. Shifts 4-6 hours of manual labor per shift to autonomous units.

Pathway B: IoT & Demand-Based Sanitation

Traffic sensors in restrooms trigger cleaning only when needed, reducing "check rounds" and cutting consumable waste by 20%.

Pathway C: Façade Robotics

Tethered climbing robots for high-rise window cleaning. Reduces insurance liability and increases cleaning speed by 300%.
