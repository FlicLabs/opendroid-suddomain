TrustHouse: Robotics Opportunity Profile

TrustHouse is positioned to transform from a labor-intensive facilities provider to an automated managed services partner. By deploying AMRs for floor care and culinary logistics, the company can reverse margin compression caused by labor shortages. The dominant ROI driver is the conversion of non-productive transit and cleaning hours into scalable, fixed-cost robotic assets.

Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Floor Care & Logistics

Impact: 1 Robot = 0.75 FTE replacement.

Payback: < 12 Months

Feasibility: High

Pathway B: Culinary

Focus: Automated Transport & Prep

Impact: 40% reduction in transit time.

Payback: 18 Months

Feasibility: Medium

Pathway C: Sensors

Focus: Predictive Maintenance

Impact: Asset longevity extension.

Payback: 3+ Years

Feasibility: Medium
