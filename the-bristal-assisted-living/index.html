The Bristal Assisted Living: Executive Summary

The Bristal Assisted Living faces the dual challenge of maintaining white-glove service standards while navigating acute labor shortages. The strongest robotics levers are Autonomous Dining Logistics, AI-Driven Safety Monitoring, and Cognitive Assistance. Implementing these technologies shifts the operational model from labor-intensive transport to high-value resident interaction.
