Strategic Robotics Integration & Automation Report

Executive Summary

FAT Brands Inc. represents a prime candidate for hospitality robotics integration due to its vast portfolio of Quick-Service (QSR) and Fast-Casual brands. The vision is to transition from traditional labor models to a "Human-Robot Collaboration" ecosystem. This strategy focuses on automating repetitive tasks to accelerate throughput, ensure food safety consistency, and mitigate labor shortages. By leveraging robotics, FAT Brands can preserve the hospitality touch while optimizing operational efficiency across 3,000+ locations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Deployment in Casual Dining (TGI Fridays) and Fast Casual (Fatburger) for bussing, runner duties, and beverage delivery. Reduces physical strain on servers, allowing coverage of larger table sections.

Table Turns +20% Labor Savings 15%

Pathway B: Back-of-House Automation

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Automated fry stations and grilling arms for QSR brands (Round Table Pizza, Fatburger). Ensures precise adherence to recipes and safety protocols, eliminating human error in high-heat environments.

Quality Consistency 99% Labor Savings 30%

Pathway C: AI-Driven Kiosks & Voice Agents

Focus: Upselling & Order Accuracy

Intelligent interfaces for all QSR locations. AI suggests add-ons based on purchase history and handles drive-thru voice ordering to reduce bottlenecks.

Check Size +10-15% Accuracy 95%+

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Discovery & Pilot

Deploy serving robots in 2-3 high-volume pilots. Integrate AI Voice Ordering at 50 drive-thrus. Establish KPI baselines.

Year 2: Optimization & Expansion

Refine fleet management. Roll out Back-of-House automated frying to 200 high-volume units. Expand franchisee support.

Year 3: Standardization

Mandate robotic bussing for new Casual Dining builds. AI Kiosks in 50% of domestic QSRs. 10% system-wide labor reduction.

Year 4: Network Effects

Full BOH automation across QSR network. Data centralization for supply chain optimization. VR/AR training integration.

Year 5: Maturity

Hybrid Workforce operating model. 30% of labor hours automated. Real-time operational intelligence across the portfolio.
