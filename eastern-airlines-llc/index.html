Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Self-navigating robots for vacuuming and trash collection. Reduces cabin cleaning time by 40% using LiDAR mapping.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots

Mobile germicidal units ensuring viral/bacterial elimination. Safety sensors halt operation upon human detection.

Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing

Automated gantry systems reducing drag and fuel consumption. Enhances paint preservation and reduces water usage.

Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection

Thermal and visual sensors for predictive maintenance. Automates FAA compliance logging and hard-to-reach inspections.
