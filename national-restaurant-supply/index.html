02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for running food and bussing tables. Reduces server fatigue and breakage.

PATHWAY B

BOH Automated Kitchen

Focus: Consistency

Automated frying and grilling arms. Ensures exact cook times for high-volume fried chicken and burger items.

PATHWAY C

AI Voice & Kiosks

Focus: Upselling

Conversational AI at the drive-thru and kiosks to increase ticket size and reduce order entry errors.
