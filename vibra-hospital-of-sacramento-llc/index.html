Executive Summary

Vibra Hospital of Sacramento is positioned to transform its Long Term Acute Care (LTACH) and Rehab model through robotics. By integrating robotic exoskeletons for gait training and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics, the facility can solve critical labor bottlenecks. The dominant driver is the shift from manual, labor-intensive therapy to high-dosage robotic rehabilitation, improving patient throughput and creating a regional center of excellence.
