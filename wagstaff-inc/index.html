Executive Vision

Wagstaff Inc. is positioned to transition from a premier hardware provider to a smart-system integrator. By adopting flexible automation within the Spokane Valley facility, the company can address High-Mix/Low-Volume challenges inherent in custom mold manufacturing.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: COBOTS

CNC Machine Tending

Deployment of collaborative robots for loading/unloading graphite and aluminum stock. Eliminates operator idle time and repetitive strain.

Pathway B: AMRs

Intralogistics & WIP Flow

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy billet tables and tooling between machining and assembly sectors.

Pathway C: QC & PALLETIZING

End-of-Line Automation

Hybrid cells combining heavy-lift arms with vision systems to inspect surface finish quality and palletize for global shipment.
