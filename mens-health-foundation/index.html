Executive Summary

The Men's Health Foundation (MHF) is positioned to transform community care through targeted robotics implementation. By addressing high-volume bottlenecks in diagnostic testing and pharmacy fulfillment, MHF can leverage automation not just for cost savings, but to drastically reduce time-to-treatment for critical HIV/STI protocols.

Lab Throughput: Manual vs. Robotic

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Diagnostic Laboratory Automation

Implementation of compact liquid handlers and automated batch processors. This reduces technician fatigue and accelerates HIV/STI panel results from 48 hours to <12 hours.

Pathway B: Pharmacy Dispensing

Robotic counting and dispensing systems for high-volume PrEP/PEP maintainers, freeing pharmacists for direct patient counseling and adherence support.

Pathway C: Service & Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for secure sample chain-of-custody transport and UV-C disinfection robots for clinical sanitization.
