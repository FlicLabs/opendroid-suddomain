Executive Summary

Nature's Way is positioned to transition from traditional GMP manufacturing to an AI-driven Industry 4.0 model. By targeting bottlenecks in gummy/softgel packaging and warehousing, the company can unlock significant throughput gains. The dominant ROI driver is Pathway A (Production Automation), projected to deliver a 25-30% uplift in line velocity and over £8M in annual incremental benefits per facility.

01. Production AI

Scope: High-speed pick & place + Computer Vision.

Impact: Eliminates manual sorting bottlenecks; increases speed from 150 to 220 units/min.
