Executive Summary

PowerRail is positioned to transition from a traditional heavy-part distribution model to a digitized "Smart Hub" ecosystem. By integrating heavy-payload AMRs and vertical storage density, PowerRail can decouple material movement from human labor schedules, drastically reducing the safety risks associated with moving locomotive components while ensuring 24/7 internal logistics capability.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Heavy Transport
Deployment of 1,000kg+ payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle engine parts and wheelsets, reducing forklift dependency and transit accidents.
Pathway B: Vertical Lift Modules (ASRS)
Utilization of VLMs for small SKU management (sensors, gaskets), recovering 85% of floor space currently utilized by static shelving.
Pathway C: Predictive AI Logistics
Integration of predictive maintenance data from rail operators to automate inventory pre-positioning and ordering.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Data sanitization and Pilot Pilot (2 AMRs + 2 VLMs).
  • Year 2: ERP Integration and Fleet Scaling to Manufacturing.
  • Year 3: AI-Driven Predictive Purchasing Implementation.
  • Year 5: Network Replication (FL & DE Hubs).
