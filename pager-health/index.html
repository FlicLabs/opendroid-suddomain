Strategic Analysis & ROI Projections for Pager Health

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Pager Health operates a comprehensive healthcare navigation and coordination platform. The strongest robotics and automation levers are Intelligent Virtual Care Assistants, Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for back-end workflows, and AI-driven predictive analytics. The dominant ROI driver is the automation of routine inquiry handling and administrative tasks, enabling human care coordinators to focus on high-complexity cases.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Intelligent Virtual Assistants & Conversational AI (Highest Value)

Deployment of advanced AI chatbots to handle Tier 1 member interactions, including eligibility checks, provider searches, and appointment scheduling.

  • Reduces human coordinator call volume by 30–40%.
  • 24/7 availability improves member engagement.
  • Estimated savings: £15–25 per automated interaction.

Pathway B: Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

RPA bots automate repetitive back-office tasks such as claims status checks and data reconciliation.

  • Reduces manual data entry errors by 80-90%.
  • Speeds up administrative workflows significantly.
  • Annual operational savings: £2–5M.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Care Navigation

Machine learning models analyze member data to predict high-risk health events and proactively suggest interventions.

  • Reduces ER utilization by 1–2 percentage points.
  • Improves chronic disease management outcomes.
  • Strategic differentiator for payer contracts.

STRATEGIC POSITIONING

Executing this roadmap positions Pager Health as a tech-enabled intelligence hub rather than just a referral service. By leveraging AI and RPA, Pager Health can offer superior scalability, lower unit costs, and demonstrably better health outcomes. This creates a sticky ecosystem for employers and payers, transforming Pager Health into a critical infrastructure layer for modern, distributed healthcare delivery.
