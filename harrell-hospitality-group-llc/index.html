Executive Summary

Harrell Hospitality Group (HHG) faces industry-wide labor shortages and rising operational costs. This research identifies three high-impact robotics pathways: Housekeeping Automation, Guest Service Delivery, and Logistics. The strategic implementation of these technologies shifts the operational model from labor-dependent to tech-enabled, reducing Cost Per Occupied Room (CPOR) and securing long-term margin stability.
