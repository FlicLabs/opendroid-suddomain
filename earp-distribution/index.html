Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deploying cold-rated Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle pallet movement. Removes forklifts from freezers, reducing energy loss and driver fatigue.

Pathway B: ASRS

Automated Storage & Retrieval for broken case picking. Increases density by 40% and ensures perfect pick accuracy for QSR clients.

Pathway C: AI Logic

Predictive outbound logistics. Using client data to pre-position inventory at the dock before orders are finalized.
