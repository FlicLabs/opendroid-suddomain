Executive Summary

Formic is positioned to revolutionize the SME manufacturing sector by transitioning automation from a Capital Expenditure (CapEx) to an Operating Expense (OpEx). The strategic vision prioritizes high-mix, low-volume flexibility, utilizing modular robotic cells to solve labor shortages. By standardizing deployment, Formic acts as the operating system for modern industrial labor.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Collaborative Machine Tending

Deploying safe, force-limited cobots (Universal Robots/Fanuc) to tend CNC machines. Focus on unblocking human operators for high-value QA tasks.

PATHWAY B AMR Material Transport

Utilizing Autonomous Mobile Robots for WIP flow. Eliminates line-side starvation and reduces fork truck congestion in tight aisles.

PATHWAY C End-of-Line Palletizing

High-speed palletization to address the most injury-prone areas of production. Integrated vision systems ensure yield quality.
