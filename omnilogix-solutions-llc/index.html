03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport

Decoupling picking from walking. Utilizing Lidar-based bots to handle movement between receiving and packing. Drastic reduction in non-value-added travel time.

Pathway B: Piece Picking

ASRS & Accuracy

High-density automated storage (Goods-to-Person). Increases vertical storage utilization by 200% and reduces picking errors by 99.9%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive Ops

Predictive analytics at dock doors. Algorithms predict volume spikes for dynamic labor allocation and smart battery management.
