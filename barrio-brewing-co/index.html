Executive Vision

Automation for Employee-Owners: As a 100% employee-owned entity, Barrio Brewing Co. is uniquely positioned to use automation not for replacement, but for retention. By offloading high-fatigue tasks (heavy tray carrying, bussing) to robotics, we protect the physical health of the staff/owners and extend their career longevity.

Key Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A (FOH): Deployment of "Runner Bots" (e.g., BellaBot) to handle the long travel distances in the industrial warehouse floor plan.
  • Pathway B (BOH): Automated frying assistance to ensure consistent bar food (wings/fries) during University game days.
  • Pathway C (AI): "Second Drink" QR ordering to bypass bar queues during peak congestion.
