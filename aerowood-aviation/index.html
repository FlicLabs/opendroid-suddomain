Executive Summary

Aerowood Aviation stands at a critical juncture where the integration of robotic process automation represents a pivotal shift from labor-intensive maintenance to precision-driven automated logistics. Our research confirms that deploying Open Droids autonomous units will standardize hygiene quality, significantly reduce ground time, and enhance operational resilience.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Narrow-aisle autonomous ground vehicles utilizing LiDAR for high-speed vacuuming and trash retrieval.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sanitation robots eliminating 99.9% of pathogens without chemical consumables.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry and drone swarm systems reducing water usage by 60% and eliminating fall risks.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision drones for rapid fuselage analysis, detecting micro-cracks and lightning damage.
