Robotics & Automation Portfolio

01 / Overview

Executive Summary

Goodside Health operates at the critical intersection of pediatric education and healthcare, serving over 1.2 million students via the SchoolMed program. The current model faces bottlenecks in diagnostic data latency and provider scheduling during erratic school hours. Our analysis identifies high-impact opportunities in Tele-Diagnostic Robotics and AI-Driven Workforce Optimization. These interventions are projected to drive net incremental benefits exceeding £70M over 5 years by increasing provider throughput and reducing unbillable encounters.

03 / Strategic Pathways

Robotics Opportunity Matrix

Pathway A: Tele-Diagnostic Terminals

Deployment of semi-autonomous diagnostic peripherals (robotic otoscopes, digital stethoscopes) to standardize remote physical exams. This reduces exam time by 3-5 minutes per patient.

Pathway B: Predictive Staffing AI

Algorithmic orchestration of the remote provider workforce using absenteeism data and local geography to dynamically staff providers, eliminating idle time.

Pathway C: Automated Logistics

Smart-cabinetry and IoT-enabled inventory tracking for point-of-care testing kits to prevent stockouts and missed billing opportunities.
