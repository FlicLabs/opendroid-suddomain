Executive Summary

Phoebe Putney Health System (PPHS) serves as the primary healthcare anchor for Southwest Georgia, operating over 1,000 beds across Phoebe Putney Memorial Hospital (Albany), Phoebe Sumter, and Phoebe Worth. With a dominant market share (approx. 85%) in its home county and a large service area covering rural territory, PPHS faces significant operational pressures: high acuity, labor shortages in a rural setting, supply chain complexity, and the need to maintain financial viability under value-based reimbursement models. The system’s scale and academic affiliation (through Mercer University and Georgia State University) provide a platform for robotic automation to drive efficiency, safety, and clinical quality.
