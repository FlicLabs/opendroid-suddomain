Executive Vision

The hospitality landscape is shifting from purely manual service to a hybrid model. For PNY Technologies, the vision is to deploy automation not to replace culinary art, but to elevate the customer experience. By integrating robotics, we eliminate logistics friction, allowing staff to focus on guest interaction.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Focus: Logistics & Turnover

Deploying autonomous delivery robots (AMR) reduces server walking distance by 60%, allowing focus on high-touch hospitality.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Automated frying arms ensure exact cook times for fries and patties, reducing food waste by 10%.

Financial Model

  • CAPEX: ~$80,000 per location (Robots + Integration).
  • OPEX Impact: Target 20% reduction in FOH labor.
  • Payback: < 14 months via labor savings and increased table turns.

Roadmap Snapshot

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase in Flagship.
  • Year 2: BOH Automation Integration.
  • Year 3: Expansion to 50% locations.
