Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Quick WinLow Risk

Deployment of Locus or Fetch robotics to handle long-distance goods transport. This reduces associate walking time by roughly 60%, allowing humans to focus purely on value-added picking and packing.

Pathway B: High-Density ASRS

High Density99.9% Accuracy

Implementation of Cube Storage (e.g., AutoStore) for the top 20% of high-velocity SKUs. This eliminates aisles, increasing storage capacity by 400% and virtually eliminating picking errors for shoe boxes and accessories.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

SoftwarePredictive

Utilization of Computer Vision for inbound manifest verification and Machine Learning for seasonal demand slotting. Ensures "hot" fashion items are automatically positioned near pack stations.
