Research Target: Peak Dental Services

Executive Summary

Peak Dental Services is positioned to leverage robotic-assisted implant surgery (Neocis Yomi) and RPA (Robotic Process Automation) to resolve critical scalability bottlenecks. By shifting complex procedures from specialists to robot-assisted general dentists, Peak can capture high-margin revenue currently lost to referral leakage. The financial model projects a 9-14 month payback period on surgical robotics with a 5-year cumulative net benefit exceeding £5M across a 5-hub pilot network.
