Executive Summary

Paragon Airways faces a critical opportunity to redefine operational efficiency. By transitioning from manual cleaning cycles to a robotics-first approach, the airline can significantly reduce Turnaround Time (TAT) and enhance hygiene standards. This report outlines the deployment of Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs) and AI-driven inspection tools to optimize the fleet of 160+ aircraft.
