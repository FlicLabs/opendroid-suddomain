Executive Summary

Greno Industries is positioned to transition from traditional discrete manufacturing to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By targeting bottlenecks in machine tending and material transport, Greno can leverage "High-Mix/Low-Volume" automation to combat labor shortages and increase throughput without sacrificing the tight tolerances required by the aerospace sector.

Robotics Opportunities

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of Cobots (UR/Fanuc) for CNC loading. Allows operators to manage 3-4 machines simultaneously. Focus on safety and reducing cycle time variance.

PATHWAY B

AMR Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to move WIP between grinding and QC. Eliminates manual cart transit and enhances shop floor flow.

PATHWAY C

Automated Inspection

Vision-based end-of-line systems for immediate dimensional verification, directly linked to CNC offsets.
