02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport.

  • Replaces manual forklift travel for long-hauls.
  • 35% Increase in pallet movement.
  • Reduces dependence on skilled forklift labor.

Pathway B: Picking & ASRS

Goods-to-Person systems for high SKU density.

  • Vertical Lift Modules for e-commerce clients.
  • 99.9% Accuracy on pick lines.
  • Increases facility storage density by 40%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive inbound/outbound modeling.

  • Computer Vision scanning tunnels.
  • Predictive slotting based on demand velocity.
  • 25% Reduction in dock-to-stock time.
