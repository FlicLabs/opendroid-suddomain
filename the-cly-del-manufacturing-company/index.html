Executive Summary

Cly-Del's transition to Industry 4.0 focuses on augmenting high-speed transfer press operations with flexible automation. By targeting material handling bottlenecks and end-of-line inspection, the facility can achieve higher throughput without compromising the deep-draw precision the brand is known for.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Collaborative arms for machine tending, specifically basket handling for degreasing and annealing stations.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for transporting raw coils and removing scrap hoppers to improve WIP flow.

Pathway C: Automated Inspection

High-speed vision tunnels and automated palletizing to ensure 100% yield quality.
