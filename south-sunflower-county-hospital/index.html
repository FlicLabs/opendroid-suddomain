Executive Summary

South Sunflower County Hospital (SSCH) is a critical access hub in the Mississippi Delta. To combat rural staffing shortages and prevent revenue leakage from patient transfers, SSCH must pivot to a technology-integrated care model. The primary robotics opportunities are Tele-presence systems for stroke/specialist retention and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for nursing logistics.
