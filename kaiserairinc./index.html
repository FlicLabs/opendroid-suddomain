01. Executive Summary & Vision

KaiserAir Inc. operates in a high-stakes niche requiring "white glove" presentation for VIP charters and corporate shuttles. The strategic vision is to transition from purely manual labor to a hybrid workforce. By deploying autonomous systems for high-precision sanitation and exterior maintenance, KaiserAir can mitigate Bay Area labor costs while elevating hygiene standards to hospital-grade levels for elite clientele.

02. Pathways A & B: Cabin & Health

Pathway A (Cabin): Autonomous vacuuming and trash-collection rovers for the Boeing 737 shuttle fleet to reduce turn time.

Pathway B (UV-C): CRITICAL Deployment of UV-C disinfection pillars to ensure pathogen-free environments for VIP passengers.

03. Pathways C & D: Exterior & Inspection

Pathway C (Exterior): Dry-wash robots to ensure California drought compliance and consistent paint preservation.

Pathway D (Inspection): AI-powered drones for General Visual Inspections (GVI), reducing downtime and predicting maintenance needs.
