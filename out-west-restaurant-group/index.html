Executive Summary

Out West Restaurant Group stands at a pivotal juncture where hospitality meets efficiency. The strategic integration of robotics is designed not to replace the human touch, but to amplify it. By automating high-friction tasks like food running and bussing, we empower servers to focus on the guest experience. This hybrid model addresses the critical challenges of labor shortages in the Western region while securing long-term profitability.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Primary Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support.

Deployment of collaborative runners (e.g., BellaBot) to transport heavy trays from the expo line to dining zones. This reduces physical fatigue and keeps servers on the floor with guests.

Pathway B: BOH Automated Frying

Primary Focus: Consistency & Safety.

Automation of the appetizer station. Robotic fry baskets ensure precise cook times for signature items while reducing burn risks for kitchen staff.

Pathway C: AI Voice Agents

Primary Focus: Upselling & Accuracy.

Integration of Conversational AI for phone orders. This system never misses a call, consistently upsells add-ons, and integrates directly into the POS system.

Financial Projections

  • CAPEX: Estimated hardware costs range between $32k - $38k per location.
  • OPEX Impact: Targeting a net monthly savings of $3,200 per location via reduced overtime and support staff hours.
  • ROI: Break-even projected between month 11 and 13, with a 220% return on hardware investment by Year 3.
