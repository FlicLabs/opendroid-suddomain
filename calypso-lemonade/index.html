Executive Summary

Calypso Lemonade is positioned to transition from a traditional high-speed linear bottling model to a flexible, data-driven manufacturing ecosystem. The strategic imperative is to decouple production volume from labor volatility, specifically addressing glass handling risks and throughput bottlenecks.


Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Cobot Quality Control

Vision-guided soft rejection for caps and labels to prevent glass breakage and ensure "Real Lemon Bit" visibility.

Pathway B: AMR Material Transport

Separating heavy machinery from humans by automating raw glass and sugar transport to line-side.

Pathway C: Automated Palletizing

High-speed end-of-line robotics to handle heavy cases, improving ergonomics and consistent stacking.
