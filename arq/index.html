The transition for ARQ is not merely about mechanization, but orchestration. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport
Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle pallet movement and cart transport. Removes non-value-add walking time from human workers.

Pathway B: ASRS

High Density Picking
Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems leveraging vertical space. Critical for e-commerce SKU proliferation.

Pathway C: AI Ops

Predictive Logistics
AI layers that manage inbound/outbound staging based on predictive order volume, reducing dock cycle times.
