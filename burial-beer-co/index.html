Executive Vision
Integration of robotics at Burial Beer Co. is not about replacing the artisanal soul of the brand, but handling the logistics of volume. By automating "the carry"—moving heavy beer trays and food across large footprints—we preserve staff energy for guest education and narrative building.
Robotics Opportunities
Pathway A (FOH): Deployment of "Runner Bots" (e.g., Servi/Pudu) to support large beer garden service.

Pathway B (BOH): Auto-lift fryers to manage burger/fry consistency during surges.

Pathway C (Digital): Integrated QR/Kiosk ordering to reduce bar line fatigue.
Implementation Roadmap
  • Year 1: Pilot 2 Runner Units at South Slope HQ.
  • Year 2: Expand to Forestry Camp; Introduce Auto-Fryers.
  • Year 3: Centralized Fleet Management & POS Integration.
  • Year 4: 50% of food running automated across all sites.
  • Year 5: Predictive Supply Chain AI integration.
Financial Snapshot
