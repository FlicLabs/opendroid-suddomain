Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Intelligent Process Automation

Focus: Admin Relief & Intake

Implementation of RPA and Conversational AI to handle triage, scheduling, and billing entry. Reclaims substantial clinical time for patient care.

Pathway B: Therapeutic Robotics

Focus: Clinical Augmentation

Deployment of social robots (Paro, Moxie) and VR therapy tools for autism and trauma support, extending therapy beyond human-only interaction.

Pathway C: Environmental Intelligence

Focus: Residential Safety

Smart sensors and automated medication dispensing for group homes. Reduces nursing travel time and improves incident response speeds.
