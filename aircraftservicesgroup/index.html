01. Executive Vision

Automating the Private Aviation Experience

Aircraft Services Group (ASG) is positioned to redefine private charter standards by integrating robotic process automation. Moving away from variable manual labor to precision robotics ensures asset preservation and increased fleet availability.

The Core Goal: Transitioning from a reactive cleaning model to a predictive, automated maintenance ecosystem that enhances the client experience.

02. Organizational Context

Target Fleet: Mixed Heavy & Super-Mid Jets

Hubs: Teterboro (TEB), Miami (OPF), Van Nuys (VNY)

Challenge: High labor costs and inconsistent turnaround times.

03. Technology Pathways

Strategic Implementation Areas

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning
    Compact LiDAR vacuums for debris removal during crew rest, freeing staff for high-detail work.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection
    Hospital-grade sterilization in < 15 mins. A marketable "Certified Sterile" safety standard.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Dry-Wash Bots
    90% water reduction. Consistent aerodynamic polishing for fuel savings.
  • Pathway D: AI Maintenance Crawlers
    Micro-drones for fuselage inspection to predict maintenance needs.
