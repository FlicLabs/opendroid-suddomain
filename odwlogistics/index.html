01. Executive Summary

The logistics landscape is shifting from static storage to dynamic, velocity-driven fulfillment. This research outlines a strategic pivot for ODW Logistics toward Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) and capital-purchased automation. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI, ODW can transition from labor-dependency to scalable, tech-first fulfillment.

PATHWAY A

AMR Internal Transport

Deploying collaborative robots (Locus/Fetch) to assist human pickers.

  • ROI: 50% reduction in travel time.
  • Throughput: Increase from 60 to 150 UPH.
PATHWAY B

Piece Picking & ASRS

High-Density storage solutions for e-commerce volume.

  • Accuracy: 99.9% order accuracy.
  • Density: 60% footprint compression.
PATHWAY C

AI Inbound/Outbound

Computer vision for dock management and dimensioning.

  • Efficiency: Predictive labor scheduling.
  • Speed: Optimized dock turnaround.
