Organizational Profile & Strategy

Core Operations

Regional network of 40+ skilled nursing and rehabilitation centers across Virginia and North Carolina. The primary driver is the LifeWorks Rehab brand, focusing on high-acuity recovery.

Automation Drivers

Addressing clinical labor shortages, reducing CNA travel time, and differentiating the LifeWorks brand through advanced rehabilitation technology.

Pathway A: Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Automating dietary transport and linen delivery to reduce CNA hallway time by 2-3 hours per shift.

ROI: 12-18 Months

Pathway B: Clinical

Robotic Rehabilitation

Exoskeletons and gait trainers to validate LifeWorks Rehab as a high-tech recovery center of excellence.

ROI: 24 Months

Pathway C: Service

Telepresence Fleet

Remote rounding for wound care and psychiatry specialists in rural centers to prevent readmissions.

ROI: Immediate
