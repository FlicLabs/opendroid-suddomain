OPEN DROIDS
Research Portfolio: Pursuant Health

Executive Summary

Pursuant Health operates a massive network of 4,600+ FDA-cleared health kiosks, largely within Walmart locations. The primary robotics opportunity is transitioning these assets from passive screening units to active, autonomous clinical outposts. By implementing IoT predictive maintenance and integrating robotic diagnostics (retinal imaging, autonomous vascular monitoring), the company can drastically reduce fleet downtime and unlock high-value clinical reimbursement codes, shifting the revenue model from advertising to value-based care.
