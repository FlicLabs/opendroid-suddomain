Executive Vision

Zoeller Company is poised to transition from legacy assembly to a flexible, automated ecosystem. By integrating Cobots and AMRs, we address labor shortages while maintaining the brand's legendary reliability. The goal is intelligent augmentation, not total replacement.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Collaborative Assembly

Deploying IP67 Cobots for machine tending and screwdriving. Increases safety by removing operators from repetitive strain tasks.

PATHWAY B AMR Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle heavy cast iron transport between foundry, machining, and assembly lines.

PATHWAY C End-of-Line Systems

Automated pressure testing and vacuum palletizing to eliminate bottlenecks at the final stage of production.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot 2 Cobots (Motor Assembly) & 1 Palletizer.
  • Year 2: Deploy AMRs for material flow & IT/OT Dashboard integration.
  • Year 3: Full scale replication across Sewage and Effluent lines.
  • Year 5: Digital Twin & Predictive Maintenance AI.
