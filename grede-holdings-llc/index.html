Executive Vision

Grede Holdings is positioned to transition from a traditional foundry model to a "Smart Foundry" ecosystem. By targeting high-fatigue areas (grinding/pouring) for automation, Grede can resolve labor shortages while enhancing consistency in safety-critical iron castings.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Finishing & Tending

Deployment of force-sensitive Cobots for grinding, deburring, and deflashing iron castings. Reduces ergonomic strain and ensures 100% machine utilization during tending cycles.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to replace forklifts for moving sand cores and cooled castings. Increases safety and reduces WIP stagnation.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Automation

Heavy-payload arms for palletizing brake drums and suspension parts, integrated with vision systems for automated porosity inspection.
