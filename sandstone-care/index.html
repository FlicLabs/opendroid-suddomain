Executive Summary

Sandstone Care faces the dual challenge of maintaining high staff-to-patient ratios for safety while managing multi-site operational overhead. The primary levers for automation are Automated Vitals Monitoring, Telepresence Robotics, and Service Robotics. Implementing these technologies shifts the operational model from labor-intensive manual surveillance to exception-based management, significantly improving margins and patient safety.
