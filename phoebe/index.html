Region: Southwest Georgia

Executive Summary

Phoebe Putney Health System (PPHS) has established itself as a regional robotics hub. With successful surgical robotics programs and a track record of saving $3M in labor costs and $10M in revenue capture through automation, PPHS is prime for Intralogistics Robotics (AMRs) and Surgical Scaling. The focus is on reallocating clinical labor to high-value care while automating manual transport and administrative audits.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Revenue Expansion

Pathway A: Surgical Robotics Scaling

Expanding the da Vinci Xi fleet to address colorectal and thoracic backlogs. Phoebe Sumter's success (1k procedures) proves regional demand.

Benefit: $10M - $15M Annual Margin Increase.
Operational Efficiency

Pathway B: AMR Logistics (AMRs)

Implementing AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots) for internal pharmacy and lab delivery to reduce nurse "travel time."

Benefit: $3M - $5M Annual Labor Reallocation.

Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Deploy AMR pilot in pharmacy and lab transport at Albany flagship hospital.

Phase 2 (Months 6-18): Scale da Vinci Xi program to Thoracic and Colorectal service lines; integrate RPA with Meditech.
