Executive Vision: Zero-Trust Logistics

The future of McCabe Associates lies in the transition from traditional secure warehousing to a Zero-Trust Automated Logistics model. By deploying autonomous systems, we minimize human contact with sensitive assets, reducing shrinkage risks and maintaining an unbroken digital chain of custody. This represents a shift from static shelving to dynamic, intelligent retrieval systems.

Throughput Velocity: Manual vs. Robotic

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport. Autonomous Mobile Robots utilize biometric locking modules to ensure chain of custody during transit between intake and vaults.

Pathway B: ASRS

High Density Storage. "Dark Warehouse" technology where robotic arms retrieve bins, increasing storage density by 300% and eliminating theft risk.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive Security. AI models analyze weight, scan times, and movement patterns to flag potential security anomalies in real-time.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Deploy 3 AMRs in flagship secure facility. Integrate WMS with fleet manager.
Year 2: ASRS Deployment Install high-density grid storage. Eliminate human foot traffic in storage zones.
Year 3: AI Integration Launch predictive analytics and achieve 24/7 dark warehouse capability.
Year 4: Expansion Replicate model across two additional global metro hubs.
Year 5: Ecosystem Full integration of drone surveillance and certification as global standard for Automated Secure Logistics.
