Experiential Manufacturing & Logistics Portfolio

Executive Summary

Hargrove, a key division of Encore, faces high labor costs and tight turnaround times in large-scale event production. The strongest automation levers are Automated Fabrication (CNC/Robotic Arms), Autonomous Event Layout (Printing Robots), and Robotic Warehousing. These initiatives project a potential annual net margin increase of over $2M with payback periods under 12 months.
