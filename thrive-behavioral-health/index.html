Executive Summary

Thrive Behavioral Health is positioned to transform community care through a strategic deployment of "software robotics" and assistive technologies. By shifting the automation focus from heavy machinery to high-volume administrative processing and medication safety, Thrive can reclaim over 45,000 clinical hours annually. This portfolio outlines a low-risk, high-reward strategy centering on Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and telepresence to combat workforce shortages and improve patient outcomes.
