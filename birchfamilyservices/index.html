Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Prepared for Birch Family Services

Executive Summary

Birch Family Services is positioned to transform its delivery of care through a strategic blend of Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) and back-office automation. By moving beyond traditional labor models, Birch can address the critical DSP shortage and enhance therapeutic outcomes for individuals with autism. The strategy focuses on high-impact, low-risk technologies: social robots for classroom engagement, smart home ecosystems for resident independence, and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to reclaim thousands of administrative hours.

Organizational Profile & Bottlenecks

The Scale

30+ Locations across NYC supporting 2,000+ individuals annually.

Operations: Early Childhood Centers, 853 Schools, and Residential Group Homes.

The Challenge

Staffing: High turnover of DSPs and Special Ed teachers.

Compliance: Heavy administrative burden for Medicaid/OPWDD reporting.

The Goal

Shift from manual compliance to automated workflows.

Shift from repetitive drilling to robotic-assisted therapy.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Social Robotics
