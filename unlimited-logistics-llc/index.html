Executive Vision

Unlimited Logistics LLC stands at the threshold of a hospitality revolution. By integrating robotic automation into your current service model, we project a shift from manual, low-value logistics to a high-value "Guest First" ecosystem. This strategy targets the specific friction points of food running and table bussing, ensuring your staff spends less time walking and more time selling.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Deployment: Autonomous Serving Robots (Keenon/Pudu).

Focuses on eliminating the "runner" role. Robots handle the 30% of shift time previously spent on transit, allowing servers to manage larger sections.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Deployment: Automated Frying Assistants.

Targets consistency and safety. By automating the fry station, we reduce liability for burns and ensure exact cook times for every order.

Pathway C: AI Agents

Deployment: Drive-Thru & Kiosk Voice AI.

Focuses on upselling. AI agents never forget to suggest add-ons, driving an estimated 8-12% increase in Average Ticket Value.
