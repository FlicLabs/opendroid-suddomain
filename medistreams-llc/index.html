Executive Summary

MediStreams sits at the critical intersection of healthcare finance and data logistics. By shifting from rule-based OCR to Cognitive Software Robotics (RPA) and AI-driven predictive analytics, the organization can reduce manual intervention in remittance processing by over 85%. This analysis projects a net incremental margin expansion of £2M+ annually through the deployment of intelligent automation.
