Tryon Medical Partners

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Opportunity: Operational Efficiency & Independence Preservation

Tryon Medical Partners can leverage robotics not for surgery, but for high-velocity operations. By implementing Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for admin tasks and automation for lab processing, Tryon can generate an estimated $6.5M - $8M in annual net benefit while reducing provider burnout.

Organizational Profile

Structure Independent, physician-owned multi-specialty practice based in Charlotte, NC.
Scale 100+ Providers, 150,000+ Patients, ~10 Locations.
Core Bottlenecks Referral management, schedule density optimization, and administrative burden.
Strategic Goal Maintain independence from health systems via superior operational efficiency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Administrative "Software Robotics" (RPA) Highest ROI

Scope: Deploy automated bots for prior authorizations, patient self-scheduling, and referral loop closure.

Impact: Reduces admin cost per visit by ~15%; Fills schedule gaps to increase revenue.

Pathway B: Central Lab Automation Logistics

Scope: Modular automation for sample sorting, centrifuging, and aliquoting at the central hub.

Impact: Faster turnaround times; ability to insource complex testing; error reduction.

Pathway C: Clinical Ambient Intelligence Provider Experience

Scope: AI-driven ambient listening to automate clinical charting (robotic scribing).

Impact: Saves 1-2 hours/day per doctor; enables higher patient throughput.

Financial Model (Annualized)

Scenario: Implementation of Admin RPA + Clinical Ambient AI across 100 providers.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Digital Foundation (Months 1-6)
Launch RPA for billing/prior auth. Pilot ambient AI with 10 primary care physicians.
Phase 2: Laboratory Upgrade (Months 7-12)
Install pre-analytic modular automation in central lab. Scale ambient AI to specialists.
Phase 3: Integration (Months 13-24)
Full autonomous scheduling integration. Evaluate physical logistics robots for large clinics.

Strategic Positioning

Implementing this portfolio transforms Tryon Medical Partners into a Tech-Enabled Independent Practice. This operational excellence acts as a defensive moat against large health system consolidation and serves as a primary recruiting tool for high-value physicians seeking to escape administrative burnout.
