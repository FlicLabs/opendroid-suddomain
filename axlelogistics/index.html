02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal cross-dock transport. Eliminates forklift congestion and increases safety.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking

Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems for high-density kitting and re-packaging services. Reduces storage footprint by 40%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive algorithms that pre-assign robotic missions based on inbound freight data before trucks arrive at the dock.
