Executive Summary

Eagle Telemedicine is pivoting from a service-based model to a technology-enabled logistics platform. By transitioning from static carts to Autonomous Mobile Telepresence Robots (AMTRs), Eagle can eliminate the "nurse-as-chauffeur" bottleneck. This shift liberates nursing labor, increases physician throughput, and creates a defensive moat in the competitive acute-care telemedicine market.
