Executive Summary

Anderson Moore Construction Corp. (AMCC) is positioned to define the next generation of hospitality infrastructure. By transitioning from standard general contracting to "Automation-First" construction, AMCC will provide critical structural support for robotics in dining environments. This strategy targets the integration of reinforced flooring for AMRs, high-voltage BOH robotic circuits, and AI-optimized layouts for clients ranging from Darden Restaurants to fast-casual leaders.
