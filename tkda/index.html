Executive Vision

TKDA is positioned to pivot from static engineering design to dynamic Industry 4.0 leadership. By integrating Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS) within internal prototyping and client facilities, TKDA will transition to adaptive automation. This strategy targets high-mix, low-volume production essential for the Rail and Aviation sectors.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly.

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for CNC tending and welding, eliminating safety cages and reducing operator fatigue.

Pathway B: AMRs for Material Transport

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots (500kg+) to automate transit of sub-assemblies in large hangars, reducing floor congestion.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Automation

Focus: Throughput & Yield.

Vision-guided systems for final quality inspection and automated palletizing to ensure 100% yield quality.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation

Site surveys and Pilot Cobot deployment (Welding/Tending).

Year 2-3: Scale & Connect

AMR Fleet integration and MES connectivity.

Year 4-5: Smart Standard

Predictive AI maintenance and turnkey "Smart Facility" certification.
