01. Executive Summary

Patterson Fan Company Inc. is positioned to transition from traditional discrete manufacturing to a connected, modular ecosystem. The integration of collaborative robotics (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) will address labor constraints and increase throughput for high-demand industrial air movement solutions. The strategy prioritizes safety, flexibility, and a high-mix/low-volume production capability.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for welding fan cages and fastening motors. Focus on reducing ergonomic strain and ensuring weld symmetry.

Pathway B: Material Transport (AMRs)

Replacing forklift dependency with heavy-payload AMRs to move steel housings and finished sub-assemblies. Enhances shop floor safety and WIP flow.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Palletizing

Automated lifting of 50+ lb boxed fans using vacuum grippers. Includes integrated machine vision for dynamic balancing checks.
