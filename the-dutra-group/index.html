Executive Vision

The Dutra Group is positioned to revolutionize marine construction logistics by shifting from manual MRO processes to an automated, resilient supply chain. By integrating heavy-payload AMRs and AI-driven predictive maintenance, Dutra can significantly reduce vessel downtime, ensuring that dredging fleets and aggregate terminals operate at peak efficiency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Heavy Internal AMRs

Focus: Fabrication Yards & Repair Depots.
Tech: 1000kg+ Payload Autonomous Mobile Robots.
Impact: Automates movement of heavy pumps and valves, allowing technicians to focus on repair rather than transport.

PATHWAY B

High-Density ASRS

Focus: MRO Spare Parts Inventory.
Tech: Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs).
Impact: Reduces storage footprint by 85% and eliminates stockouts of critical fasteners and electronics.

PATHWAY C

AI Predictive Logistics

Focus: Fleet Uptime.
Tech: Machine Learning Telemetry.
Impact: Predicts component failure on dredging vessels and pre-positions parts before breakdown occurs.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Foundation
    WMS Upgrades and VLM installation for small parts.
  • Year 2: Pilot Deployment
    Heavy AMRs deployed in fabrication yards; AI pilot for critical spares.
  • Year 3-5: Scale & Integration
    Full telemetry integration and autonomous yard operations for aggregate materials.
