THE RECREATIONAL GROUP

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

RG is a vertically integrated leader in recreational surfacing (XGrass, VersaCourt). Operational constraints include labor shortages and high logistics costs for heavy materials.

Dominant ROI Driver
Labor & Logistics Optimization

Organizational Profile

Model: Manufacturing, Distribution, Franchise Install

Pain Points: Heavy material handling (2,000lb rolls), manual installation consistency, seasonal spikes.

Revenue: Driven by material sales + installation services.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: High Efficiency

Warehouse & Manufacturing

AGVs for heavy turf rolls and automated tile palletizing.

Impact: -30% Warehouse Labor, Reduced Injuries.

Pathway B: Strategic

Field Installation Bots

GPS line marking robots and automated infill spreaders.

Impact: Faster job completion, 100% accuracy.

Pathway C: Complementary

AI Design Automation

Software robotics for CAD-to-manufacturing workflow.

Impact: Instant quotes, zero picking errors.

~$1.2M
