1. Executive Summary

Alta Warehousing & Logistics is positioned to transition from a labor-heavy 3PL model to an AI-orchestrated fulfillment network. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and predictive AI, Alta can achieve a 40% increase in throughput capacity without expanding physical footprint. This strategy mitigates labor volatility and positions Alta as the region's technology leader.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Replacement of long-haul manual forklift travel with Autonomous Mobile Robots. This reduces non-value-added travel time by 60%, allowing human operators to remain in active zones.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking & ASRS

High-density automated storage for slower-moving SKUs. Vertical utilization allows for 3x inventory storage in the same footprint compared to standard racking.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

Predictive logic layers utilizing computer vision to audit outbound loads and optimize dock scheduling, reducing truck turnaround time by 25%.
