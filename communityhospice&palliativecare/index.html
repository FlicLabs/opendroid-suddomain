Executive Summary

Community Hospice (NE Florida) faces the "Silver Tsunami" challenge: increasing patient census vs. clinical labor shortages. The strategic pivot is shifting from purely human-labor scaling to technology-enabled care. The primary value lever is Field Logistics AI to recapture lost windshield time, followed by Telepresence Robotics to reduce after-hours triage burdens.
