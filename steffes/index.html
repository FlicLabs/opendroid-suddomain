01. Executive Summary

Steffes stands at a critical juncture to leverage Smart Manufacturing to mitigate labor shortages inherent in the North Dakota region. By integrating flexible automation, specifically collaborative robotics and autonomous material transport, Steffes can decouple labor hours from production volume. This strategy prioritizes the retention of skilled welders for high-value tasks while automating repetitive machine tending.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Cobot Machine Tending

Deployment of IP66-rated Cobots at press brake and laser stations. Reduces operator fatigue and ensures consistent bend angles.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) for transporting heavy steel parts between cutting, bending, and welding islands.

PATHWAY C

Auto-QC & Palletizing

End-of-line systems for ETS components using machine vision to inspect paint quality while stacking for shipment.
