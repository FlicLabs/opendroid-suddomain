02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending

Focus: Safety & Precision.

Deployment of 6-axis cobots for CNC routing and press brake tending. Removes operators from hazardous zones and ensures consistent machine utilization.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport cut materials (acrylic/wood) between fabrication shops, reducing forklift traffic and indirect labor costs.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield.

Automated palletizing coupled with vision inspection systems to ensure 100% aesthetic compliance before shipping.

03. Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 Pilot Program: 2 Cobots + 1 AMR in Metal Fab.
Year 2 Expansion to Wood & Print divisions. Fleet Management setup.
Year 3 Vision System integration for Quality Control.
Year 4 Lights-out capability for CNC machining.
Year 5 Full AI-driven production scheduling.
