Executive Summary

Eye Health America (EHA) stands at a pivotal juncture to leverage robotics not just for clinical prestige, but for massive operational throughput in its Ambulatory Surgery Centers (ASCs). The strategy prioritizes throughput volume in cataract surgeries, the economic engine of the network.

Executing this roadmap transforms Eye Health America into a Tech-Enabled Surgical Network.
