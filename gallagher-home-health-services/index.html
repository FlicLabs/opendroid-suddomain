Executive Summary

Gallagher Home Health Services faces significant challenges in clinical labor costs and regional travel inefficiencies across Western Pennsylvania. Our analysis identifies three primary automation levers: AI-Driven Logistics, Telepresence Robotics, and Staff Safety Exoskeletons. Implementation of Pathway A (Logistics) alone is projected to unlock nearly $5M in annual incremental revenue through increased clinician capacity.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: AI Logistics

Scope: Dynamic territory rebalancing and predictive routing for 100+ clinicians.
Impact: Converts drive time to care time. Immediate revenue lift.

Pathway B: Telepresence Robotics

Scope: Remote "drive-in" robots for high-risk chronic patients (CHF/COPD).
Impact: Reduces hospital readmissions and LUPA penalties.

Pathway C: Staff Safety Exoskeletons

Scope: Active-assist wearables for lifting and transfers.
Impact: Decreases workers' comp claims and improves retention in a tight labor market.
