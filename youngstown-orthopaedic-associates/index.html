Executive Summary

Youngstown Orthopaedic Associates (YOA) is uniquely positioned to capitalize on the industry shift of joint replacements to Ambulatory Surgery Centers (ASCs). The dominant ROI driver is the retention of high-margin surgical cases and the attraction of new patient volume through marketed technological superiority via Robotic-Assisted Joint Replacement (RAJR).

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Surgical Robotics

Implementation of robotic-arm assisted surgery systems (Mako/ROSA) for TKA/THA in the ASC.

Pathway B: Diagnostic Automation

AI-driven MRI workflow optimization to reduce scan times and automate scheduling.

Pathway C: Robotic Rehab

Exoskeletal gait trainers for post-op recovery to close the loop of care.
