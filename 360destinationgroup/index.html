360 Destination Group: Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Executive Summary: The integration of telepresence robotics and AI-driven logistics offers 360DG a unique opportunity to compress sales cycles and optimize fleet management. The primary ROI driver is the reduction of client travel friction and the automation of complex transportation logistics.

1. Organizational Profile

Model: Full-service Destination Management Company (DMC) covering event design, transport, and logistics.

Scale: Nationwide US presence (California, Texas, Florida, Chicago, NYC, Vegas).

Operational Friction: High costs associated with manual site inspections, complex shuttle logistics, and labor-intensive event staffing.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Telepresence Robotics (Sales & Operations)

Concept: Deployment of mobile telepresence units in partner venues to allow remote clients to conduct "walkthroughs" without travel.

Impact: Reduces sales cycle by 30-40%; eliminates thousands in non-billable travel costs.

Pathway B: Intelligent Transportation Logistics

Concept: AI-driven dispatching software to optimize shuttle loops for large conventions.

Impact: 10-15% reduction in vehicle charter spend; improved attendee satisfaction.

Pathway C: Experiential Robotics

Concept: Rental fleet of robotic bartenders, wayfinding kiosks, and drones.

Impact: New revenue streams and brand differentiation as an innovation leader.
