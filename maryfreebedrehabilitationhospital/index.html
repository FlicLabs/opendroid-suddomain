Mary Free Bed Rehabilitation Hospital

Robotics Opportunities & Strategic ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

Mary Free Bed is a regional giant in sub-acute and post-acute care. While historically an early adopter of therapeutic robotics (exoskeletons, Lokomat), the next frontier of value creation lies in operational fleet automation and data interoperability. By deploying service robotics to handle logistics in their expanded campus, MFB can recover thousands of nursing hours, while data integration of therapeutic devices will drive research grants and reimbursement maximization.
