METROCARE SERVICES

Robotics & Automation Strategic Portfolio

Executive Summary

Metrocare Services is the largest provider of mental health services in Dallas County, serving 55,000+ individuals. The automation strategy shifts focus from manual administrative tasks to high-value clinical interaction.

Top Opportunities:

  • Pharmacy Automation: High-volume robotic dispensing to improve adherence.
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA): Automating claims and compliance reporting.
  • Therapeutic Robotics: Augmenting autism and IDD therapy programs.

Strategic Positioning

By automating the backend (RPA) and the pharmacy (Robotics), Metrocare stabilizes its financial core. This allows the organization to pivot resources toward the "human" element of care—case management and therapy—while using social robotics as a cutting-edge differentiator in the grant funding landscape.
