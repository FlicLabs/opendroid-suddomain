Executive Summary

IMS stands at a critical juncture where retail execution complexity meets automation scalability. The vision proposed is "Precision at Speed."

By transitioning from manual kitting to a hybrid automated workflow, IMS can stabilize operational costs against market volatility and increase throughput capacity by 250% during peak seasons.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Deploy Lidar-based deck-load robots (MiR/Fetch) to reduce non-value-added walking time by 50%. High flexibility for changing campaign layouts.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Utilize ASRS and suction-based robotic arms for high-mix merchandising items. Reduces storage footprint by 60% and eliminates kitting errors.

Pathway C: AI Predictive Logistics

Machine Learning integration to synchronize inbound print vendor supply with outbound shipping, pre-slotting inventory based on campaign predictions.
