Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Machine Tending.

Deployment of collaborative robots for CNC loading and unloading. Eliminates wait times and allows high-density floor layouts without safety caging.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Material Transport.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace static conveyors and manual forklifts. Improves WIP flow and reduces facility accidents.

Pathway C: Vision & EOL

Focus: Palletizing & Quality.

Automated end-of-line systems with integrated Machine Vision. targets 100% inspection rates and a 200% increase in packing speed.
