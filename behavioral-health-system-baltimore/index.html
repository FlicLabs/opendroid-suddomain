Executive Summary

BHSB acts as the system orchestrator for Baltimore's public mental health. The primary opportunity is shifting from reactive administration to AI-driven "Care Traffic Control." By automating crisis dispatch and network bed management, BHSB can divert patient volume from high-cost emergency rooms to appropriate community care, generating millions in system-wide savings.
