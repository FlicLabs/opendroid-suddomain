Organizational Profile & Strategy

Current State

Weber LLC operates in Discrete Manufacturing with a centralized facility (approx. 150k sq. ft.). Current processes rely on a mix of manual and semi-automated assembly, creating bottlenecks in material handling and end-of-line packaging.

  • Production: 5k - 10k units/month
  • Shifts: 2-shift system (16 hours)
  • Primary Process: Batch Assembly & Fabrication

Industry 4.0 Vision

To transition from reactive manufacturing to a proactive, data-driven Smart Factory. This involves deploying cyber-physical systems that enable flexible manufacturing—adapting to custom orders rapidly without sacrificing efficiency.

Core Objective: Elevate human workforce to supervision roles while automating hazardous/repetitive tasks.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Cobots (Machine Tending)

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deploying collaborative robots for CNC tending and assembly. Safe for human interaction, no caging required.

  • ±0.03mm Repeatability
  • Lights-out operation
  • Reduced ergonomic strain
Pathway B

AMRs (Logistics)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Autonomous Mobile Robots for material transport and line-side replenishment.

  • Dynamic path planning
  • Eliminates forklift dependency
  • Real-time inventory tracking
Pathway C

Automated End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Robotic palletizing combined with machine vision inspection.

  • 100% Visual inspection
  • High-speed packaging (60+ UPH)
  • Consistent load stability
