01. Executive Vision

ANS Geo Inc. is positioned to transition from legacy batch processing to a Smart Flexible Manufacturing Ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport, we decouple labor hours from production volume. This "Autonomy with Human-in-the-Loop" strategy ensures skilled technicians focus on high-value calibration while automation handles repetitive material movement.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Integration

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for CNC machine tending. Focus on safety and precision to double operator output.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for line-side replenishment, creating a "Pulled" inventory system to reduce congestion.

Pathway C: Auto-QC

Vision-guided palletizing systems to ensure 100% outbound quality control and packaging uniformity.
