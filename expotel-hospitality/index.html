Executive Summary

Expotel Hospitality is positioned to revolutionize its third-party management model by integrating autonomous solutions. The current analysis identifies three high-impact levers: Automated Public Area Cleaning, Autonomous Guest Delivery, and Back-of-House F&B Support. Implementing this portfolio shifts the operational focus from crisis-level labor management to high-touch guest service, driving an estimated $250k - $300k incremental benefit per full-service property over 5 years.

Opportunity Pathways
Pathway A

Automated Cleaning

Scope: Autonomous vacuum sweepers and scrubbers for corridors and ballrooms.

Impact: Offloads 2 hours/shift per worker. Consistent sanitation standards.

Pathway B

Guest Delivery

Scope: Mobile robots for room service, amenities, and external food delivery.

Impact: Increases RevPAR via 24/7 service. Boosts social media engagement.

Pathway C

F&B Automation

Scope: Robotic food runners and bussing tubs in dining areas.

Impact: 15-20% faster table turnover. Reduces physical strain on staff.
