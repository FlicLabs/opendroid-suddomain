02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Machine Tending

Deploying Cobots (Universal Robots/Fanuc) for CNC Mill and Lathe tending. This enables "lights-out" manufacturing and increases spindle uptime from 65% to 85% while improving safety.

Pathway B: AMR Material Transport

Utilizing Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving heavy tool steel and blanks. This interfaces with MES to automate line-side replenishment, reducing non-value-added travel by 40%.

Pathway C: Automated QC & Palletizing

Implementation of high-resolution vision systems for dimensional verification of cutting edges, ensuring 100% inspection rates and a 20% increase in packaging speed.
