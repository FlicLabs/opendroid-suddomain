Executive Summary

Anderson Engineering is positioned to pivot from a traditional discrete job-shop model to a high-connectivity Smart Factory. By integrating IP67-rated Collaborative Robots and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), we project a reduction in non-value-added motion by 40%.

This strategy moves operations from reactive scheduling to predictive workflow optimization, securing a competitive moat in the precision processing sector.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of Cobots (10kg payload) for CNC loading. Enables one operator to manage three machines simultaneously, shifting focus from loading to quality control.

PATHWAY B

AMR Material Transport

Fleet of AMRs for line-side replenishment. Eliminates forklift congestion and reduces operator walking time by 25%.

PATHWAY C

Automated Palletizing

End-of-line vacuum gripping and vision inspection systems to ensure 100% yield consistency and reduce ergonomic injuries.
