STRATEGY

Executive Summary

Steele Canvas Basket Corp represents a unique intersection of heritage American craftsmanship and industrial utility. Our strategic vision focuses on a Hybrid Manufacturing Model. By introducing flexible automation for material transport and repetitive machine tending, Steele Canvas can maintain its "Made in USA" integrity while drastically reducing non-value-added time (NVAT). The goal is not replacement, but augmentation of the skilled workforce.

AUTOMATION

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly

Deploying Universal Robots or Fanuc CRX series in the metal forming department to feed wire benders. Reduces RSI risks and ensures frame geometry consistency.

Pathway B: AMRs

Intralogistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport cut canvas patterns to sewing stations. Eliminates manual carrying of heavy materials and keeps sewers at their stations.

Pathway C: Palletizing

End-of-Line

Automated boxing and stacking of finished hampers. Increases throughput for B2B and e-commerce shipping demands.

TIMELINE

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program. Digital twin simulation and installation of metal bending Cobot.
  • Year 2: Logistics. Deployment of 2 AMRs for canvas transport between cutting and sewing.
  • Year 3: Expansion. End-of-line palletizing and automated simple stitching solutions.
  • Year 4-5: Smart Factory. Full IIoT sensor integration for predictive maintenance.
