01. Executive Vision

The integration of robotics into CG Family Foods represents a strategic pivot toward a Hybrid Service Model. By automating high-repetition tasks such as food running and frying, we do not replace the human element of hospitality; we elevate it. This strategy is engineered to stabilize OPEX volatility while modernizing the guest experience.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving

The Runner Network

  • Autonomous bussing & food running.
  • Reduces server fatigue.
  • Increases face-time with guests.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Precision Cooking

  • Auto-fryers and flipping arms.
  • Enhanced safety protocols.
  • Zero-variance consistency.

Pathway C: AI Interface

Digital Order Intake

  • Voice AI at Drive-thru/Kiosk.
  • 100% Upsell attempt rate.
  • Eliminates order entry errors.
