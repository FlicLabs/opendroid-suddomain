01. Executive Summary

Arora Engineers is positioned to transcend traditional infrastructure management by adopting a "Smart Manufacturing" ethos. Our research indicates that shifting from static asset management to a dynamic, automated fabrication model for specialized components will secure market leadership. By integrating IoT and Robotics, Arora can create a closed-loop system where facility data directly informs production.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly. Deployment of 6-axis collaborative robots for the assembly of complex control panels. Reduces ergonomic strain and increases consistency.

Pathway B: AMRs

Intralogistics. Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle WIP flow between engineering bays and assembly lines, reducing non-value-added walking time.

Pathway C: Auto-QA

Vision & Palletizing. Automated visual inspection ensuring 100% compliance of safety-critical infrastructure components prior to shipment.
