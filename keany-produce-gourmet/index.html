1. Organizational Profile

Operational Model

Keany operates as a high-volume Fast-Casual / Corporate Cafeteria hybrid. This model relies on speed during peak windows (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM) and quality consistency.

  • Cuisine: Fresh Gourmet, Salads, Grab-and-Go
  • Locations: 5-10 High Traffic Sites
  • Challenge: Labor volatility during peak vs. off-peak hours.

Current State Analysis

Manual processes in bussing, frying, and order taking create bottlenecks. Staff burnout is high in back-of-house roles due to repetitive hot-line tasks.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous bussing and drink running. Reduces staff fatigue and clears tables 2x faster during lunch rushes.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Automated Cooking

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Robotic arms for frying and grilling. Eliminates burns, ensures perfect cook times, and reduces oil waste.

Pathway C: Digital Ordering

AI Kiosks

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

Smart kiosks with inventory-aware upselling. Reduces queue length and order errors by 99%.

3. Financial Model & Projections

CAPEX Overview

Investment scales with deployment size.

  • Entry Unit: $15,000 (Service/Kiosk)
  • High-End Unit: $40,000 (Frying/Grilling)
  • Pilot Budget: ~$350k (5 Locations)

OPEX Impact

Robotics shift fixed labor costs to variable asset costs.

  • Target labor reduction: 15-25% FOH
  • Maintenance: ~5-8% of CAPEX/year
  • Energy: Negligible impact

4. Implementation Roadmap (Year 1-5)

Year 1: Pilot & Integration

Deploy FOH serving bots and AI kiosks at 2 flagship locations. Staff training and data collection.

Year 2: Back-of-House Automation

Install automated frying/grilling units in central production kitchens. Standardize recipes via code.

Year 3: Fleet Expansion

Rollout to all remaining locations. Central fleet management dashboard implementation.

Year 4: AI Optimization

Predictive staffing models based on 3 years of data. Dynamic pricing/promotions via kiosks.

Year 5: Full Ecosystem

High-autonomy shifts. Potential B2B consulting revenue from Keany's automation IP.
