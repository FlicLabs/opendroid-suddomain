Executive Summary

Together Women's Health (TWH) is a high-growth Management Service Organization (MSO) uniquely positioned to capitalize on Robotic Process Automation (RPA). The strongest levers for TWH are not hardware-based but software-driven "digital workers" that optimize the revenue cycle and patient access.

Our analysis projects that implementing Pathway A (Revenue Cycle Automation) will generate the dominant ROI by reducing cost-to-collect and accelerating cash flow across the provider network.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Revenue Cycle RPA (High Value)

Deployment of software robots to handle repetitive, high-volume billing tasks.

  • Key Levers: Automated eligibility checks, Claims scrubbing, Status inquiries.
  • Impact: 30% reduction in cost-to-collect; 40% billing staff time liberated for high-value tasks.

Pathway B: Patient Access & Scheduling Bots

AI-driven conversational agents to manage inbound patient requests and calendar optimization.

  • Key Levers: 24/7 Appointment booking, Waitlist automation, Triage algorithms.
  • Impact: Higher schedule density, reduced call center overhead, improved patient satisfaction.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: RPA Pilot & Rollout. Select one region for eligibility verification pilot, then scale to full claims automation.
  • Year 2: Patient Access Bots. Launch smart scheduling to optimize calendar density and fill cancellations.
  • Year 3+: Clinical AI. Roll out ambient voice AI scribes and predictive analytics for obstetrics demand.
