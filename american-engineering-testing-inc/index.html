01. Executive Vision

Transitioning American Engineering Testing Inc. from manual laboratory processes to a Smart Inspection Hub. By treating sample processing as discrete manufacturing, AET can leverage flexible robotics to eliminate bottlenecks in concrete and soil testing, ensuring higher throughput and rigorous data integrity.

02. Robotics Pathways

  • Pathway A (Cobots): Automated machine tending for concrete cylinder compression tests. Increases safety by removing technicians from failure zones.
  • Pathway B (AMRs): Autonomous transport of heavy aggregate and soil samples between loading docks, curing rooms, and test cells.
  • Pathway C (End-of-Line): Automated sample archiving and disposal systems to manage waste yield efficiently.

03. Financial Model

  • CAPEX Estimate: $170,000 (Pilot Cell: Cobot + AMR + Integration).
  • Labor Reallocation: 2,000 hrs/yr moved from manual lifting to data analysis.
  • Strategic Value: Ability to handle peak construction season volumes without temporary staffing.
