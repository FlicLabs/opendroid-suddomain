Client Profile

Boston Senior Home Care

BSHC is a critical connector in the healthcare ecosystem, bridging the gap between clinical needs and social determinants of health. Our research identifies a high-yield opportunity to transition from manual coordination to an AI-orchestrated support network. By deploying social and safety robotics, BSHC can mitigate workforce shortages and significantly delay high-cost institutional placements.
