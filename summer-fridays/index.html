Executive Summary

Summer Fridays is poised to transition from boutique batching to a scalable Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By integrating a Smart Manufacturing Ecosystem, the brand can maintain its high aesthetic standards while meeting the rigorous demands of global distribution partners like Sephora. Our strategy focuses on protecting the delicate nature of cosmetic packaging through precision robotics while optimizing workflow via autonomous transport.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Collaborative Assembly (Cobots)

Deployment of 6-axis Cobots for machine tending and cartoning. Focus on handling aluminum tubes with force-torque sensitivity to prevent dents while increasing filling speed.

Pathway B

Autonomous Material Transport (AMR)

Implementation of AMRs to transport raw packaging and finished goods. This reduces floor traffic and minimizes contamination risks in the production environment.

Pathway C

Vision AI & Palletizing

Automated visual inspection to detect micro-scratches and crimping errors, followed by robotic palletizing to eliminate end-of-line bottlenecks.
