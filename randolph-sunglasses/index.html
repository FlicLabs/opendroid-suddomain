Executive Summary

Randolph Sunglasses represents a hallmark of American manufacturing. The strategic vision must balance heritage craftsmanship with smart manufacturing efficiency. By integrating flexible automation, Randolph can alleviate bottlenecks in the 200-step production process while preserving the "Made in USA" artisan value.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Assembly

CobotsMachine Tending

Focus on soldering and screw insertion. Deploy UR-series cobots to handle frame holding and CNC tending, reducing operator fatigue and increasing precision.

Pathway B: Intra-Logistics

AMRMaterial Flow

Utilize Autonomous Mobile Robots (MiR250) to transport WIP trays between plating and assembly, eliminating non-value-added walking time.

Pathway C: Automated Inspection

Computer VisionQuality Control

Implement high-resolution vision systems for lens scratch detection and frame symmetry checks prior to packaging.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot

Deploy single Cobot for soldering. Collect cycle time data.

Year 2: Logistics

Deploy AMRs for shop floor transport. Integrate fleet software.

Year 3-5: Scale

Full IIoT integration, predictive maintenance, and AI-driven quality control.
