1. Executive Summary & Profile

Beyond Inc. is pivoting from a pure-play e-commerce model to an "Asset-Light" omnichannel strategy. The re-introduction of physical touchpoints requires a rejection of the legacy "cluttered" store model. By implementing autonomous inventory scanning and floor care, Beyond Inc. can operate high-efficiency "showrooms" that serve as pristine fulfillment nodes.


Store Footprint

Projected 50-100 "Next-Gen" locations. 15k-25k sq. ft. curated showrooms focusing on home enthusiasts.

Operational Goal

Bridge the digital-physical gap. Ensure app inventory data matches shelf reality 100% of the time.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Autonomous Inventory Scanning

Tech: Computer Vision AMRs (e.g., Simbe Tally).
Impact: Scans RFID/Visuals to detect stockouts and pricing errors. Eliminates "phantom inventory" and supports reliable BOPIS (Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store).

PATHWAY B Automated Floor Care

Tech: LiDAR-enabled Scrubbers (e.g., Brain Corp).
Impact: Autonomous cleaning ensures premium aesthetics and detects hazards (spills/glass), reducing liability claims by ~40%.

PATHWAY C Service & Security

Tech: Security Droids & Wayfinding Kiosks.
Impact: Visible deterrence for Loss Prevention and non-confrontational surveillance.
