Strategic Analysis & ROI Modeling

Executive Summary

StarVista is uniquely positioned to leverage robotics to stabilize operations amidst industry-wide staffing shortages. The strongest robotics/automation levers are: (1) Autonomous Mobile Robots for dining and logistics, (2) Automated industrial cleaning fleets, and (3) Social and cognitive assistive robotics for memory care. The dominant ROI driver is the deployment of logistics robots, liberating over 40,000 clinical and hospitality labor hours annually.

Organizational Profile

  • Model: Continuum of care (IL, AL, Memory Care, SNF).
  • Scale: 22 facilities, ~2,800 residents, ~1,500 FTEs.
  • Challenges: High turnover (60%) in hospitality roles; clinical staff burdened with logistics.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot & Logistics

Launch Dining AMRs in 3 flagship campuses. Validate workflow and establish the StarVista Robotics Center of Excellence.

Year 2: Network Scale

Roll out Dining AMRs to remaining 19 facilities. Introduce heavy-payload logistics bots for linen transport.

Years 3-5: Advanced Integration

Full deployment of cleaning fleet. EHR integration for automated supply requests. Social robot pilots.
