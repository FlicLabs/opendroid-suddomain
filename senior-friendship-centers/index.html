Executive Summary

Senior Friendship Centers faces the "Silver Tsunami" with a critical need for operational scalability. By deploying assistive robotics, the organization can shift from labor-intensive service models to tech-enabled community care. The dominant ROI drivers are Service Robotics in dining centers (labor efficiency), Social AI (dementia care quality), and Telepresence (expanding rural reach). This portfolio generates quantifiable margin improvements and validates the center as an innovation leader for future grant funding.
