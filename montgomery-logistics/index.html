  • Year 2 Integration: AI Logistics integration at docks and expansion to secondary hubs.
  • Year 3 Optimization: Full ASRS deployment for high-value inventory and data aggregation.
  • Year 5 Autonomy: "Lights-out" operations for third-shift cycles and full fleet interoperability.
