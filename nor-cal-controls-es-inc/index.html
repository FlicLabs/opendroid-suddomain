Executive Summary

Nor-Cal Controls is positioned to transition from traditional discrete manufacturing to a Smart Panel Manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating robotics into the modification and wiring phases, the company can scale operations to meet the surging demand in the renewable energy sector without linear increases in labor costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • PATHWAY A Cobots for Assembly
    Automated drilling of backplates and torqueing of terminal blocks to ensure precision and reduce repetitive strain.
  • PATHWAY B AMR Logistics
    Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving inverters and heavy components, keeping skilled electricians focused on value-added wiring.
  • PATHWAY C Automated QC
    End-of-line optical inspection and palletizing assistance for heavy control cabinets.
