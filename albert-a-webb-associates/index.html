Executive Summary

To transition from civil engineering design to Industry 4.0 Manufacturing, Webb Associates must leverage DfMA (Design for Manufacture and Assembly). This strategy bridges digital engineering with physical production, focusing on pre-fabricated infrastructure components and smart city hardware.

Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: Collaborative Assembly
Deployment of 6-axis Cobots for traffic control box assembly. Focus on reducing Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI) and ensuring consistent torque application.
PATHWAY B: AMR Logistics
Autonomous Mobile Robots for transporting heavy concrete molds and raw materials, eliminating forklift risks in pedestrian zones.
PATHWAY C: EOL Automation
Automated palletizing and vision-based quality inspection to increase final shipping throughput by 30%.
