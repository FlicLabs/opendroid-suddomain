Client: South Central Alabama Mental Health

Executive Summary

South Central Alabama Mental Health (SCAMH) serves a critical role across Butler, Coffee, Covington, and Crenshaw counties. Facing the typical constraints of rural healthcare—geographic dispersion and staffing shortages—SCAMH is uniquely positioned to leverage Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and Therapeutic Robotics.

The dominant ROI driver is the reclamation of 4,000+ annual clinical hours currently lost to administration, alongside a drastic reduction in appointment no-shows via automated scheduling logistics.
