Executive Summary

CLS Health is a rapidly expanding, physician-owned multi-specialty group based in Webster, Texas. To maintain competitive advantage against large hospital systems, CLS must leverage automation to improve surgical case complexity and administrative efficiency. The primary drivers are high-margin robotic surgery expansion and AI-driven revenue cycle management.
