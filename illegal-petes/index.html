Executive Summary

The strategic vision for Illegal Pete's is High-Friction Removal. By integrating collaborative robotics for heavy lifting (bussing) and repetitive consistency (chip frying), the brand can maintain its high-energy "Mission-style" vibe while securing operations against labor volatility.

Key Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Food Runners & Bussers to clear tables for high-volume bar crowds.
  • Pathway B: Automated Back-of-House frying for consistent chip/shell production and safety.
  • Pathway C: Voice AI for catering upsells and order accuracy.
