OPEN DROIDS
Robotics Portfolio: 24 Hour Home Care

Executive Summary

24 Hour Home Care is uniquely positioned to transition from a labor-heavy agency model to a tech-enabled care platform. By leveraging "software robotics" for logistics and physical robotics for caregiver safety, the organization can address its two largest cost drivers: administrative bloat and caregiver churn.

Pathway A: Logistics AI HIGHEST ROI

Scope: Implementation of RPA and predictive algorithms to automate the matching of thousands of caregivers to clients.

Impact:
• Doubles coordinator capacity (1:40 → 1:80).
• Reduces unfulfilled shifts by ~30%.
• £2M–£4M annual admin savings.

Pathway B: Assistive Robotics STRATEGIC

Scope: Deployment of wearable active exoskeletons for caregivers handling high-acuity transfer patients.

Impact:
• Drastic reduction in Work Comp claims.
• Extends career lifespan of best caregivers.
• Market differentiation as "Safety First" employer.
