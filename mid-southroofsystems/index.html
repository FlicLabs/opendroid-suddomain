Executive Summary

Mid-South Roof Systems is positioned to revolutionize regional construction logistics. By transitioning from manual staging to a semi-automated DC ecosystem, the company can reduce staging time by 40% and eliminate accessory shrinkage. The strategy focuses on heavy-payload robotics for membranes and insulation, ensuring materials reach job sites faster and safer.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Heavy-Duty AMRs

Deployment of LiDAR-enabled mobile robots capable of moving 3,000 lbs pallets. Impact: Reclaims 35% of operator time previously spent on long-haul driving across the yard.

Pathway B: Automated Accessory Kitting (ASRS)

Vertical Lift Modules for small parts (fasteners, plates, sealants). Impact: 99.9% picking accuracy, eliminating job-site stoppages due to missing small components.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive analytics integrated with ERP to forecast material surges based on weather and project timelines.
