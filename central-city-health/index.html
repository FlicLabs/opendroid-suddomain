Executive Summary

Central City Health is an integrated urban health system facing acute pressure from rising labor costs and logistics bottlenecks. The strongest automation levers are (1) Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics, (2) Automated Pharmacy Dispensing, and (3) Surgical Robotics Expansion.

The dominant ROI driver is labor reallocation—moving clinical staff from low-skill transport tasks to top-of-license patient care—generating millions in operational savings and capacity retention.
