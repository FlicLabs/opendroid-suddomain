01. Executive Vision

Legacy Supply Chain is positioned to transition from a capacity-based 3PL to a high-velocity, tech-first fulfillment provider. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven logic, the organization can decouple revenue growth from labor market volatility, creating a "Smart Mesh Network" where inventory movement is predictive rather than reactive.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Internal Transport.
Replacing manual pallet movement and long-walk picking to increase pick rates from 60 UPH to 150+ UPH.

Pathway B: Piece Picking

Focus: Accuracy & Density.
High-density Goods-to-Person stations for small consumer goods, achieving 99.9% order accuracy.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Focus: Predictive Efficiency.
Computer vision at loading docks and algorithmic slotting to reduce put-away time by 30%.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Foundation: Data analysis and initial AMR Pilot in primary hub. YEAR 1
  • Expansion: Roll out to regional hubs; Begin ASRS testing. YEAR 2
  • AI Shift: Full autonomous slotting and predictive logic integration. YEAR 3
  • Saturation: Network-wide standardization of robotics fleet. YEAR 4
  • Innovation: Evaluation of humanoid robotics for complex tasks. YEAR 5
