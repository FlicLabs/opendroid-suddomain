Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport: Deploying autonomous mobile robots to handle long-haul pallet movements. Decouples picking from traveling, reducing forklift traffic in pizza box staging zones.

Pathway B: ASRS

Piece Picking: Implementing Goods-to-Person technology for "Small Wares" and spices. High-density storage reduces footprint and ensures accuracy for low-volume SKUs.

Pathway C: AI Logic

Predictive Logistics: Algorithms that re-slot inventory nightly based on restaurant demand spikes (e.g., Super Bowl prep), optimizing dock flow.

Implementation Roadmap

  • YEAR 1 Foundation & Pilot: WMS Upgrades and Initial deployment of 6 AMRs in Receiving.
  • YEAR 2 Expansion: Rollout of Piece Picking stations. Achievement of initial ROI targets.
  • YEAR 3 Optimization: Full integration of AI-driven slotting and predictive logistics.
  • YEAR 4 Autonomy: Autonomous loading trials. Manual touchpoints reduced to < 10%.
  • YEAR 5 Ecosystem: Fully digitized supply chain with real-time client visibility.
