Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Internal Transport (AMRs)

Implementation of Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace manual cart pushing. AMRs connect receiving docks to sorting zones, eliminating non-value-added walking time.

Pathway B: Robotic Sorting

Deployment of "Put-to-Light" walls assisted by robotic arms. Increases sorting accuracy to 99.9% for small legal/medical parcels, critical for WEX's client base.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive dock scheduling and route optimization using AI to pre-sort inventory before drivers arrive, reducing dwell time by 20 minutes per route.
