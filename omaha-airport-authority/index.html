Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

High-suction robotic vacuums with electrostatic sprayers. Reduces cabin turnover by 10-15 minutes per narrow-body aircraft.

B. UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen neutralization robots for lavatories and galleys. 99.9% sterilization rate without chemical residue.

C. Exterior Washing

Mobile gantry bots for fuselage cleaning. Reduces water usage by 60% and eliminates manual labor hazards.

D. AI Inspection Bots

Computer vision rovers for automated lightning strike and rivet inspection, creating digital safety audits.
