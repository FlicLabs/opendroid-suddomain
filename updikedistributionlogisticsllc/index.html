03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport & Pallet Movement. Replaces long-haul manual forklift travel, focusing on receiving-to-buffer zones.

Pathway B: ASRS

High-Density Piece Picking. Goods-to-Person systems increasing accuracy to 99.9% and utilizing vertical space.

Pathway C: AI Ops

Inbound/Outbound Logic. Computer vision at docks and predictive modeling for yard management.

05. Implementation Roadmap

Year 1
Pilot Phase: Deploy AMRs in Phoenix Hub; WMS Integration.
Year 2
Expansion: Introduce Goods-to-Person (ASRS) for high-velocity SKUs.
Year 3
Integration: Full AI Dock Management & Control Tower rollout.
Year 4
Optimization: Machine Learning driven inventory slotting.
Year 5
Ecosystem: Fully autonomous environment; supervisory labor only.
