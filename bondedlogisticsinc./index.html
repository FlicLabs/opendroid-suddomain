01. Executive Summary

Bonded Logistics Inc. operates in a high-demand 3PL environment where flexibility is currency. The strategic recommendation is not static automation, but a modular, scalable robotics fleet. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven logic, Bonded can decouple revenue growth from labor headcount, ensuring scalability during peak seasons in the Charlotte metro area.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Replacing long-haul manual forklift travel with pallet-moving AMRs. This targets the 50% of operator time currently spent driving empty. Impact: Higher safety scores and optimized labor allocation.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Deployment of Locus/6 River style bots for e-commerce fulfillment contracts. This reduces walking time for pickers and increases pick rates from 100 UPH (Units Per Hour) to 350+ UPH.

Pathway C: AI Predictive Logistics

Utilizing Computer Vision for dock management and algorithmic slotting to place high-velocity items in the most accessible locations automatically.
