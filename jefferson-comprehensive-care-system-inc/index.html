Executive Summary

Jefferson Comprehensive Care System Inc. (JCCSI) is a critical FQHC network in Arkansas. Our analysis identifies three primary automation levers: (1) Automated Pharmacy Dispensing, (2) Telepresence Kiosks, and (3) Administrative RPA. Implementation is projected to generate $1.5M in cumulative net value over 5 years by optimizing clinical labor and expanding rural access.
