Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Compact, autonomous vacuums designed for the narrow aisles of Cessna Caravans. Essential for removing bush strip dust.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Medical-grade sterilization robots for post-MedEvac flights. Ensures pilot safety without harsh liquid chemicals.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated washing systems to remove red clay and mud. Critical for preventing corrosion in tropical environments.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision drones to detect stress fractures on landing gear and propellers caused by rough runway operations.
