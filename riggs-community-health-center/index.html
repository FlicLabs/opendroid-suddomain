Executive Summary

Riggs Community Health Center faces the unique FQHC challenge of maximizing patient access within strict funding constraints. The most effective robotics levers are high-volume operational tasks. The dominant strategy focuses on Pathway A: Pharmacy Automation to handle 340B prescription volumes and Pathway B: Administrative RPA for revenue cycle management. These initiatives drive ROI by reallocating clinical labor from manual tasks to billable patient interactions.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pharmacy Focus

Procurement and installation of vial filling robotics. Integration with existing pharmacy management systems to stabilize high-volume 340B workflows.

Year 1-2: Admin RPA

Deploy software bots for insurance verification and prior authorizations, targeting dental and medical referral bottlenecks.

Year 3: Facility Scale

Trial autonomous scrubbers for waiting rooms and corridors to reduce janitorial strain and improve facility optics.
