Executive Summary

Laufer Group International is positioned to transition from a traditional logistics provider to a tech-enabled "Smart Node" network. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven predictive logistics, Laufer can decouple revenue growth from labor volatility, targeting a 35% reduction in handling costs and a 2.5x increase in throughput capacity.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport Deployment of heavy-payload autonomous robots for cross-docking. Reduces forklift travel by 50% and virtually eliminates internal accidents.
  • Pathway B: Robotic Picking & ASRS Utilization of vertical space and Goods-to-Person systems for accurate breakdown and fulfillment. Targets 99.9% picking accuracy.
  • Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics Predictive modeling for inbound freight to pre-allocate robotic resources, reducing gate wait times by 25%.
