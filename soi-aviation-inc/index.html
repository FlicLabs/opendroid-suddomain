Executive Summary

Automation First Strategy

Open Droids proposes a transformative shift for SOI Aviation Inc., moving from manual dependency to a high-efficiency robotic ecosystem. By implementing autonomous pathways, SOI Aviation can expect to decouple operational costs from labor volatility while achieving a 45% reduction in OPEX and superior hygiene consistency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
A

Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous vacuuming and debris removal using LIDAR mapping to navigate aisles and under-seat areas.

B

UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen eradication using clinical-grade UV-C light, ensuring 99.9% sterilization without chemicals.

C

Exterior Wash

Gantry and mobile robot systems designed to wash fuselages in <2 hours with 80% water recycling.

D

AI Inspection

Drone and crawler bots using thermal imaging to detect structural anomalies and lightning strikes.
