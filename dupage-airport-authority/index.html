Aviation Automation Strategy

DuPage Airport Authority stands at the precipice of a technological evolution. By integrating autonomous maintenance and cleaning systems, the Authority can solidify its status as a premier General Aviation hub, offering unparalleled service speed and sanitation standards to corporate tenants and charter operators.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-guided vacuum and debris removal systems designed for business jet interiors. Drastically reduces turnaround time between flight legs.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sterilization using concentrated UV light. Neutralizes pathogens on high-touch surfaces in cockpits and cabins.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated dry-wash robots that ensure consistent pressure application while conserving water and preventing environmental runoff.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision drones and crawlers for detecting surface anomalies, rivets issues, and composite delamination.
