Management and Network Services LLC

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Management and Network Services LLC (MNS) is a critical intermediary in post-acute care. Unlike clinical providers, MNS's automation opportunity lies in Robotic Process Automation (RPA) rather than physical hardware.

The strategic focus is deploying "Software Robots" to automate high-volume claims, authorizations, and credentialing. This shift promises to reduce administrative costs by 60-70% and drastically accelerate cash flow for network partners.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
Pathway A: RPA for Claims (Highest Value)

Deployment of software bots to handle repetitive tasks: claims intake, error checking, and adjudication.

  • Automated data extraction from facility EMRs.
  • Rules-based scrubbing to prevent denials.
  • Impact: 60-70% reduction in processing costs.
Pathway B: AI Utilization Mgmt (Strategic)

AI algorithms to automate prior authorization requests for patients transitioning to skilled nursing.

  • Accelerates decision times from days to minutes.
  • Reduces administrative burden on clinical staff.
  • Increases hospital throughput.
Pathway C: Automated Credentialing

Bots that continuously monitor provider licenses, exclusions, and insurance expirations.

  • Zero-gap compliance risk.
  • Faster facility onboarding.
  • Replaces manual audits.
