Strategic Pathways

PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Machine Tending & Assembly. High-mix/low-volume flexibility for press brakes. Eliminates crush-zone risks and reduces operator fatigue.

PATHWAY B: AMRS

Material Transport. Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving bins between Stamping and Welding bays. Reduces forklift traffic and WIP stagnation.

PATHWAY C: INSPECTION

End-of-Line Quality. Automated palletizing combined with vision systems for weld seam inspection. Ensures 100% yield quality.
