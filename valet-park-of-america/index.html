Executive Summary

Valet Park of America (VPA) is transitioning from a traditional labor-heavy service model to a tech-enabled mobility logistics provider. By integrating robotics and automation, VPA can resolve critical bottlenecks in labor shortages, liability claims, and peak-hour throughput. The strongest levers identified are Automated Vehicle Inspection, Autonomous Shuttles, and Robotic Valet Systems.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Inspection (High Value)

Scope: Deploy AI gantries at garage entries to scan for pre-existing damage.

  • Eliminates fraudulent damage claims (approx. $150k/year savings per site).
  • Reduces vehicle check-in time from 45s to 10s via LPR integration.

Pathway B: Autonomous Shuttles (Strategic)

Scope: Level 4 electric shuttles for fixed hospital/campus loops.

  • Reduces dependency on CDL drivers and overtime costs.
  • Enables 24/7 operations for hospital staff transport.

Pathway C: Robotic Valet (Long Term)

Scope: Low-profile skid robots for high-density parking.

  • Increases garage capacity by 30-50%.
  • Zero door dings or in-garage accidents.
