Executive Summary

Raba Kistner operates at the intersection of engineering consulting and high-volume material testing. By adopting Industry 4.0 principles, the firm can transform its CoMET laboratories into Smart Manufacturing hubs.

The strategic goal is to automate the "heavy lifting" of sample processing—specifically concrete cylinder crushing and soil movement—to reduce technician fatigue and drastically improve throughput speed and data integrity.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Machine Tending

Use of IP66 Cobots to load/unload concrete compression machines. Eliminates repetitive lifting and ensures consistent crush test alignment.

Pathway B: AMR Material Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to ferry heavy samples between curing rooms and testing rigs. Converts 100% of walking time into value-added analysis time.

Pathway C: Automated Archiving

End-of-line gantry systems for automated sample disposal and digital archiving, improving lab safety and cleanliness.
