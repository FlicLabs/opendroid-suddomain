01. Executive Summary

The Hair Shop is positioned to lead the beauty sector by adopting Industry 4.0 standards. Our strategic analysis identifies a shift from manual assembly to a flexible, smart manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics, we respect the delicate nature of hair products while securing consistency and throughput.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Deployment of collaborative arms for machine tending and delicate packaging of hair wefts. Focus on safety and human-robot interaction.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal logistics. Automating the flow of raw bundles and finished liquids to shipping docks.

Pathway C: Vision Systems

High-speed end-of-line palletizing combined with computer vision for color-code verification and quality assurance.
