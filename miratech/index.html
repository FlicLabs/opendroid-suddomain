01. Executive Summary

Miratech is positioned to transition from traditional fabrication to a Connected Flexible Manufacturing Ecosystem. By leveraging Industry 4.0 standards, the entity can meet stringent environmental regulations (Tier 4 Final) with higher precision. The strategy focuses on high-mix, low-volume automation to reduce lead times on custom acoustic engineering projects while ensuring 100% traceability.

02. Organizational Profile

Primary Operations: Heavy fabrication, sheet metal cutting, rolling, and welding.

Classification: Hybrid Discrete/Process Manufacturing.

Current State: High reliance on skilled manual labor for welding and material transport. Production is batch-oriented, creating significant opportunity for flow optimization via automation.

03. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A Collaborative Welding

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for repetitive linear welds on silencer bodies. This mitigates the skilled welder shortage and ensures consistent bead appearance.

Pathway B AMR Material Handling

Utilization of heavy-duty Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) to automate the movement of steel sheets and housings, reducing forklift traffic and WIP stagnation.

Pathway C Auto-Inspection

End-of-Line machine vision systems to scan for coating defects and weld porosity, ensuring brand reputation in critical infrastructure markets.
