Arion Care

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Arion Care is a pivotal provider of Home and Community-Based Services (HCBS), managing complex logistics for IDD and aging populations. The primary robotics and automation opportunity lies not in replacing care, but in optimizing the workforce.

The proposed strategy leverages AI-driven logistics, Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and assistive technologies to reclaim millions in lost operational efficiency and improve Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) compliance.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Workforce AI

Highest ROI

Implementation of AI-driven matching for caregivers and clients, plus RPA for automated billing reconciliation.

Efficiency Gain:+20%
Payback:< 9 Mos

Pathway B: Social Robotics

Differentiation

Deployment of companion robots and automated medication dispensers for high-acuity IDD clients.

Pathway C: Remote Safety

Scalability
