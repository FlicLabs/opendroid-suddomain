03. Strategic Pathways

PATHWAY A: Heavy AMRs

Deployment of high-payload autonomous tuggers to replace manual forklifts for internal transport of steel coils and finished water heaters. Increases safety and reduces material damage.

PATHWAY B: Parts ASRS

Implementation of Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for the spare parts division. This ensures rapid, accurate fulfillment for warranty claims and wholesaler orders.

PATHWAY C: AI Logistics

Utilization of Computer Vision at dock doors to optimize trailer loading density, reducing "shipping air" and lowering overall freight costs.

05. Implementation Roadmap

  • YEAR 1: Pilot Phase - 6 Heavy AMRs in Middleville & Pilot VLM for top 500 Parts.
  • YEAR 2-3: Scale & Optimize - Full Parts Catalog to ASRS & WES Integration.
  • YEAR 4-5: AI Ecosystem - Predictive maintenance and autonomous supply chain orchestration.
