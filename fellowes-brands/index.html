Executive Vision

Fellowes Brands is positioned to transition from traditional discrete lines to a flexible, smart ecosystem. This strategy targets the high-mix nature of ergonomic furniture and the precision requirements of AeraMax air purification systems, ensuring the "Worklife" promise is delivered with operational excellence.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots
Machine tending for injection molding & screw driving for shredder assembly.
Pathway B: AMRs
Autonomous transport of motors and corrugated sheets (Bankers Box) to line-side.
Pathway C: End-of-Line
Automated palletizing and vision-based quality inspection for finished goods.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot AeraMax Line (2 Cobots, 2 AMRs).
  • Year 2: Expand to Shredder Assembly; ERP Integration.
  • Year 3: Full AMR Fleet & Manual Tugger Replacement.
  • Year 4: Predictive Maintenance & "Lights Out" Molding.
  • Year 5: Global Standardization (US, CN, EU).
