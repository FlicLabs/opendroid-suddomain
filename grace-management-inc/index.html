Executive Summary

Grace Management Inc. is positioned to leverage robotics to stabilize operations against industry-wide labor shortages. The proposed integration of dining runners and autonomous cleaning units offers a dual benefit: alleviating physical strain on staff to reduce turnover, and enhancing resident experience. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of agency labor usage, with a projected net margin expansion in the millions annually.
