Executive Summary

This report outlines the strategic integration of robotic automation into Heli's aviation operations. By deploying Open Droids' autonomous systems for cabin cleaning, disinfection, and exterior maintenance, Heli can achieve a 40% reduction in operational costs while improving turnaround consistency. The roadmap proposes a phased implementation starting at primary hubs, targeting a full payback period of 14-18 months. This transition addresses labor volatility, enhances hygiene standards, and aligns with long-term sustainability goals through water conservation and predictive maintenance.
