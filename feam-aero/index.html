Executive Strategic Vision

The MRO sector currently faces a critical convergence of labor shortages and high fleet utilization. For Feam Aero, the integration of robotic cleaning and inspection automation is a strategic mechanism to reduce Turnaround Time (TAT) and standardize compliance. This report outlines the transition to a precision-based automated ecosystem, positioning Feam Aero as the first AI-integrated MRO network in the Americas.
