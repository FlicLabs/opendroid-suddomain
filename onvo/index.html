Executive Summary

Onvo is uniquely positioned to disrupt the healthcare logistics and Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (NEMT) sectors. By leveraging its battery-swapping ecosystem and autonomous driving stack, Onvo can pivot from a pure mobility player to a critical healthcare infrastructure partner. The core opportunity lies in "Pathway A": Autonomous Medical Supply Chain Logistics, which offers high ROI through labor reduction and 24/7 fleet uptime.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Pilot (Year 1)

Launch limited fleet in a geo-fenced university hospital campus. Validate battery-swapping efficiency and autonomous dock integration.

Phase 2: Scale & RaaS (Year 2)

Expand to city-wide hospital networks using Robotics-as-a-Service model. Begin NEMT certification process for patient transport.

Phase 3: Ecosystem (Year 3+)

Full deployment of biometric sensor suite via OTA updates. License health data streams to insurance providers.
