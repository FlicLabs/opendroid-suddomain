Executive Summary

Strategic Thesis: True Health's integrated care model relies on high-volume throughput. The dominant ROI driver is the automation of ancillary services (Pharmacy & Labs) and administrative workflows. This portfolio recommends a focused investment in dispensing robotics and RPA to recover clinical hours and reduce patient wait times.

Organizational Profile

Model: Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) & Integrated Primary Care Network.

Scale: Multi-site operations (8-12 locations), >150,000 annual visits. High-traffic outpatient environment.

Economics: Volume-driven revenue (Medicaid/Medicare). Operational bottlenecks exist in pharmacy throughput and intake administration.
