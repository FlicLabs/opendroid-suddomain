Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: AMRs & Transport

Deployment of heavy-payload AMRs for cross-docking velocity. Eliminates forklift dead-heading and increases safety.

PATHWAY B: Piece Picking

Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems enabling micro-fulfillment services for SPI franchise partners.

PATHWAY C: AI Logistics

Vision tunnels for instant dimensioning and automated integration into the SPI Platform.

Implementation Roadmap

  • YEAR 1 Pilot Program: Vision Tunnels & Initial AMR Fleet deployment.
  • YEAR 2 Integration: Full telemetry sync with SPI Platform software.
  • YEAR 3 Expansion: RaaS rollout to top 10 franchise partners.
  • YEAR 4 Network: Micro-fulfillment ASRS hubs in major metros.
