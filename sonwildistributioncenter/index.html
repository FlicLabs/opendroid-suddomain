Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Internal AMRs

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots replacing manual forklifts for long-haul transport. Reduces operator fatigue and optimizes dock-to-stock travel.

B. Robotic Picking

Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems for high-velocity e-commerce fulfillment. Maximizes vertical storage density and eliminates picking errors.

C. AI Logistics

Predictive algorithms for inbound rail/truck manifests. Enables "Hot Landing" scenarios to reduce dwell time by 20%.
