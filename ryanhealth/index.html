RYAN HEALTH

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Ryan Health is a cornerstone FQHC network in Manhattan serving 50,000+ patients. The strategic focus for robotics here is not surgical intervention, but High-Volume Process Automation. By leveraging pharmacy robotics, RPA for administrative workflows, and smart diagnostics, Ryan Health can unlock millions in 340B revenue and administrative savings, directly reinvesting into patient access and care quality.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Pharmacy Automation

High ROIImmediate

Implementation of robotic dispensing systems (ScriptPro/Parata) to automate counting, filling, and labeling.

  • Increases throughput by 30-50%
  • Reduces medication errors to near zero
  • Maximizes 340B program revenue capture

Pathway B: RPA & Smart Scheduling

EfficiencySoftware

Digital workers to handle prior authorizations, referral coding, and AI-driven appointment reminders.

  • Cuts no-show rates from 20% to <10%
  • Automates multilingual patient outreach
  • Reduces provider administrative burnout

Pathway C: Lab Logistics

WorkflowPhysical

Autonomous mobile robots or pneumatic systems for specimen transport in multi-story clinics.

  • Faster STAT lab turnaround
  • Reduces clinical staff motion waste
  • Standardizes sample handling

Strategic Positioning

Adopting this portfolio transforms Ryan Health into a Tech-Enabled Community Health System. It solves the "Iron Triangle" of healthcare—improving access and quality while controlling costs. This modernization attracts federal technology grants, retains top-tier providers by reducing burnout, and ensures long-term financial sustainability through optimized 340B operations.
