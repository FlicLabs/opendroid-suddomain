Founders Table Restaurant Group

Strategic Automation & Robotics Report

Executive Summary: Founders Table is positioned to leverage robotics not to replace their signature "assembly line" service, but to optimize the friction points surrounding it. By automating low-value tasks (bussing, running) and implementing AI-driven ordering, the group can significantly increase throughput during critical lunch hours.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Automation

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for table bussing and order running. This removes physical congestion from the dining area and allows staff to focus on the high-touch assembly line.

Pathway B: BOH Consistency

Automated dispensing of salad bases and automated grilling assistants for proteins. This standardizes portion control and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Upselling

Voice AI at kiosks and drive-thrus to intelligently suggest add-ons based on inventory levels, driving higher average check size without slowing down the line.
