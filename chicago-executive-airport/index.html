OPEN DROIDS

STRATEGIC AUTOMATION RESEARCH: CHICAGO EXECUTIVE AIRPORT

Executive Summary

Chicago Executive Airport (PWK) stands at a pivotal juncture where operational volume meets the demand for hyper-hygienic, efficient service. This report outlines a strategic vision to deploy Open Droids' autonomous systems to handle the delicate needs of corporate fleets, significantly reducing ground time while ensuring hospital-grade cleanliness.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous HEPA-filtered bots for delicate VIP interiors, navigating narrow aisles without damaging luxury finishes.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen elimination systems ensuring sterile environments for high-net-worth clients without chemical residue.

Pathway C: Exterior Wash

Dry-wash gantry systems and mobile units that clean fuselages while maintaining EPA compliance.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Thermal imaging drones for early detection of fuselage stress, rivets, and static wick integrity.
