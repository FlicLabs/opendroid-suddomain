Client: Hino Motors Manufacturing USA Inc.

01. Executive Summary

Hino Motors stands at the threshold of a logistics revolution. By transitioning from manual MHE dependence to a Smart Factory Logistics Ecosystem, Hino can secure JIT precision matching the Toyota Production System standards. This report outlines the strategic integration of AMRs and ASRS to combat labor shortages in key hubs (WV, AR) and optimize the handling of heavy-duty truck components.

Pathway A

AMR Transport

Deployment of heavy-payload autonomous mobile robots (1,000kg+) for chassis and axle movement. Replaces manual tuggers, reducing traffic variability and increasing safety in high-traffic assembly zones.

Pathway B

Robotic Piece Picking

High-density Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) integrated with robotic picking for small parts. Targets 85% floor space recovery and 99.9% picking accuracy for aftermarket distribution.

Pathway C

AI Logistics

Predictive supply chain modeling and computer vision at dock doors. Automates quality checks on inbound shipments and optimizes labor allocation based on real-time data.
