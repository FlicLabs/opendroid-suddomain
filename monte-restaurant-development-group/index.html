Executive Summary

Monte Restaurant Development Group stands at the intersection of authentic Italian hospitality and scalable franchising. Our strategic research indicates that a "High-Touch, High-Tech" model is critical for future growth.

By automating low-value tasks such as food running (Squisito Pizza & Pasta) and fry-station monitoring, the brand can stabilize labor costs while increasing franchise value. The goal is not to replace the human touch, but to liberate staff to focus on the guest experience.

Strategic Pathways

  • A
    FOH Serving Robots:

    Deploy automated runners for heavy pasta/pizza trays to reduce server fatigue and increase floor time.

  • B
    BOH Auto-Frying:

    Ensure 100% consistency in appetizers across all franchise units while improving safety.

  • C
    AI Ordering Kiosks:

    Focus on "Squisito Too" concepts to drive speed and algorithmic upselling.
