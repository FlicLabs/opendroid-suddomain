Executive Summary

Lakeland Behavioral Health System faces unique challenges in patient safety monitoring and staff utilization. Our analysis identifies Pathway A (Automated Monitoring) as the highest value lever, capable of automating 15-minute safety checks and reducing the dependency on expensive 1:1 sitter observation. This shifts resources from passive observation to active clinical care.

Strategic Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation of Safety

Pilot non-contact monitoring in geriatric wings to validate fall reduction. Roll out automated digital charting for regulatory compliance.

Year 2: Operational Logistics

Deploy secure delivery robots (AMRs) for controlled substance transport from pharmacy to secure nursing units.

Year 3: Clinical Differentiation

Full integration of social robotics into pediatric and trauma-informed care curriculums.
