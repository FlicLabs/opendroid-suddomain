Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Welding & Assembly

Deployment of heavy-payload collaborative robots for root pass welding on pipe spools. Reduces exposure to fumes and ensures geometric consistency.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for transporting flanges and steel plates in the laydown yard. Eliminates bottlenecks and reduces forklift traffic risks.

Pathway C: Inspection

Focus: Quality Assurance

LiDAR and 3D Vision systems for "Digital Twin" verification of fabricated modules against BIM schematics before site shipment.
