01. Executive Vision: Metrology 4.0

MSI Viking is positioned to transition from manual verification to a fully automated, data-driven inspection ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics and smart logistics within laboratory environments, the organization will reduce cycle times, enhance repeatability, and create a "living showroom" for their custom engineering solutions.

02. Organizational Profile

Operational Scope

HQ: Duncan, SC (with Detroit, Charleston, Cincinnati hubs).

Type: High-Mix, Low-Volume (HMLV) Lab Services & Custom Engineering.

Current State

Highly skilled metrologists currently perform manual transport and loading. High accuracy, but limited throughput due to human dependency.

03. Financial Model

  • CAPEX: $65k - $90k per robotic cell/fleet.
  • OPEX Impact: 20-30% increase in throughput via lights-out processing.
  • ROI: 11-14 months via labor reallocation ($75/hr burden rate) and scrap reduction.
