Executive Summary & Vision

The strategic vision for ICC is a transition to a "High-Tech, High-Touch" ecosystem. By automating dull and dangerous tasks (running, frying), we elevate human staff to focus on guest connection. This model stabilizes labor costs while meeting modern consumer demands for speed.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House (Serving Robots)

  • Role: Autonomous food running and table bussing.
  • Impact: Reduces server walking distance by 60%. Allows staff to remain in zones.

Pathway B: Back-of-House (Auto-Frying)

  • Role: Robotic arms or auto-lift baskets for consistent frying.
  • Impact: drastic reduction in injury liability and food waste.

Pathway C: AI Kiosks & Voice

  • Role: Natural Language Processing for ordering.
  • Impact: Consistent upselling increases check average by 12-18%.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase
Deploy 2 Serving Robots & 1 Kiosk at flagship locations.
Year 2: Standardization
Rollout hardware to 50% of locations. Begin BOH automation.
Year 3-5: Integration & Ecosystem
Full POS integration, predictive staffing, and biometric loyalty programs.
