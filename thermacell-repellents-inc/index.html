01. Executive Summary

Thermacell Repellents Inc. stands at a pivotal juncture to leverage Industry 4.0. Transitioning from traditional discrete manufacturing to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS) will address seasonal demand volatility. Our strategy focuses on high-mix, low-volume automation to support both fuel-powered and rechargeable product lines.
