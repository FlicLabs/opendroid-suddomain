Executive Summary

Civana Wellness Resort and Spa faces the classic hospitality challenge: maintaining high-touch, personalized service while managing rising labor costs. This research identifies three key automation levers: (1) Logistics/Amenity delivery, (2) Revenue-generating "Touchless Wellness" tech, and (3) Back-of-house maintenance. The strategy pivots from pure labor substitution to revenue augmentation.

Pathway A: Logistics & Amenities

Scope: Autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for room service, towels, and luggage.

Impact: Reduces staff transit time. Enhances privacy for guests desiring contactless service.

High Visibility

Pathway B: Touchless Wellness

Scope: Automated spa technologies (Somadome, Cryotherapy, Hydromassage).

Impact: Breaks the 1:1 therapist ratio. Generates high-margin revenue ($70-90/session) with near-zero labor cost.

High ROI
