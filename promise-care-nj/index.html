Executive Summary Strategic Analysis

Promise Care NJ faces high-density routing challenges in Hudson and Bergen counties. The strongest ROI lever is Pathway A (Logistics AI), which shifts optimization from static scheduling to dynamic, traffic-aware routing. This "Software Robotics" approach can unlock over $3M in incremental annual revenue by increasing clinician capacity without additional headcount. Secondary opportunities include RPA for admin tasks and telepresence robots for CHF management.
