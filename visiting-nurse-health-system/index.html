Executive Summary

Visiting Nurse Health System faces a critical juncture where demand outpaces clinical supply. Our analysis confirms that an "AI-First" logistics strategy combined with social robotics for hospice care is the optimal path forward. The primary value driver is the conversion of "windshield time" (travel) into "clinical time" via algorithmic routing.


Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Intelligent Logistics

Implementation of AI-driven scheduling and routing software. This shifts the operational model from static territories to dynamic, optimized routing.

Pathway B: Social Robotics

Deployment of therapeutic robotic pets (e.g., PARO, Joy for All) in dementia and hospice settings to reduce agitation and pharmacological dependence.
