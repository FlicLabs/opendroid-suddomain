Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Compact SLAM-enabled robots for vacuuming and surface wiping, reducing turnaround time between flights.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots

Mobile units utilizing high-intensity UV-C light for chemical-free pathogen neutralization (99.9% efficacy).

Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing

Automated gantry systems reducing water consumption by 60% and eliminating fall hazards for ground crew.

Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection

LiDAR and HD camera-equipped bots for predictive maintenance and micro-crack detection.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase
Deploy 10 units (Cabin/UV-C). Focus on SOP development and data collection.
Year 2: Integration & Expansion
Scale to 40% of primary hub. Introduce AI Inspection (Engine Bay).
Year 3: Full Interior Automation
Deploy across all hubs. Pilot Exterior Washing (Pathway C).
Year 4: Exterior & Maintenance
Full Pathway C & D deployment. Implement predictive dashboards.
Year 5: Ecosystem Optimization
Fully integrated data-driven operations. Human staff transition to oversight roles.
