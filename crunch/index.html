1. Operational Friction Points

The "No Judgments" philosophy requires a premium environment. Automation addresses three critical bottlenecks:

  • Sanitation Fatigue: High-traffic HIITZones require cleaning frequencies that outpace human labor capacity.
  • Access Security: Revenue loss from "tailgating" and unauthorized membership sharing.
  • Night Shift Risk: High costs and safety liabilities of 24/7 staffing for unstaffed access.

2. Strategic Robotics Applications

A. HIITZone Autonomous Sanitation

Deploying Pudu CC1 for deep scrubbing turf and mats every 4 hours. Digital heatmaps provide "Proof of Clean" to members via gym displays.

B. Computer Vision Access Control

AI-enabled turnstiles to flag tailgating in real-time. Protects membership dues by detecting non-authorized entries instantly.

C. Promotional Patrol Bots

KettyBot deployment in cardio areas to upsell Personal Training and retail products (Crunch Gear) while distributing samples.
