Prepared for: Capitol Helicopters Inc.

Executive Summary

The aviation industry is undergoing a paradigm shift toward ground operations automation. For Capitol Helicopters Inc., integrating robotic cleaning and maintenance solutions presents an opportunity to elevate operational efficiency. This report outlines a strategic transition to a hybrid automated workflow, reducing aircraft downtime and reallocating skilled labor toward critical mechanical maintenance.
