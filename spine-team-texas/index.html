Spine Team Texas

Executive Summary

Spine Team Texas is a premier regional spine center of excellence based in the DFW metroplex. Their model integrates physiatry, physical therapy, and minimally invasive surgery. The primary automation levers are: (1) Robotic Guidance Systems for ASC surgery, (2) RPA for patient intake/auth, and (3) Sensor-based Rehab. The dominant ROI driver is the conversion of complex cases to outpatient settings via robotic precision.

Organizational Profile

  • Model: Integrated spine care (PM&R + PT + Surgery).
  • Scale: Multiple DFW locations & associated ASCs.
  • Differentiation: 90% non-surgical treatment rate; focus on MIS.
  • Economics: High-margin elective spine procedures & diagnostic imaging.

Operational Constraints

  • Surgical scheduling bottlenecks.
  • High friction in insurance prior authorization (MRI/Surgery).
  • Managing patient flow between conservative care and surgical escalation.
