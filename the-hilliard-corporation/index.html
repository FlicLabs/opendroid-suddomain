Executive Strategic Vision

The Hilliard Corporation is positioned to revolutionize its discrete manufacturing processes. Moving away from purely manual machine tending for Motion Control and Filtration lines, the introduction of Hybrid Automation will decouple labor hours from machine cycle times.

The Goal: Increase throughput for High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) components without expanding the Elmira facility footprint.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of IP66-rated Cobots to tend CNC centers for clutch housings. Allows 1 machinist to supervise 4 machines.

Pathway B

AMR Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy castings (500kg+) between welding, machining, and assembly, improving safety.

Pathway C

Quality & Palletizing

Robotic arms with vision systems to inspect Hilco filter elements and automate end-of-line packaging.
