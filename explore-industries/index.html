Executive Vision

Transition to Smart Manufacturing: Explore Industries will pivot from purely manual fiberglass lamination to a Hybrid Flexible Manufacturing System.

This strategy leverages the "Best of Both Worlds": maintaining the custom artistry of pool shell design while automating the dangerous, high-repetition tasks in the Integra Pool Covers division and resin handling logistics.

Organizational Profile

Sector: Composites & Leisure Manufacturing

Key Process: Hybrid (Process & Discrete)

Primary Friction Points: High labor turnover in lamination, ergonomic strain in cover assembly, and material transport inefficiencies.

Financial Projection

Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Collaborative Assembly (Cobots)

Deployment of IP67-rated cobots for precise drilling and screw-driving in mechanical pool cover assembly. Reduces RSI and improves track alignment.

PATHWAY B AMR Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle resin drums and fiberglass spools. Eliminates non-value-added walking time for skilled laminators.

PATHWAY C Vision Quality Inspection

Automated optical inspection of gel coat thickness and palletizing of finished cover systems. Reduces warranty claims.
