01. Executive Summary

Antelope is positioned to lead the Light Electric Vehicle (LEV) sector by pivoting from linear assembly to a Smart Factory ecosystem. This transformation focuses on high-mix, low-volume flexibility to address global customization demands.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of Cobots (ISO/TS 15066) for motor housing assembly. Focuses on precise torque control and reducing repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots replacing static forklifts to transport frames between welding and painting, reducing WIP congestion.

Pathway C: Automated Palletizing

End-of-Line high-speed palletizers with vision systems to automate boxing, weighing, and stacking for distribution.
