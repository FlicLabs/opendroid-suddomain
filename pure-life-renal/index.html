Executive Summary

Pure Life Renal operates in a labor-intensive sector where clinical staffing and turnover times are critical bottlenecks. Our analysis identifies Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) as the primary levers for value. By automating the "heavy lifting" of fluid logistics and administrative compliance, Pure Life can unlock over $5M in annual network value while significantly improving staff retention and safety.
