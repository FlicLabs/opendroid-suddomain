Executive Summary

Barrett Distribution Centers Inc. operates in a competitive 3PL landscape where urban density and labor costs are primary constraints. The vision for robotics integration is not simply automation, but the creation of a cyber-physical logistics network. By leveraging Open Droids' technology, Barrett can transition from reactive labor models to predictive, AI-driven operations. The goal is to maximize vertical storage density, eliminate non-value-added walking time, and provide clients with data-driven fulfillment guarantees.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Goods-to-Person)

  • Focus: Internal transport & aisle navigation.
  • Benefit: Reduces picker travel by 60%.
  • ROI Driver: High labor cost avoidance in metro areas.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking & ASRS

  • Focus: High-density storage & precision kitting.
  • Benefit: 99.9% accuracy; 40% storage density increase.
  • ROI Driver: Reduced shrinkage & expanded client SKUs.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

  • Focus: Dock scheduling & predictive analytics.
  • Benefit: Optimizes truck turnarounds & load planning.
  • ROI Driver: Elimination of detention fees & energy savings.

Implementation Roadmap
  • Year 1: Infrastructure upgrade & Pilot deployment of AMRs (Pathway A). Focus on WMS integration.
