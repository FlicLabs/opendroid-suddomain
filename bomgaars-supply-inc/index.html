Executive Summary

Bomgaars Supply Inc. requires a scalable logistics framework to support its expanded 180+ store network. Our analysis recommends a hybrid automation approach focusing on Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for heavy transport and AI-driven Goods-to-Person systems for high-mix SKU fulfillment. This strategy addresses the Midwest labor shortage while ensuring peak season resilience.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
PATHWAY A

AMR Internal Transport

Deployment of heavy-payload AMRs to handle pallet movements of feed, seed, and fluids. This decouples transit time from operator action.

  • Reduces forklift traffic/accidents
  • Handles heavy/bulky SKUs
PATHWAY B

Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of high-density storage (Cube or VLM) for small parts, hardware, and clothing. Increases picking accuracy to 99.9%.

  • 60% Floor space reclamation
  • Ideal for high-mix inventory
