Executive Summary

Kettering Anesthesia Associates: Robotics Analysis

Kettering Anesthesia Associates Inc. (KAA) is a critical component of the Kettering Health network in Ohio. As the healthcare landscape shifts towards value-based care, the traditional manual models of drug compounding and staffing are becoming obsolete. This report identifies three high-impact automation levers: (1) AI-Driven Scheduling, (2) Automated Medication Management, and (3) Tele-Anesthesia Robotics.

Strategic Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Medication Automation

Deployment of automated point-of-care dispensing and robotic IV compounding. Reduces drug waste by 15-20% and eliminates manual billing errors.

IMPACT: Safety & Cost Containment

Pathway B: AI Staffing Logistics

Algorithmic software robotics to align CRNA/MD shifts with predicted surgical volume. Reduces overtime and optimizes OR turnover.

IMPACT: Efficiency & Throughput

Pathway C: Tele-Anesthesia

Remote presence robotics allowing supervising anesthesiologists to monitor multiple satellite locations simultaneously.

IMPACT: Reach & Scale
