Walker Methodist: Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Walker Methodist faces a critical turning point in senior living. By leveraging automation in culinary services, sanitation, and logistics, the organization can shift capital from inefficiency to care quality. The proposed strategy targets a network-wide labor savings of 43,800 hours annually, directly addressing the CNA and culinary staffing crisis.

Pathway A: Culinary

Automated runners & bussers

• 60-70% less walking for staff
• 20% faster table turnover
• Payback: 6-9 Months

Pathway B: Logistics

Scrubbers & Supply Tugs

• Autonomous night cleaning
• Reduced staff injury risk
• Payback: 12-18 Months
