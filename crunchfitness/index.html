Crunch Fitness

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Crunch Fitness operates on a High-Volume, Low-Cost (HVLC) model across 400+ locations. The primary robotics opportunity lies in shifting operational focus from labor-intensive maintenance to automated efficiency.

The strategy focuses on three core pillars: (1) Autonomous Janitorial Services to reduce OpEx, (2) Robotic Retail/Nutrition to increase ancillary revenue, and (3) AI-Driven Asset Management for capital efficiency. The dominant ROI driver is labor reduction in facility management.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cleaning Automation

Scope: Autonomous mobile scrubbers & UV-C disinfection robots.

Impact: 30-40% reduction in janitorial hours. Consistent hygiene standards.

1.5 Years Payback Period

Pathway B: Robotic Retail

Scope: Automated smoothie kiosks & smart vending.

Impact: 24/7 revenue generation without front-desk labor bottlenecks.

~$40k Annual Rev Lift

Pathway C: Smart Assets

Scope: AI usage tracking & predictive maintenance.

Impact: Optimized floor planning and reduced equipment downtime.
