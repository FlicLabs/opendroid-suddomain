Robotics Opportunity Portfolio & ROI Model

Executive Summary

Alliance for a Healthier Generation (AHG) supports 54,000+ schools and 31M+ children. Integrating robotics offers a scalable path to impact. The primary levers are (1) Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) for student wellness, (2) Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for assessment data, and (3) Autonomous logistics for food equity. This shift promises a 4.2x Social Return on Investment (SROI) over 5 years.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Social Robotics

Deploying robots like Misty II or Nao in schools to lead physical activity and mindfulness. Standardizes delivery and increases student engagement.

Pathway B: RPA Assessment

Automating data scraping and compliance reporting for the "Healthy Schools Program." Saves 15,000 staff hours annually.

Pathway C: Autonomous Logistics

Partnerships with autonomous delivery fleets to distribute fresh food in food deserts, reducing logistics costs by 60%.
