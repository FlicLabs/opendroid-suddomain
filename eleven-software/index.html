Executive Summary: Eleven Software

Eleven Software is positioned to capture the emerging "Robotics Middleware" market. The core opportunity is not hardware, but the "intelligence layer" that connects disparate robots to hospital infrastructure (IoT), significantly increasing valuation multiples and long-term margins.
