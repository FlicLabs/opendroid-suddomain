01. Executive Vision

The hospitality sector is navigating a pivotal transition to hybrid automated ecosystems. For Brown Foodservice Inc., robotics offers a dual advantage: a buffer against labor shortages in the Appalachian corridor and a modernized dining experience. The goal is staff augmentation, not replacement, allowing human servers to focus on high-value guest interactions.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Serving Robots

Focus: LiDAR units for heavy lifting (trays/busing). Reduces server fatigue and increases floor presence.

Pathway B: Auto-Frying

Focus: Automated arms for consistency and safety. Crucial for high-volume fried food menus to reduce waste.

Pathway C: AI Kiosks

Focus: Upselling logic and order accuracy. Drive-thru voice agents to maximize check averages.
