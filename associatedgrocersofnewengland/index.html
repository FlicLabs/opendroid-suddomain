Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Internal Transport (AMRs)

Decoupling human operators from long-haul travel. Reduces forklift leases and liability while increasing pick-face replenishment speed by 35%.

Pathway B: ASRS & Piece Picking

High-density automated storage reduces footprint by 60%, critical for expensive frozen/refrigerated zones. Increases pick rates from 150 to 450 lines/hr.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive algorithms for route density optimization. Pre-positions inventory based on local event data and historical velocity.
