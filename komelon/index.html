Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

AMR Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace static conveyors and pallet jacks. Automating transport of heavy master cases reduces fatigue and increases transfer rates.

Pathway B

Piece Picking & ASRS

Vertical automated storage for uniform products (tape measures). Increases density by 85% and virtually eliminates picking errors.

Pathway C

AI Logistics

Predictive modeling for seasonal construction demand. Pre-positions stock for 20% faster outbound cycle times.
