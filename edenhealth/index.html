Eden Health // Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Executive Summary: Eden Health is uniquely positioned to leverage "Software Robotics" (RPA) and "Service Robotics" to scale its hybrid concierge care model. By automating administrative navigation and clinical intake, the company can decouple revenue growth from headcount, generating projected annual savings of $2M - $4M with a payback period of under 9 months.

1. Organizational Profile

Model: Direct-to-employer hybrid care (Virtual + Physical Clinics + Navigation).

Scale: National coverage, physical hubs in major metros (NY, Boston, DC, etc.).

Bottleneck: Linear scaling of "Care Navigator" staff and administrative overhead.

2. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Admin RPA

Software Robotics High ROI

Deployment of AI "digital workers" to handle insurance verification, prior auths, and referral booking.

Impact: 30-40% reduction in admin time per encounter.

Pathway B: Clinical Intake

Hardware Experience

Automated diagnostic kiosks for vitals (BP, SpO2) and check-in at physical clinics.

Impact: Increases clinic throughput by 15%.

Pathway C: Telepresence

Remote Expansion

Robotic telepresence for remote specialist access in satellite/pop-up locations.

Impact: Reduces provider travel; retains specialty referrals.
