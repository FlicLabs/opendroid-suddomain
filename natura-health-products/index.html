Executive Overview

Natura Health Products faces critical bottlenecks in warehouse picking and quality control due to manual processes in a GMP environment. Our analysis identifies Warehouse Automation (AMRs) as the primary lever for growth, capable of increasing throughput capacity by 60% while reducing logistics operating costs. Secondary opportunities in automated visual inspection ensure brand protection and regulatory compliance.
