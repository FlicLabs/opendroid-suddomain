Pathway A: Cath Lab Robotics

Scope: Deployment of systems like CorPath GRX for robotic-assisted PCI.

  • Precision stent placement (1mm accuracy).
  • Allows physicians to operate from a radiation-shielded cockpit.
  • Reduces orthopedic strain, extending career longevity.

Pathway B: Admin Automation

Scope: RPA bots for prior authorizations and revenue cycle management.

  • Automates insurance verification and status checks.
  • Reduces denial rates by 15-20%.
  • Frees up clinical staff to focus on patient care rather than paperwork.

Strategic Positioning

Implementing this roadmap positions Fort Worth Heart not just as a care provider, but as a regional Tech-Enabled Cardiovascular Institute. This differentiation attracts top talent seeking safety from radiation and appeals to payers looking for the lowered readmission rates associated with robotic precision.
