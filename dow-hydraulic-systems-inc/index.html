DOW HYDRAULIC SYSTEMS INC.

Strategic Research: Aviation Cleaning & Maintenance Automation

Executive Summary

This report delineates a strategic framework for the integration of robotic automation within Dow Hydraulic Systems Inc.'s operational infrastructure. The vision centers on transitioning from manual-intensive workflows to a highly efficient, automated ecosystem. By leveraging advanced robotics, Dow aims to elevate safety standards, optimize turnaround times, and achieve significant OPEX reductions. The proposed strategy ensures scalability and aligns with future aviation maintenance standards.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Deployment Roadmap

  • Pathway A (Cabin Cleaning): Autonomous AMRs for vacuuming and seat sanitization. Reduces manual labor by 40% per cycle.
  • Pathway B (UV-C Disinfection): Stationary UV bots for pathogen neutralization. Cycles under 10 mins vs 45 mins manual.
  • Pathway C (Exterior Washing): Rail-guided high-pressure systems. 40% reduction in water usage.
  • Pathway D (AI Inspection): Thermal imaging crawlers for crack detection. Integrates with predictive MRO systems.

Performance Analytics

Implementation Roadmap (5 Years)

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase at Primary Hub. Infrastructure assessment and baseline metric collection.
  • Year 2: Expansion of Cabin & UV-C bots. Integration with MRO software systems.
  • Year 3: Deployment of Exterior Washing Systems. Limited trials of AI Inspection Bots.
  • Year 4: Full Fleet Deployment across all hubs. 60% routine task automation achieved.
  • Year 5: Optimization and Autonomy. Data-driven workflow refinement and next-gen evaluation.
