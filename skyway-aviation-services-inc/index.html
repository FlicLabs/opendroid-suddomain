Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Mobile pathogen neutralization. Chemical-free sanitation for high passenger confidence.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated fuselage crawling systems. Improves fuel efficiency via surface smoothness.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Integrated anomaly detection during cleaning cycles to prevent costly repairs.
