Executive Vision

Cleveland Hopkins International Airport stands at a critical juncture of modernization. The vision is to deploy a centralized, airport-managed fleet of autonomous units to service partner airlines. This strategy aims to drastically reduce aircraft turnaround times (TAT), enhance passenger health safety standards, and optimize labor allocation toward high-value maintenance tasks. Open Droids proposes a transition from manual labor to a tech-forward operational hub.
