EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Piston2Jet stands at a critical operational juncture where traditional manual cleaning and maintenance processes are becoming a bottleneck to scalability. Our vision involves a holistic integration of autonomous robotics to handle repetitive, high-risk, and labor-intensive tasks.

By deploying intelligent robotics for cabin sanitation, exterior washing, and structural inspection, Piston2Jet can expect to reduce aircraft ground time, improve technical dispatch reliability, and significantly lower long-term operational expenditures.
