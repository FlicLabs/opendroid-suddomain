Executive Summary

For PEM-AIR, the integration of robotic cleaning and maintenance technologies represents a critical lever for increasing fleet utilization. Our research indicates that by adopting the Open Droids automation framework, PEM-AIR can reduce aircraft turnaround times, elevate hygiene standards, and stabilize operational costs against labor market volatility.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-guided rovers designed for single-aisle aircraft. simultaneous vacuuming and surface wiping.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection

Dry disinfection utilizing ultraviolet light to eliminate 99.9% of pathogens without liquid residue.

Pathway C

Exterior Systems

Automated gantry or tethered drone systems for fuselage washing, improving fuel efficiency via drag reduction.

Pathway D

AI Inspection

Computer vision drones and magnetic crawlers for micro-crack and lightning strike detection.
