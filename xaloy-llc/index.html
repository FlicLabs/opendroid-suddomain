Executive Vision: Smart Plasticization

Xaloy LLC is positioned to transition from traditional discrete machining to a connected, flexible manufacturing ecosystem. The focus is on handling "High-Mix, Low-Volume" specialized screw and barrel manufacturing through targeted robotics.

By deploying cobots for machine tending and AMRs for material transport, Xaloy can reduce lead times, enhance safety in heavy metal handling, and ensure consistent bimetallic quality.

Robotic Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A (Cobots): Deployment of heavy-payload Cobots (Fanuc CRX/UR16e) for machine tending CNC grinders. Removes operator fatigue from lifting heavy steel shafts.

PATHWAY B (AMRs): Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) to move billets and barrels between casting and finishing. Connects islands of automation.

PATHWAY C (Quality): Vision-guided systems for inspecting bimetallic coating porosity and automated end-of-line palletizing.
