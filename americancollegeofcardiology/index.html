American College of Cardiology (ACC)

Executive Summary

The ACC is uniquely positioned to monetize the transition to robotic cardiology not by performing surgery, but by becoming the Data & Credentialing Central Bank. The opportunity lies in integrating robotic telemetry directly into the NCDR registries and establishing a high-margin robotic certification business.
