Executive Vision

The strategic goal for Fadó is "Invisible Support Automation." By deploying robotics to handle high-weight logistics (bussing) and high-precision cooking (frying), we preserve the authentic "Craic" by allowing human staff to focus entirely on guest rapport rather than manual labor.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Support Bots

Deployment of tiered bussing robots to manage glassware logistics. Reduces server fatigue and eliminates "empty hands" trips to the kitchen.

Pathway B: Automated Frying

Back-of-house integration for "Fish & Chips" consistency. Ensures perfect cook times and improves kitchen safety.

Pathway C: Inventory AI

Backend prediction algorithms to manage import draft depletion rates, preventing stock-outs during major sporting events.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Bussing Robots in 2 flagship locations.
Year 2: Chain-wide FOH rollout & BOH Fryer pilot.
Year 3: Full POS Integration for auto-dispatch.
Year 5: Full Autonomy Ecosystem.
