Executive Summary

P3 Health Partners is a physician-led population health management company operating strictly within value-based care. Unlike fee-for-service providers, P3 takes on global risk. Therefore, the robotics strategy focuses on Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to lower SG&A costs and Remote Patient Monitoring to suppress hospital utilization, rather than surgical hardware.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: High ROI

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Focus: Claims, Coding, Prior Auth

  • Automate data scraping for patient records.
  • Algorithmic processing of referrals.
  • Automated risk adjustment coding review.
Pathway B: Risk Control

Remote Home Robotics

Focus: Top 5% High-Risk Patients

  • Smart Hubs for daily vital tracking.
  • Robotic medication dispensers.
  • Virtual presence devices for care teams.
Pathway C: Retention

Clinical Workflow AI

Focus: Physician Network

  • Ambient digital scribes for notes.
  • Automated inbox management.
  • Reduced physician burnout.
