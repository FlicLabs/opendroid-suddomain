ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

Avmax Group is a diversified aviation company with a primary focus on aircraft leasing, regional airline operations, and comprehensive MRO services.

  • Fleet Composition: Avmax maintains a fleet exceeding 100 aircraft, primarily regional jets (CRJ series) and turboprops (Dash 8), alongside narrow-body jet assets.
  • Operational Hubs: Major maintenance and leasing hubs in Calgary, Vancouver, and strategic US locations, with reach into Africa and the Caribbean.
  • Current Model: Relies on manual labor for cabin cleaning and periodic deep cleaning. Exterior washing is traditionally outsourced or performed manually, creating safety risks and scheduling bottlenecks.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Mobile autonomous robots for vacuuming and wiping tray tables. Addresses labor shortages and standardizes lease return preservation.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous UV-C robots for rapid disinfection. Enhances passenger confidence without increasing chemical usage.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems or crawlers for fuselage washing. Reduces scaffolding requirements and improves safety.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drones/crawlers with thermal imaging. Reduces MRO inspection times and improves predictive maintenance data.

FINANCIAL MODEL

  • CAPEX Estimates: 75,000 to 150,000 USD per unit (Cabin vs. Exterior/AI systems). Initial Pilot Acquisition: ~2.5M USD.
  • OPEX Reduction Targets: 35% to 45% reduction in direct cleaning labor hours. 15% reduction in consumables via precision application.
  • Payback Period: Under 24 months. ROI driven by labor reallocation to revenue-generating MRO activities and increased aircraft utilization.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP

  • Year 1: Feasibility & Pilot. Deployment of 2 units (1 Cabin, 1 Exterior) at primary hub.
  • Year 2: Expansion. Scale to 10 units. Integration with MRO management systems.
  • Year 3: Standardization. Exterior washing robots at all bases. Full UV-C adoption for lease returns.
  • Year 4: AI Integration. Deployment of AI-Powered Inspection Bots. Predictive analytics implementation.
  • Year 5: Autonomous Ecosystem. Full operational autonomy. Potential "Cleaning-as-a-Service" revenue stream.
