Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs & Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for pallet movements. Targets cross-docking efficiency and reduction of forklift travel time.

Pathway B: ASRS & Picking

Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems for vertical density. Essential for scaling food/beverage storage without expanding footprint.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer vision for inbound/outbound inspection. Predictive logic for dock scheduling to eliminate detention fees.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 Pilot Program: 4 AMRs at Harrisonburg facility + WMS Integration.
  • Year 2 Expansion: Fleet expansion to Front Royal & AI Vision at docks.
  • Year 3 High Density: Installation of ASRS infrastructure for cold storage.
  • Year 4 Ecosystem: Full Yard Management System integration with robotics.
  • Year 5 Optimization: Digital Twin simulation for predictive bottlenecking.
