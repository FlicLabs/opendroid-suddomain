Docs Dermatology

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model
Prepared for Strategy Team
2024 Outlook
Executive Summary: Docs Dermatology is positioned to leverage robotics to drive high-margin aesthetic revenue and streamline diagnostic workflows. The primary ROI driver is Robotic Hair Restoration (ARTAS iX), offering rapid payback and significant incremental margin. Secondary opportunities in Automated Imaging enhance patient retention and screening efficiency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A

Robotic Hair Restoration

Focus: Aesthetic Revenue & Surgeon Ergonomics

Deploying ARTAS iX systems in regional "Centers of Excellence." Reduces surgeon fatigue and commands premium pricing.

High ROI Marketing Asset
$710k+ Annual Net Benefit Per Hub
B

Automated 3D Imaging

Focus: Diagnostics & Patient Retention

3D full-body mole mapping (Vectra WB360). Automates screening documentation and uses AI to track lesion evolution.

Standard of Care Retention
12-18 Mo Payback Period
C

Mohs Lab Automation

Focus: Operational Efficiency

Automated tissue staining and processing to reduce "frozen section" wait times and increase surgical throughput.
