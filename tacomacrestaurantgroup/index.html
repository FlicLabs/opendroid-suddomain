Strategic Vision: The Hybrid Sports Bar

Taco Mac sits at a critical intersection of high-volume casual dining and sports entertainment. Our research indicates that the "Game Day Bottleneck"—where kitchen output and server logistics fail to meet peak demand—costs the group approximately 12-15% in lost potential sales per major event.

The Solution: By offloading low-skill transport tasks (food running) and dangerous repetition (wing frying) to automation, human staff can focus on the "Brewniversity" guest experience and beverage upselling.

Implementation Roadmap
  • Year 1: Pilot 2 Servi robots in Atlanta Flagship stores.
  • Year 2: Chain-wide FOH rollout; Kitchen automation pilot.
  • Year 3: Full AI-Voice integration for takeout/delivery.
