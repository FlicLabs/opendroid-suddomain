Rochester Primary Care Network Inc.

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Executive Summary

Rochester Primary Care Network Inc (RPCN) is positioned to transform its operational efficiency through strategic automation. Operating within the tight margins of FQHC reimbursement, RPCN's primary friction points are administrative burden and clinical throughput. The analysis identifies Administrative RPA and Diagnostic Intake Kiosks as the highest ROI levers, capable of generating millions in operational capacity and cost avoidance.

Pathway A: Administrative RPA

Scope

Deployment of software bots for scheduling, insurance verification, and care gap closure.

Impact

Reduces no-show rates by 15-20% and recovers 2-3 hours of provider time weekly per clinician.

Pathway B: Intake Kiosks

Scope

Semi-autonomous diagnostic stations in waiting areas for vitals collection.

Impact

Reduces Medical Assistant workload by 5-7 mins/patient, allowing 1.5 extra visits per provider/day.
