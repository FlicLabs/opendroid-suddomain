Executive Vision: Industrialized Construction

MKA is positioned to lead the AEC sector's transition to Industry 4.0. By adopting Discrete Manufacturing principles for structural nodes and modular assemblies, MKA can bridge the gap between digital BIM design and physical fabrication.

Strategic Goal: Reduce project delivery times by 30% through high-fidelity robotic intervention.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • PATHWAY A Collaborative Assembly: Utilizing high-payload Cobots for adaptive welding of custom steel nodes and fastening of CLT timber panels.
  • PATHWAY B AMR Logistics: Deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots for JIT line-side replenishment of heavy structural components, eliminating forklift bottlenecks.
  • PATHWAY C Automated Inspection: Implementing 3D Metrology Scanning at End-of-Line to ensure millimeter-perfect tolerance matching BIM Digital Twins.
