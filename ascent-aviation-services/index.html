STRATEGIC AUTOMATION RESEARCH

Prepared for Ascent Aviation Services

Executive Summary

Ascent Aviation Services stands to revolutionize its MRO capabilities through the integration of autonomous robotics. This research outlines a transition from manual intensive cleaning and inspection to a digitized, automated workflow. The proposed droids will handle repetitive hazardous tasks, allowing skilled technicians to focus on complex maintenance, thereby reducing turnaround times and operational overhead.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous LiDAR-guided vacuums and sanitizers for rapid A-Check turnaround.

B. UVC Disinfection

Chemical-free pathogen neutralization using high-intensity ultraviolet light arrays.

C. Exterior Wash

Large-scale gantry and drone systems for precise, eco-friendly fuselage washing.

D. AI Inspection

Drone swarms utilizing computer vision to detect dents, lightning strikes, and buckles.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program at Marana Facility (Data Collection).
  • Year 2: Deployment of Exterior Wash Systems at Tucson Hub.
  • Year 3: Full Fleet Integration & Software Telemetry connection.
  • Year 4: Autonomous Night Shift Operations (Lights-out maintenance).
  • Year 5: Hardware Optimization and Sensor Array Upgrades.
