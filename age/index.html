Executive Vision: Industry 4.0

AG&E stands at the precipice of a major operational shift. By moving from rigid production lines to flexible, modular robotic cells, the organization can secure its future against labor volatility and supply chain disruptions. The strategy focuses on high-mix/low-volume resilience, aiming for "Dark Factory" capabilities for night shifts while elevating human staff to supervisory roles.
