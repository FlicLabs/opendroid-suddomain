Executive Vision

Harvest Hill stands at the precipice of a major operational evolution. By transitioning from rigid batch processing to an agile, data-driven ecosystem, the company can address market volatility with precision. The proposed integration of Industry 4.0 technologies focuses on "High-Mix, High-Volume" capabilities, specifically targeting changeover reduction and end-of-line logistics to maximize profitability per SKU.

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deploying collaborative robots for machine tending and variety pack assembly. Removes human hands from dangerous hoppers and enables flexible repacking.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle heavy liquid concentrate drums and packaging supplies. Reduces forklift traffic and optimizes just-in-time delivery.

Pathway C: Palletizing

Focus: Throughput

High-speed robotic arms with vision systems for end-of-line stacking. Ensures load stability for heavy beverage pallets and reduces physical strain injuries.
