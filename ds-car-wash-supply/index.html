D&S Car Wash Supply is positioned to transition from traditional discrete assembly to a flexible Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By integrating robotic precision into the manufacturing of IQ Softouch and Quicksilver systems, D&S can achieve high-mix customization without sacrificing throughput speed.
