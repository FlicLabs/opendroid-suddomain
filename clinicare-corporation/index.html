Client Profile: Clinicare Corporation

Clinicare operates 150 outpatient clinics and a regional home health network. The primary robotics opportunity lies in hybrid automation: optimizing field logistics for home health while deploying physical supply-chain robotics within clinics. This strategy unlocks ~$18M in annual operational savings and addresses critical staffing bottlenecks.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A Logistics AI & Scheduling

RPA for intelligent slotting and route optimization. Highest immediate ROI.

Impact: £12M/Year
Pathway B Clinic Service AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for supplies and sanitation. Reduces staff burnout.

Impact: £3M/Year
Pathway C Therapeutic Robotics

Exoskeletons and gait trainers for neuro-rehab. Strategic market differentiator.

Impact: Strategic Growth

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 (Year 1) Launch Pathway A (Logistics AI) & Pilot Pathway C in 5 urban hubs.
Phase 2 (Year 2) Deploy Service AMRs to top 20 clinics. Expand AI scheduling network-wide.
Phase 3 (Years 3-5) Full Therapeutic Robotics integration & Autonomous Vehicle pilots.
