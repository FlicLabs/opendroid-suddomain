Executive Vision

For 23 Restaurant Services, the integration of robotics represents a shift from a labor-dependent operational structure to a scalable, tech-enabled framework. By deploying autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for low-value logistical tasks, we empower human staff to focus entirely on guest experience and upselling, securing margins against rising wage volatility.

Pathway A: FOH Service

Deployment of runner/busser bots. Eliminates kitchen-to-table travel time, allowing servers to manage larger sections with less fatigue.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Automated frying and grilling stations. Ensures precise consistency across all locations and drastically reduces workplace burn injuries.

Pathway C: AI Agents

Voice-enabled kiosks and drive-thru agents. Designed to increase average check size via algorithmic upselling and zero-error order entry.
