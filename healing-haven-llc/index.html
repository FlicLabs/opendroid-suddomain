Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Facility Logistics

Deployment of AMRs for cleaning and supply delivery reduces non-clinical overhead.

  • Impact: 30-40% reduction in cleaning labor costs.
  • Technology: UV-C Sanitization Robots, Supply Delivery AMRs.
  • ROI: Capital payback in 12-18 months via labor savings.

Pathway B: AI Scheduling & Workflow

Software robotics to optimize appointment gaps and automate documentation.

  • Impact: Increases clinician utilization from 65% to 80%.
  • Technology: Predictive Algorithms, NLP Documentation.
  • ROI: Immediate revenue uplift from recovered billable hours.

Pathway C: Assistive & Social Robotics

Integration of therapeutic robots for ASD and social skills training.

  • Impact: Improved patient outcomes and market differentiation.
  • Technology: Socially Assistive Robots (SARs), Motor support exoskeletons.
  • ROI: Intangible brand value and premium service offering.
