Executive Summary

Multi Image Group operates at the intersection of artistic expression and heavy industrial logistics. The strongest robotics levers are (1) Creative Robotics for live stage automation to drive premium fees, and (2) Automated Fabrication using multi-axis robotic milling to streamline scenic construction. This dual approach secures top-line growth through differentiation and bottom-line growth through efficiency.

Implementation Roadmap
  • Year 1: Pathway B (Fabrication). Install 7-axis robotic milling cell in the scenic shop to cut overtime costs.
  • Year 2: Pathway A (Creative). Acquire robotic arms for a "Kinetic Screens" touring package.
  • Year 3: Pathway C (Logistics). Deploy Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for warehouse inventory movement.
