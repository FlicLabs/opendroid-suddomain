FORD'S GARAGE

Automation Strategy & Financial Impact Report

Executive Summary

Ford's Garage represents a unique intersection of nostalgia and industrial efficiency. By leveraging the brand's association with Henry Ford—the father of the assembly line—there is a distinct narrative opportunity to introduce automation not just as a cost-saving measure, but as a thematic evolution of the brand. Our strategy focuses on "Hybrid Service," using robotics for heavy lifting (food running/bussing) to allow human staff to focus on the guest experience.
