Executive Summary

Institute on Aging (IOA) faces critical challenges in caregiver staffing and managing the complex needs of the PACE population. By deploying an integrated robotics portfolio—specifically Social AI, Exoskeletons, and Telepresence—IOA can shift from a labor-heavy operational model to a tech-enabled longevity platform. The primary ROI driver is the reduction of acute episodes (ER/Nursing Home) via continuous AI monitoring.
