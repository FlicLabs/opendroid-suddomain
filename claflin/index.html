Executive Summary

Claflin Company is a premier medical distributor and supply chain solutions provider specializing in acute care and LUM (Low Unit of Measure) delivery across the Northeast. The strongest robotics levers are Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for warehouse picking and Automated Inventory Cycle Counting. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of travel time for warehouse associates, enabling millions in operational savings and increased capacity without expanding physical footprint.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Assisted Picking (Highest Value)

Deployment of collaborative bots (Locus/Fetch) that travel to pick locations. Associates stay in zones while bots handle the transport.

  • Reduction of non-value-added travel time by 60%.
  • Elimination of cart pushing and long walks to packing stations.
  • Near elimination of picking errors via put-to-light interfaces.

Pathway B: Automated Inventory

Drones or ground robots with RFID/Vision for nightly cycle counts.

Pathway C: Supply Chain AI
