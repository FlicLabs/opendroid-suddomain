Executive Summary

Catalyst Nutraceuticals operates in a high-velocity market where speed-to-shelf is critical. The strongest robotics levers identified are (1) End-of-Line Packaging Automation, (2) Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics, and (3) Automated Vision QC. The dominant ROI driver is the 30% increase in throughput capacity, enabling millions in incremental revenue while reducing labor dependency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: End-of-Line Packaging (Highest Value)

Deployment of robotic case packers and collaborative palletizers. Key impact includes a lift from 60-70% theoretical capacity to 95%, with a labor reduction of 4-6 FTEs per shift.

Pathway B: Autonomous Logistics (High Efficiency)

Fleet of AMRs transporting raw ingredients from warehouse to weigh rooms. Reduces forklift traffic, improves safety, and ensures GMP traceability.

Pathway C: Vision Inspection (Quality Control)

High-speed inline vision systems for 100% capsule inspection and label verification (OCR), drastically reducing recall risk and material waste.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 (Pilot): Install robotic palletizer/packer on highest volume protein or capsule line.
  • Year 2 (Scale): Deploy 3-5 AMRs for raw material transport; expand packaging automation to secondary lines.
  • Year 3 (Integration): Full Vision QC rollout and ERP integration for automated material calls.
