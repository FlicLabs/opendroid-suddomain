2. Organizational Profile

Manufacturing Type: Hybrid (Discrete Fabrication + Process/Project-Based).

Operational Focus: High-volume projects for major OEMs (GM, Ford, Stellantis) involving modular steel components, air supply houses, and oven panels.

Current State: Heavy reliance on manual welding and forklift transport. High potential for automation retrofit in repetitive joining and material handling.

3. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Application: High-Mix Welding.

Benefit: Addresses welder shortage. Ensures uniform bead consistency on modular ductwork without safety fencing.

Pathway B: AMRs

Application: Material Transport.

Benefit: Heavy-duty AMRs (1000kg) automate movement of raw sheet metal, reducing forklift risks and wait times.

Pathway C: Inspection

Application: Digital Twin Verification.

Benefit: LiDAR scanning validates modules against BIM models before shipping to minimize field rework.
