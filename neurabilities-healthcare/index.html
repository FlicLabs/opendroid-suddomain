NeurAbilities Healthcare

Executive Summary

NeurAbilities Healthcare operates at the intersection of neurology and behavioral health. The primary opportunity lies in deploying Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) to reduce clinician burnout and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to streamline complex billing. This strategy shifts the model from labor-constrained to tech-enabled, unlocking significant margin via retention and utilization.
