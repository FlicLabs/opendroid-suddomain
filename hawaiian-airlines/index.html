** Hawaiian Airlines: Robotics Automation Strategy

HAWAIIAN AIRLINES

Aviation Cleaning & Maintenance Automation Strategy

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Hawaiian Airlines stands at a pivotal opportunity to enhance its operational excellence and brand reputation through the strategic integration of aviation cleaning and maintenance automation. As the largest airline in Hawaii, renowned for its commitment to safety, comfort, and the spirit of Aloha, adopting advanced robotic solutions will streamline ground operations, ensure superior hygiene standards, and reduce aircraft turnaround times. This report outlines a comprehensive strategy for deploying robotic cleaning automation, focusing on four key technological pathways designed to increase efficiency, reduce operational costs, and elevate the passenger experience across their unique island-hopping and long-haul routes.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Deploying AMRs for aisle carpets, seats, and overhead bins. Ensures consistent coverage without extending ground time, vital for high-volume inter-island routes.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection

Self-navigating UV-C robots for cabin surfaces and air during overnight layovers. Addresses health concerns and supports the "Clean Aircraft" commitment.

Pathway C

Exterior Washing

Automated robotic systems to wash and polish exteriors, removing salt residue. Reduces water usage and chemical runoff in coastal environments.

Pathway D

AI Inspection Bots

Miniature drones/crawlers with AI analytics for fuselage and wing inspection. Transitions maintenance from reactive to predictive, critical for corrosion control.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP

YEAR 1: Pilot Program

Deploy 5 Cabin Cleaning & 2 UV-C units at HNL. Beta test AI Inspection Bots on A330 fleet.

YEAR 2: Expansion

Expand to OGG. Integrate UV-C for wide-bodies. Install first Exterior Washing System at HNL.

YEAR 3: Standardization

Fleet-wide Cabin Robots for narrow-bodies. Full deployment of AI Inspection Bots.

YEAR 4: Optimization

Upgrade fleet with newer gen tech. Full Exterior Washing implementation. Water waste reduction focus.

YEAR 5: Future-Proofing

Complete integration. Evaluation of next-gen transport tech. Target 40% OPEX reduction achieved.
