Executive Vision

Dswdist stands at a critical juncture. The proposed "Adaptive Footwear Grid" transitions operations from static conveyor belts to a modular, robot-driven ecosystem. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and specialized Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems capable of handling rigid shoe boxes, Dswdist can decouple labor from transit, ensuring high-speed accuracy for both Direct-to-Consumer fulfillment and store replenishment.
