Executive Vision

Flow-Rite Controls is positioned to transition from traditional manufacturing to a Connected Factory model. By leveraging flexible robotics for fluid control components, we project a 40% reduction in changeover times and a significant leap in quality assurance consistency.

Organizational Profile

  • HQ Location: Byron Center, Michigan
  • Process Type: Hybrid (Injection Molding & Discrete Assembly)
  • Key Challenge: Manual WIP conveyance & Machine Tending bottlenecks

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67 Cobots for injection molding. Reduces human exposure to heat and ensures cycle consistency.

Pathway B: Intralogistics

AMRs for material transport between molding and assembly. Creates a continuous, autonomous material loop.

Pathway C: EOL Palletizing

Vision-guided palletizing to inspect fluid indicators and stack final kits, ensuring 100% yield.
