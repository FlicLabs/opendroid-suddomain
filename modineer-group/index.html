Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for press brakes and stamping machines. Reduces operator injury risk and stabilizes cycle times.

Pathway B: AMR Material Transport

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for WIP flow. Replaces forklifts for transporting stamped parts to welding cells.

Pathway C: Automated Inspection

End-of-line robotic arms with Machine Vision systems. Ensures 100% quality verification on metal finishes and weld integrity.
