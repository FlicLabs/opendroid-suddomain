Executive Summary

Polaris Healthcare is uniquely positioned to leverage robotics to stabilize workforce dynamics in the post-acute sector. The strategic deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics and Robotic Rehabilitation systems will drive operational efficiency and revenue growth. Our analysis indicates a potential net operational benefit of over £720k annually in the initial phase alone, with payback periods of less than 12 months for logistics automation.
