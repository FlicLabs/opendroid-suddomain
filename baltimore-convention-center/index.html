Baltimore Convention Center Opportunities

The Baltimore Convention Center (BCC) represents a high-impact environment for robotic automation due to its 1.2M sq. ft. scale and high turnover frequency. Our analysis indicates that shifting from manual labor to autonomous systems in floor care and logistics will drive the highest ROI. By deploying AI-driven hygiene robots and logistic AMRs, BCC can reduce operational turnover costs by ~30% while enhancing service quality for high-value medical and tech conventions.
