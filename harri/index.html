Executive Strategic Insight

Harri Hospitality is positioned to leverage "Pathway A" (FOH Robotics) immediately. By offloading the logistical burden of food running, your brand can maintain high-touch hospitality with a leaner staff. The projected ROI indicates a break-even point in under 12 months, funded largely by the reduction in runner/busser hours and increased peak-hour throughput.
