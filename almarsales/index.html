Executive Vision

Almar Sales is positioned to revolutionize its wholesale distribution of uniforms and safety gear by transitioning from a manual pick model to a high-density, automated ecosystem. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI, we target a 90% reduction in pick errors and a doubling of throughput capacity in existing Canadian facilities.
