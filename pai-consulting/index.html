Pathway D: AI Inspection

Focus: Safety & Predictive Maintenance

AI-powered bots detect micro-cracks and corrosion, shifting from scheduled to condition-based maintenance.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase

Deployment of 2-3 Cabin Cleaning robots and 1 UV-C unit at a primary hub. Focus on data integration and staff training. Establishing baseline metrics for ROI validation.

Year 2: Expansion

Scale to 3 major hubs. Introduction of Exterior Washing Systems for specific fleet types. Optimization of robotic routes using AI learning from Year 1 data.

Year 3: Full Penetration

50% of active fleet serviced by robotic units. Deployment of AI Inspection Bots for line maintenance checks. Significant reduction in manual labor hours.

Year 4: Ecosystem Automation

100% major hub coverage. Inter-robot communication (IoT mesh) enables fully coordinated cleaning schedules. Predictive maintenance fully integrated.

Year 5: Maturity

Standardized SOPs. Robotic units handle 100% of routine cleaning. Human staff transitioned to quality assurance and specialized detailing roles. Full 40% OPEX reduction realized.
