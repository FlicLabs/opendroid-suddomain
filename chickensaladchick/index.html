01. Executive Vision

Southern Hospitality Meets Innovation: The strategic goal is to automate logistics (food running) to maximize human connection. By offloading the physical burden of tray delivery to collaborative robots, Chicken Salad Chick can maintain its high-touch service model while immunizing operations against labor volatility.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots High Priority

Deployment of shelf-based delivery robots (e.g., PuduBot/Servi). This transforms servers into "Dining Room Ambassadors," reducing fatigue and increasing face time with guests.

Pathway B: BOH Prep Automation Medium Priority

Focus on automated mixing and portioning for chicken salad consistency. Unlike traditional QSRs, frying automation is replaced here by cold-prep logistics and automated dishwashing.

Pathway C: Voice AI Medium Priority

Implementation of Conversational AI in Drive-Thrus to standardize upselling of drinks and cookies, projected to increase ticket size by 5-8%.
