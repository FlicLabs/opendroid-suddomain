Executive Summary

TechStar is positioned to transition from legacy fixed-automation to a flexible, data-driven Industry 4.0 framework. By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), TechStar will create dynamic production cells, projected to reduce downtime by 40% and increase floor-space utilization significantly.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Machine tending for CNC and PCB assembly. Eliminates repetitive strain and ensures consistent torque application.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Automated Material Transport to decouple logistics from production. Targets 35% increase in JIT delivery precision.

PATHWAY C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Vision-guided palletizing and QA inspection. Aims for near-zero defect leakage and reduced physical bottlenecks.
