Executive Summary

The integration of robotics at Pick Up Stix offers a strategic bridge between QSR speed and fast-casual quality. By offloading low-value tasks (running/bussing) to autonomous units, the brand can stabilize labor costs while enhancing the customer experience. The proposed Hybrid Service Model targets a 25% reduction in FOH labor costs while increasing throughput.

Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: FOH ROBOTS
  • Shelf-based delivery units (Pudu/Servi).
  • Reduces staff physical strain.
  • Ensures immediate food delivery.
PATHWAY B: BOH AUTOMATION
  • Automated Wok Systems.
  • Guarantees "Wok Hei" consistency.
  • Reduces kitchen injury risks.
