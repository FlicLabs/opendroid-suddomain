A Place for Mom

Senior Living Advisory Automation & Robotics Portfolio

Executive Summary

High Impact

A Place for Mom operates as a digital advisory marketplace connecting families with over 20,000 senior living communities. By leveraging AI-driven workflow automation and intelligent matching algorithms, the company can significantly scale advisor efficiency and reduce customer acquisition costs. The primary opportunity lies in Pathway A: AI Workflow & Matching, transforming the manual inquiry process into a high-speed, automated recommendation engine.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: AI Workflow & Matching
Deploy RPA for data entry, implement AI lead scoring, and roll out intelligent matching algorithms to advisors.
Year 2: Scale RPA & VR Pilot
Expand back-office automation to billing/compliance. Pilot VR community tours in 3 major metro areas.
Year 3: Strategic Expansion
Scale VR/Telepresence tech to 50% of partners. Explore smart home device integration for wellness monitoring.

Strategic Positioning

Executing this roadmap positions A Place for Mom as a Technology-First Senior Living Navigator. By moving beyond a traditional call-center model to an AI-driven digital ecosystem, the company creates a competitive moat through superior data intelligence and user experience. This transformation enables scalable growth, improved margins, and deeper integration into the continuum of senior care.
