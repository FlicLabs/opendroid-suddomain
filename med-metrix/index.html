03. Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

RPA Claims Logistics

Scope: Unattended bots for status checks, 835 reconciliation, and payment posting.

Impact: Reduces "Cost to Collect" by 15-20%. Enables 24/7 processing cycles.

PATHWAY B

Cognitive Denial AI

Scope: ML models to predict denials pre-submission and auto-generate appeals.

Impact: Reduces DSO by 10-15 days. Recovers complex revenue leakage.

PATHWAY C

Patient Access Bots

Scope: Front-end auth verification and registration chat interfaces.

Impact: Prevents 30-40% of downstream denials at the source.

04. Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1 Pilot & Foundation
Deploy Pathway A (Claims Status Bots) on top 3 payer portals. Establish Automation Center of Excellence.
YEAR 2 Scale & Intelligence
Scale RPA to all payers. Launch Pathway C (Access Bots). Begin data training for Cognitive Denial Management.
YEAR 3+ Full Autonomy
Full integration of predictive ML into AnalytX. Autonomous coding evaluation and deployment.
