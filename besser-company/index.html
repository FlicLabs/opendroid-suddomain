Executive Summary

Besser Company, the global leader in concrete products equipment, is positioned to revolutionize its discrete manufacturing processes through Industry 4.0 adoption. By integrating flexible robotic automation into its Alpena facilities, Besser can mitigate labor shortages, enhance high-mix/low-volume production, and reduce lead times for critical machinery components.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Machine Tending Cobots

Deploying Collaborative Robots for CNC machine tending allows for "lights-out" manufacturing of standard parts, increasing spindle uptime by 35% and freeing skilled machinists for programming tasks.

PATHWAY B: AMR Material Transport

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to transport heavy steel plates and WIP sub-assemblies, reducing forklift traffic and improving shop floor safety.

PATHWAY C: Auto-Palletizing & Inspection

Robotic arms equipped with vision systems for end-of-line packaging of spare parts and dimensional quality inspection of concrete molds.
