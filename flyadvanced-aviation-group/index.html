Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots for vacuuming, wiping, and trash removal during flight preparation. Targets high-frequency narrow-body operations.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots Deployed for overnight deep cleans to eliminate pathogens on surfaces and air. Ensures clinical hygiene standards.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing Automated gantry systems reducing water consumption by 50% and eliminating high-altitude manual labor risks.
  • Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection Bots Drones and crawlers utilizing LiDAR for structural integrity and tire wear analysis. Enables predictive maintenance.
