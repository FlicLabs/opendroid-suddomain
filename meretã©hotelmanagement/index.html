Mereté Hotel Management

Robotics & Automation Feasibility Study

Executive Summary

Mereté Hotel Management operates in the high-wage Pacific Northwest hospitality market. The primary opportunity lies in deploying Housekeeping Assistive Robotics and Delivery Automation to combat labor shortages and protect Gross Operating Profit (GOP). The strategy focuses on reallocating human labor from repetitive tasks (vacuuming, running items) to high-value guest interactions.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
PATHWAY A - HIGHEST VALUE

Housekeeping Automation

Tech: Autonomous Vacuum Sweepers (Whiz/Cobot)

Impact: Saves ~2-3 hours of manual labor per day per hotel. Allows staff to focus on deep cleaning and room turnover.

ROI: Immediate

PATHWAY B - STRATEGIC

Guest Delivery Robots

Tech: Relay / Savioke Delivery Bots

Impact: Secures night audit shifts by handling room deliveries. Increases GSS scores via "cool factor" and speed.

ROI: 6-12 Months

PATHWAY C - EFFICIENCY

Back Office RPA

Tech: Software Robotics

Impact: Automates night audit consolidation and cross-platform revenue management, reducing GM administrative load.

ROI: 3-6 Months

Implementation Roadmap
Y1

Pilot & Standardize

Deploy cleaning bots in 2 flagship Eugene/Springfield properties. Measure cleanliness delta.

Y2

Expand Fleet

Rollout to 80% of portfolio. Introduce delivery bots to high-RevPAR business hotels.

Y3

Smart Integration

Full fleet deployment. Integrate robot data with Facility Management for predictive maintenance.

Strategic Positioning

Executing this roadmap transforms Mereté from a traditional operator into a tech-enabled management platform. This attracts premium asset owners, improves valuation, and creates a distinct competitive advantage in the Pacific Northwest labor market.
