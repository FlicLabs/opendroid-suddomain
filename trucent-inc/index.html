Executive Vision

Trucent is positioned to transition from a manual-heavy assembly model to a Flexible Hybrid Manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating Cobots into equipment fabrication and AMRs into fluid logistics, Trucent can reduce lead times and enhance safety. The vision is a semi-autonomous factory floor that supports high-precision filtration equipment builds.

Organizational Profile

  • Core Focus: Fluid separation, purification chemistry, and centrifuge manufacturing.
  • Model: Hybrid (Discrete Equipment Build + Process Chemistry).
  • Key Opportunity: Reducing WIP bottlenecks in centrifuge stack assembly and chemical drum transport.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus on Machine Tending & Assembly. Utilizing collaborative robots for torqueing centrifuge stacks and dispensing sealants. Ensures 100% consistency and reduces repetitive strain.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus on Intralogistics. Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving heavy chemical drums and sub-assemblies. Reclaims 15% of skilled labor time currently lost to transport.
