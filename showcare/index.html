Executive Summary

Showcare operates within the highly competitive home healthcare sector. The primary challenges facing Showcare include distinct labor shortages, high coordinator-to-caregiver ratios, and unbillable travel time. The strongest robotics and automation levers are (1) Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for scheduling, (2) Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM), and (3) Assistive Robotics.

Strategic Positioning

By adopting this roadmap, Showcare transitions from a traditional staffing agency model to a technology-enabled care platform. This differentiation is critical for winning contracts with value-based care payers who prioritize data transparency over simple hourly staffing.
