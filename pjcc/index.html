PJCC Analysis & ROI

01 // Executive Summary

The Peninsula Jewish Community Center (PJCC) represents a high-volume wellness campus environment. The optimization strategy focuses on "Silent Automation"—autonomous cleaning and logistics—that improves operational margins without disrupting the member experience. The dominant ROI drivers are labor redeployment from low-skill maintenance tasks and fleet optimization for the senior transport program.
