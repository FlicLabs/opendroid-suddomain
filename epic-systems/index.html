03 // Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of IP66-rated Cobots for orbital welding and CNC machine tending. Allows skilled pipefitters to focus on complex fit-ups while robots handle repetitive tasks.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow

Autonomous Mobile Robots to automate transport of raw materials (valves, pumps, tubing) to fabrication bays, significantly reducing non-value-added walking time.

Pathway C: Inspection

Focus: Throughput

Automated 3D scanning of finished process skids to verify dimensional accuracy against CAD models, plus robotic palletizing for spare parts kits.
