Jewell Events Catering

Robotics Opportunity Portfolio

Executive Summary

Jewell Events Catering faces pressure to maintain white-glove service standards amidst volatile labor markets. The strongest automation levers identified are Culinary Prep Automation, Warehouse Logistics (AMRs), and Service Runner Bots. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of overtime labor and food waste, enabling scalable capacity.

Pathway A: Culinary Prep

Scope: Automated chopping, slicing, and mixing units.

  • 25% reduction in prep labor hours.
  • 15% reduction in food waste.
  • Consistent high-volume mise en place.

Pathway B: Logistics

Scope: AMRs for heavy lifting and RFID tracking.

  • Reduces worker compensation claims.
  • Real-time inventory tracking for china/glass.
  • Faster truck turnaround times.

Pathway C: Service Runners

Scope: Autonomous runners for food/dish transport.

  • Reduces staff fatigue and injury.
  • Keeps servers customer-facing.
  • Visual innovation marker for clients.
