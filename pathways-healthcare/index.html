Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Mobile Logistics & Smart Scheduling

AI-algorithmic "software robotics" to optimize dispatch, routing, and dynamic scheduling for field clinicians.

  • Impact: Increases daily visits from 5.0 to ~5.8 per clinician.
  • Financial: $12-15M incremental revenue capacity.
  • Status: Highest Priority / Low Risk.

Pathway B: Robotic Medication & Monitoring

Deployment of smart medication dispensers and cellular sensor suites for high-risk chronic patients.

  • Impact: Reduces low-value "medication prompt" nursing visits.
  • Financial: Reduces readmission penalties (Value-Based Care).

Pathway C: Assistive Workforce Robotics

Wearable exoskeletons and lumbar support devices for hospice aides and physical therapists.

  • Impact: 30-50% reduction in spinal load during patient transfers.
  • Financial: Long-term savings on workers' comp and retention.
