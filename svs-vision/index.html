Executive Summary

SVS Vision represents a high-value opportunity for industrial automation within its centralized Mt. Clemens optical laboratory. By shifting from manual lens processing to fully automated generating, blocking, and edging, the company can achieve significant throughput gains. Simultaneously, retail-level robotics (Tele-Optometry) offers a strategic defense against provider shortages.

Dominant Driver: Central Lab Throughput & Quality Control
