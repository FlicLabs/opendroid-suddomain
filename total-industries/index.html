Executive Vision

Total Industries is positioned to transition from legacy discrete manufacturing to a connected Smart Factory ecosystem. By integrating Cyber-Physical Systems, the organization aims to reduce custom order lead times by 35% while maintaining critical safety standards.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATH A Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of Cobots (ISO/TS 15066) for machine tending and torque fastening. Focus on reducing ergonomic strain and ensuring consistent hydraulic assembly.

PATH B Autonomous Transport

Fleet implementation of AMRs for line-side replenishment and chassis movement. Decouples material flow from human shift schedules.

PATH C Vision & Inspection

Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) for paint and seal verification, coupled with robotic palletizing for end-of-line efficiency.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Digital Twin Modeling & Pilot Cobot Cells
  • Year 2: AMR Fleet Rollout & MES Integration
  • Year 3: Vision Systems & Data Analytics Optimization
  • Year 5: Fully Autonomous Scheduling
