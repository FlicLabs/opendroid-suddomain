Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Replacing manual forklifts on long operational runs. By deploying heavy-payload AMRs, Jones Logistics can automate the movement of pallets from receiving to staging lanes.

  • Increases pallets-per-hour moved by 35%.
  • Eliminates empty manual travel time.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of ASRS or goods-to-person robots for fulfillment services to increase storage density and picking velocity.

  • Increases pick accuracy to 99.9%.
  • Reduces warehouse footprint by 40%.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

Computer vision at dock doors to automate check-ins, verify load integrity, and optimize trailer loading sequences.

  • Reduces dwell time by 20%.
  • Creates a digital twin of facility operations.
