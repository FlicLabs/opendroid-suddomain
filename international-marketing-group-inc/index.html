Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Internal Transport

Pathway B: ASRS

Focus: Piece Picking

High-density storage for marketing collateral. Increases accuracy to 99.9% and reduces physical footprint by 85%.

Algorithmic demand forecasting to pre-position inventory before campaigns launch, drastically cutting rush shipping costs.
