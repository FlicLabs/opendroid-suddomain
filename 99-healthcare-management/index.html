Executive Summary

99 Healthcare Management is positioned to transform from a traditional SNF/MSO operator into a tech-enabled care leader. By automating low-skill logistics and administrative burdens, the organization can recapture thousands of clinical hours annually. The primary opportunity lies in Pathway A: Autonomous Mobile Logistics, offering immediate labor relief and measurable ROI.

Key Insight: Shifting 40-60 hours/week of transport labor per facility back to resident care can improve Star Ratings and reduce turnover costs by ~15%.
