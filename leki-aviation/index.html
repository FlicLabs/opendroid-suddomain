Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Self-navigating robots for vacuuming and surface wiping. Optimizes overnight cleaning and rapid turnarounds, reducing manual labor dependency.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Mobile units utilizing UV-C light to neutralize pathogens. Ensures chemical-free, hospital-grade sanitation between flights.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing Systems

Robotic crawlers for fuselage cleaning. Reduces water usage by 50% and allows operation in varied weather conditions.

Pathway D: AI Inspection Bots

High-res camera systems with ML algorithms. Detects micro-cracks and structural anomalies for predictive maintenance.
