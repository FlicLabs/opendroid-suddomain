Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

AdhereHealth is uniquely positioned to transform from a tech-enabled services company into an AI-orchestrated adherence infrastructure provider. By leveraging robotics in physical fulfillment (AdhereRx) and digital automation (RPA/AI) in the Resolve platform, the company can decouple revenue growth from clinical labor costs.

Key Insight: The dominant ROI driver is the transition from manual tele-pharmacy and packaging to automated, high-velocity workflows, potentially unlocking $10M+ in annualized EBITDA improvements.
