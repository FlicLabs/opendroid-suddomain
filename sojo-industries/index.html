01. Executive Vision

The strategic objective is to transition Sojo Industries from high-volume manual variety packing to a flexible, automated Industry 4.0 model. By integrating modular robotics into "Sojo Flight" mobile units and fixed facilities, Sojo can drastically reduce cost-per-case, mitigate labor shortages, and lower carbon emissions through decentralized manufacturing.

02. Org Profile
  • HQ & Operations: Bristol, PA & Redlands, CA
  • Model: Hybrid Fixed Facility & Mobile Manufacturing
  • Focus: High-Mix / High-Volume Variety Packing
  • Current State: Discrete manufacturing relying on manual sorting. Ready for physical automation to match "Sojo Shield" digital maturity.
03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways
Pathway A: Cobots

Variety Packing Assembly

Deployment of collaborative robots with vacuum grippers to pick single-flavor cans and assemble variety packs. Safe for tight mobile trailer environments.

Pathway B: AMRs

Material Transport

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to move liquid pallets from receiving to the line, reducing forklift congestion.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Automated Palletizing

High-speed palletizing cells to ensure stack stability and eliminate heavy lifting bottlenecks at the end of the line.
