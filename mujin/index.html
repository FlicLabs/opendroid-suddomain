01. Executive Summary

Mujin is poised to transition from manual assembly of its advanced controllers to a fully automated Industry 4.0 model. By leveraging its own "Machine Intelligence" philosophy internally, Mujin can reduce lead times, showcase its technology, and achieve 24/7 production capabilities.

02. Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Collaborative robots for precise PCB-to-chassis insertion and screw driving. Focus on reducing repetitive strain and ensuring torque consistency.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace manual cart pushing. AMRs will handle WIP flow between warehouse, assembly, and burn-in testing.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Automated palletizing and vision-based quality inspection to eliminate bottlenecks at the shipping dock.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot AMR logistics & Single Cobot Cell.
  • Year 2: MES Integration & End-of-Line Palletizing.
  • Year 3: "Lights Out" Night Shift Operations.
  • Year 5: Global standardization (US/China).
