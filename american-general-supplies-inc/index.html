EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

1. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Self-navigating units for seat sanitization, trash removal, and vacuuming during short turnaround times. Ensures pristine cabin environments without delaying departure.

2. UV-C Disinfection Robots

High-intensity, non-chemical disinfection cycles eliminating bacteria and viruses on high-touch surfaces. Standardizes hygiene protocols across the fleet.

3. Exterior Washing Systems

Automated gantries or crawling drones for eco-friendly, high-pressure washing. Reduces water consumption and aircraft downtime while improving aerodynamics.

4. AI-Powered Inspection Bots

Drones and crawlers utilizing ML algorithms to detect structural anomalies. Integrates with predictive maintenance to prevent unscheduled repairs.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP (5-YEAR STRATEGY)

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase at 2 major hubs (Cabin & UV-C). Procure 10 units. Establish baseline metrics.
  • Year 2: Expansion to 4 additional hubs. Procure 30 units. Integrate data streams with maintenance systems.
  • Year 3: Scale-up to 70% of network. Standardize protocols. Achieve full ROI on initial capital.
  • Year 4: Deploy Exterior Washing Systems. Integrate AI Inspection Bots for routine checks.
  • Year 5: Optimization of fleet. Upgrade legacy units. Establish industry benchmark status.
