Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67 Cobots for hydraulic pump rebuilding and precision welding of bucket attachments, allowing senior staff to supervise multiple cells.

Pathway B: Material Transport

High-payload AMRs to autonomously transport 1,000kg sub-assemblies from warehousing to service bays, eliminating technician travel time.

Pathway C: Quality Inspection

Automated vision systems for spare parts kitting and track inspection to ensure 99.9% shipping accuracy.
