Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots for Assembly

Deployment of heavy-payload collaborative robots for tool loading and surface preparation on MasterPrint systems. Focus on safety compliance and reducing human entry into machining envelopes.

Pathway B: AMRs for Material Transport

Heavy-duty Autonomous Mobile Robots (>1000kg payload) to automate the flow of composite tooling and raw materials, reducing overhead crane dependency by 40%.

Pathway C: Automated Metrology

Shift from manual handheld inspection to robotic arms equipped with laser scanners for "End-of-Line" verification of large-scale aerospace parts.
