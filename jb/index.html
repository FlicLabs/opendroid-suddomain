01. Executive Vision

JB requires a transition from a traditional rigid supply chain to a Hybrid Automated Ecosystem. By integrating high-density ASRS for small electronics and AMRs for bulk appliances, JB can secure a competitive advantage against pure-play e-commerce giants. The goal is same-day dispatch velocity with a 40% reduction in operational friction.

02. Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A (Transport): LiDAR-guided AMRs to replace manual forklifts for dock-to-stock travel.
  • Pathway B (Piece Picking): High-density Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems for small electronics (Phones, Games).
  • Pathway C (AI Logic): Predictive slotting algorithms to position high-velocity SKUs (e.g., iPhone launches) near packing zones.
