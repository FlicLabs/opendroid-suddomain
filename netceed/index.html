Executive Summary

The Intelligent Supply Chain Ecosystem

The strategic vision for Netceed is the transition from traditional distribution to a highly automated ecosystem. By leveraging a hybrid robotics model—combining heavy-duty AMRs for cable reels and high-density ASRS for delicate electronics—Netceed can decouple material transport from skilled technical labor. This shift addresses the critical bottleneck in Value-Added Services (kitting/configuration) and streamlines global infrastructure deployment.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: AMRs for Transport
Deployment of autonomous mobile robots to handle internal logistics. Focus on moving "Kits" to configuration benches and heavy cable reels to cutting stations. Target: 35% reduction in technician travel time.
PATHWAY B: Piece Picking ASRS
Implementation of Goods-to-Person cube storage (e.g., AutoStore/Exotec). Critical for managing high-mix, low-volume SKUs like fiber connectors and splitters.
PATHWAY C: AI Logistics
Predictive modeling for network rollouts. AI anticipates regional demand and pre-positions inventory using robotics during off-peak hours to ensure Just-In-Time delivery.
