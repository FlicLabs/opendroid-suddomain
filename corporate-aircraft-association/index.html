Corporate Aircraft Association

Executive Summary

For the Corporate Aircraft Association (CAA), the integration of robotic automation represents a pivotal shift in Part 91 operations. By adopting autonomous cabin cleaning, UV-C disinfection, and exterior washing systems, CAA can elevate the service standards across its preferred FBO network. This strategy promises hospital-grade sanitation, significantly reduced ground times, and enhanced asset preservation for member aircraft.

Implementation Roadmap
  • YEAR 1 Pilot Program: Deployment at 3 major preferred FBOs. Data gathering on pathway A & B robots.
  • YEAR 2 Expansion Phase: Scale to top 20 FBOs. Introduction of exterior washing systems.
  • YEAR 3 Digital Integration: Robotic scheduling integrated into CAA member mobile application.
  • YEAR 4 Advanced Inspection: Rollout of AI-powered inspection bots for maintenance checks.
  • YEAR 5 Standardization: Establishment of "CAA Certified Robotic Clean" standard globally.
