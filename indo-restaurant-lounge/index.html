01 // Executive Vision

Hybrid Hospitality Model

The strategic objective for Indo Restaurant & Lounge is to implement a "Hybrid Service Model." By utilizing robotics for low-value, high-repetition tasks (food running, bussing), we liberate human staff to focus on guest engagement, wine service, and atmosphere curation. This preserves the premium lounge feel while injecting QSR-level efficiency.

02 // Robotics Pathways

Targeted Implementation Areas

Pathway A: FOH Serving Assistance
Deployment of 2 units (e.g., PuduBot or Servi) to handle the "Long Haul" from kitchen pass to dining zones.

Pathway B: BOH Consistency
Introduction of automated wok systems to ensure consistent "Wok Hei" for rice and noodle dishes, reducing chef fatigue.
