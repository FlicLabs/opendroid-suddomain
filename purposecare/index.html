Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Mobile Workforce Logistics & AI

Scope: Algorithmic "software robotics" to optimize routing, skill-matching, and scheduling for ~1,000 distributed clinicians.

Impact: Reduces non-billable travel time by 18%. Increases capacity without adding headcount.

Pathway B: In-Home Medication & Telepresence Robots

Scope: Deployment of countertop medication dispensers and video-enabled monitoring units for high-risk polypharmacy patients.

Impact: Reduces low-value nursing visits (pill counts) and cuts hospital readmissions by ~20%.

Pathway C: Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Scope: Back-office bots for claims processing and revenue cycle management across acquired agencies.

Impact: Accelerates cash collections and reduces administrative overhead.
