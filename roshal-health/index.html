Executive Synthesis

Roshal Health is positioned to transition from a logistics-heavy mobile ultrasound provider to a technology-first distributed care platform. The implementation of Telerobotic Ultrasound and AI-Driven Dispatch solves the critical bottleneck of professional travel time.

This strategy decouples clinical expertise from geographic location, allowing a centralized "Hub" of sonographers to service remote "Spoke" facilities instantly, creating significant margin expansion and competitive differentiation.
