Community Health Center of Buffalo

Robotics & Automation Feasibility Portfolio

Executive Summary

CHCB is a critical safety-net provider where robotics should focus on Operational Throughput and Pharmacy Revenue rather than surgical intervention. The primary goal is to increase patient capacity by 15-20% and capture 340B pharmacy revenue through automation.

Opportunity Pathways
Priority A

Pharmacy Automation

Automated pill counting, labeling, and adherence packaging (blister packs).

$250k - $400k Annual Incremental Margin
Priority B

Admin RPA

Software bots for insurance verification, prior auths, and referrals.

3 FTEs Labor Capacity Saved
Priority C

Triage Kiosks

Self-service vital signs and multi-lingual intake in lobbies.

+2 Slots Per Provider / Per Day
Financial Analysis: Pathway A
Metric Value Notes
Hardware Capex $150,000 One-time cost for dispensing robot
Script Volume Increase +20% Improved speed & adherence capture
Incremental Scripts 12,500 / yr Based on 50 additional daily scripts
5-Year Net Benefit > $1,000,000 Payback period: < 9 months
