Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Safety & Precision

Ideal for machine tending and precision assembly. ISO 10218-1 certified for safe human interaction. Perfect for lights-out machining operations.

Pathway B

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

LiDAR-based navigation for transporting heavy industrial parts. Reduces forklift traffic and non-value-added movement between receiving and production.

Pathway C

Automated End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Robotic palletizing combined with machine vision inspection. Standardizes shipping loads and ensures 100% quality checks on high-volume distribution items.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation & Pilot

Deploy pilot cells (Cobots for CNC tending). Establish baseline KPIs and data infrastructure.

Year 2: Expansion & Integration

Scale to 10 facilities. Introduce AMRs for material transport. Begin connecting isolated cells.

Year 3: Optimization

Full OT/IT integration. Predictive maintenance implementation. Data-driven process tuning.

Year 4: Agility

Rapid changeover programming for high-mix, low-volume orders. Swarm robotics concepts.

Year 5: Autonomous Ops

AI-driven scheduling and self-optimizing production flow. Target: Lights-out manufacturing in key lines.
