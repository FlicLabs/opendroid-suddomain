Executive Vision

Robotics should not replace the high-touch hospitality that defines DineAmic, but elevate it. By automating low-skill, repetitive tasks (bussing/running), human staff can focus entirely on guest engagement and experiential dining. The goal is a 20% increase in operational efficiency.

Organizational Profile

Market: Chicago, IL (Full Service/Upscale)
Challenge: High-volume turnover vs. labor fatigue.
Strategy: Hybrid Service Model. Robots handle logistics; Humans handle hospitality.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: FOH

Runners & Bussers: Deployment of tiered robots for heavy lifting. Increases server floor time for upsells.

Pathway B: BOH

Auto-Frying: Automated baskets for consistency in high-volume appetizers (calamari/fries).

Pathway C: AI

Voice Agents: AI-driven catering intake and phone orders to ensure 100% accuracy and algorithmic upselling.
