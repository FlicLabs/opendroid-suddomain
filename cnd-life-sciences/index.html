Client: CND Life Sciences

Executive Summary

CND Life Sciences is at a critical inflection point where converting from manual histology to an automated, digital pathology workflow will define its ability to scale. The "Syn-One Test" volume growth necessitates a shift from human-dependent lab processes to robotic sample handling and AI-assisted diagnostics. The dominant ROI driver is the implementation of Robotic Whole Slide Imaging (WSI) combined with AI analysis, which can increase pathologist throughput by 40-50%.

Pathway A

Lab Automation

Scope: Automated IHC staining and cover-slipping robotics.

Reduces technician hands-on time by 60% and standardizes tissue quality for the Syn-One Test.

TIMELINE: IMMEDIATE (0-12 MONTHS)

Pathway B

Robotic Scanning

Scope: High-throughput Whole Slide Imaging (WSI) scanners.

Enables remote pathology, digital archiving, and prepares infrastructure for AI.

TIMELINE: YEARS 1-2

Pathway C

AI Diagnostics

Scope: "Software Robotics" for biomarker detection.

Triages slides for pathologists, highlighting alpha-synuclein deposits automatically.

TIMELINE: YEARS 2-3
