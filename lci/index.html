EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The aviation leasing sector stands at a pivotal juncture where asset preservation determines market leadership. For LCI, the integration of robotic cleaning and maintenance automation ensures the preservation of asset residual value through precision cleaning and corrosion control. This report outlines a strategic vision for deploying autonomous systems to revolutionize the maintenance lifecycle of the LCI fleet.
