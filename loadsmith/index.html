Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

INTERNAL TRANSPORT

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace forklifts for cross-docking. Ensures 24/7 continuous operations to match autonomous truck arrival schedules.

Pathway B: ASRS

DENSITY & STORAGE

Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems for LTL consolidation hubs. Maximizes vertical space usage and reduces facility footprint by up to 60%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

PREDICTIVE DATA

Integration of freight matching algorithms with Terminal Operating Systems. Pre-positions robots based on GPS ETA of inbound trucks.
