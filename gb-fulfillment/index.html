Executive Summary

The logistics landscape is shifting from static storage to dynamic throughput. For G&B Fulfillment, the strategic vision is to transition from a traditional high-touch 3PL model to an AI-orchestrated Automated Fulfillment Center. By integrating robotics, G&B can decouple revenue growth from labor market volatility.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle non-value-added travel time.

  • ROI Projection: 12-16 months
  • Throughput: Increases from 60 to 150+ UPH

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking & ASRS

High-density automated storage and robotic arms for picking.

  • Space Recovery: Up to 40% floor space reclaimed
  • Accuracy: 99.99% via computer vision verification
