Executive Summary

RiverSpring Health features a massive 32-acre campus where the primary operational bottlenecks are logistics (campus-wide transport) and staff physical safety (patient lifting). Our analysis identifies that Service Robotics and Assistive Exoskeletons offer the highest immediate ROI by mitigating labor shortages and reducing workers' compensation claims.
