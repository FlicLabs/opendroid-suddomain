Executive Vision

For Jim S Community Bar-B-Q, the goal is not to replace the Pitmaster, but to amplify the service speed. By implementing a Hybrid Service Model, we utilize robotics for heavy lifting (trays of brisket/ribs) and bussing, allowing human staff to focus on guest hospitality.

Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: SERVICE

FOH Autonomous Serving

Deploying runners to handle heavy BBQ platters reduces physical strain and increases speed from pass-to-table.

PATHWAY B: KITCHEN

Automated Frying Assistant

Ensuring perfectly timed hushpuppies and fries while improving safety by removing staff from hot oil proximity.

PATHWAY C: DIGITAL

AI Voice Ordering

Future deployment for drive-thru upselling to increase average check size through consistent suggestive selling.
