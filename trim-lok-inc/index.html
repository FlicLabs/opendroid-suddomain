Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Deployment: Universal Robots/Fanuc CRX for Tape Application.

Focuses on eliminating human error in the application of PSA tape systems. Cobots provide constant pressure application and precision cutting, directly impacting product longevity and reducing scrap rates by 4-7%.

Pathway B: AMR Intralogistics

Deployment: MiR/Otto Motors for WIP Transport.

Automating the movement of heavy rubber coils from extrusion lines to vulcanizing stations. This removes forklifts from narrow aisles, enhancing safety and ensuring continuous material flow.

Pathway C: Automated QC & Palletizing

Deployment: Machine Vision + Industrial Arms.

End-of-line automation to handle high-payload palletizing while simultaneously performing surface inspection for pitting or die lines using Cognex vision systems.
