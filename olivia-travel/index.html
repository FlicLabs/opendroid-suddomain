Executive Vision

Olivia Travel is positioned to revolutionize the transit-dining sector by adopting a Hybrid Service Model. By integrating autonomous runners and AI-driven kiosks, the brand will maintain high hospitality standards while eliminating operational bottlenecks caused by labor shortages.


Robotics Opportunities

  • Pathway A: Front-of-House Serving Robots. Focus on heavy lifting and bussing to keep human staff engaging with guests.
  • Pathway B: Automated Frying. Ensures consistency for high-volume items and improves kitchen safety.
  • Pathway C: Voice AI & Kiosks. Drastically reduces ordering friction during peak travel windows.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1
Pilot Phase: Deploy runners & kiosks in 3 flagship locations.
Year 2
Standardization: Roll out to 50% of locations. Intro BOH automation.
Year 3
Integration: Full POS-Robot synchronization & marketing push.
Year 5
Innovation: Experimental drone delivery in transit hubs.
