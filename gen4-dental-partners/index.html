Pathway A: Surgical Robotics

Focus: Clinical Precision & Revenue

  • Deployment of haptic robotic guidance (e.g., Yomi).

Pathway B: Lab Automation

Focus: Margin Expansion

  • Centralized 3D printing & milling hubs.
  • In-housing crowns, bridges, and aligners.
  • Reduces external lab fees by up to 60%.
  • Faster patient turnaround (Same-week delivery).

Pathway C: Sterilization

Focus: Operational Efficiency

  • Automated instrument processing centers.
  • Reduces clinical staff labor hours by 30%.
  • Robotic inventory management.
  • Mitigates staff burnout and turnover risks.
