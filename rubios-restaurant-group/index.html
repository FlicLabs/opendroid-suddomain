Executive Summary

Rubio's stands at a critical juncture where the integration of robotics into the fast-casual service model can stabilize operational costs and revitalize the guest experience. By delegating low-skill tasks (food running, frying) to autonomous units, Rubio's can transition to a technology-enabled hospitality model, increasing throughput during peak rushes.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House (FOH)

Serving Robots Deployment of shelving-style robots to handle food running. This eliminates the need for dedicated runners and allows staff to focus on salsa bar upkeep.

Pathway B: Back-of-House (BOH)

Auto-Frying Automated frying arms to ensure consistent texture for fish tacos and reduce oil waste/burns.

Pathway C: AI Integration

Smart Kiosks AI-driven voice agents at kiosks to drive consistent upselling of premium add-ons (Guacamole, Drinks).
