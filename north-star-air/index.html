EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This strategy proposes a phased integration of robotics to transform North Star Air's cleaning and maintenance operations. By leveraging autonomous systems, we project significant gains in efficiency, hygiene standards, and cost control. The roadmap targets a 35-45% reduction in operational expenditures with a payback period under 24 months.

ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

Fleet & Hubs

North Star Air operates a fleet of 150 aircraft across global hubs. The current manual cleaning and inspection model faces capacity constraints during peak hours. Automation offers a scalable solution to maintain high standards without compromising turnaround times.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous vacuuming and sanitizing robots for rapid turnaround.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Chemical-free pathogen neutralization for enhanced passenger safety.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems reducing water usage and downtime.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Proactive maintenance detection to minimize AOG incidents.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP (5-YEAR PHASE)

YEAR 1: PILOT PHASE Deploy 5 Cleaning Robots & 2 UV-C units at primary hub. Establish KPIs.
YEAR 2: VALIDATION Scale to 20 Cleaning Units. Optimize workflows based on pilot data.
YEAR 3: EXPANSION Deploy Exterior Washing Systems & 10 AI Inspection Bots.
YEAR 4: INTEGRATION 60% Fleet Coverage. Full data integration into Ops Center.
YEAR 5: MATURITY Full fleet coverage. Future-proofing for specialized maintenance bots.
