Executive Summary

LaRosa's is positioned to leverage its "One Number" centralized infrastructure by integrating Voice AI and robotic logistics. The strategy shifts human labor from low-value transport tasks to high-value guest interactions, preserving the brand's family-centric atmosphere while modernizing operations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A (FOH): Deployment of shelf-based runner robots to handle heavy tray delivery and bussing, reducing server fatigue.

Pathway B (BOH): Automated fry-station assistants to ensure consistency in appetizers and reduce burn risks.

Pathway C (AI): Enterprise Voice AI to manage peak call volume, eliminating hold times and standardizing upsell scripts.
