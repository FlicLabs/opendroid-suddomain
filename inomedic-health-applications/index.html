Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Screening Pods

Deployment of semi-autonomous diagnostic kiosks for vitals, vision, and audiometry to streamline annual medical surveillance.

  • Impact: Reduces exam cycle time by 15-20 minutes.
  • Economics: ~$650k Net Annual Benefit across network.
  • Status: Recommended immediate pilot.

Pathway B: Autonomous Environmental Fleet

Utilization of UGVs and UAVs for industrial hygiene sampling in hazardous zones (radiation, chemical, heat).

  • Impact: Zero human exposure to hazards; continuous 24/7 monitoring.
  • Economics: High value in risk mitigation and liability reduction.
  • Status: Phase 2 Implementation.
