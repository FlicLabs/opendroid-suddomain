Executive Summary

SSN Hotels operates a diverse portfolio of franchise properties (Marriott, Hilton, IHG) facing critical labor shortages. This analysis targets three high-impact automation levers: Automated Floor Care, Guest Room Delivery, and BOH Transport. By shifting repetitive logistics to autonomous systems, SSN can reclaim over 300,000 labor hours annually across the network, improving guest satisfaction scores (GSS) and future-proofing against wage inflation.

Pathway A: Floor Care

Scope: Autonomous vacuuming for corridors & lobbies.

Impact: Offloads 2-3 hours/day/property. Consistent brand-standard cleanliness.

Status: Highest Value

Pathway B: Delivery

Scope: Room service & amenity delivery bots.

Impact: Increases ancillary revenue. Allows front desk to remain stationary.

Status: High Visibility

Pathway C: BOH Transport

Scope: Tug robots for linens and F&B support.

Impact: Reduces staff injury risk. Speeds up banquet turnover.

Status: Complementary
