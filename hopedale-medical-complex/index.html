Executive Summary

Hopedale Medical Complex (HMC) is uniquely positioned to leverage its integrated campus (Hospital, Wellness Center, Nursing Home) for high-impact robotics. The strategic focus is shifting from pure acute care to becoming a regional center of excellence for robotic rehabilitation, while simultaneously deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to solve rural logistic inefficiencies across the sprawling campus.
