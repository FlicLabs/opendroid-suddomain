Executive Summary

Hoist Fitness Systems is a prominent manufacturer of premium strength training equipment. The heavy manufacturing nature of strength equipment involving steel cutting, welding, powder coating, and assembly presents significant opportunities for robotic automation. The most substantial Return on Investment lies in automated robotic welding cells, Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for heavy material handling, and AI-driven quality control.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Welding Cells

Deployment of collaborative welding robots and 6-axis industrial arms. This increases arc-on time from 25% (manual) to 85% (robotic) and significantly reduces defects.

Pathway B: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR)

Implementation of heavy-duty AMRs to transport raw steel bundles and finished frames. This reduces forklift dependency and improves facility safety.

Pathway C: AI Finishing & QC

Automated powder coating and computer vision inspection to ensure uniform coating thickness and detect weld defects before shipping.
