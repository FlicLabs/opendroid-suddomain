RESEARCH PORTFOLIO: INTRAPRISE HEALTH

01. Executive Summary

Intraprise Health operates at the critical intersection of healthcare cybersecurity and third-party risk management (TPRM). Currently, the industry relies on human-intensive workflows to assess vendor risk.

The core robotics opportunity is Software Robotics (RPA/AI). By automating the ingestion, parsing, and scoring of complex security questionnaires, Intraprise can decouple revenue growth from headcount. This shift transforms the company from a tech-enabled service provider into a scalable automated platform, driving an estimated £14.8M in annual net incremental benefit.
