Executive Summary

SBNC is a vital safety-net provider for 22,000+ patients. The integration of robotics (specifically RPA and Telepresence) offers a critical lever to solve staffing shortages and administrative bottlenecks. By shifting focus from manual data entry to automated workflows, SBNC can realize an estimated $1.35M annual net benefit while improving patient access and care quality.
