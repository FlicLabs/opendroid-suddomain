02. Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots for cross-docking. Focus on reducing forklift lease costs and transit time.

Pathway B: AI Logistics

Computer vision at dock doors to automate freight scanning, dimensioning, and damage detection.

Pathway C: ASRS

High-density automated storage for "Value-Added Services" divisions, increasing storage capacity by 40%.
