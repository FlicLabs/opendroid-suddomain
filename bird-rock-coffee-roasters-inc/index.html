Executive Vision

Bird Rock Coffee Roasters faces a critical pivot point. By integrating robotics, the brand can maintain its "Farm-to-Cup" artisan integrity while solving modern labor challenges. The goal is a Hybrid-Service Model: Robots handle the "drudgery" (bussing, inventory), while humans handle the "art" (extraction, hospitality).
