Current Model

Reliance on contracted ground handling crews. Manual cleaning protocols result in 45-60 minute turnaround times, creating bottlenecks during the Fair's peak weeks.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots - Compact mobile units for rapid trash removal, seat wiping, and vacuuming. Reduces turnover time to under 20 minutes.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots - High-intensity UV light neutralization of pathogens on high-touch surfaces post-cleaning. Verifiable chemical-free sanitation.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing - Automated gantry or mobile systems. Reduces water consumption by 30% while maintaining fuselage integrity.
  • Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection Bots - Drones and ground bots with thermal imaging for pre-flight safety compliance and digital historical records.
