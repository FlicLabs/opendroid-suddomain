Executive Summary

Eslinger Biodiesel stands at the threshold of a logistics transformation. By shifting heavy payload transport from manual forklifts to autonomous systems, we project a drastic reduction in safety incidents and a significant leap in operational consistency. The target is a "High-Safety Automated Loop" handling hazardous materials with robotic precision.

STRATEGY

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: AMR Transport

Deployment of Heavy-Duty Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving IBC totes and drums. Eliminates forklift congestion in production zones.

Pathway B: ASRS & MRO

Vertical Lift Modules for maintenance parts. Reduces storage footprint by 80% and ensures critical spares are always accessible.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive dock scheduling linked to production output, reducing truck dwell times by ~20 minutes per load.
