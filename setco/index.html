EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Vision: Precision 4.0

Setco is positioned to transition from traditional craftsmanship to Augmented Craftsmanship. By integrating collaborative robotics and AMRs, Setco can automate non-value-added tasks (transport, heavy lifting) while allowing master technicians to focus on complex spindle assembly and failure analysis.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Machine Tending (Cobots)

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for loading CNC grinders. Focus on 0.03mm repeatability to ensure shaft run-out tolerances and enable "lights-out" processing for standard shafts.

PATHWAY B: Material Transport (AMRs)

Autonomous Mobile Robots to bridge the gap between "Teardown," "Wash," and "Assembly." Eliminates manual forklift risks and improves WIP flow efficiency.

PATHWAY C: EOL Inspection

Automated vibration analysis and run-off testing cells. Increases throughput capacity and generates digital certificates of compliance automatically.
