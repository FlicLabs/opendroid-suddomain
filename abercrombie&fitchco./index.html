Executive Summary

A&F is pivoting from mall-based dominance to a digitally-led "Always Forward" strategy. To sustain premium margins, A&F must adopt an Intelligent Edge Robotics model. This ensures the digital promise (App inventory) matches physical reality (Store Shelf), securing the omnichannel funnel and freeing associates to focus on styling rather than counting.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Shelf Intelligence

Deployment of RFID-enabled AMRs to eliminate phantom inventory. Ensures 99% stock accuracy for BOPIS fulfillment.

Pathway B: Automated Floor Care

Small-footprint scrubbers for new, brighter store concepts. Maintains premium aesthetics without associate labor.

Pathway C: Service & Security

Mobile sensor units to deter shrinkage and organized retail crime while providing wayfinding assistance.
