Robotics Pathways

A. Front-of-House Serving

Autonomous delivery robots for food and beverage running. Reduces server fatigue and allows staff to focus on table-side hospitality. Increases table turnover by ensuring immediate dish delivery.

B. Back-of-House Automation

Automated frying and grilling stations for consistency. Perfect for staples like Patatas Bravas. Ensures food safety and reduces waste through precise portioning.

C. AI-Driven Ordering

AI Kiosks for takeout and wine pairing suggestions. Eliminates order errors and acts as a perpetual upsell engine, driving higher check averages.
