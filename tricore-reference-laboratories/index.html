TriCore Reference Laboratories

Robotics & Automation Opportunity Portfolio

Executive Summary

TriCore Reference Laboratories processes over 60,000 patient encounters daily across New Mexico. The integration of high-throughput automation and laboratory robotics offers a transformative opportunity to scale capacity, reduce error rates, and accelerate turnaround times. This report outlines a phased approach focusing on Total Laboratory Automation (TLA) and LIMS optimization to drive significant operational and financial benefits.

Strategic Roadmap

  • Year 1: Implement LIMS optimization (Pathway B) for automated validation and workflow orchestration.
  • Year 1-2: Deploy Total Laboratory Automation (Pathway A) in the Central Reference Laboratory, focusing on pre-analytical sorting and chemistry tracks.
  • Year 2-3: Scale TLA to North Reference Laboratories and expand to microbiology.
  • Year 3-5: Integrate Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems (AS/RS) for sample archiving and inventory management.
