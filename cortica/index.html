Executive Summary: Cortica

Cortica is a high-growth, tech-enabled provider specializing in neurodevelopmental therapies. Their multidisciplinary model (ABA, Speech, OT, Neurology) creates complex operational friction. The strongest robotics and automation levers are AI-driven care logistics, Ambient Clinical Intelligence, and Therapeutic Social Robotics.

The dominant ROI driver is the optimization of clinical throughput, capable of generating over £13M in incremental annual margin by optimizing scheduling density and reducing administrative overhead.
