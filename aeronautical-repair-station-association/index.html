Strategic Automation Research

A high-level vision for integrating robotic cleaning and maintenance automation into MRO operations.

Executive Summary

Open Droids presents a strategic vision for integrating robotic automation into ARSA member facilities. By adopting autonomous systems, members can standardize quality, reduce aircraft turnaround time, and mitigate labor shortages, ensuring regulatory compliance while maximizing operational efficiency.

Organizational Profile

Fleet Scope: Mixed narrow/wide-body commercial maintenance.

Hub Presence: 24/7 High-traffic logistics hubs.

Current Model: High reliance on manual labor, resulting in variable consistency and excessive overtime costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Cabin Bots

Autonomous vacuuming and debris removal using LiDAR mapping to free up human staff for deep cleaning.

B. UV-C Robots

Hospital-grade disinfection traversing aisles to neutralize pathogens without chemical erosion.

C. Ext. Washing

Automated gantries and climbing robots using reclaimed water to reduce time and technician risk.

D. AI Inspection

Drones and crawlers utilizing computer vision to detect micro-fractures and corrosion.
