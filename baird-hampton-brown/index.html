04. Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Machine Tending. Deploy collaborative arms to handle repetitive CNC loading, improving safety and allowing 1 operator to manage 4 machines.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Logistics. Autonomous Mobile Robots for material replenishment, eliminating line-starvation and forklift risks.

Pathway C: Inspection

Focus: Quality. Automated End-of-Line palletizing combined with machine vision to ensure 100% defect-free shipment.
