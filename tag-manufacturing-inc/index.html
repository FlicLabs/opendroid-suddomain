Executive Summary

Tag Manufacturing Inc. is positioned to dominate the heavy equipment attachment market by transitioning from traditional discrete manufacturing to a flexible, automated model. This strategy addresses the critical shortage of skilled welders while decoupling production volume from linear labor costs. The focus is on high-mix/low-volume welding automation and heavy material handling.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Cobot Welding

Deployment of IP66-rated Cobot arms for long-seam welding on buckets. Allows skilled humans to focus on fitting while robots handle arc-on time.

PATHWAY B

Heavy Duty AMRs

Autonomous mobile robots (1,500kg capacity) to transport cut steel plate from laser nesting to bending, reducing forklift congestion.

PATHWAY C

Vision Inspection

End-of-line gantry systems using 3D profilometry to scan weld seams and bucket geometry before powder coating.
