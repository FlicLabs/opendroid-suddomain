ENABLE DENTAL: ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY

Executive Summary

Enable Dental is a premier mobile dental support organization serving geriatric and special needs populations. The current operational model relies heavily on manual logistics and physical equipment handling. The introduction of automation and robotics offers a transformative opportunity to shift from a labor-intensive service model to a technology-enabled logistics platform. The primary value drivers are AI-driven scheduling optimization and assistive hardware for equipment transport.
