Executive Vision

FEI is positioned to leverage its employee-owned culture to drive a shift toward Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS). By integrating collaborative robotics, FEI will mitigate labor risks and increase spindle uptime in its high-mix manufacturing environment.

Robotics Pathways

  • Pathway A (Cobots): Machine tending for CNC Lathes. "Lights out" capabilities.
  • Pathway B (AMRs): Autonomous transport for raw bar stock and finished bins.
  • Pathway C (QC/Vision): Automated dimensional inspection and palletizing.
