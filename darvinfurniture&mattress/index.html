Executive Vision

Darvin Furniture operates in a high-stakes logistics environment where heavy lifting meets high customer expectations. Our analysis proposes a Hybrid Automation Ecosystem designed to augment your Mokena distribution center. By offloading heavy transport to AMRs and utilizing AI for routing, Darvin can reduce product damage by 15% and accelerate dock-to-stock times by 40%.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Heavy AMRs

Deployment of pallet-moving bots and tuggers to handle heavy furniture carts, removing long-distance forklift travel.

PATHWAY B

Picking & ASRS

Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) for accessories and small parts to recover 60% of floor space and ensure 99.9% accuracy.

AI Logistics

Warehouse Execution System (WES) to sequence outbound staging in reverse-route order for delivery trucks.
