Executive Summary

Pain Specialists of America (PSA) operates in a high-volume, regulation-heavy environment. The most potent robotics levers are not physical androids, but "software robotics" (RPA) to solve administrative bottlenecks, and surgical navigation to enhance ASC throughput and safety.

  • Primary Opportunity: Automating Revenue Cycle & Prior Authorizations.
  • Secondary Opportunity: Robotic Assistance for Interventional Spine Procedures.
  • Projected ROI: $750k - $1.2M annual incremental margin via efficiency gains.
