Orthodontic Partners

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model for Dental Support Organizations

Executive Summary

Orthodontic Partners is a national OSO shifting the paradigm from manual clinical workflows to an automated, vertically integrated platform. The strongest leverage point is the internalization of clear aligner manufacturing via robotic trimming and 3D printing. This shift addresses the organization's largest variable cost—third-party lab fees—and has the potential to generate ~$6M in annual incremental EBITDA per 5,000 cases with a payback period of under 18 months.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Manufacturing

Centralized Automated Lab: Moving from Invisalign/Spark to in-house white-label production using industrial 3D printing and robotic laser trimming.

$1,200 Margin Gain Per Patient

Pathway B: Operations

RPA (Robotic Process Automation): Deploying software bots for insurance verification, claims processing, and revenue cycle management.

30% Admin Labor Savings

Pathway C: Clinical AI

Remote Monitoring: Using computer vision AI to track tooth movement remotely, reducing unnecessary chair time and increasing doctor capacity.

1.5x Doctor Capacity Increase
