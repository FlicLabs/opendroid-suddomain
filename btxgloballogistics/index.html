02. Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Heavy AMRs

Deployment of LiDAR-guided pallet movers (1000kg+) to replace manual forklifts for long-haul internal transport. Focus on safety and consistency.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking

Implementation of Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for high-value spare parts and medical logistics. maximize density by 85%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer Vision tunnels for automated dimensioning (DWS) and AI-driven load planning to optimize truck fill rates.
