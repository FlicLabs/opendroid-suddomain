01. Executive Vision

Pearson Packaging Systems is positioned to revolutionize its internal manufacturing capabilities. By shifting from static workstations to dynamic, automated cells, Pearson can address skilled labor shortages and increase production velocity for its secondary packaging equipment. The integration of collaborative robotics and AMRs will modernize the Spokane facility into a flexible Industry 4.0 hub.
