Executive Summary

St. Croix Hospice operates in a high-touch, decentralized logistics environment across the Midwest. The primary opportunity lies in shifting from a purely labor-intensive model to a tech-enabled logistics platform. By leveraging AI for routing and scheduling (Pathway A), the organization can recapture thousands of hours of non-productive "windshield time." Secondary opportunities in supply chain automation and telepresence robotics reinforce quality of care and rural access.
