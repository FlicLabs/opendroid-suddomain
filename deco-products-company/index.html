Executive Vision

Deco Products is positioned to evolve from North America's premier zinc die caster into a fully integrated "Smart Foundry." By deploying cyber-physical systems, Deco will transition from traditional heavy manufacturing to data-driven production. The core objective is to leverage collaborative robotics and autonomous transport to enhance yield rates, improve safety in high-temp environments, and secure operational flexibility.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobots Focus on Machine Tending. Deploying IP67-rated cobots for gate removal and CNC loading. Removes operators from heat zones.
PATHWAY B: AMRs Focus on Material Transport. Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving zinc ingots and WIP bins.
PATHWAY C: Vision & Palletizing Focus on End-of-Line. Automated defect detection for powder-coated parts and heavy-lift palletizing.
