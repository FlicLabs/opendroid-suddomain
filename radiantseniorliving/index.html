Radiant Senior Living

Robotics Analysis & Opportunity Portfolio

1. Executive Summary

Radiant Senior Living operates 19 communities across the Western US. Facing industry-wide labor shortages, the organization is prime for automation that reduces physical staff burden. The strongest levers are Dining Hospitality Automation and Autonomous Floor Care. These initiatives promise to reduce labor costs, improve resident safety, and increase staff retention by eliminating low-value transport tasks.

2. Organizational Profile

Scale & Reach

~19 Communities across Oregon, Washington, Montana, Nevada, Colorado, and Idaho.

Service Model

Independent Living, Assisted Living, and Memory Care with a focus on hospitality and home-like environments.

Operational Pain Points

Dining staff turnover, housekeeping physical strain, and rising agency labor costs.

3. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Dining Automation

The "Runner" Model: Using autonomous mobile robots to bus tables and run food.

Impact: Saves ~6 hours of walking per day/staff. Allows servers to stay on the floor interacting with residents.

Pathway B: Floor Care

The "Janitor" Model: Autonomous vacuum/scrubbers for large hallways and lobbies.

Impact: Standardized hygiene, infection control reporting, and reduced night-shift burden.

Pathway C: Safety Sensing

The "Guardian" Model: Passive radar/optical sensors for fall detection without wearables.

Impact: Reduced liability, faster response times, and premium pricing for safety suites.

Deploy dining robots in 2 flagship Oregon/Washington sites. Measure step-count reduction.
2
Portfolio Rollout (Months 4-12)
Expand dining bots to all 19 sites. Begin floor care pilots in largest buildings.
3

7. Strategic Positioning

Executing this roadmap positions Radiant Senior Living as a Tech-Enabled Hospitality Provider. By automating the heavy lifting of transport and cleaning, Radiant ensures that human capital is spent on human connection. This creates a defensible market advantage in both resident acquisition and staff retention.
