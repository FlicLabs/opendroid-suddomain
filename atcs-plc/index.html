Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots
Safety & Precision

Focus: Machine tending and assembly tasks.

  • • Cobot deployment for CNC loading/unloading.
  • • Safe human-robot collaboration.
  • • ±0.03mm repeatability for quality consistency.
Pathway B: AMRs
WIP Flow Efficiency

Focus: Material transport and logistics.

  • • Autonomous Mobile Robots for line-side replenishment.
  • • Dynamic routing via SLAM navigation.
  • • Reduction of forklift dependency and transit time.
Pathway C: End-of-Line
Throughput & Yield

Focus: Automated palletizing and inspection.

  • • High-payload robotic palletizing.
  • • Integrated vision systems for defect detection.
  • • Injury risk mitigation for staff.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

Y1

Foundation & Pilot Phase

Digital infrastructure upgrade (IIoT). Deployment of Pilot Cobot Cell for machine tending. Operator training program initiation.

Y2

Material Flow Expansion

Integration of AMRs for WIP transport. Expansion of cobot cells to assembly lines. Data synchronization with MES.

Y3

End-of-Line Automation

Automated palletizing and vision inspection deployment. Predictive maintenance implementation on legacy assets.

Y4

System Optimization

AI/ML integration for dynamic scheduling. Digital Twin implementation for process simulation.

Y5

Full Industry 4.0 Maturity

Lights-out manufacturing capability. Autonomous supply chain synchronization. Full ROI realization.
