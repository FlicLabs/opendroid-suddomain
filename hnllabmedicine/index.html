HNL Lab Medicine

Robotics & Automation Consultation Portfolio
Executive Snapshot: HNL processes 60M+ tests annually. The current bottleneck is manual pre-analytical sorting and ground-based logistics. Robotics intervention targets a 15% reduction in Cost Per Test (CPT) through AI-driven track automation and autonomous logistics.

Organizational Profile

Scale
60M+ Annual Tests | 50+ Patient Service Centers

Geography
Pennsylvania, New Jersey (Lehigh Valley Hub)

Core Challenge
Staffing shortages in phlebotomy/accessioning & courier latency.

Economic Focus
Volume-driven, low-margin. Need to lower variable labor cost.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AI Total Lab Automation
High ImpactPre-Analytics

Scope: Upgrade tracks with computer vision for auto-decapping, sorting, and volume checks.

Impact: 80% reduction in manual touchpoints. 25% throughput increase.

Pathway B: AMR Intralogistics
OperationsMobility

Scope: Fleet of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) connecting central lines to isolated specialty labs (Micro/Genetics).

Impact: Eliminates 4-6 miles/day of technician walking. Immediate chain-of-custody.

Pathway C: Drone Logistics
StrategicTransport

Scope: UAV delivery network for STAT samples from hospital rooftops to central lab.

Impact: Bypasses traffic congestion. Reduces ground fleet fuel costs.
