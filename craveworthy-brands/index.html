Executive Summary

The strategic vision for integrating robotics into the Craveworthy ecosystem centers on "Operational Hybridization." The objective is to deploy automation that respects the experiential nature of stir-fry concepts (Genghis Grill, BD's) while maximizing throughput for quick-service brands (Wing It On!). This approach mitigates labor volatility and ensures brand consistency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Serving

Front-of-House

Focus: Genghis Grill & BD's.

Deploying autonomous runners to handle heavy ceramic bowl transport, allowing human servers to focus on guest hospitality and drink refills.

Pathway B: Cooking

Back-of-House

Focus: Wing It On! & Budlong.

Automated frying arms to manage basket drops and lifts. Ensures safety and product consistency in high-volume chicken preparation.

Pathway C: AI Ops

Digital Interaction

Focus: All Brands.

AI-driven kiosks for complex bowl customization and Voice AI for drive-thru speed. Targets 99.9% order accuracy.
