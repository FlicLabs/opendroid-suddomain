Executive Summary

Congregational Home Inc. is positioned to leverage automation to combat staffing shortages in the senior living sector. The primary opportunities lie in Autonomous Service Robots (AMRs) for dining, Robotic Assistive Devices for staff safety, and Social AI for memory care. The projected impact includes significant labor optimization and reduced workplace injuries.
