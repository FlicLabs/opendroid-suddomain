Executive Summary & Vision

Beauty Industry Group (BIG) is positioned to transition from high-touch craftsmanship to Precision Scalability. The core objective is to integrate flexible automation that respects the delicate nature of hair substrates while eliminating bottlenecks in sorting and packaging.

This strategy addresses the High-Mix/High-Volume challenge, leveraging automation to reduce lead times by 30% and stabilize global labor costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A (Cobots): Force-limited robots for sewing machine tending and blister pack loading. Focus on safety and reducing repetitive strain.
  • Pathway B (AMRs): Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport hair batches between "Wet" (Dye) and "Dry" (Assembly) rooms, increasing active assembly time.
  • Pathway C (End-of-Line): Computer Vision for spectral color analysis and automated palletizing for distribution efficiency.
