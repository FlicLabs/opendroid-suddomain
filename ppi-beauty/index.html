Executive Summary

PPI Beauty requires a shift from rigid production to Agile Flexible Manufacturing. By integrating Industry 4.0 standards, PPI can address the "high-mix, low-volume" nature of modern beauty trends. The strategy focuses on deploying collaborative robotics to handle complex kitting and packaging tasks, ensuring scalability and reduced time-to-market.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

  • Application: Kitting gift sets & blister packing.
  • Benefit: Safe interaction with humans; force-sensing for delicate brushes.

Pathway B: AMRs (Logistics)

  • Application: Transporting raw substrates to filling lines.
  • Benefit: 40% reduction in manual travel time; improved hygiene.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Automation

  • Application: Palletizing & Vision Inspection.
  • Benefit: Zero-defect logistics and higher throughput.
