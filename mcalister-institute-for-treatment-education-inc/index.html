Client: McAlister Institute

Executive Summary

McAlister Institute faces critical bottlenecks in medication management (MAT), administrative intake, and facility maintenance. By deploying a portfolio of automated dispensing systems, Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and facility service robots, the organization can recapture 15-20% of clinical capacity. This shift moves the focus from repetitive compliance tasks to patient recovery, potentially generating over $3.5M in cumulative value over 5 years.

Pathway A: Meds

Scope: Automated Dispensing Cabinets for MAT clinics.

Impact: Reduces nurse admin time by 30-40%. Eliminates diversion risks.

Benefit: ~$200k/year.

Pathway B: RPA

Scope: "Software Robots" for intake & billing.

Impact: Reduces claim denials by 15%. Cuts intake time by 66%.

Benefit: ~$500k/year.

Pathway C: Facility

Scope: Autonomous cleaning & logistics.

Impact: Improves hygiene scores. Saves 2-3 labor hours/day.

Benefit: ~$80k/year.
