Jefferson Healthcare Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Executive Summary

Jefferson Health presents a high-potential opportunity for robotics integration, focusing on internal logistics automation and AI-driven mobile care coordination. Unlike rural systems, Jefferson's dense urban environment offers unique challenges solved by Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for hospital operations and optimized routing for community-based care. The portfolio targets a reduction in clinical friction, improved patient throughput, and significant labor reallocation.

Strategic Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Internal Logistics Automation

Focus: AMRs for hospital transport (labs, meds, linens).

Impact: Reduces nurse non-clinical burden, improves lab turnaround.

Pathway B: Mobile Care AI

Focus: Smart routing for in-home acute care teams.

Impact: Increases visits per day, reduces response times in dense traffic.

Pathway C: Tele-Rehabilitation

Focus: Robotic exoskeletons and telepresence for high-acuity recovery.

Impact: Reduces readmissions, differentiates neurology/orthopedic institutes.
