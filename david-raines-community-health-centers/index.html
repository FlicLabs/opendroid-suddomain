01. Executive Summary

David Raines Community Health Centers (DRCHC) is an FQHC system operating across Northwest Louisiana. The primary robotics opportunities lie in modernizing pharmacy operations to capture 340B revenue and utilizing administrative automation to reduce overhead. The dominant ROI driver is Pharmacy Automation, offering rapid payback through increased volume capacity.

03. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Pharmacy Automation (High Priority)

Implementation of robotic counting and blistering systems to optimize 340B drug program revenue. This shifts pharmacist focus from manual counting to clinical verification and patient counseling.

Pathway B: Revenue Cycle RPA

Deployment of AI-agents to handle eligibility verification and claim denials. This reduces uncompensated care costs and accelerates cash flow.

Pathway C: School-Based Telepresence

Mobile tele-diagnostic robots deployed in schools, allowing a single provider to service multiple remote locations effectively.

04. Strategic Positioning

Adopting this roadmap transforms DRCHC from a traditional clinic model into a tech-enabled Integrated Care Network. It secures financial sustainability through efficiency rather than grant dependency, ensuring long-term access for underserved communities in Louisiana.
