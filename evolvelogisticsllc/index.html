Executive Vision

The transition to an Automated Intelligent Fulfillment Center is critical. By decoupling revenue growth from headcount dependency, Evolve Logistics can create a fluid, data-driven ecosystem. This strategy elevates human capital to supervisory roles while AMRs handle high-frequency tasks.

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport & GTP

  • Eliminates 60% of walking time.
  • ROI Driver: 50% labor reduction per order.
  • Scalable fleet deployment.

Pathway B: ASRS

Picking & Vertical Storage

  • 30% more inventory in same footprint.
  • 0.01% Error rates via Computer Vision.
  • High-density storage racks.

Pathway C: AI Ops

Predictive Logistics

  • Predictive inbound/outbound scheduling.
  • 25% faster dock turnover.
  • Pre-staging pallets autonomously.
