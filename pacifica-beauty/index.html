01. Executive Vision

Pacifica Beauty is positioned to lead the clean beauty sector by adopting Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS). Transitioning from rigid batch processing to agile, robotic-assisted production will address high-mix SKU challenges, seasonal demand spikes, and sustainability goals through significant scrap reduction.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots High Priority

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly.
Deployment of force-limited collaborative robots for bottle capping and carton loading. Enhances safety and reduces ergonomic strain.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Material Flow.
Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport raw ingredients and packaging from warehouse to line-side, replacing manual forklift operations.

Pathway C: Auto-Palletizing

Focus: End-of-Line Throughput.
Robotic palletizing with integrated Vision Systems to ensure label compliance and 100% finished goods inspection.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase
    Site survey, selection of Cobot Capping application, and initial deployment.
  • Year 2: Expansion
    Rollout of Palletizing (Pathway C) and AMR logistics (Pathway B).
  • Year 3: Integration
    Connection of robotic PLCs to ERP for real-time inventory and predictive maintenance.
  • Year 5: Smart Factory
    Full Industry 4.0 realization with AI-driven batch scheduling.
