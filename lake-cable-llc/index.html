Executive Vision

Lake Cable LLC is positioned to transition from traditional semi-automated manufacturing to a fully integrated Industry 4.0 Smart Factory. By addressing high-mix production challenges with flexible robotics, Lake Cable can secure its position as a Midwest leader in wire and cable innovation, significantly reducing operator strain and maximizing throughput.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobot Integration

Focus: Machine Tending & Spool Handling

Utilization of high-payload collaborative robots to manage extruder loading and unloading. Enhances safety by removing operators from repetitive heavy lifting tasks.

PATHWAY B: AMR Logistics

Focus: Material Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving WIP reels between cabling, braiding, and jacketing stations. Reduces forklift traffic and improves floor safety.

PATHWAY C: End-of-Line

Focus: Palletizing & Inspection

Automated palletizing cells with integrated vision systems to detect jacket defects in real-time. Ensures 100% quality yield and consistent packaging.
