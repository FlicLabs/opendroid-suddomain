Executive Summary

Panorama Eyecare is a high-volume ophthalmic management platform operating in the Rocky Mountain region. The current operational model relies heavily on manual technician labor for diagnostics and sterile logistics. The strategic introduction of Pathway A (Automated Diagnostic Lanes) and Pathway C (ASC Logistics Robots) presents a transformative opportunity to shift from linear labor dependency to exponential technological scalability.

The dominant ROI driver is diagnostic velocity—increasing patient throughput by ~20% without expanding physical real estate—yielding an estimated $14M annual net benefit upon full network deployment.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Diagnostic Automation Pilot

  • Retrofit 2 flagship clinics with fully automated refraction lanes.
  • Integrate data flow directly into EMR to eliminate transcription errors.
  • Goal: Validate 20% throughput increase.

Year 2: Surgical Robotics Upgrade

  • Deploy next-gen Femtosecond Lasers (FLACS) in primary ASCs.
  • Marketing launch for "Precision Cataract Surgery" to drive premium IOL conversion.

Year 3: Logistics & AI Integration

  • Deploy Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for tray transport in high-volume ASCs.
  • Predictive scheduling AI based on Year 1 diagnostic data.
