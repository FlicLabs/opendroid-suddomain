Chestnut Health Systems

Robotics Opportunities & Automation Strategy

Executive Summary: Chestnut Health Systems operates at the intersection of clinical care, behavioral health, and research. The primary opportunity lies in "Software Robotics" to alleviate chronic staffing shortages, followed by physical safety automation in residential facilities.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Administrative RPA

Focus: Automating high-volume intake, billing, and scheduling workflows.

  • AI Chatbots for triage
  • Automated Prior Auth scrapping
  • Predictive scheduling
High ROI Immediate Impact
Pathway B: Meds Automation

Focus: Automated Dispensing Cabinets (ADCs) in residential units.

  • Reduces diversion risk
  • Frees up nursing hours
  • Inventory compliance
Safety Critical Residential
Pathway C: Therapeutic Bots

Focus: Socially Assistive Robotics (e.g., PARO) for therapy.

  • Anxiety reduction
  • Non-pharma intervention
  • Research grant potential
