Executive Summary

The Center for Applied Behavior Analysis (CABA) is positioned to transform its operational efficiency through the adoption of robotics. Currently, high burn-out rates and non-billable travel time compress margins. By deploying Telepresence Robotics for supervision and Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) for clinical engagement, CABA can unlock over $790k in annual network value while significantly improving clinician retention and patient outcomes.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Pilot Telepresence units with 5 Lead BCBAs to validate travel reduction.
  • Phase 2 (Months 4-12): Full fleet rollout of Telepresence; Integration of AI Scheduling software.
  • Phase 3 (Year 2+): Deployment of Socially Assistive Robots (SAR) for specialized social skills clinics.
