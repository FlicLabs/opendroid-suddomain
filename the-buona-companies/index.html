Executive Summary

The Buona Companies stands at the intersection of Chicago heritage and modern scalability. This strategic analysis proposes a hybrid automation model. By deploying FOH runners and AI-driven drive-thru agents, Buona can maintain its signature "Family Recipe" hospitality while eliminating operational friction. The goal is not staff replacement, but the elevation of human staff to brand ambassadors.

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Deployment of autonomous runners to handle food delivery and bussing. This keeps human staff in the dining room, increasing touchpoints with guests and speeding up table availability during the lunch rush.

Pathway B: BOH Automated Frying

Focus: Consistency & Safety

High-volume automated frying baskets ensure perfect consistency for fries and tenders. This mitigates injury risks and stabilizes the most volatile station in the kitchen during peak hours.

Pathway C: Drive-Thru AI

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

Conversational AI agents integrated into headsets. These agents never miss an upsell opportunity ("Add cheese?") and eliminate order errors caused by headset fatigue.

Financial Projections & Impact

Analysis based on a 5-location pilot program with a target 15-25% reduction in OPEX.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot AI Drive-Thru & FOH Runners in Top 3 Volume Stores.
  • Year 2: Standardization & Franchise Tech Package Rollout.
  • Year 3: BOH Deep Integration (Auto-Fryers) in New Builds.
  • Year 5: Fully Autonomous Workflow (Cooking to Serving).
