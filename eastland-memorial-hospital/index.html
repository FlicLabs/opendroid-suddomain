Eastland Memorial Hospital: Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Executive Summary

Eastland Memorial Hospital faces the distinct challenges of a Level IV Trauma Center operating in a rural environment. Operational pressures include staffing shortages and transfer logistics. By integrating Tele-presence, Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), and UV-C Disinfection, Eastland can transition to a technology-enabled smart community hospital.
