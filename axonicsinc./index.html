Axonics Inc.

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Medical Technology / Neuromodulation
Executive Summary

Axonics Inc. is a leader in Sacral Neuromodulation (SNM). The primary robotics opportunity lies in shifting from manual micro-assembly to Pathway A: Automated Manufacturing. This transition addresses the company's need to scale production for its R20 and F15 systems while significantly improving gross margins through reduced labor and scrap costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Manufacturing

Scope: 6-axis robotic arms for micro-assembly of IPGs and leads.

$10M - $14M Est. Annual COGS Savings

Focuses on yield improvement (reducing scrap) and reducing direct labor in the cleanroom.

Pathway B: Quality Assurance

Scope: Automated battery cycling and lead fatigue testing gantries.

Speed & Compliance Primary Driver

Enables 24/7 testing for R&D acceleration and regulatory reliability.

Pathway C: Surgical Nav

Scope: AI-guided robotic assistance for precise lead placement.

Strategic Market Differentiator

Standardizes clinical outcomes and drives adoption among generalist urologists.
