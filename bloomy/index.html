02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Deployment of 5-10kg payload Cobots for automated screwdriving and torque application. Ensures 100% compliance for aerospace/defense contracts.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving heavy chassis and power supplies. Reduces non-value-added technician travel time by automating WIP flow.

Pathway C: Auto-Inspection

End-of-Line automated optical inspection (AOI) to verify connector seating and labeling before final software validation. Improves yield.

03. Implementation Roadmap

Year 1-2

Foundation & Logistics. Deploy single Cobot cell and AMR fleet. Integrate MES for data tracking.

Year 3-4

Expansion. Implement Vision Inspection. Scale Cobots to wiring harness prep. Connect assets to dashboard.

Year 5

Optimization. AI-driven process refinement. Modular floor capable of 4-hour reconfiguration.
