01. Executive Vision

The vision for EC Scott Group is to pivot from manual-heavy logistics to a flexible, automated fulfillment ecosystem. By integrating robotics into kitting and distribution, the organization will reduce lead times, improve compliance accuracy for retail partners (Sephora, Ulta), and scale operations without proportional labor cost increases.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Kitting

Deploying collaborative robots for value-added services.
Goal: Precision in gift-set assembly & safety.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for material transport.
Goal: WIP flow efficiency & walking reduction.

Pathway C: Auto-Palletizing

End-of-line robotics with vision systems.
Goal: Throughput speed & shipping yield.

03. Implementation Roadmap

1

Pilot & Proof of Concept

Select one high-volume kitting line for Cobot integration. Deploy 2 AMRs for waste/dunnage removal.

2

Integration & Expansion

Expand Cobots to 3 lines. Install Palletizing cell for main shipping lane. Integrate with WMS.

3

AI & Predictive Logistics

Full AMR fleet. Machine Learning for predictive bottleneck analysis and seasonal scaling.
