01. Executive Vision

The seafood processing industry demands a Zero-Breakage Cold Chain. Our research indicates that Seafood Factory is positioned to transition from high-touch manual logistics to an IP67-rated automated ecosystem. This shift addresses critical pain points: corrosion, cold-induced labor inefficiency, and perishability. The goal is a touchless logistics model that guarantees freshness from dock to dispatch.
