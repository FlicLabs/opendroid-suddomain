01. Executive Summary

The transition to natural refrigerants (Ammonia/CO2) requires manufacturing precision that exceeds current manual capabilities. Our strategy proposes a shift to a Smart Flexible Manufacturing Ecosystem. By integrating Industry 4.0 technologies, the Institute will ensure leak-proof containment integrity, enhance safety, and scale throughput to meet global regulatory demands.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Assembly

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for TIG welding and brazing. Ensures 100% joint consistency critical for high-pressure ammonia systems.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) to transport compressor racks between Fabrication and Assembly, reducing operator fatigue.

PATHWAY C

Automated QA

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Robotic arms with high-sensitivity "sniffers" and thermal imaging to reduce leak detection cycles from 20 minutes to 3 minutes.
