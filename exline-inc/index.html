01. Executive Summary

Exline Inc. is positioned to transition from a traditional high-mix/low-volume job shop into a digitally integrated Smart Repair Facility. By leveraging flexible automation in thermal spray coatings and material transport, Exline can address skilled labor shortages while enhancing the precision and safety of its industrial repair services. This strategy honors the 150-year legacy of craftsmanship by augmenting human skill with robotic consistency.

02. Organizational Profile

Location: Salina, Kansas

Core Focus: Industrial Machinery Repair, Thermal Spray, Ignition Systems.

Operational Mode: HMLV (High-Mix / Low-Volume). Combining discrete assembly for ignition systems with process-heavy workflows for large engine block repair.

03. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Automating thermal spray coating to reduce operator risk and ensure uniform thickness.

Pathway B: AMRs

Heavy-payload transport for engine blocks between teardown, machining, and plating.

Pathway C: Quality Vision

Automated bore inspection and digital "birth certificate" generation for repaired parts.
