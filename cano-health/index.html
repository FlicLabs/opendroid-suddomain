Executive Summary

Cano Health is a value-based primary care provider focusing on Medicare Advantage members. Following restructuring, the operational imperative is maximizing clinical efficiency and patient logistics. The strongest robotics levers are (1) Automated Pharmacy Fulfillment, (2) Intelligent Fleet Logistics, and (3) Remote Patient Monitoring Robotics.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Pharmacy Robotics

Implementation of central fill robotics to automate sorting, counting, and pouch packaging for chronic maintenance medications.

  • Impact: 40-60% labor reduction.
  • ROI: 9-14 month payback period.

Pathway B: Fleet Logistics

AI-driven routing immediately, followed by ADAS retrofits and autonomous shuttle pilots for patient transport.

  • Impact: Fuel efficiency & appointment density.
  • ROI: 2-3 year payback period.
