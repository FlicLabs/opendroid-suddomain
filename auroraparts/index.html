01. Executive Vision

Aurora Parts is positioned to transition from a manual-heavy distribution model to a High-Velocity Automated Fulfillment Ecosystem. By leveraging heavy-payload AMRs and ASRS, Aurora will solve the "heavy part" logistics challenge, reducing physical labor strain while accelerating same-day delivery capabilities for the dealer network.

03. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

  • Focus: Heavy internal transport (Brake drums, axles).
  • Benefit: Reduces forklift traffic & accidents.
  • Metric: 50% reduction in travel time.

Pathway B: ASRS & Picking

  • Benefit: 400% storage density increase.
