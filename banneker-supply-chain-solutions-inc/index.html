ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of Lidar-enabled Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle dunnage, trash, and long-distance internal transport. Decouples travel time from task time.

Pathway B: Robotic Kitting

Integration of Collaborative Robots (Cobots) into value-added service lines. Enhances kitting accuracy and allows for high-mix, low-volume flexibility.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive slotting algorithms layered over the WMS to pre-position inventory based on inbound shipping data analysis.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP

Year 1 Foundation: Audit WMS, deploy pilot AMRs for waste removal, form Automation COE.
Year 2 Pilot: Implement Cobots for primary kitting contracts. Begin zone picking with AMRs.
Year 3 Expansion: Scale automation to 50% of floor space. Activate AI predictive slotting.
Year 4 Integration: Dark Warehouse capabilities for night shifts. Real-time client dashboards.
Year 5 Innovation: Drone cycle counting. Regional Automated 3PL Hub status.
