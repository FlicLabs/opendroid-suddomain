Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Housekeeping Pilot (Year 1)

Deploy vacuuming cobots in 3 flagship properties. Establish cleanliness baselines and train staff. Roll out to 17 remaining properties in Q3/Q4.

Phase 2: Guest Logistics (Year 2)

Introduce delivery bots in top 5 RevPAR locations. Integrate with PMS for automated dispatching of amenities.

Phase 3: Deep Integration (Year 3)

Advanced data analytics on cleaning patterns. Explore kitchen automation for breakfast service standardization.
