Executive Summary

Codman Square Health Center (CSHC) is a critical FQHC anchor in Dorchester. The value proposition for robotics here is not luxury surgery, but operational throughput and health equity.

By implementing pharmacy automation, administrative RPA, and facilities robotics, CSHC can reclaim roughly $1.5M in annual operational capacity, redirecting resources from low-value tasks to direct patient care and chronic disease management.
