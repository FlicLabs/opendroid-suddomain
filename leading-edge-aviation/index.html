Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-guided vacuuming and electrostatic spraying units designed for rapid gate turnarounds. Reduces interior cleaning time by 30%.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade UV-C robotics for lavatory and galley sterilization, eliminating chemical residue and ensuring biological safety.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Gantry and drone-based dry wash systems that maintain aerodynamic efficiency while drastically reducing water consumption.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision crawlers for fuselage inspection, detecting lightning strikes and micro-dents with higher accuracy than human visual checks.
