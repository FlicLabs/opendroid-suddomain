Research Portfolio: Van Diest Medical Center

Executive Summary

Van Diest Medical Center (VDMC) is a Critical Access Hospital positioned to leverage automation to combat rural staffing shortages and surgical outmigration. The analysis identifies Surgical Robotics as the primary revenue driver, enabling the retention of ~20% more cases locally, while Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) provide essential labor relief.

Implementation transforms VDMC into a "Tech-Enabled Critical Access Hub." By automating logistics and enhancing surgical capabilities, VDMC secures its position as the premier healthcare destination in Hamilton County, effectively stopping patient leakage to Des Moines.
