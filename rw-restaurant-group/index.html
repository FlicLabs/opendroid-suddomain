Executive Summary

The hospitality landscape is undergoing a seismic shift toward hybrid service models. For RW Restaurant Group, transitioning toward automation signifies an elevation of the human element. By offloading repetitive logistics to autonomous units, staff can focus on guest experience. This strategy targets a stabilized labor model within 18 months.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support.

Deployment of Tier-2 runner robots to transport heavy trays. Servers remain on the floor 90% of the time, increasing beverage upselling.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Focus: Consistency & Safety.

Automated fry baskets and robotic flipping arms. Ensures zero food waste due to overcooking and reduces burn injuries.

Pathway C: AI Agents

Focus: Upselling & Order Accuracy.

AI Voice agents for reservation management. Tableside tablets for secondary drink ordering to bypass service bottlenecks.
