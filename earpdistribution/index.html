01. Executive Vision

Earp Distribution stands at a pivotal juncture. To maintain leadership in the Midwest food service sector, the transition to a Hybrid-Automated Cold Chain is essential. Our strategy integrates heavy-payload AMRs and temperature-hardened ASRS to decouple labor from travel time. This shift moves operations from reactive fulfillment to predictive, continuous flow, securing cold-chain integrity and scalability.

02. Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

AMR Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal pallet transport. Decouples human operators from long-haul travel.

Impact: 35% more picks/hr

PATHWAY B

Freezer ASRS

High-density automated storage and retrieval for frozen goods. Eliminates "hunt time" in harsh environments.

Impact: 60% Space Reclaimed

PATHWAY C

AI Logistics

Predictive slotting logic and outbound loading optimization to reduce breakage and improve truck density.

Impact: 15% Less Breakage

03. Implementation Timeline

YEAR 2

YEAR 3
