Executive Summary

MaxHealth operates 17 multi-specialty hospitals with over 3,400 beds. The primary optimization strategy leverages Surgical Robotics for revenue growth and Logistics Automation for cost control. The combined approach is projected to yield a net incremental benefit of ~£9.5M annually with a payback period of under 18 months.
