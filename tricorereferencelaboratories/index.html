TriCore Reference Laboratories

Robotics Analysis & Automated Workflow Portfolio

Organizational Profile

12M+ Annual Tests
1,700 Providers Served
Regional Southwest Focus

TriCore is a non-profit clinical reference lab co-sponsored by Presbyterian Healthcare Services and UNM Health Sciences Center. They are pivoting from volume-based testing to a data-driven health information organization. The primary operational bottlenecks are labor shortages in pre-analytical stages and rural logistics challenges.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Total Lab Automation (TLA) High Impact

Scope: Track-based automation connecting centrifuges, analyzers, and storage.

Impact: Reduces manual touchpoints by 80%. Allocates 15-20 FTEs to higher-value tasks.

Pathway B: Pre-Analytical Robotics Immediate ROI

Scope: Computer vision robotic sorters for specimen intake, decapping, and sorting.

Impact: Eliminates primary intake bottlenecks and reduces labeling errors by 99%.

Pathway C: Autonomous Logistics Strategic

Scope: Drone (UAV) delivery network for remote clinics and stat hospital transfers.

Impact: 40-60% reduction in transport time for rural samples.
