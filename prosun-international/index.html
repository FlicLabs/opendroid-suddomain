Executive Summary

ProSun International is a vertically integrated manufacturer of commercial tanning and wellness equipment. The current operational model relies on skilled manual labor for metal fabrication and assembly. The strongest robotics levers are (1) Automated Fabrication (Welding/Coating), (2) Warehouse Intralogistics (AMRs), and (3) AI-Driven Support. Implementation promises a 5-year cumulative net savings exceeding £1.5M with a payback period of under 18 months for primary manufacturing automation.
