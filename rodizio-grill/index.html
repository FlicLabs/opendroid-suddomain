01. Executive Vision

The Rodizio Grill experience is defined by the "Gaucho" service model. Robotics should not attempt to replace this artisanal interaction. Instead, the strategy is to deploy Automated Logistics Support. By offloading low-skill transport tasks (bussing dirty dishware and restocking the salad bar) to autonomous units, human staff can maximize guest engagement and tableside carving time.

04. Strategic Pathways

PRIORITY A

Front-of-House Serving Logistics

Deployment of heavy-load autonomous bases (e.g., Keenon T-Series) dedicated to bussing. This reduces physical fatigue for servers and clears the "Gaucho" pathways faster.

PRIORITY B

Back-of-House Prep Automation

Automated slicing and dicing machinery for the high-volume Gourmet Salad Bar to ensure consistency and reduce morning prep labor hours.

PRIORITY C

AI Voice Reception

Voice AI agents to handle 100% of incoming reservation calls, capturing missed revenue opportunities during peak service hours.
