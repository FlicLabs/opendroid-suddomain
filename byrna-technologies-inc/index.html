1. Executive Summary

Byrna Technologies is positioned to transition from niche manufacturing to a mass-market Industry 4.0 leader in the less-lethal defense sector. This report outlines a "Hybrid Flexible Manufacturing" strategy, leveraging robotics to enhance the Fort Wayne facility. The focus is on stabilizing labor costs, increasing surge capacity, and ensuring 100% traceability for defense-grade products.

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Utilization of collaborative robots for screwdriving and adhesive dispensing on Byrna SD/LE launchers to ensure torque precision.
