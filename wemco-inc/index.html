Executive Vision

Wemco Inc. is positioned to transition from a traditional discrete manufacturer to a data-driven Smart Factory. By implementing High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) automation, the organization will decouple labor hours from production volume, stabilizing throughput against labor shortages.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deploying Cobots (Fanuc CRX/UR16e) to load CNC billets. Enables "lights-out" manufacturing during breaks and shift changes.

PATHWAY B

Autonomous Material Transport

Utilizing AMRs (MiR/OTTO) for moving heavy sub-assemblies between Welding and Machining, reducing forklift reliance.

PATHWAY C

Automated QC & Palletizing

Vision-guided robotics for final dimensional verification and palletizing, ensuring 100% inspection rates.
