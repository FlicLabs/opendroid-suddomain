Executive Summary

North Olympic Healthcare Network (NOHN) is a pivotal Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) serving the geographically isolated Olympic Peninsula. The analysis identifies Administrative Process Automation and Telepresence Robotics as the highest ROI levers. By implementing these technologies, NOHN can overcome critical rural staffing shortages and administrative burdens, projecting an annual net benefit exceeding $2.5M while significantly expanding patient access.
