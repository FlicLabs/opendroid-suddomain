JET OUT AUTOMATION

Executive Summary

Jet OUT is positioned to redefine regional private aviation. By deploying autonomous systems, the company can achieve hospital-grade sterilization, reduce turnaround times, and extend airframe longevity, effectively decoupling labor constraints from fleet scalability.

Organizational Profile

Fleet: Pilatus PC-12 & Cessna Citation CJ4
Focus: High-frequency regional utilization.
Challenge: Maintaining consistent hygiene and turnaround speed across dispersed hubs (Waukesha, FL) amidst labor volatility.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Compact, articulating robots using LIDAR for PC-12/CJ4 interiors.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Portable emitters neutralizing 99.9% of pathogens without chemicals.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Semi-autonomous gantries calibrated for small airframes to reduce water usage.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision bots for detecting micro-cracks and rivet failures.
