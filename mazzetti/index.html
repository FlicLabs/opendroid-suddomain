Robotics & Automation Opportunity Portfolio

Strategic Overview

Mazzetti is positioned at the intersection of sustainable engineering and technology consulting. The opportunity is not to build robots, but to become the architect of the "Robotics-Ready" hospital. By combining infrastructure design for client robotics (AMRs) with internal Generative Design AI, Mazzetti can unlock significant margins and market differentiation.
