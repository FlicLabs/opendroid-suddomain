Executive Summary

Mass Advantage stands at a critical juncture where operational efficiency meets member experience. By pivoting from manual administration to an AI-Orchestrated Payer Model, the organization can unlock significant value. The primary levers are Enterprise Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for claims and Intelligent Agents for member services. These initiatives directly attack the Administrative Loss Ratio while enhancing Star Ratings.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation Phase. Deployment of core RPA infrastructure focusing on "low hanging fruit" like standard claims adjudication and provider credentialing verification.

Year 2: Expansion Phase. Rollout of Member Facing AI agents to handle Open Enrollment volume. Integration of data for risk adjustment.

Year 3+: Innovation Phase. Pilot of physical assistive robots for high-risk senior members to reduce isolation and hospital readmissions.
