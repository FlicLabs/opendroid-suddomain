02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots handle internal transport, implementing a Goods-to-Person model that eliminates 60% of associate walking time.

Pathway B: High-Density ASRS

Vertical Lift Modules utilizing ceiling height to triple storage capacity while maintaining near-zero picking error rates.

Pathway C: AI & Vision

Computer vision at inbound docks for instant dimensioning and predictive slotting of high-velocity SKUs.

03. Financial & Operational Model

  • CAPEX Strategy: Modular deployment costing $50k - $100k per autonomous station.
  • OPEX Impact: Targeting a 30-40% reduction in variable labor and energy costs through "Dark Warehouse" storage zones.
  • Break-Even: Projected between Month 18 and Month 22, with net positive cash flow starting in Year 2, Q3.
