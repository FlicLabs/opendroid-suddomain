Research Portfolio: Emergency Staffing Solutions
Executive Summary

Emergency Staffing Solutions (ESS) is a healthcare workforce management provider placing physicians and nurses across the US. The strongest automation opportunities are Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for credentialing, AI-driven logistics for matching, and Telepresence Robotics as a service extension. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of "time-to-fill" shifts and administrative overhead compression.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Year 1 (Core Automation)

Deploy RPA for license verification and OCR for document handling. Target: Cut onboarding time by 50%.

Phase 2: Year 1-2 (Intelligent Matching)

Launch mobile app with algorithmic matching and dynamic pricing for urgent shifts. Target: 85% Fill Rate.

Phase 3: Year 2-3 (Telepresence Pilot)

Partner with rural critical access hospitals to deploy telepresence robots for remote specialty staffing.
