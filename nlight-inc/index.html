Executive Summary

nLIGHT Inc. is positioned to dominate the high-power semiconductor laser market by transitioning from manual-heavy assembly to Automatable Precision Manufacturing (APM). By integrating collaborative robotics into the discrete assembly of pump modules and utilizing AMRs for cleanroom logistics, nLIGHT can significantly improve yield rates and protect intellectual property while maintaining the flexibility required for high-mix production.

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly

Focus: Micron-level precision in diode stacking.

Deployment of force-sensitive collaborative robots for handling delicate optical components, reducing operator fatigue and ensuring repeatability in beam quality.

Pathway B: AMRs

Logistics & Replenishment

Focus: ISO Cleanroom Efficiency.

Autonomous mobile units to transport wafer cassettes and sub-assemblies. Reduces human contaminants in cleanrooms and optimizes WIP flow between fabrication and assembly.

Pathway C: Auto-QA

End-of-Line Inspection

Focus: Throughput & Data Integrity.

Automated docking for burn-in testing and M-squared beam profiling. Removes bottlenecks in final QA and feeds data directly into the MES.
