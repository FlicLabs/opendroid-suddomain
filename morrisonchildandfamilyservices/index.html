Morrison Child & Family Services

Robotics & Automation Consultation Portfolio

Executive Summary

Morrison Child and Family Services is a critical provider of trauma-informed care in Oregon. Our analysis identifies that the highest value robotics opportunities are not in physical labor, but in Cognitive & Process Automation. By deploying Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for administrative tasks and Social Assistive Robotics for pediatric therapy, Morrison can reclaim thousands of clinical hours annually, directly combating staff burnout and increasing patient capacity.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Therapeutic Robotics

ClinicalPediatric

Concept: Deploying social robots (e.g., Moxie, Paro) to assist in trauma and ASD therapy.

Impact: Acceleration of rapport building and improved emotional regulation in children.

Pathway B: Admin Automation (RPA)

High ROIOperations

Concept: "Software Robots" to handle intake, insurance verification, and compliance reporting.

Impact: Recovers 15% of clinician time for billable services.

Pathway C: Telepresence

Remote AccessSafety

Concept: Mobile robots for remote psychiatrist rounding in residential facilities.

Impact: Reduces travel time and ensures rapid specialist access during crises.
