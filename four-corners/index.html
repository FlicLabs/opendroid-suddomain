Executive Vision

Four Corners is poised to transition from traditional rigid manufacturing to a high-agility Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous logistics, the organization will decouple labor hours from revenue growth, ensuring scalability and precision.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of ISO/TS 15066 compliant cobots for machine tending. Focus on reducing ergonomic strain and stabilizing cycle times at CNC stations.

PATHWAY B

Autonomous Logistics

Implementation of AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots) for line-side replenishment, eliminating manual forklift dependency and search time.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Automation

High-speed vision systems integrated with palletizing arms to ensure 100% outgoing quality and maximize shipping throughput.
