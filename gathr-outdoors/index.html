EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Gathr Outdoors is positioned to leverage Industry 4.0 to transition from labor-intensive assembly to a flexible, data-driven manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating collaborative automation and intelligent logistics, the company can secure a competitive advantage through increased production agility, handling the seasonality of brands like Orca, Klymit, and GCI Outdoor.

ROBOTICS PATHWAYS
Pathway A: Cobots

Collaborative robots for riveting chair frames and trimming rotomolded coolers. Focus on safety and human augmentation.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving raw materials and finished sub-assemblies. Focus on WIP flow efficiency.

Pathway C: Palletizing

Automated end-of-line stacking for heavy coolers and boxed furniture. Focus on throughput and injury reduction.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
Year 1 Pilot Automated Palletizing & Digital Baseline.
Year 2 Cobot integration in Assembly & AMR Pilot.
Year 3 Full AMR Fleet & WMS Integration.
Year 4 Digital Twins & Predictive Maintenance.
Year 5 AI Demand Forecasting & Lights-Out Capabilities.
