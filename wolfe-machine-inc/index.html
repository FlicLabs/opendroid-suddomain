Labor Hour Redistribution

Automation does not eliminate the workforce; it evolves it. Low-value, high-strain tasks (vacuuming, scrubbing) are offloaded to robots, allowing human capital to focus on high-value tasks (Tech Ops, QA, Maintenance).

Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1: PILOT PROGRAM
Deployment of 5 Pathway A (Cabin) & 2 Pathway B (UV-C) units at primary hub. Data collection phase.
YEAR 2: HYBRID INTEGRATION
Expansion to three hubs. Introduction of Pathway C (Exterior Washing). Training of technical staff.
YEAR 3: FLEET-WIDE ROLLOUT
50% automation of overnight cleaning. Full deployment of Pathway A & B across network.
YEAR 5: FULL AUTONOMY
Pathway D (AI Inspection) integration. Steady state operations with 45% OPEX reduction realized.
