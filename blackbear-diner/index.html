STATUS: CONFIDENTIAL

Executive Vision

A strategic shift to a hybrid service model that preserves Black Bear Diner's "homey" atmosphere while integrating robotics to handle heavy lifting and logistics. The goal is to elevate the human server role to "Guest Experience Manager" by automating the runner and busser functions.

Entity: Black Bear Diner
Sector: Family Dining / Full Service
Locations: ~157 Units

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

FOH Service Bots

Deployment of Pudu/BellaBot units to handle food running and heavy bus tub returns, reducing physical strain.

PATHWAY B

Automated Grilling

Integration of automated fry arms and clam-shell grills to ensure consistency for hash browns and fried sides.

PATHWAY C

Voice AI Agents

AI-driven telephony for takeout orders to eliminate hold times and guarantee upselling scripts.

Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1 Pilot Program: 5 High-Volume California Locations.
YEAR 2 Regional Rollout (50% Locations) & BOH Testing.
YEAR 3 Full FOH Integration & Voice AI Systemwide.
YEAR 4 Smart Kitchen (KDS) & Robot Interconnectivity.
YEAR 5 Fully Autonomous Logistics Ecosystem.
