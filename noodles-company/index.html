Executive Summary

The fast-casual dining landscape faces a critical pivot point. For Noodles & Company, the vision is not merely replacing labor but augmenting the service model. Our strategic objective is the deployment of collaborative robotics to bridge the gap between QSR speed and full-service hospitality.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Serving Robots: Deploying autonomous shelf-delivery robots allows expediters to load multiple orders. This keeps human staff in the dining room focusing on guest satisfaction rather than ferrying plates.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Automated Sauté: Implementation of automated induction woks to handle high-heat, repetitive motion tasks. This ensures consistency in sauce distribution and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase in 10 high-volume locations.
  • Year 2: Regional Rollout & KDS Integration (50 locations).
  • Year 3: BOH Automated Sauté Pilots & 75% FOH Fleet.
  • Year 4: Full fleet saturation & Voice AI implementation.
  • Year 5: Hardware refresh and optimization.
