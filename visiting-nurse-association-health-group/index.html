Executive Summary

VNA Health Group is a critical provider of home health and hospice services in NJ, OH, and FL. The primary automation opportunity lies in shifting from a labor-constrained model to a technology-leveraged network.

The dominant ROI drivers are AI-driven workforce logistics, RPA for documentation, and Connected Health robotics. These interventions target the ~2M annual visit volume to unlock capacity and margin.

Org Profile

Scale: ~2M+ Annual Visits

Connected Health

Medication robots and telepresence.
