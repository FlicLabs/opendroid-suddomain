Executive Vision

Assurance Mfg is positioned to transition from labor-intensive throughput to High-Mix/Low-Volume Flexible Automation. Our strategy focuses on deploying collaborative robotics to handle repetitive precision tasks, mitigating labor shortages while ensuring production continuity.

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly

  • Deployment of IP66 rated Cobots (UR10e/Fanuc CRX).
  • Direct integration with CNC & Stamping Presses.
  • Eliminates idle time; 1 operator manages 4 cells.

Pathway B: AMRs

Material Logistics

  • Autonomous Mobile Robots for WIP transport.
  • Connects Stamping to Deburring/Finishing.
  • Reduces non-value-added walking time by 40%.
