Executive Vision

The Fluid Warehouse Model: Transitioning from static manual picking to a dynamic, automated ecosystem. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven mixed-case palletizing, Andrews Distributing can address SKU proliferation and labor scarcity while securing a competitive edge in the Texas beverage market.

Strategic Pathways

PATHWAY A

AMR Internal Transport

Replacing forklifts for long-haul travel (Receiving to Reserve). Reduces safety incidents and optimizes travel time.

PATHWAY B

Robotic Piece Picking

Automated mixed-case palletizing ensuring 99.9% accuracy and perfect stability for delivery trucks.

PATHWAY C

AI Logistics

Predictive route sequencing. Robots buffer pallets in reverse-route order for rapid truck loading.
