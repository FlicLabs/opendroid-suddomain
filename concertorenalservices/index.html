ConcertoCare

(Formerly Concerto Renal Services)

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

ConcertoCare is a leading value-based provider of in-home care for patients with complex chronic conditions, specifically End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD). The primary opportunity for robotics lies in shifting the "site of care" from expensive clinics to the home.

Core Strategy: Utilize predictive AI and automated home dialysis support to reduce the Total Cost of Care (TCOC) by an estimated £20M+ annually across the network.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AI-Logistics & Risk Engine

The "Software Robot" Approach. Utilizing machine learning to predict kidney failure events and optimize the routing of mobile care teams.

£15-25M Projected Annual Savings

Pathway B: Home Dialysis Automation

Next-gen home hemodialysis machines with robotic cannulation assistance to remove the need for in-clinic visits.

£25,000 Savings Per Patient/Year

Pathway C: Med-Adherence Robotics

Smart dispensing robots for polypharmacy management to prevent ER trips due to missed medications.

6 Months Payback Period
