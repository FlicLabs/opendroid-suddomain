Executive Vision

Veltri Logistics is positioned to transition from a traditional asset-based 3PL to a "Smart Logistics Hub." By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for pallet movement and AI-driven inbound logic, Veltri can decouple labor hours from warehouse throughput. This strategy directly addresses the acute labor shortages in the NJ/PA logistics corridor while reducing truck dwell times by an estimated 25%.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

01. Internal Transport

Deployment of AMRs for Dock-to-Stock movements. This removes the need for forklift operators to drive long distances, keeping them focused on high-value loading tasks.

02. Automated Picking

Introduction of vertical ASRS systems to increase storage density by 40% and improve picking accuracy to 99.9% for piece-picking fulfillment operations.

03. AI Vision Logic

Installation of computer vision at dock doors for automated dimensioning and damage inspection, streamlining the inbound receiving process.

  • YEAR 3-5: AUTONOMY High-density ASRS implementation and full "Lights-out" third-shift capabilities.
