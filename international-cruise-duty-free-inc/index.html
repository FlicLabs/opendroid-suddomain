Executive Vision

To transition International Cruise Duty Free Inc. from a manual operational model to a High-Velocity Secure Fulfillment Center. By integrating robotics, we solve the "Ship-to-Shore" bottleneck, securing bonded goods against shrinkage while meeting the erratic demand spikes of cruise turnaround days.

Robotics Pathways & Financial Impact

Pathway A: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for pallet movement. Replaces forklifts in long-haul transport, improving safety and reducing congestion.

Pathway B: ASRS (Cube Storage)

High-density automated storage for duty-free luxury goods. Eliminates theft and maximizes vertical warehouse space.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive engines linked to cruise manifests to pre-position inventory before orders are placed.

Implementation Roadmap
