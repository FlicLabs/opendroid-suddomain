Executive Summary

Crusader Community Health, serving the Rock River Valley, faces high volume and scheduling optimization challenges typical of FQHCs. By leveraging robotics—specifically Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for admin tasks and Automated Pharmacy Dispensing—Crusader can shift focus from administrative overhead to patient care. The dominant ROI driver is capacity expansion, enabling millions in incremental margin.
