Executive Summary

Pediatrica Health Group is a prominent multi-state network facing acute staffing shortages. The integration of robotics offers a critical lever for scalability. The most viable opportunities lie in Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) to reduce procedural anxiety, Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for triage, and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR) for logistics. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of appointment cycle time.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Socially Assistive Robotics

Deployment of interactive social robots (e.g., Moxie, Robin) in exam rooms. Reduces procedural anxiety, cutting appointment times by 5-7 minutes.

Pathway B: RPA & Intelligent Triage

AI-driven software robotics for pre-visit intake. Reduces administrative work by 20% per clinician and provides £3-5M operational savings.

Pathway C: Autonomous Logistics

AMRs in large-format urgent care hubs to transport lab samples. Keeps nurses at the bedside and optimizes license utilization.
