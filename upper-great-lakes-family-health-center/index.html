Executive Summary

UGLFHC operates in a challenging rural environment where distance and provider shortages are the primary adversaries. Our analysis identifies a high-ROI pivot from manual operations to a "Tech-Enabled Rural System." By leveraging Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for admin tasks and Telepresence Robotics for remote clinical delivery, UGLFHC can reclaim critical clinical hours and reduce patient leakage.
