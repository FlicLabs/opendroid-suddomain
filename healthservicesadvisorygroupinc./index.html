Health Services Advisory Group Inc.

Robotics & Process Automation Portfolio Analysis

1. Executive Summary

HSAG is a premier Quality Improvement Organization (QIO) and External Quality Review Organization (EQRO). As a knowledge-based entity, the automation portfolio focuses on Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and Cognitive Automation (AI/ML) rather than physical robotics.

Key Insight: The dominant ROI driver is the automation of clinical medical record abstraction. Moving from manual nurse review to AI-driven "review by exception" can unlock $5.5M+ in annual savings with a payback period under 9 months.

2. Organizational Profile

  • Model: Healthcare quality consulting, data analytics, and external quality review.
  • Scale: Multi-state operations reviewing hundreds of thousands of medical records annually.
  • Economics: Government contracts (CMS/Medicaid) where margin is defined by labor efficiency.
  • Core Bottleneck: High-volume manual data ingestion and expensive clinical labor required for chart audits.

3. Automation Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA Data Ingestion

Scope: Software robots to handle ingestion, validation, and scrubbing of massive claims datasets.

Impact: Reduces data analyst time by 60%. Ensures near-zero error rates in audit preparation.

Pathway B: Cognitive Review

Scope: NLP and OCR to read PDF medical records and abstract clinical data (HEDIS markers).

Impact: Shifts workflow to "Review by Exception." Increases review throughput by 300-400%.

Pathway C: Automated Surveys

Scope: AI-driven chatbots and voice systems for CAHPS survey administration.

Impact: 24/7 availability for providers and patients; higher response rates.

6. Strategic Positioning

Implementing this roadmap transitions HSAG from a labor-intensive consultancy to a Technology-Enabled Quality Platform. This allows for aggressive bidding on federal contracts due to lower cost structures and creates a potential secondary revenue stream by licensing the "Automated Quality Review" engine directly to health plans as a SaaS product.
