~14 Months ROI Payback Period

Key Benefit: Eliminates count discrepancies and frees up nursing hours for direct care.

Pathway B: Ambient Safety AI

Radar/LiDAR room sensors for fall detection and agitation monitoring without cameras.

-40% Adverse Safety Events
High Liability Risk Reduction

Key Benefit: Protects patient privacy while ensuring 24/7 monitoring in high-acuity crisis units.

Pathway C: Admin RPA

Software robotics for intake, insurance verification, and billing.

$500k+ Annual Revenue Capture
6-9 Months Rapid Payback

Key Benefit: Reduces denial rates and accelerates patient admission processing.
