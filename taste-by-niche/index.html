Executive Summary

High Tech for High Touch Hospitality

Niche Food Group represents the pinnacle of St. Louis dining. This research proposes a "Cobbot" (Collaborative Robot) strategy designed to eliminate the logistical burden of food running in high-volume venues like Pastaria. By automating the "carry," we empower human staff to focus on the "care," preserving the James Beard heritage while modernizing operations against labor volatility.

Strategic Opportunities

Pathway A: FOH Serving Bots

Focus: Pastaria & Brasserie.

Deploying autonomous runners to handle heavy tray transport. Reduces physical strain on staff and keeps servers in their sections to drive beverage sales and guest satisfaction.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Focus: Prep Kitchens.

Automated frying and pasta boiling lifts. Ensures consistent product quality during rush hours and reduces safety hazards for line cooks.

Pathway C: AI Voice Agents

Focus: Fordo's Pizza & Reservations.

AI-driven phone answering to capture every reservation and upsell carryout orders without distracting on-site hosts.
