Executive Summary

Primary Health Solutions (PHS) is a regional safety-net provider operating a network of health centers and mobile units. The strongest robotics opportunities involve shifting from manual administrative workflows to automated systems. Key levers include Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for revenue cycle management, automated in-house laboratory processing, and telepresence robotics for school-based health.

Organizational Profile

Model: Integrated primary care network (FQHC style) including family medicine, dental, and behavioral health.

Scale: 10-15 locations, mobile units, and school-based programs serving ~40k patients annually.

Bottlenecks: High no-show rates, administrative burden, and logistical challenges in distributed care.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA for Administrative Logistics (Highest Value)

Implementation of software robotics to handle appointment scheduling, insurance verification, and referrals.

  • Automated patient outreach to reduce no-shows.
  • Bot-driven insurance verification to minimize claim denials.
  • Impact: £1.5M - £3M incremental revenue annually.

Pathway B: Automated In-House Laboratory

Deployment of compact, high-throughput benchtop analyzers with robotic sample handling.

  • Reduction in outsourced lab costs.
  • Faster turnaround times for same-visit medication adjustments.

Pathway C: Telepresence Robotics

Mobile telepresence carts with robotic PTZ cameras for school-based health centers.

  • Allows nurse practitioners to cover multiple sites remotely.
  • Increases school-based visit volume by 30-40%.
