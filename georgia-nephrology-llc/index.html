Executive Summary

Georgia Nephrology LLC is positioned to transform from a traditional specialty practice to a tech-enabled renal care platform. The primary friction points—data processing and medication management—are ideal candidates for Robotic Process Automation (RPA). Additionally, the deployment of Automated Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) hardware offers immediate revenue streams and improved clinical outcomes for high-risk CKD patients.

Organizational Profile

  • Model: Multi-site specialty practice (CKD, Hypertension, Transplant).
  • Challenges: High administrative burden, fragmented lab data, complex care coordination.
  • Economics: Consult fees, dialysis capitation, value-based care incentives.
  • Opportunity: Shifting administrative labor to software bots to increase clinical panel size.
