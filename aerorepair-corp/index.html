Executive Summary

AeroRepair Corp is positioned to revolutionize its MRO operations by pivoting from manual maintenance to a robotics-first workflow. This strategy aims to drastically reduce Aircraft on Ground (AOG) time, enhance safety compliance, and secure long-term financial health through automation.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning Autonomous ground vehicles for vacuuming and trash collection in narrow aisles.
Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robotic arms utilizing UV-C arrays for 99.9% pathogen elimination.
Pathway C: Exterior Washing Automated gantry systems reducing water usage by 60% and eliminating height risks.
Pathway D: AI Inspection Drone and crawler bots for automated fuselage and wing structural analysis.
