01. Executive Vision

The Intelligent Fulfillment Node

Aero Fulfillment Services is positioned to pivot from a traditional 3PL model to a technology-first logistics hub. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven logic, Aero can mitigate Midwest labor volatility while delivering the speed required by modern e-commerce standards. This strategy preserves high-touch service while injecting enterprise-grade scalability.

PATHWAY A

AMR Transport

Deployment of collaborative robots to handle internal travel.

  • Eliminates manual cart pushing
  • Reduces walking time by 45%
  • Scalable "RaaS" (Robots as a Service) model
PATHWAY B

Robotic Picking

High-density Goods-to-Person systems for high velocity SKUs.

  • Vertical Lift Modules (VLM)
  • 60% reduction in storage footprint
  • Light-directed picking accuracy
PATHWAY C

AI Logic Layer

Middleware ensuring predictive efficiency.

  • Intelligent batching
  • Dynamic slotting optimization
  • Predictive carrier selection
