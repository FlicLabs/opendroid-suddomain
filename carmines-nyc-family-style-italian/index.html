CARMINE'S NYC

Strategic Automation Report: Optimizing Family Style Dining

Executive Summary

The dining landscape is shifting toward a hybrid model. For Carmine's, robotics serves as a force multiplier. By automating the transport of heavy family-style platters and standardizing high-heat cooking processes, we shift human focus from logistics to hospitality. The goal is to increase table turnover and safety while maintaining the warmth of Italian service.
