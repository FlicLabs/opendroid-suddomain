Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous ground vehicles for vacuuming and surface wiping. Reduces turn time by handling repetitive tasks.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen neutralization using UV-C light. Zero chemical residue and instant sterilization for passenger safety.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Robotic gantries and mobile units for fuselage cleaning. Improves aerodynamics and fuel efficiency.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision drones for detecting lightning strikes and structural anomalies.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Deployment. 5 Units at Primary Hub. Testing overnight maintenance efficacy.
  • Year 2-3: Expansion Phase. Scale to 50 units across top hubs. Integration with flight ops.
  • Year 4: Fleet-Wide Adoption. Standardized robotic cleaning for all domestic turns.
  • Year 5: Full Optimization. Autonomous orchestration and predictive maintenance.
