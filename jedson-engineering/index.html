01. Executive Summary

Jedson Engineering is positioned to transition from traditional EPC delivery to Integrated Smart Facilities. By embedding flexible manufacturing and Industry 4.0 principles, Jedson can deliver "Born Intelligent" facilities.

Strategic Focus: High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) flexibility and Modular Automation to bridge the gap between Process and Discrete manufacturing flows.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots

Focus on safety and machine tending in Consumer Products sectors. Implementation of cobots for line loading/unloading.

Pathway B: AMRs (Mobile Robots)

Focus on WIP flow efficiency. Replacing forklifts with Autonomous Mobile Robots for Just-In-Time material replenishment.

Pathway C: Automated Quality

End-of-Line palletizing combined with vision systems to ensure throughput and zero-defect yield.

03. Financial Model

  • CAPEX: $60k - $120k per robotic cell deployment.
  • OPEX Impact: Stabilization of monthly spend via predictive maintenance.
  • Payback Drivers: Labor reallocation, 15% scrap reduction, and increased cycle yields.

04. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot & Validation (Retrofit Projects)
  • Year 2-3: Integration & Expansion (Standardize "Smart Cells")
  • Year 4-5: AI Prediction & Dark Factory Capabilities
