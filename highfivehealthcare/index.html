HighFive Healthcare: Robotics Analysis & Strategy

Executive Summary

HighFive Healthcare is a regional leader in outpatient physical and hand therapy. The strategic integration of robotics focuses on alleviating therapist burnout and increasing patient throughput. The primary value driver is Robotic-Assisted Upper Extremity Therapy, which offers a projected £4.1M annual incremental benefit with a payback period of under 12 months.

Organizational Profile

Scale & Model

~45 Clinics focused on PT, OT, and Hand Therapy.

~450k Visits/Year serving fee-for-service and value-based care contracts.

Operational Friction

Therapist cap on billable units/hour.

High staff turnover due to physical strain.

Patient drop-off due to low engagement.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Upper Extremity Robotics (Highest Value)

Concept: Deploy sensorized gloves and desktop robotic assistants for hand/arm therapy across all sites.

Impact: Gamification increases retention; automation allows concurrent supervision of patients.

Projected Return: £4.4M/year incremental revenue.

Pathway B: Gait Exoskeletons

Concept: Wearable lower-limb robots for neuro-rehab at 5 flagship "Centers of Excellence."

Value: Market differentiation and premium case acquisition.

Pathway C: Clinic Logistics

Concept: Autonomous service robots for equipment transport and smart kiosks.

Value: Operational efficiency and reduced non-clinical labor.

Financial Analysis (Pathway A)
