Executive Summary

Langton Green operates at the intersection of residential care and vocational training for adults with intellectual disabilities. The primary operational bottlenecks are labor shortages (DSPs), safety monitoring, and physical strain on staff. The strongest robotics levers are Ambient Assisted Living (AAL) to reduce 24/7 awake staff requirements, Exoskeletons to mitigate injury claims, and Agricultural Robotics to democratize work at the Community Farm.
