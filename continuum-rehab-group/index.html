Executive Summary

Continuum Rehab Group: Automation Strategy

CRG operates in a high-pressure environment defined by staffing scarcity and value-based care mandates. Our analysis identifies a £1.5M+ annual opportunity through the integration of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for administrative tasks and Therapeutic Robotics for clinical differentiation. This strategy shifts the operational model from labor-intensive to technology-leveraged, directly addressing burnout and PDPM efficiency.
