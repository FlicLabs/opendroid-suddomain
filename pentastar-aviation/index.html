PENTASTAR AVIATION

Strategic Automation Research & Robotics Integration Proposal

Executive Summary

The integration of robotic automation represents a paradigm shift for Pentastar Aviation's operational efficiency. By deploying a specialized fleet of cleaning and inspection robots, Pentastar can achieve hospital-grade sanitization, reduce aircraft downtime, and optimize labor allocation. This report details a strategic vision focusing on high-ROI pathways, targeting a 35-45% reduction in operational expenses with a payback period under 24 months.

Organizational Profile

Headquarters: Waterford, MI (KPTK)

Fleet Size: 100+ Aircraft (Light to Long-Range Jets)

Operations: Full-service FBO, MRO, and Charter Services.

Current Model: Labor-intensive manual detailing and inspection processes.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robotic vacuums and sanitizers for interior detailing using LiDAR navigation.
