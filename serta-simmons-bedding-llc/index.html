Automation Strategy Report

Entity: Serta Simmons Bedding LLC Prepared by: Open Droids Date: October 2023

Executive Summary

Serta Simmons Bedding LLC is positioned to lead the sleep sector through Industry 4.0 adoption. The strategic vision focuses on flexible manufacturing capable of handling "Mass Customization"—producing personalized mattresses at mass-production speeds. By integrating semi-autonomous robotic cells, SSB can transition from linear manual processes to discrete smart manufacturing, ensuring resilience against labor shortages and optimizing supply chain logistics.

Production Scale & Locations

Operating as a high-volume discrete manufacturer, SSB maintains major hubs in:

  • Wayne, PA: Strategic Corporate HQ
  • Athens/Newnan, GA: Primary high-volume manufacturing
  • CRE Plants (West Coast/South): Specialized and contract manufacturing

Current Manufacturing Process

SSB utilizes a linear Discrete Manufacturing flow. While some legacy automation exists (quilting), the process relies heavily on manual material handling and assembly. This creates bottlenecks where machine speed exceeds human throughput, specifically in foam layering and end-of-line palletizing.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Application: Machine tending for presses and assistant roles in mattress assembly (layer positioning). Force-sensing cobots work alongside humans without caging.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Application: Autonomous transport of raw materials (foam rolls, springs) to line-side. Reduces operator walking time and enables Just-in-Time delivery.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Application: High-speed robotic palletizing and AI-driven visual inspection. Eliminates ergonomic injuries and reduces shipping defects.

Implementation Roadmap (Year 1 - 5)

Year 1 Assessment & Pilot

Time-motion studies, deployment of Pilot Pathway A (Cobots) for foam layering. Establish baseline OEE.

Year 2 Material Handling (AMRs)

Standardize AMR deployment for line-side replenishment. Reduce internal logistics lead time by 30%.

Year 3 End-of-Line Automation

Roll out Pathway C (Robotic Palletizing & Vision Inspection) across primary hubs.

Year 4 Digital Twin & IoT

Implement predictive maintenance sensors. Create digital twins for simulating changeovers.

Year 5 Full Autonomy

AI-optimized "Lights Out" manufacturing capability for standard models. Fully flexible custom assembly lines.
