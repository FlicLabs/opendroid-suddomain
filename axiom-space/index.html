STRATEGIC RESEARCH REPORT

Robotic Cleaning & Maintenance Automation for Axiom Space

Executive Summary

This report outlines a strategic pathway for integrating robotic automation into the aviation cleaning and maintenance operations of Axiom Space. The vision is to transition from manual labor-intensive processes to a hybrid workforce where specialized robotic units handle repetitive, time-consuming, and hazardous tasks.

By deploying Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots, UV-C Disinfection Systems, Exterior Washing Units, and AI-Powered Inspection Bots, Axiom Space can achieve a pristine operational standard, enhance passenger safety, and realize significant cost efficiencies.

Organizational Profile

Focus: Commercial human spaceflight and microgravity research.

Operational Model: High-precision ground support, training facility management, and asset protection.

Current State: Reliance on skilled technicians for GSE maintenance and cabin preparation.

Requirement: Logistics infrastructure that matches the sophistication of flight hardware, ensuring rapid turnaround and data-driven cleanliness.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

1. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Self-navigating units for training modules and mock-ups. Prioritizes high-touch zones with microfiber tech.

2. UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous carts emitting UV-C light. Chemical-free sanitization for sterile simulation environments.

3. Exterior Washing

High-pressure robotic systems with AI vision. Reduces water waste and eliminates scaffolding risks.

4. AI Inspection Bots

Drones/crawlers with thermal cameras. Detects micro-fractures and corrosion, feeding digital twin data.
