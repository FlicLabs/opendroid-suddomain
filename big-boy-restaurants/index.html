Big Boy Restaurants

Automated Systems & Robotics Integration Strategy

01. Executive Vision

Big Boy represents a heritage of American comfort. To preserve this legacy while combating modern labor shortages, our strategy focuses on "Augmented Hospitality." By deploying robotics for heavy lifting (bussing/running), we protect the physical well-being of staff and ensure they remain at the table, focused on the guest experience. This is not replacement; it is elevation.
