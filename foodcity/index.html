01 // Executive Summary

Food City is positioned to revolutionize its regional distribution network by transitioning from traditional warehousing to a Hybrid Fulfillment Model. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven slotting, Food City can decouple throughput from labor availability constraints. This strategy secures operational continuity against labor shortages while enabling high-velocity replenishment for the growing network of 130+ stores.

02 // Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

AMR Transport Fleet

Replacing manual forklift travel. Associates remain in zones while robots handle the long-haul transit of pallets from receiving to reserve. Reduces non-value-added walking time by 45%.

PATHWAY B

Robotic Piece Picking

Automated Storage and Retrieval (ASRS) for slow-moving and HBC items. High-density storage releases floor space for expanded cold chain capacity. Target accuracy: 99.9%.

PATHWAY C

AI Inbound Logistics

Predictive slotting utilizing WMS data. AI positions high-velocity seasonal goods near shipping docks before the demand surge hits, optimizing flow.
