BAMBOO HEALTH

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Bamboo Health is a software infrastructure entity, making "Robotics" a play on Software Robotics (RPA) rather than hardware. The primary opportunity lies in automating the "Last Mile" of care coordination—converting massive ADT data streams into automated workflows to reduce alert fatigue.

1 Billion+ Annual Patient Encounters
2,500+ Hospital Partners
Software Primary Robotics Type

Opportunity Pathways

HIGHEST VALUE

Pathway A: RPA for Care Coordination

Concept: "Bots" that auto-intake alerts into EHRs, filtering noise and creating tasks without human clicking.

Impact: 60-70% reduction in admin time per alert.

Immediate ROI
GROWTH DRIVER

Pathway B: Automated Engagement Agents

Concept: AI/Robotic conversational agents that text/call patients immediately post-discharge.

Impact: Reduces readmissions; creates "outcome guarantees."

Mid-Term ROI
FUTURE STATE

Pathway C: Physical Integration

Concept: Using ADT data to trigger physical hospital robots (logistics/pharmacy) automatically.

Impact: Deep operational stickiness; high complexity.
