01. Executive Vision

LTB Logistics is positioned to leapfrog competitors by transitioning from a "Person-to-Goods" model to a high-velocity "Goods-to-Person" ecosystem. Our strategic analysis indicates that integrating AMRs will transform the warehouse from a cost center into a competitive asset, enabling 24/7 "Dark Warehouse" capabilities.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Transport)

Goal: Decouple transport from picking.
Impact: Pickers remain in zones. Pallet jacks are replaced by intelligent swarms.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking (ASRS)

Goal: Vertical density.
Impact: 400% increase in SKU density using vertical bins and robotic retrieval.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Goal: Predictive shipping.
Impact: Algorithms predict truck loading patterns to minimize shipping "air".
