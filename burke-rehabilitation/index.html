Burke Rehabilitation: Strategic Robotics Analysis

Burke Rehabilitation stands as a regional leader in high-acuity physical rehabilitation. The primary opportunity lies in shifting from a labor-limited manual therapy model to a robotic-amplified high-repetition model. This transition addresses the critical bottlenecks of therapist fatigue, patient throughput limits, and operational logistics.
