Implementation Strategy

The transition for Legacy Supply Chain focuses on high-impact zones where human-robotic collaboration yields the highest ROI. By automating the transport layer (AMRs) first, we stabilize the baseline operations before introducing complex picking algorithms.

YEAR 1 Pilot: AMR Integration for pallet and bulk transport across primary corridors.
YEAR 2 E-commerce Acceleration: Deployment of ASRS and Robotic Piece Picking systems.
YEAR 3 AI Expansion: Real-time slotting optimization and predictive inbound processing.
YEAR 4 Inbound/Outbound: Vision-guided robotic arms for automated trailer loading.
YEAR 5 Full Orchestration: Global dashboard controlling autonomous fleet across all nodes.
