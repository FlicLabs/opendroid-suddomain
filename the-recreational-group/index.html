Executive Summary

The Recreational Group (RG) stands as a vertically integrated leader in recreational surfacing. To maintain market dominance and improve margins, RG must transition from manual-heavy operations to an automated precision model. The identified opportunities lie in Manufacturing Logistics, Field Installation Robotics, and Autonomous Maintenance. Execution of this strategy is projected to yield multi-million dollar margin improvements within 24 months.
