Executive Vision: Industry 4.0

Royal Wine Corp is positioned to transition from traditional semi-automated lines to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By integrating collaborative robotics and AMRs, the facility can decouple labor from repetitive transit tasks, focusing human expertise on the art of winemaking. This strategy addresses seasonal volatility and ensures consistent high-volume throughput for brands like Kedem and Baron Herzog.
