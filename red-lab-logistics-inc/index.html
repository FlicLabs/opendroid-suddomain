03. Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Goods-to-Person robotics to eliminate travel time. Enables 24/7 internal movement without shift premiums.

Pathway B: ASRS

High-density storage with robotic picking arms. Maximizes vertical space and ensures 99.9% pick accuracy.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive algorithms for dynamic slotting and loading dock optimization to reduce detention fees.
