Executive Vision

Burger 21 operates in a unique hybrid gap between QSR and Casual Dining. Our analysis suggests that Pathway A (Serving Robots) resolves the critical bottleneck of staff leaving the register to run food.

By automating the "last 20 yards" of service, Burger 21 preserves its "Beyond the Better Burger" quality while operating with the speed of a QSR.
