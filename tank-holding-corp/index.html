Organizational Profile

Tank Holding Corp operates ~40 facilities specializing in Rotational Molding. The primary challenge is decoupling labor from the harsh physical environment of demolding and finishing large polyethylene tanks. The transition strategy moves from discrete manual processing to connected, semi-automated flows.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Finishing & Safety

Deploying force-torque sensitive Cobots for routing flash and drilling flanges. Eliminates manual heavy tool usage and ensures consistent wall thickness.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport pulverized resin to ovens and move cooled, heavy tanks to staging. Reduces forklift traffic in pedestrian zones.

Pathway C: Quality Vision

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Automated end-of-line palletizing for ancillary parts combined with ultrasonic wall-thickness inspections to create immediate feedback loops.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 Pilot Deployment: Install Pathway A (Trimming) in flagship Lincoln, NE facility.
  • Year 2 Intra-Logistics: Deploy AMR fleet and integrate with ERP for auto-replenishment.
  • Year 3 Network Scale: Standardize the automation cell and roll out to 50% of plants.
  • Year 4 Smart Integration: Cloud dashboarding for predictive maintenance on molds.
  • Year 5 The Autonomous Plant: Full maturity with 80% automated material handling.
