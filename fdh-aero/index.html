01 // Executive Summary

The vision for FDH Aero is to implement a flexible, data-driven fulfillment ecosystem. By integrating robotics into kitting, chemical management, and inventory logistics, FDH Aero can reduce AOG (Aircraft on Ground) response times and ensure 100% quality compliance. This strategy transitions the operational model from reactive logistics to predictive, high-velocity distribution.

02 // Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Smart Kitting (Cobots)

Focus: Precision. Cobots equipped with vision systems and force-sensing grippers pick and place fasteners and rivets. Ensures exact counts and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

B. Intralogistics (AMRs)

Focus: Flow. Fleet-managed AMRs automate transport from storage to shipping. This Goods-to-Person approach maximizes human value-add time.

C. Auto-Inspection

Focus: Compliance. Automated optical inspection (AOI) verifies part numbers in milliseconds, ensuring zero-defect delivery for aerospace clients.
