Pager Health

Robotics & Automation Portfolio Analysis

Executive Summary

Pager Health is a "digital front door" for 28M+ lives, orchestrating care via chat and video. The primary robotics opportunity is Software Robotics (RPA) and Conversational AI Agents. By automating the high-volume traffic control of healthcare, Pager can shift from a service-heavy model to a scalable SaaS platform.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Triage Bots

Scope: AI Agents for Tier-1 support.

Impact: 30-40% deflection of human chat volume.

Value: £8-12M Operational Savings.

Pathway B: Admin RPA

Scope: Back-end "Swivel-chair" robots for claims & referrals.

Impact: 90% reduction in data errors.

Value: £3-5M Efficiency Gain.

Pathway C: RPM Integration

Scope: Connecting physical home monitoring devices (scales, cuffs) to the digital stream for chronic care management. Increases platform retention and improves clinical outcomes.

Strategic Positioning

Implementing this portfolio transitions Pager Health from a Tech-Enabled Service (lower margin) to a Robotic-Orchestrated SaaS Platform (high margin). This creates a scalable "Central Nervous System" for virtual care that is difficult for competitors to replicate without similar automation density.
