Executive Summary

Northern Lights Candles is poised to transition from a traditional hybrid manufacturing model to an Industry 4.0 enabled Smart Factory. By integrating Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS), specifically Collaborative Robots and AMRs, the facility can automate high-repetition tasks without compromising the artisan quality of the brand. This strategy targets a 25% increase in throughput while resolving labor allocation inefficiencies.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Cobot Assembly

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of IP66 Cobots for automated wick centering immediately post-pour and pick-and-place jar handling. Eliminates human variance in burn quality.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Heavy payload Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving wax tanks and finished pallets. Line-side replenishment bots to reduce operator walking time.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Automated palletizing cells and AI Vision Systems for final quality inspection. Detects glass defects and label misalignment before shipping.
