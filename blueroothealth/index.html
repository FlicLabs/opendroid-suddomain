BLUEROOT HEALTH

Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Opportunity: Blueroot Health sits at the intersection of functional medicine and consumer health. The primary opportunity is to shift from a labor-heavy clinic and fulfillment model to a tech-enabled platform.

Strategy: Deployment of robotics in three key areas: (1) Automated Micro-Fulfillment for supplements, (2) Diagnostic Automation for clinical lab work, and (3) Telepresence for scaling specialist care.

Outcome: Projected 40% reduction in fulfillment costs and a 30% increase in patient throughput capacity.

Robotics Pathways
High ROI

Pathway A: Fulfillment

Concept: Robotics for picking, packing, and sorting personalized supplement regimens in regional hubs.

Impact: Reduces picking errors, enables mass-customization of "Daily Packs", and cuts labor overhead by ~50%.

Efficiency

Pathway B: Diagnostics

Concept: Automated phlebotomy assist devices and self-service intake kiosks in clinics.

Impact: Standardizes the blood draw process, reduces patient anxiety, and frees up nursing staff.

Scale

Pathway C: Telepresence

Concept: Mobile robotic avatars allowing top-tier specialists to consult across multiple satellite locations.

Impact: Maximizes utilization of high-cost providers and expands geographic reach.

Financial Model (Pathway A Focus)
400% Throughput Increase
£1.15M Annual Savings
18 Mo Payback Period

*Model assumes automation of 500k annual orders, reducing cost-per-order from £4.50 to £2.20 via labor and error reduction.

Strategic Roadmap
1

Year 1: Supply Chain Modernization

Select automated picking vendors for central warehouse. Launch "Personalized Daily Packs" to leverage new capabilities.

2

Year 1.5: Clinic Pilots

Deploy robotic phlebotomy assist devices in top 3 high-volume clinics. Measure patient satisfaction and safety data.

3

Year 2+: Network Expansion

Roll out diagnostic automation network-wide. Introduce telepresence robots for expansion into rural markets.
