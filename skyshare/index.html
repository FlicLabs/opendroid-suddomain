ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Mobile robots for vacuuming and surface wiping. Reduces staff requirements by 30-40%.


B. UV-C Disinfection

Automated UV sanitation cycles for pathogen control. Ensures standardized hygiene.

C. Exterior Washing

High-pressure robotic systems improving aerodynamics and fuel efficiency.


D. AI Inspection Bots

Drone-based visual inspections for structural integrity and early defect detection.
