Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Medication Dispensing (Highest Value)

Deployment of robotic dispensing kiosks for Methadone/MAT programs. This eliminates manual pouring, integrates biometrics for patient ID, and links directly to the EHR.

Pathway B: Residential Facility Robotics

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for floor care and sanitation in supportive housing corridors, improving hygiene and freeing staff for maintenance tasks.

Pathway C: Administrative RPA

Software robotics to automate insurance verification and state compliance reporting, acting as the digital backbone for increased patient volume.
