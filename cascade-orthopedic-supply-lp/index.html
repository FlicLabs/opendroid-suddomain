Executive Summary

Cascade Orthopedic Supply operates in a high-complexity, high-accuracy niche. The transition to a Goods-to-Person (GTP) ecosystem using Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) represents a critical evolution. By automating internal transport, Cascade can reduce picker travel time by 60%, ensuring the speed and precision required for medical logistics while mitigating labor shortages in key distribution hubs like California and Texas.
