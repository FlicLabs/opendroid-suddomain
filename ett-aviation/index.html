eTT AVIATION AUTOMATION STRATEGY

Strategic Robotics Implementation & Financial Roadmap

Executive Summary

The integration of robotics into eTT Aviation's operations represents a transformative leap toward operational excellence and hygiene supremacy. By deploying a suite of autonomous systems ranging from cabin cleaning to UV-C disinfection and AI-driven inspection, eTT Aviation can achieve a projected OPEX reduction of 35-45%. This strategy prioritizes safety, efficiency, and passenger confidence, ensuring a payback period of under 24 months while modernizing the airline's maintenance infrastructure.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous mobile robots for vacuuming and wiping. Optimized for quick turnarounds.

Est. CAPEX: $75k - $90k

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Chemical-free sanitation using high-intensity UV light. Enhances passenger safety trust.

Est. CAPEX: $80k - $100k

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

High-pressure automated gantries. Reduces water usage and improves aerodynamics.

Est. CAPEX: $120k - $150k

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Thermal and visual scanning bots for structural integrity and predictive maintenance.

Est. CAPEX: $85k - $110k

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot Phase

  • Deployment of 5 Cabin & 2 UV-C units.
  • Hub 1 Operational Integration.
  • Data collection and baseline analysis.

Year 3: Expansion

  • Exterior Washing Systems deployment.
  • 50% Fleet coverage (UV-C & Inspection).
  • Integration with Central MMS.

Year 2: Scaling

  • Scale to 20 Cabin units (2 Hubs).
  • AI Inspection Bots (20% Fleet).
  • Protocol optimization.

Year 4-5: Full Automation

  • 100% Active Fleet Coverage.
  • Predictive Maintenance Algorithms.
  • Renewable Energy Integration.
