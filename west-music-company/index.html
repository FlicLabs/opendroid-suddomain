Executive Vision

For West Music Company, the future of logistics lies in Hybrid Automation. By combining the precision of AMRs with the density of ASRS, we target a solution that respects the fragile nature of musical instruments while aggressively tackling the velocity requirements of the "Back-to-School" season. This strategy positions West Music to reduce fulfillment times by 40% and stabilize labor costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

AMR Transport

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for heavy payload movement (pianos, amps). Decouples picking from transport to reduce fatigue and injury risk.

PATHWAY B

ASRS Piece Picking

High-density automated storage for sheet music and small accessories. Increases picking accuracy to 99.9% and reduces storage footprint.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase & AMR Deployment for heavy goods.
  • Year 2: Installation of ASRS for small parts (sheet music).
  • Year 3-4: AI Integration for seasonal demand forecasting & scaling.
  • Year 5: Full Ecosystem Maturity.
