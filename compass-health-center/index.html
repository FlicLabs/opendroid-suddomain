Compass Health Center

Robotics & Process Automation Strategy

Compass Health Center operates in a high-complexity environment bridging inpatient and outpatient mental healthcare. The primary robotics opportunity is not mechanical, but rather "Process Robotics" (RPA) and Therapeutic Technologies. By automating the complex logistics of patient intake, insurance authorization, and cohort scheduling, Compass can unlock massive capacity.
