Executive Summary

Retina & Vitreous of Texas (RVT) stands at a pivotal juncture where high-volume clinical demands meet precision surgical requirements. Our analysis identifies three primary automation levers: (1) Robotic Microsurgery, (2) Automated Imaging Lanes, and (3) Pharmacy Robotics. The deployment of these technologies addresses critical bottlenecks in patient wait times and inventory control, promising a net incremental benefit exceeding $20M over a five-year horizon.
