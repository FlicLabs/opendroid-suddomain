Detailed Opportunity Analysis

Pathway A: Telerobotic Ultrasound

Deploys remote robotic arm systems allowing specialists on Oahu to perform scans on patients in rural/outer-island clinics. Reduces travel costs and democratizes access to specialist care.

Pathway B: Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Software robots handle insurance verification, prior authorizations, and scheduling 24/7. This immediately reduces administrative overhead and denial rates.

Pathway C: AI Diagnostic Triage

AI algorithms pre-read scans to prioritize urgent cases and automate measurements, increasing radiologist reading speed and accuracy.
