Executive Vision

Pei Wei stands to gain significant competitive advantage by pivoting to a technology-assisted hybrid model. The implementation of robotics will not replace the culinary artistry of the wok, but rather streamline the logistics of service, allowing staff to focus on high-value guest interactions.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service
Autonomous runners to handle heavy tray delivery, reducing staff physical strain and increasing dining room presence.

Pathway B: BOH Automation
Rotary induction woks to ensure "Wok Hei" consistency and mitigate skilled labor shortages.

Pathway C: AI Integration
Voice agents for drive-thru and phone orders to maximize upselling accuracy.

Financial Impact Model

  • CAPEX: Est. $55,000 per store (2 Robots + 1 Auto-Wok).
  • OPEX Savings: Elimination of 1 runner shift/day + increased throughput.
  • Revenue Boost: 12% faster table turnover during peak lunch hours.
"Targeting a < 14 month payback period through combined labor reduction and increased table turns."

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program (5 High-Volume Locations).
  • Year 2: Regional Rollout (FOH Robots).
  • Year 3: BOH Wok Automation Integration.
  • Year 5: Full Ecosystem Maturity & Predictive Analytics.
