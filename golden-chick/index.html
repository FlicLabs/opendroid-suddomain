Executive Vision

To modernize Golden Chick's operations by integrating robotic frying assistance and AI-driven order taking. The goal is to elevate Southern Hospitality by removing logistical friction, allowing staff to focus on guest engagement rather than repetitive tasks.

Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A (FOH): Serving Robots for table delivery and bussing, reducing server fatigue and improving dining room cleanliness.
  • Pathway B (BOH): Automated Frying Arms for "Golden Tenders" to ensure consistent cook times and reduce oil burn hazards.
  • Pathway C (Voice AI): Conversational AI for Drive-Thru to automate order taking and guarantee upsell scripts are read every time.
