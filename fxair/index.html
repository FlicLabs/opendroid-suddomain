Hubs: Major International Airports

FINANCIAL MODEL

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

AMRs for vacuuming and surface wiping. Optimized for tight turnarounds.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous UV-C light application for pathogen neutralization without chemicals.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Robotic crawlers for fuselage washing, reducing water usage by 40%.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drones equipped with thermal/optical sensors for predictive maintenance.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP (5-YEAR)

YEAR 1: Pilot Program

Deploy Pathways A & B at two primary hubs. Establish performance baselines.

YEAR 2: Expansion

Scale to 50% of fleet. Introduce AI Inspection (Pathway D).

YEAR 3: Hub Integration

Full deployment at primary hubs. Introduce Exterior Washing (Pathway C).

YEAR 4: Optimization

Data-driven route optimization. Predictive maintenance cycles.

YEAR 5: Standardization

Fleet-wide adoption. Achieve max OPEX reduction (45%).
