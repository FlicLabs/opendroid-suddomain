Executive Vision

Otter Air Services stands at a critical juncture in aviation maintenance. This report analyzes the integration of robotic automation to shift from labor-intensive manual processes to a hybrid human-machine workforce. The goal is to reduce ground time, enhance sanitation, and lower long-term operational expenditures.
