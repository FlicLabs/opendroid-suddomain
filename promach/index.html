01. Executive Summary

ProMach is a global leader in packaging machinery solutions. To maintain market dominance, the entity must pivot its internal manufacturing strategy towards Industry 4.0 connectivity. By transitioning from manual, batch-based workflows to automated, flexible manufacturing, ProMach can secure supply chain resilience and reduce lead times for capital equipment.

Vision: A fully connected ecosystem where machine tending, material flow, and quality assurance operate autonomously to support high-mix/low-volume production.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Safety & Precision in Machine Tending.

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for CNC tending frees skilled machinists for programming and QC tasks. Includes torque-critical screwdriving for electrical panel assembly.

Pathway B: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.

Implementation of heavy-payload AMRs to transport raw sheet metal and finished sub-assemblies between fabrication islands, reducing non-value-added forklift travel.

Pathway C: Automated Quality Inspection

Focus: Throughput & Yield.

Utilization of Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) to verify circuit board integrity and mechanical tolerances prior to Final Acceptance Testing (FAT).
