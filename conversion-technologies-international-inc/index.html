01. Executive Vision

Transitioning CTI to an Autonomous Converting Facility. By decoupling labor availability from production capacity, CTI can orchestrate a fluid, automated material transport system connecting slitting, laminating, and die-cutting stations. This shift addresses labor shortages and enhances safety in handling heavy adhesive rolls.

02. Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

  • Automated transport of "master rolls" and pallets.
  • Elimination of forklift waiting times.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking & ASRS

  • Vacuum gripper arms for stacking die-cut parts.
  • Vision systems for real-time defect detection.
  • Vertical Lift Modules (VLM) for tooling storage.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

  • Predictive material staging based on job schedules.
  • Reduction of "search and retrieve" downtime.

03. Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1 Foundation & Pilot: Network upgrades and 2 Heavy Payload AMRs.
YEAR 2 Expansion: Rollout to Slitting/Die-Cutting & First Picking Arm.
YEAR 3 Data Integration: AI-driven predictive logistics software.
YEAR 5 Full Ecosystem: Dock-to-dock automated material flow.
