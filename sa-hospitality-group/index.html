Executive Vision

For SA Hospitality Group, the strategy is Invisible Automation. We recommend deploying "Runner Bots" to handle the long-distance transport of dishes in your expansive Sant Ambroeus and Felice layouts.

This preserves the elegance of Milanese hospitality by keeping human servers at the table, pouring wine and building relationships, rather than running back and forth to the kitchen.

Robotics Pathways
