Advanced Physical Therapy of Alaska

01 // Executive Summary

Hybrid Automation Strategy

Advanced Physical Therapy of Alaska (APTA) is positioned to revolutionize regional care by pivoting from a labor-dependent model to a technology-leveraged framework. The proposed portfolio targets three core pillars: (1) Admin RPA to reduce overhead in a high-cost labor market, (2) Therapeutic Robotics to increase throughput per clinician, and (3) Remote Monitoring to bridge the geographic gaps inherent to Alaska.
