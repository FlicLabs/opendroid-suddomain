Executive Summary

Southshore Companies is positioned to leverage its strategic Midwest location by transitioning from a labor-dependent 3PL model to a Tech-Enabled Logistics Hub. This report outlines a roadmap to integrate Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems. The strategic goal is to increase operational density by 25% and achieve a sub-24-month ROI through labor optimization and energy efficiency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Goal: Replace manual long-haul forklift travel.
Tech: Pallet-moving Autonomous Mobile Robots.
Impact: Separates transport tasks from high-skill picking, increasing forklift operator efficiency by 35%.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Goal: Optimize high-volume fulfillment.
Tech: Goods-to-Person (GTP) Systems.
Impact: Increases vertical storage density by 40% and achieves 99.9% picking accuracy.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Goal: Predictive efficiency for Inbound/Outbound.
Tech: AI Layer on top of WMS.
Impact: Dynamic dock scheduling reduces truck turnaround times by 20%.

Year 1 Pilot Program: 8 AMRs & WMS Integration.
Year 2 Expansion: Fulfillment Goods-to-Person rollout.
Year 3 Optimization: Full AI Predictive Analytics.
Year 4 Scaling: Replicate success to secondary sites.
