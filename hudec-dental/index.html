Executive Summary

Modernizing Regional Dental Care

Hudec Dental operates in a high-pressure environment defined by staffing shortages and rising operational costs. By shifting from a purely labor-dependent model to a technology-enabled platform, Hudec can reclaim approximately 7,800 clinical hours annually via sterilization logistics automation. The integration of robotic precision in surgery and automation in administration creates a scalable engine for margin growth and market differentiation.

Pathway A

Sterilization Logistics

Automating the "dirty-to-clean" loop of instrument processing. Reclaims ~1.5 hours per day for dental assistants.

Impact: +£1M Annual Revenue
Pathway B

Surgical Robotics

Haptic robotic guidance (e.g., Yomi) for implants. Increases precision and serves as a major marketing differentiator.

Impact: High Strategic Value
Pathway C

Admin RPA

Software robotics for scheduling, billing, and insurance verification to reduce overhead and denials.

Impact: Instant Payback
