Executive Vision

For Broadway Restaurant Group LLC, the integration of robotics is a margin preservation strategy. By deploying a hybrid workforce model—combining autonomous fry-station management and AI voice ordering—we aim to stabilize operations against labor shortages while increasing peak-hour throughput.

Strategic Pathways

  • Pathway A: Serving Automation
    Autonomous runners for dine-in tray delivery to reduce FOH foot traffic.
  • Pathway B: BOH Robotics (Priority)
    Automated fry-stations (e.g., Flippy) to ensure consistent cook times and safety.
  • Pathway C: Voice AI
    Drive-thru assistants to automate upselling and guarantee 99% order accuracy.
