Executive Summary

Possibilities Northeast LLC is positioned to transform its operational model from labor-intensive 1:1 supervision to a technology-enabled support network. By leveraging Remote Support Robotics, Sensor Fusion, and RPA, the organization can overcome regional staffing shortages in the Northeast. The dominant ROI driver is the conversion of passive overnight staffing into active remote monitoring, generating significant margin improvements while enhancing client independence.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation & Pilot

  • Select 2 flagship residential sites for Remote Support retrofitting (Sensors + Dispensers).
  • Deploy RPA bots for "Section 21" billing audits and EVV compliance.

Year 2: Scale & Optimize

  • Scale Remote Support to all eligible homes (approx. 5-10 sites).
  • Introduce Telepresence robots for clinical supervision in rural locations.

Year 3: Advanced Integration
