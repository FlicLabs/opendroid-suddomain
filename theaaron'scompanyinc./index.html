Executive Summary

The Aaron's Company Inc. is positioned to transform its lease-to-own showrooms into data-driven experience centers. By integrating autonomous inventory auditing and automated facility maintenance, Aaron's can significantly reduce liability, optimize asset tracking, and elevate the customer consultation experience.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Inventory & Asset Intelligence

COMPLIANCESHRINKAGE

Deployment of computer-vision enabled units to track high-value electronics and verify lease terms on planograms. Ensures "returned" stock is graded and priced correctly.

Pathway B: Automated Care

SAFETYLABOR

Industrial autonomous scrubbers to maintain large showroom floors. Reduces slip-and-fall liability and saves ~15 hours of associate labor per week.

Pathway C: Service & Security

LOSS PREVENTION

Roaming presence for off-hour security and peak-hour customer triage, allowing remote management to virtually tour stores.

Financial Model

  • Revenue Upside: ~$2,500/mo via better stock accuracy.
  • ROI Speed: < 1.5 Years.
