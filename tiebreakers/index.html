Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-guided floor scrubbers and seat sanitizers that operate during boarding sequences. Reduces dedicated cleaning time by 40%.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Mobile pulsed Xenon units eliminating pathogens on high-touch surfaces in under 10 minutes. Chemical-free and validated.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Robotic gantry systems with water reclamation. Improves aerodynamics and paint longevity while eliminating manual risks.

Pathway D: AI Inspection Bots

Thermal imaging and ML algorithms for predictive structural analysis. Shifts maintenance from reactive to predictive.

Labor Hour Redistribution Post-Automation

Automation shifts focus from repetitive physical tasks to quality assurance, customer service, and technical oversight.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Strategy)

Year 2: Core Expansion
