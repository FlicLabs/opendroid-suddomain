Executive Vision: Safety-First Automation

SureWerx has the opportunity to align its manufacturing processes with its brand mission. By transitioning to a flexible, data-driven Smart Manufacturing ecosystem, the company can offload dangerous, repetitive tasks to collaborative systems. This "Safety-First" approach improves throughput while virtually eliminating ergonomic risks.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Assembly (Cobots)

Focus: Safety & Precision
Deployment of ISO/TS 15066 compliant Cobots for snap-fitting hard hat suspensions and tending injection molding machines for eyewear. Aligns with high-mix manufacturing needs.

PATHWAY B

Intralogistics (AMRs)

Focus: WIP Flow
Fleet deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to move raw pellets and finished safety gear, replacing forklifts in tight aisles and reducing collision risks.

PATHWAY C

Quality & Palletizing

Focus: Yield & Throughput
Vision-guided inspection for optical clarity in PPE lenses, combined with heavy-lift cobots for end-of-line palletizing to reduce back strain.

Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1
Pilot Phase: 2 Cobot Cells + 1 Vision System (NA Facility).
YEAR 2

YEAR 3

YEAR 4
