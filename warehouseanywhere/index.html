03. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of Locus/Fetch style bots for internal transport. reduces walking time and integrates easily into narrow storage aisles.

Pathway B: Compact ASRS

Vertical lift modules customized for 10x20 units. Increases storage density by 400% and secures high-value inventory.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive pre-positioning. Robots move high-velocity items to dispatch zones before the courier arrives.
