02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of high-payload Cobots for machine tending and panel assembly. This strategy focuses on safety and precision, allowing skilled technicians to oversee operations without physical strain.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots for WIP flow. This creates a Just-In-Time internal logistics model, removing the need for manual material transport.
