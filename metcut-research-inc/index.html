ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Mobile robots mapping interiors for rapid turnaround. Focus on high-touch surface sanitization and seat organization. Reduces manual labor by 60% during short ground times.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous units utilizing UV-C light to sterilize cabins post-cleaning. Ensures verifiable hygiene standards and increases passenger confidence without chemical downtime.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry or robotic arm systems for fuselage and wing cleaning. Minimizes water usage and safety risks while maintaining aerodynamic efficiency.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drone and ground-based bots with ML algorithms for structural anomaly detection. Shifts maintenance from reactive to predictive, extending asset lifespan.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP

  • Phase 1 (Year 1): Pilot Program. Deployment of Cabin Cleaning and UV-C robots at primary hub. Focus on data collection and workflow validation.
  • Phase 2 (Year 2): Hub Expansion. Scaling to secondary hubs. Introduction of Exterior Washing Systems. Staff upskilling for robot supervision.
  • Phase 3 (Years 3-4): AI Integration. Deployment of AI Inspection Bots. Centralized data platform for predictive maintenance analytics.
  • Phase 4 (Year 5): Full Autonomy. Integrated ecosystem managed by central AI. Optimization of total ground time and resource allocation.
