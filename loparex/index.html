Executive Vision

Loparex stands at the threshold of a Smart Converting Ecosystem. The strategic pivot involves transitioning from reactive manual handling in high-speed coating and slitting environments to a predictive, autonomous workflow. By integrating robotics, Loparex will secure throughput consistency, reduce ergonomic risks, and guarantee 100% surface inspection for premium medical and electronic grade liners.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobots

Focus: Slitting & Rewinding

Deployment of 10-20kg payload cobots for core loading and finished roll extraction. Eliminates RSI and ensures precise telescope-free winding.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Focus: Intralogistics

Heavy-duty Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving jumbo rolls from Coating to Converting. Reduces forklift traffic by 60% and lowers product damage.

PATHWAY C: Quality

Focus: EOL & Inspection

Automated palletizing cells coupled with high-speed vision systems to detect coating skips. Removes physical bottlenecks at the packaging line.
