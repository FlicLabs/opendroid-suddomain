ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

Simcom Aviation Training operates a network of high-fidelity flight simulation centers.

  • Asset Base: 50+ Full-Flight Simulators globally.
  • Operational Model: 16-18 hour daily operations requiring rapid asset turnover.
  • Current Challenges: Labor-intensive simulator cabin cleaning; subjective manual inspections; dust sensitivity in optical systems.
