Charlottesville-Albemarle Airport Authority

Strategic Automation Report: Aviation Cleaning & Maintenance

Executive Summary

The integration of robotic automation at CHO represents a pivotal shift in operational strategy. By deploying autonomous units for cabin cleaning, UV-C disinfection, and exterior maintenance, the airport targets a 35% to 45% reduction in operational expenditures. This report outlines the pathway to achieving full deployment within a 5-year horizon, ensuring enhanced safety standards and superior passenger experiences.

Core Vision: Transform CHO into a regional leader in automated aviation hygiene and maintenance efficiency.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous vacuums and wipers for regional jets. Reduces manual labor during short turnarounds.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Mobile UV-C units for terminal and cabin sanitation. Reduces chemical usage and improves hygiene.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated fuselage cleaning systems. Improves aerodynamics and prevents corrosion.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drones and bots for structural and runway inspections. Enables predictive maintenance.
