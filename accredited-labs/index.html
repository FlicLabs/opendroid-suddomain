Executive Summary

Accredited Labs is positioned to revolutionize its sector by pivoting from legacy batch processing to a flexible, Industry 4.0-enabled manufacturing model. This report identifies high-impact opportunities in collaborative robotics and autonomous transport to solve labor volatility and traceability challenges.

Strategic Goal: Transition to a high-mix, low-volume agile production environment with full digital traceability.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly.
Deployment of 6-axis cobots (e.g., UR10e) for bottle feeding and capping. This eliminates repetitive strain injuries and stabilizes cycle times without requiring safety fencing.

Pathway B: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.
Utilization of AMRs (e.g., MiR250) to transport bulk ingredients and finished goods. This decouples human operators from low-value transit tasks.

Pathway C: Automated Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield.
End-of-line robotic cells to handle heavy payloads, ensuring consistent stack quality and reducing physical fatigue.
