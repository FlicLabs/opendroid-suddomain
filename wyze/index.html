Executive Summary

Wyze has disrupted the smart home market through hyper-competitive pricing. To maintain margins while scaling, the next phase requires a transition to Industry 4.0. This strategy proposes integrating smart robotics to reduce COGS by 12% and improve yield rates on optical sensors. The vision is a fully connected, data-driven production ecosystem.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Assembly

Deployment of 6-axis cobots for precise handling of camera lens modules. Reduces "dead-on-arrival" sensor defects by eliminating human handling variance.

Pathway B: AMR Intralogistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for line-side replenishment. Ensures high-volume lines for Wyze Cam v3 never face material starvation downtime.

Pathway C: Automated EOL

High-speed Delta robots for boxing and palletizing. Increases packaging throughput by 250 units per hour and optimizes shipping density.
