Executive Opportunity Overview

Brown Bag Seafood Co. is uniquely positioned to leverage Front-of-House (FOH) logistics robotics to bridge the gap between premium seafood quality and the speed of fast-casual dining. By automating the "final mile" of food delivery within the restaurant, staff can focus on the guest experience and hospitality touchpoints.

Pathway A: FOH Autonomous serving robots to reduce staff travel time by 40% during peak hours.
Pathway B: BOH Smart frying systems to ensure thermal consistency for sensitive seafood proteins.
Pathway C: AI Predictive ordering kiosks that increase average check size via smart upselling.
