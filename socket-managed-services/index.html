01. Executive Summary

This report outlines the strategic pivot for Socket Managed Services from manual legacy assembly to a high-velocity Smart Manufacturing entity. By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), we target the elimination of production bottlenecks and the establishment of a data-driven, flexible production ecosystem.

03. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Assembly & Tending

Deployment of ISO/TS 15066 compliant arms for precision torque application and PCB seating. Removes RSI risks and ensures 100% digital torque validation.


Pathway B: AMRs

Material Transport

Fleet-managed autonomous units for line-side replenishment. Expected to reclaim 45 minutes of operator value-add time per shift.


Pathway C: End-of-Line

Palletizing & QA

High-speed vision inspection combined with heavy-payload vacuum grippers for final boxing and shipping preparation.
