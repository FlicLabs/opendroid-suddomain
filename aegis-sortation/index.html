Executive Vision

Aegis Sortation is poised to transition from a traditional assembly model to a Modular Adaptive Manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating cyber-physical systems, Aegis will mitigate labor volatility and increase throughput. The strategic pivot involves three core pillars: Collaborative Robotics for assembly, Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics, and Computer Vision for quality assurance.
