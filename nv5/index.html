Executive Summary

NV5 is positioned to transition from traditional manufacturing to a responsive, data-driven entity. The core vision involves adopting flexible manufacturing principles to handle high-mix, low-volume orders with rapid changeover times. By integrating Industry 4.0 technologies—specifically robotics and AI analytics—NV5 aims to achieve real-time decision-making capabilities, minimizing downtime and maximizing throughput in a competitive global market.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Application: Machine Tending (CNC/Press) & Precision Assembly.

Benefit: Optimizes floor space by removing safety cages; reduces assembly variance.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Application: Material Transport & Line-Side Replenishment.

Benefit: Eliminates "waste of motion" for operators; enables JIT material delivery.

Pathway C: Automation

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Application: End-of-Line Palletizing & Vision Inspection.

Benefit: Reduces physical strain injuries and catches microscopic defects at speed.
