Executive Summary

SOB Hospitality stands at the precipice of a technological evolution. By integrating a hybrid workforce model—utilizing robotics for logistics and humans for hospitality—the group can mitigate labor volatility while increasing operational throughput. This report outlines a capital-efficient path to modernizing service delivery, focusing on a Service-First Automation philosophy.

Projected Performance Analytics

Pathway A: FOH Robotics

Deployment of autonomous runners for food delivery and busing.

  • Reduces server physical fatigue.
  • Increases face-time with guests.
  • Target: 4 Units across flagship sites.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Automated frying solutions for high-volume consistency.

  • Standardizes cooking times.
  • Improves late-night safety.
  • Target: 2 Automated Basket modules.

Pathway C: AI Agents

Voice AI for reservation management and ordering.

  • 0% Missed Call Rate.
  • Upsell integration script.
  • Target: Full CRM integration.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1
    Pilot Phase Deploy FOH runners at flagship location. Map floor plans and integrate AI Voice for reservations.
  • Year 2
    Standardization Scale robotics to all locations. Install BOH automated fryers for late-night menu consistency.
  • Year 3
    Data Integration Deep POS integration. Optimize staffing pars based on robot uptime and efficiency data.
  • Year 5
    Full Autonomy Complete ecosystem integration including inventory robots. Market leadership in tech-enabled dining.
