Executive Summary

Advanced Care Hospitalist (ACH) is positioned to pivot from a traditional staffing model to a tech-enabled clinical logistics platform. By deploying Telepresence Robotics (Pathway A), ACH can decouple physician capacity from geography, allowing instant specialized rounding across distributed facilities.

This shift addresses the critical bottleneck of provider travel time and rural coverage gaps. Coupled with Robotic Process Automation (Pathway B) for discharge planning, ACH can realize reduced length of stay and significant margin expansion.
