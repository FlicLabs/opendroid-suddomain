Logistics Automation Strategy

Executive Vision

Nyrstar is positioned to transition from traditional industrial material handling to a Smart Smelting & Logistics Ecosystem. By integrating Heavy-Payload AMRs and AI-driven supply chain engines, operations can achieve a target of 99.9% spare part availability and remove human operators from high-risk transport zones.

22 Mo Target Payback Period
35% OPEX Reduction
$4M Annual Savings (Year 3+)

Projected Performance Analytics

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Heavy-Payload AMRs

Replacing manual forklifts for transporting zinc cathodes and lead ingots. Focus on 2-ton payload autonomous units to ensure 24/7 internal movement without shift changeovers.

Pathway B: MRO ASRS

High-density Vertical Lift Modules for maintenance inventory. Reducing retrieval time for critical spares from 20 mins to <2 mins, directly impacting smelter uptime.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive engines to synchronize raw concentrate arrival with smelter throughput, minimizing port demurrage costs and optimizing outbound routing.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 Pilot Fleet (5 AMRs) & Initial VLM Installation
  • Year 2 MES Integration & Expansion to 50% MRO Inventory
  • Year 3 Full Automation & "Lights-Out" Ingot Movement
  • Year 4 Algorithm Optimization & Battery Tech Upgrades
  • Year 5 Digital Twin Integration & Next-Gen Humanoid Pilots
