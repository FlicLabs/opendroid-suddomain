Executive Summary

Amoskeag Health, a critical FQHC in Manchester, NH, faces operational challenges typical of safety-net providers: high volume, complex reimbursement structures, and staffing constraints. This analysis identifies high-value automation levers in Pharmacy (dispensing/packaging), Administration (RPA), and Interpretation (Telepresence). Implementation of these technologies shifts the operational model from labor-intensive administration to technology-enabled clinical care.
