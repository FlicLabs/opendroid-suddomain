Executive Vision

The transition to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS) is critical for Deep South Equipment Co. By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), the organization transforms from a fixed legacy model to an agile, data-driven ecosystem. This strategy targets a 35% reduction in lead times.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots (10-20kg payload) to tend CNC milling machines and press brakes. This relieves skilled machinists from repetitive loading, allowing one operator to manage three cells.

Pathway B

AMR Logistics

Replacing manual forklifts in high-traffic zones with Autonomous Mobile Robots for line-side replenishment. This eliminates WIP bottlenecks and digitizes inventory flow.

Pathway C

Automated QC & Palletizing

Heavy-payload arms combined with 3D vision systems at the End-of-Line. Ensures 100% inspection of weld integrity and dimensional accuracy while increasing palletizing throughput.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1
Pilot: Single Cobot CNC cell & Digital Twin baseline setup.
Year 2
Scale: Roll out Cobots to fabrication; Deploy AMR fleet.
Year 3
Integration: EOL Palletizing & Vision System integration.
Year 4-5
Autonomy: Full lights-out machining & AI predictive maintenance.
