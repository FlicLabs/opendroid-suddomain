Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport: Deployment of LiDAR-guided pallet movers to replace long-haul forklift travel. Expected internal velocity increase of 35%.

Pathway B: Picking & ASRS

Density & Accuracy: Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems for high-density kitting. Reclaims 40% of vertical floor space.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive Efficiency: Computer vision at inbound docks for auto-dimensioning and load pattern optimization.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot

Select Metro Hub Alpha. Deploy 10 AMRs and AI Dock Dimensioning.

Year 2: Scale

Expand fleet to secondary hubs. Implement ASRS in primary facility.

Year 3: Rollout

Standardize workflows North America-wide. Create "Dark Warehouse" zones.

Year 4: AI Core

Predictive scheduling and automated robot charging cycles.

Year 5: Maturity

Drone cycle counting and autonomous yard management.
