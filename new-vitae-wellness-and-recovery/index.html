Executive Summary

New Vitae Wellness and Recovery operates in a high-touch environment where staffing shortages and medication management represent the most significant operational risks. This analysis identifies a primary opportunity in automated medication dispensing, capable of saving ~60-80 nursing hours daily across the network. Secondary opportunities in AI-driven safety monitoring (fall detection) offer substantial risk mitigation without increasing labor costs.
