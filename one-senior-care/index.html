Executive Summary

One Senior Care operates within the PACE model, bearing full financial risk for frail elderly participants. The dominant ROI drivers for robotics are cost avoidance (hospitalization reduction) and operational efficiency (logistics). Key opportunities include AI-driven fleet logistics, in-home robotic medication adherence systems, and service robotics for day centers.
