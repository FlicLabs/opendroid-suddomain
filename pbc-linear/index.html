01. Executive Summary

PBC Linear is positioned to transition from isolated islands of automation to a fully integrated, data-driven ecosystem. By leveraging advanced robotics in high-mix/low-volume discrete manufacturing, the organization can address skilled labor shortages while ensuring micron-level precision.

Core Objective: Reduce custom part lead times by 40% and secure "lights-out" manufacturing capabilities.

02. Organizational Profile

Location: Roscoe, Illinois

Process Type: Discrete Manufacturing

Primary Operations:

  • Heavy machining (Centerless grinding, Milling)
  • Precision Assembly (Linear rails, actuators)
  • High variability, rapid changeover requirements
