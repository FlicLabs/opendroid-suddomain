Executive Summary

Zephyr Products Inc. faces a critical pivot point in the transition to Industry 4.0. Our research indicates a high-value opportunity to transition from traditional batch processing to a "Smart Fabrication Ecosystem." By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous logistics, Zephyr can address skilled labor shortages and reduce changeover times by 40%. The ultimate vision is a facility capable of 24/7 autonomous production for standard SKUs while empowering human artisans for complex custom work.

Organizational Profile

Sector: Precision Metal Fabrication

Throughput: 400+ Unique SKUs/Month

Current State: Discrete manufacturing with significant manual handling bottlenecks between laser cutting, bending, and welding stations. High WIP inventory due to disconnected workflows.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Cobots

Machine Tending: Deployment of 10-20kg payload cobots at press brakes to handle repetitive heavy lifting, doubling operator throughput.

Pathway B

AMRs

Material Transport: Autonomous Mobile Robots for line-side replenishment, connecting laser nesting to bending stations.

Pathway C

Vision Systems

Quality & Palletizing: Automated end-of-line inspection and palletizing to ensure 100% yield and reduce shipping damage.
