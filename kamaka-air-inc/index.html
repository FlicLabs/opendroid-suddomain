Executive Summary

Kamaka Air Inc. is positioned to revolutionize its ground operations through the integration of robotics. By automating cleaning and maintenance tasks, we target a 35-45% reduction in operational costs while elevating hygiene standards. The vision is to move from labor-intensive manual cleaning to a tech-enabled, efficient workflow, ensuring faster turnaround times and superior asset longevity.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning: HEPA vacuuming and sanitization for E-Jets and ATRs.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection: Automated biological threat neutralization in lavatories and galleys.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Washing: Gantry systems for drag reduction and fuel efficiency.
  • Pathway D: AI Inspection: Thermal imaging drones for fuselage integrity checks.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Strategy)

Year 1: Pilot Phase at HNL. Deployment of 5 Cabin & 3 UV-C units. Data baselining.
Year 2: Hub Consolidation. Expansion to OGG/KOA. Exterior Washing System installation.
Year 3: Fleet-Wide Rollout (70% coverage). Introduction of AI Inspection Bots.
Year 4: Optimization. Predictive maintenance integration. Cargo hold protocols.
Year 5: Full Autonomy. 100% coverage. Zero-waste targets. Staff transition to technical oversight.
