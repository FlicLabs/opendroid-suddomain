Cheyenne County Hospital

Strategic Robotics Implementation & Automation Portfolio

Executive Summary

Cheyenne County Hospital operates as a critical access hub in a challenging rural environment. The strategic focus of this research highlights automation not as a replacement for labor, but as an extender of clinical reach. By implementing Telerobotics (Pathway A) and Environmental Automation (Pathway B), the hospital can retain high-acuity patients locally and reduce the burden on limited staff.

Pathway A: Telerobotics

  • Scope: ER & Acute Care Wings
  • Function: Remote specialist navigation
  • Goal: Reduce patient transfers by 20%
  • Impact: ~$160k net annual benefit

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

  • Scope: Facility-wide hygiene
  • Function: Autonomous room sterilization
  • Goal: Reduce HAIs & room turnover time
  • Impact: Staff augmentation & safety
