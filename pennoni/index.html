Organizational Profile

Pennoni operates within a Discrete Manufacturing environment. Unlike continuous process industries, production is characterized by distinct units and high-mix configurations.

Production Scale

High-mix, medium-volume. Capable of rapid SKU changes but currently limited by manual setup times.

Current Processes

Manual Machine Tending, Assembly, and Palletizing. Reliance on operator availability creates bottlenecks during shift changes.

2

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Safety & Precision | Application: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deploying UR10e class cobots to handle repetitive loading/unloading of CNCs. This increases machine utilization from ~60% to >85% by eliminating operator fatigue and cycle time variability.

Pathway B: AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency | Application: Material Transport

Replacing manual forklifts with AMRs for line-side replenishment. Utilizing SLAM navigation to adapt to dynamic floor layouts, reducing material transit time by 40%.

Pathway C: Automated End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield | Application: Palletizing & Inspection

Integrating 6-axis arms with 2D/3D vision systems. Automates pallet stacking and performs inline quality checks, reducing packaging errors and shipping damages.

3

Financial Model & Visualization

The strategy relies on a targeted CAPEX deployment yielding a rapid payback period through OEE improvements and labor reallocation.

4

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation & Pilot

Network infrastructure upgrade. Deployment of 2 Cobot cells for machine tending. Establishing baseline KPIs.

Year 2: Material Flow

Rollout of AMR fleet for WIP transport. Expansion of cobot cells to secondary lines. Focus on reducing material handling latency.

Year 3: End-of-Line Automation

Implementation of robotic palletizing and vision-based quality inspection. Full integration with WMS/MES systems.

Year 4: Process Integration

System-wide data aggregation. Introduction of predictive maintenance analytics. Closed-loop feedback between inspection and assembly.

Year 5: Smart Factory Maturity

Scaling to satellite facilities. Implementation of Digital Twins for simulation. Fully autonomous material flow loops.
