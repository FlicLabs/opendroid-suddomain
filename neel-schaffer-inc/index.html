01. Executive Vision

Neel-Schaffer Inc. is positioned to redefine civil infrastructure support through the adoption of Flexible Manufacturing Systems. By integrating robotics into materials testing labs and component fabrication, the firm will transition from reactive manual processes to predictive, high-precision automated workflows.
