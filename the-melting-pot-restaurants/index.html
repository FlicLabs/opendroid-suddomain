Executive Vision

The strategic integration of automation for The Melting Pot is not about replacing the human connection, but elevating it. By utilizing autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for heavy lifting—specifically the transport of hot fondue pots and refill trays—we unlock the server's capacity to focus entirely on the experiential dining narrative.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Staff Safety & Table Turns

  • Automated transport of heavy, hot liquids.
  • Reduced physical strain on servers.
  • Faster table resets increasing revenue per night.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Focus: Consistency & Prep

  • Automated precision slicing for proteins.
  • Induction automation for sauce consistency.
  • High-volume dishwashing automation.
