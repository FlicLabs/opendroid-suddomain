01. Executive Vision

The "Smart Artistry" Shift

Anisa International is positioned to revolutionize the cosmetic tool industry by integrating flexible automation into its Tianjin operations. The strategic goal is to decouple production capacity from linear labor scaling.

By implementing Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), Anisa can maintain the high-touch, artisanal quality of its brush heads while automating the repetitive, non-value-added tasks of glue dispensing, material transport, and palletizing.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Precision Assembly

Tech: 6-Axis Cobots with Dispensing Effectors.
Impact: Automates the gluing of ferrule to handle. Eliminates adhesive waste and ensures micron-level consistency.

Pathway B: Intralogistics (AMRs)

Tech: Autonomous Mobile Robots.
Impact: Automates transport of WIP bins between Anodizing and Tufting. Increases skilled labor availability by 12%.

Pathway C: Automated QC

Tech: Machine Vision Gantries.
Impact: High-speed inspection for bristle symmetry and logo alignment. 40% faster packaging throughput.
