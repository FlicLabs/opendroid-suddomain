Executive Summary

Fenway Health operates at the intersection of high-volume outpatient care and clinical research. The primary value drivers for automation are Pharmacy Throughput (critical for revenue) and Laboratory Processing (critical for research grants). Our model projects significant margin retention through automated dispensing systems.
