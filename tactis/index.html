01. Executive Vision

Tactis must transition from siloed automation to a connected Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By adopting High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) capabilities, the organization will reduce changeover times and establish itself as a Smart Manufacturing lighthouse. The strategy prioritizes data-driven decision-making and human-centric robotic augmentation.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending
Deploying collaborative robots for CNC and Injection Molding to ensure consistent cycle times.

Pathway B: Internal Logistics
Utilizing AMRs for dynamic material transport, reducing non-value-added walking time by 40%.

Pathway C: Quality Control
Automated vision systems for defect detection and end-of-line palletizing.

03. Financial Outlook

  • CAPEX: $60k - $120k per robotic cell (Cobot vs. Complex Palletizer).
  • OPEX Benefit: Maintenance costs capped at 3% hardware cost/year.
  • Labor Impact: Reallocation of low-skill labor to high-value supervision roles.
