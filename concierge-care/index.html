Executive Summary

Transforming Agency Care via Automation

Concierge Care is positioned to evolve from a traditional nurse registry into a tech-enabled care platform. The integration of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for logistics and Telepresence Robotics for hybrid care delivery addresses critical bottlenecks in staffing and margin scalability. Our model projects a 40% reduction in admin costs and the creation of entirely new recurring revenue streams.
