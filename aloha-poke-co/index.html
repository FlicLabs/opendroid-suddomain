01. Executive Summary

The fast-casual dining landscape is undergoing a tectonic shift toward hyper-efficiency. For Aloha Poke Co., integrating robotics is a scalability necessity. This report outlines a vision to transition from a labor-heavy QSR model to a tech-enabled "Smart-Casual" entity, focusing on stabilized labor costs, portion consistency, and elevated customer throughput.

15-25% Labor Cost Reduction
< 14 mo Target Payback Period
$40k Max Capex Per Unit

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Front-of-House Serving

Deployment of "Runner Bots" for dining room management and delivery driver hand-offs. Focuses on clearing tables and reducing foot traffic for human staff.

PATHWAY B

Automated Prep (BOH)

Implementation of Rice Robotics and automated dispensers. Ensures gram-perfect consistency for premium proteins, drastically reducing food cost variance.

PATHWAY C

AI-Driven Kiosks

Smart ordering terminals with predictive upselling. Increases average ticket size by removing social friction and ensuring consistent "add-on" suggestions.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot & Calibration
    Alpha testing at 3 high-volume locations. Data gathering on portion control.
  • Year 2: Standardization
    Mandate AI Kiosks for new franchises. Optional Service Robot rollout.
  • Year 3-5: Integration & Dominance
    Full API integration, Loyalty-AI, and market leadership in "Smart-Casual" dining.
