Executive Vision

For BRW, the future of logistics lies in a Hybrid Automation Model. By transitioning from manual heavy-lifting to an ecosystem of Heavy-Payload AMRs and High-Density ASRS, BRW can secure its position as the dominant furniture logistics force in Central Europe. This strategy specifically targets the challenges of omni-channel fulfillment for bulky, flat-pack inventory.

Pathway A

AMR Transport

Deployment of Heavy-Payload AMRs (1000kg+) to automate the movement of raw wood panels and finished furniture pallets. Eliminates internal traffic jams.

Pathway B

Robotic Picking & ASRS

High-bay Shuttle ASRS for flat-pack density (40% space savings) combined with robotic arms for precise accessory picking.

Pathway C

AI Logistics

Predictive WES (Warehouse Execution System) to pre-position high-velocity SKUs at docks before trucks arrive.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot

Deploy heavy AMRs in Mielec facility receiving zones. Integrate WES software.

Year 2: Scale

Construct Shuttle ASRS for flat-packs. Expand AMRs to shipping docks.

Year 3-4: Network

Roll out to secondary plants. Implement robotic piece-picking for e-comm.

Year 5: Dark Warehouse

Full "lights-out" capability for night shift storage operations.
