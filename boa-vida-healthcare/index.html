Robotics Opportunities Portfolio & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

Boa Vida Healthcare specializes in the turnaround of rural critical access hospitals (CAHs) and skilled nursing facilities. The strongest automation levers are Telepresence Robotics to reduce patient transfers, Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics, and RPA for revenue cycle management. The dominant ROI driver is revenue retention through reduced out-migration.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A – Telepresence & Clinical Specialist Robots

Scope: Mobile telepresence carts in ERs/ICUs connecting to remote specialists.

Impact: Reduces transfers by 20-30%, retaining high-acuity billing locally.

Pathway B – Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Scope: TUG-style robots for linen, meal, and medication delivery in SNFs.

Impact: Reduces nursing burnout and workplace injury; improves staff retention.

Pathway C – Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Scope: Automated billing and claims processing software.

Impact: Accelerates cash flow and reduces administrative overhead.
