01. Executive Summary

Cecelia Health is a virtual specialty clinic optimizing chronic disease management through human expertise. The primary robotics opportunity lies in Software Robotics (RPA) and Intelligent Agents to unburden clinicians. By automating administrative tasks, the company can expand patient capacity by 30-40% without proportional headcount growth, transforming from a service provider to a tech-enabled scalable platform.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA (Ops)

Focus: Internal Operations

Automating data entry, scheduling, and billing between proprietary systems and partner EMRs.

Impact: +20% Clinician Capacity

Pathway B: AI Agents

Focus: Patient Triage

Conversational AI for FAQ handling, medication reminders, and 24/7 low-acuity support.

Impact: 24/7 Availability

Pathway C: Logistics

Focus: Device Supply Chain

Automated fulfillment for Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) devices (glucometers, cuffs).

Impact: Cost Reduction
