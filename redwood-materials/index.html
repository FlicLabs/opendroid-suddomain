EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Redwood Materials is positioned to dominate the domestic EV battery supply chain. To meet the 100 GWh annual production goal, the organization must transition from manual-heavy pilot lines to automated, data-driven manufacturing. Our strategy focuses on "Closing the Loop" with zero yield loss, utilizing Cobots for hazardous disassembly and Machine Vision for microscopic quality assurance.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS
Pathway A: Safety Cobots

Deploying force-limited Cobots for battery pack dismantling. Focus on removing human hands from high-voltage risks and handling diverse form factors.

Pathway B: Logistics AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport "Black Mass" and chemical drums. Replaces forklifts to improve WIP flow and reduce floor accidents.

Pathway C: Quality Inspection

High-speed machine vision systems integrated with packing lines to detect defects in Copper Foil and Cathode Active Materials.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP
  • Year 1: Pilot Safety Integration & Digital Twin setup.
  • Year 2: Deployment of AMR Logistics Fleet.
  • Year 3: Expansion to South Carolina Campus.
  • Year 4: Predictive Maintenance via Sensor Data.
  • Year 5: Full Autonomy (Lights Out Material Handling).
