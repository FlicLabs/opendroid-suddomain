Robotics & Automation Analysis

Executive Summary
Arkos Health operates as a risk-bearing entity and value-based care service partner. By integrating virtual care, mobile field teams, and disparate clinical data, Arkos assumes financial risk to lower the total cost of care. The primary automation opportunities lie in Cognitive Automation for risk adjustment (Pathway A), Logistics Robotics for mobile care teams (Pathway B), and In-Home Adherence Robotics (Pathway C). The dominant ROI driver is the expansion of the shared savings pool through administrative efficiency and reduced hospital admissions.
