Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics

Highest Value

AI-driven scheduling and routing automation to maximize team visits.

Potential: £80M - £94M / year

  • Predictive demand modeling
  • Dynamic re-routing
  • Reduced idle time (30% → 15%)

Pathway C: Clinical Robotics

Moderate Value

Portable diagnostic devices to reduce ER escalations and standardize care.

Potential: £3.2M - £3.3M / year

  • Standardized vital signs
  • Automated specimen collection
  • Improved remote decision support

Pathway B: Auto Fleet

Long Term

Autonomous/Semi-autonomous vans to reduce driver fatigue and labor.

Timeline: 3-10 Years

  • ADAS safety retrofits
  • Level 4 Autonomous pilot
  • 20-40% fleet labor savings

Phase 1: Months 1–6 Pathway A Pilot in high-volume markets (e.g., Denver/LA). 25-35% utilization lift expected.
Phase 2: Months 7–14 Full-network rollout of AI scheduling across 50+ markets.

Phase 4+: Years 2–5 Scale Clinical Robotics and begin Level 3-4 Autonomous vehicle pilots.
