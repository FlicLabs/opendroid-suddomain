Strategic Robotics Report

Client: Dewberry

Executive Summary

The transition to Industry 4.0 is essential for Dewberry to maintain competitiveness. The vision focuses on a "connected factory" ecosystem using cyber-physical systems for decentralized decision-making. By adopting flexible manufacturing, Dewberry can shift from rigid mass-production to agile high-mix/low-volume configurations, allowing rapid adaptation to customer specifications while maintaining efficiency.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 Pilot Phase: Digital audit, Cobot pilot cell installation, baseline data collection.
Year 2 Expansion: Rollout of Pathway A (Cobots) & Pathway C (End-of-Line). MES integration.
