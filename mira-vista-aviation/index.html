Executive Summary

Mira Vista Aviation stands as a premier operator in the heavy jet management and charter sector. To maintain competitive supremacy in the high-touch luxury aviation market, the integration of robotics into maintenance and cleaning operations is essential. This research outlines a transition from manual, labor-intensive turnaround processes to a hybrid automated workflow. By deploying Open Droids automation solutions, Mira Vista can achieve higher aircraft availability, superior hygiene standards consistent with ARGUS Platinum ratings, and significant reduction in operational overhead.
