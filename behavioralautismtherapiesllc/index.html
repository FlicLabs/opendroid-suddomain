Behavioral Autism Therapies LLC

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Behavioral Autism Therapies LLC (BAT) is positioned to transform the delivery of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) through the strategic adoption of Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) and AI-driven documentation. By shifting from a purely labor-intensive model to a tech-enabled hybrid model, BAT can address the industry's most critical pain points: staff burnout, data fidelity, and client engagement. The projected ROI is driven by a ~15-20% increase in billable capacity and significant reduction in administrative overhead.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Clinical SAR

Revenue Driver

Deploying robots (e.g., Moxie, NAO) as co-therapists to increase engagement.

  • Standardizes social protocol delivery.
  • Reduces session cancellations by ~15%.
  • Gamifies therapy for higher retention.

Pathway B: Admin AI

Cost Saver

"Software Robotics" to listen to sessions and automate SOAP notes.

  • Recovers 3-5 hrs/week per clinician.
  • Drastically improves data audit fidelity.
  • Reduces RBT burnout/turnover.

Pathway C: Telepresence

Efficiency

Mobile robots for remote BCBA supervision of in-home sessions.

  • Reduces supervisor travel by 50-80%.
  • Allows better environmental observation.
  • Multiplies supervisor capacity.
