Strategic Roadmap

  • Year 1: Digital Foundation
    Deploy RPA for Perinatal Risk Assessment (PRA) data entry and state reporting compliance.

    Pilot telepresence robots in remote partner clinics for specialist consultation and nurse training.
  • Year 3: Logistics Automation
