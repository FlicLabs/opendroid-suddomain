Illinois Restaurant Association

Strategic Automation Research & ROI Analysis

01. Executive Vision

The Illinois Restaurant Association is positioned to lead a statewide transformation in hospitality. Facing labor volatility and rising wages, the strategic imperative is to facilitate a "Hybrid Service Model." This approach integrates robotics to handle low-value repetition (running, frying, data entry), allowing human staff to focus exclusively on high-value guest interactions.
