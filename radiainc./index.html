Executive Summary

Radia Inc. is positioned to transform from a service provider to a logistics platform. By leveraging "Software Robotics" for workflow and "Tele-Robotics" for remote imaging, Radia can unlock millions in margin while solving the critical shortage of rural specialists.

Strategic Pathways
Pathway A: Intelligent Workflow (AI/RPA) Highest Value

Algorithmic orchestration of the radiologist worklist. Moves beyond FIFO (First-In-First-Out) to dynamic load balancing based on fatigue, sub-specialty, and efficiency metrics.

Impact: Adds capacity for 250k+ studies/year without new hires.

Pathway B: Tele-Robotic Imaging Strategic Lock-in

Deployment of robotic ultrasound arms and remote guidance systems in rural clinics. Allows central experts to perform scans remotely.

Impact: Secures hospital contracts and expands geographic reach.

Pathway C: Admin Automation Cost Control

Back-office bots for credentialing, billing code reconciliation, and report distribution.

Impact: Reduces SG&A costs by ~30%.
