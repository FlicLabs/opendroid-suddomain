1. Executive Summary & Profile

Paséa Hotel & Spa is a luxury oceanfront resort in Huntington Beach, CA. The property faces challenges common to coastal hospitality: high labor costs and logistical friction in vertical transport. The operational strategy must shift from manual labor intensity to automated logistics.

The strongest robotics levers are (1) Autonomous Guest Delivery for room service, (2) Commercial Floor Care Robotics, and (3) Robotic Food Runners for the pool deck. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of non-value-added walking time.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Guest Delivery (Highest Value)

  • Scope: Deployment of 3-4 Relay-style robots for room service and amenities.
  • Key Levers: Integration with elevator controls and PMS; secure compartment delivery.
  • Impact: Reduces manual delivery trips by ~70%, improving delivery times to under 10 minutes.

Pathway B: Commercial Floor Care (Efficiency)

  • Scope: Autonomous vacuum sweepers for 8 floors of corridors and scrubbers for the lobby.
  • Benefits: Consistent cleanliness; housekeeping staff reallocated to room detailing.

Pathway C: Robotic Food Runners (Revenue)

  • Scope: Heavy payload shelving robots for Tanners Restaurant and the pool deck.
  • Benefits: Servers remain in sections; 20% increase in table turnover during peak hours.
