Executive Summary

Thorne HealthTech is a vertically integrated wellness company operating at the intersection of manufacturing and personalized health data. With a massive 272,000 sq ft facility in Summerville, SC, Thorne's primary friction points are labor-intensive kitting and biological sample turnaround times.

The dominant ROI drivers are (1) Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for intralogistics, (2) High-speed robotic pick-and-pack for personalized subscriptions, and (3) Lab automation. These levers can generate over £20M in 5-year net value.
