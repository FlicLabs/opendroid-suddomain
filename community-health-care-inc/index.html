Executive Summary

Community Health Care Inc. (CHC) is positioned to transform its FQHC operational model through targeted automation. By deploying centralized pharmacy robotics, administrative RPA, and clinic logistics robots, CHC can shift resources from manual tasks to direct patient care.

Primary Driver: Automated pharmacy dispensing is the dominant ROI lever, projecting millions in recovered 340B revenue and a 300% increase in script capacity.

HIGHEST VALUE

Pathway A: Pharmacy Automation

Implementation of central fill robotics to handle high-volume maintenance medications.

  • Impact: 300% dispensing capacity increase.
  • Revenue: £2.5M - £4M annual incremental revenue.
  • Safety: Near-zero dispensing errors.
QUICK WIN

Pathway B: Administrative RPA

Software robotics to automate insurance verification and referral management.

  • Impact: Equivalent to 15-20 FTEs in productivity.
  • Benefit: Drastic reduction in claim denials.
  • Cost: Low CapEx, high immediate return.
