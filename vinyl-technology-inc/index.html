Executive Summary

Vinyl Technology Inc. is poised to transition from a traditional job-shop model to a digitized, flexible manufacturing entity. By integrating Collaborative Robotics (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), the company can resolve labor bottlenecks in RF welding and material transport. This strategy targets "Flexible Hybrid Automation," preserving the craftsmanship required for aerospace/defense contracts while significantly boosting throughput and traceability.

STRATEGY

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: RF Heat Sealing Tending.
Impact: Replaces manual holding of fabric during dwell times. Improves seam consistency and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.
Impact: Automates transport from cutting to sewing lines. Creates a "Bus Stop" delivery system, keeping skilled labor at their stations.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Palletizing & QA.
Impact: Vision systems check weld integrity for ISO compliance. Automated palletizing increases shipping throughput.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase
    Site survey, safety assessment, and deployment of single Cobot cell for RF Sealing.
  • Year 2-3: Scaled Integration
    Expansion to 3 lines, AMR fleet integration, and central OEE dashboard deployment.
  • Year 4-5: AI & Optimization
    Predictive maintenance, AI-driven scheduling, and Digital Twin simulation.
