01 / Executive Vision

GRW stands at the precipice of a major Industry 4.0 evolution. The strategic objective is to transition from static, isolated manufacturing cells to a fluid, data-driven ecosystem. By integrating Cleanroom Cobots, AMRs, and Automated Inspection, GRW can reduce micro-contamination risks while increasing throughput flexibility for aerospace and medical clients.

02 / Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Deployment of force-sensitive Cobots for micro-assembly of bearing cages, reducing human-induced contamination.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous mobile units handling tray transport between Heat Treat and Grinding to eliminate WIP stagnation.

Pathway C: Auto-Inspection

High-speed vision systems for 100% surface scanning and automated palletizing of finished goods.
