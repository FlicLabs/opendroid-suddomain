01. Executive Vision

Plastikon Industries is positioned to transition from isolated automation islands to a connected manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating Co-bots and data synchronization, the company can address high-mix medical demands and high-volume automotive requirements simultaneously, reducing cycle times and ensuring ISO 13485 compliance.

02. Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A

Cobots & Assembly

Direct operator collaboration for insert molding and delicate medical part extraction. Focus on eliminating ergonomic strain and ensuring thermal consistency.

PATHWAY B

AMR Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving raw resin and finished goods. Focus on removing forklifts from high-traffic aisles to improve safety and WIP flow.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Quality

Automated visual inspection and palletizing. Focus on 40% throughput increase and zero-defect guarantee for automotive clients.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase - 2 Cobot cells in Hayward HQ.
  • Year 2: Standardization - Scale to 10 units & AMR Fleet integration.
  • Year 3: Expansion - Roll out to Asian facilities & Vision QC.
  • Year 4: Optimization - Predictive maintenance & Lights-out trials.
  • Year 5: AI Integration - Real-time process parameter adjustment.
