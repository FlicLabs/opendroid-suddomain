UNIVERSITY OF BEER

Strategic Automation & Robotics Report

01. Executive Summary

The strategic integration of robotics at University of Beer targets the specific operational bottlenecks of a high-volume gastropub: weight logistics and table turnover. By deploying "Cobots" to handle the physical burden of beer flights and heavy food trays, we protect staff health while accelerating service speed. This is not a replacement strategy, but an augmentation strategy to enhance the guest experience during peak sports seasons.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving

  • Runner Bots: Transporting kegs & plates.
  • Busser Bots: Rapid table clearing.
  • Impact: 40% less staff walking.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

  • Auto-Fryers: Wings & Fries consistency.
  • Safety: Reduces burn risks.
  • Impact: Standardized cook times.

Pathway C: AI Intelligence

  • Smart Ordering: Beer pairing suggestions.
  • Inventory: Real-time keg tracking.
