Executive Vision

DWFritz is positioned to redefine "Engineer-to-Order" efficiency by integrating modular robotics into its own build processes. The strategic shift moves from static bench assembly to dynamic, autonomous cells. This transition targets a 25% reduction in lead times while maintaining the micron-level precision required for consumer electronics and EV markets.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Precision Assembly

Deployment of force-sensing cobots for torque-critical fastening on gantries. Ensures 100% digital traceability and reduces repetitive strain.

Pathway B: AMRs

Intralogistics Flow

Autonomous Mobile Robots for JIT delivery of optics and sensors from warehouse to build cells, reducing technician travel time.

Pathway C: Inspection

Auto-QC Buyoff

Automated vision-based "burn-in" and calibration testing to standardize final machine verification and throughput.
