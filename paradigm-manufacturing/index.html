Executive Vision

Paradigm Manufacturing is positioned to pivot from static production lines to dynamic, flexible manufacturing cells. By integrating Cobots and AMRs, we decouple labor dependencies from capacity.

Strategic Goal: Achieve "High-Mix, Low-Volume" flexibility while maintaining mass-production cost efficiency.

Financial Impact: Shift CAPEX to modular investments ($60k-$120k/cell) to protect cash flow while aggressively targeting operational variance reduction.

Production Efficiency

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Focus: Safety & Precision.

Deployment of force-limited 6-axis arms for machine tending and assembly. Removes ergonomic risks and stabilizes cycle times.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.

Autonomous Material Transport to eliminate forklift traffic. Ensures Just-In-Time line-side delivery and reduces inventory bloat.

PATHWAY C: EOL AUTOMATION

Focus: Throughput & Yield.

Automated Palletizing combined with vision inspection. Targets the shipping bottleneck to increase final throughput by 25%.
