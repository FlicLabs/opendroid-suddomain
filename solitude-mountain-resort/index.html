Executive Summary

Solitude Mountain Resort faces unique operational challenges defined by limited geographic footprint, high seasonal labor dependency, and critical safety requirements. The strongest robotics levers are (1) Autonomous Snow Operations, (2) F&B Service Automation, and (3) AI-Driven Logistics. The dominant ROI driver is the extension of grooming windows and reduction of onsite labor requirements.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Snow Operations

Deployment of autonomous snow groomers and drone-based avalanche mitigation. Shifts grooming from human-intensive night shifts to supervised autonomous fleet operations.

Pathway B: Food & Beverage Automation

Implementation of robotic food preparation units at Moonbeam and Roundhouse lodges combined with smart lockers to handle peak lunch rushes.

Pathway C: Logistics & Parking AI

AI-driven camera systems for real-time parking inventory management integrated with autonomous low-speed village shuttles.
