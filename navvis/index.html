Navvis: Healthcare Robotics & Automation Analysis

Navvis focuses on population health and value-based care. The automation strategy shifts from hardware-heavy robotics to "Digital Workforce" optimization. By deploying Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and AI-driven care orchestration, Navvis can decouple operational costs from patient volume growth. The primary ROI is driven by a 30-40% reduction in administrative burden, enabling tens of millions in scalable growth and accelerated shared savings.

Strategic Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation

Deploy Pathway A (RPA) to automate top 5 manual workflows. Validate data accuracy and capture immediate FTE savings.

Year 2-3: Scale & Engage

Launch Pathway B (Virtual Agents) for low-risk populations. Integrate AI outputs directly into Coreo work queues.

Year 3-5: Hardware Integration

Full deployment of Pathway C (RPM Hardware) for high-risk cohorts. Complete automation of the alert-to-intervention loop.
