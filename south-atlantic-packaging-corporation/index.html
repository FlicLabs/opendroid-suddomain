South Atlantic Packaging is poised to transition from labor-heavy copacking to a Flexible Automated Manufacturing ecosystem. By leveraging Agile Co-packing methodologies, the organization can decouple throughput from labor volatility, ensuring consistent fulfillment during seasonal peaks.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Collaborative arms for kitting and blister loading. Focus on safety and reducing repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for WIP flow. Eliminates manual pallet jack movement and "dead walking time."

Pathway C: Palletizing

End-of-line automated stacking with integrated vision inspection. Ensures 100% retailer compliance.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 Pilot Phase: Single-line Palletizing pilot in Winston-Salem facility.
Year 2 Expansion: Roll out to remaining lines; Begin Cobot Kitting pilot.
Year 3 Integration: Deploy AMRs for warehouse-to-line transport; Central data dashboard.
Year 4 Optimization: AI-driven scheduling and predictive maintenance.
Year 5 Scale: Full ecosystem standardization across all geographic locations.
