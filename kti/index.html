Executive Vision

This research identifies a critical opportunity for KTI to transition from manual drayage support to a "Smart Logistics Hub" model. By integrating AMRs for cross-docking and AI for yard management, KTI can synchronize warehouse velocity with transportation schedules, effectively eliminating 40% of container dwell time.

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport. Deploying pallet-moving robots to handle long-haul transport within the warehouse. This separates human value (loading/decision making) from low-value travel time.

Pathway B: ASRS

High Density. Implementing vertical lift modules for distribution clients. Increases storage density by 400% and picking accuracy to 99.9%.

Pathway C: AI Logic

Predictive Flow. Computer vision integration at bay doors to predict unload times and automate drayage scheduling, reducing demurrage.
