Executive Summary

Our vision for PSS Industrial Group is the creation of The Autonomous Industrial Hub. This strategy moves beyond generic e-commerce models to address the specific challenges of heavy PVF (Pipe, Valve, Fittings) handling. By integrating high-payload AMRs and ASRS, PSS can achieve near-zero error rates in safety-critical environments while reducing operational overhead by nearly 35%.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Heavy AMRs

Deployment of 1,500kg payload autonomous carriers to replace manual forklifts for long-haul internal transport. Focus on safety and reducing "deadhead" travel for skilled operators.

Pathway B: MRO ASRS

Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for high-density storage of small industrial parts (gaskets, fasteners). Reduces footprint by 85% and ensures picking accuracy.
