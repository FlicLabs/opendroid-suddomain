Executive Summary

Lift Brands (Snap Fitness, Fitness On Demand) faces the classic optimization problem of the 24/7 franchise model: balancing low labor costs with high facility standards. The robotics opportunity lies in shifting from human-dependent operational hygiene to autonomous standardization. By deploying robotic floor scrubbers and AI-driven safety monitoring, the network can unlock over £14M in annual systemic savings while reducing liability risks in unstaffed operating hours.
