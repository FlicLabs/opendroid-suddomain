ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Mobile units for vacuuming, wiping, and sanitizing high-touch points during turnaround windows.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection Robots

Autonomous UV-C light technology to neutralize pathogens on seating, tables, and bins.

Pathway C

Exterior Washing Systems

Automated gantry or crawler systems for consistent airframe cleaning and waxing.

Pathway D

AI Inspection Bots

Drones and crawlers with LiDAR for micro-damage detection and predictive maintenance.
