03. Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: AMR Internal Transport

Goal: Automate Point-to-Point travel between Receiving, QA, and Kitting.
Impact: Eliminates manual cart pushing, increasing value-added labor hours by 25%.

PATHWAY B: Vertical ASRS

Goal: High-density storage for small parts (fasteners, electronics).
Impact: Reduces storage footprint by 85% and secures inventory chain-of-custody.
