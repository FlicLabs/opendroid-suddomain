Robotics & Integration

Executive Strategic Vision

Komar Industries is positioned to lead the Heavy Machinery sector by transitioning from traditional fabrication to an Industry 4.0 enabled ecosystem. By integrating high-torque precision automation into the manufacturing of augers and shredders, Komar can reduce custom order lead times while enhancing workforce safety.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A Collaborative Arc Welding

Deployment of heavy-duty Cobots (Fanuc CRX/UR e-Series) for consistent bead quality on auger sub-assemblies and machine tending for drive shaft CNC lathes.

Pathway B AMR Material Transport

Utilization of high-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to move steel plates between laser cutting and welding bays, reducing forklift dependency.

Pathway C Spare Parts Logistics

Automated kitting and vision inspection for aftermarket cutter teeth and hydraulic seals to ensure 100% outgoing quality yield.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Digital Twin modeling & Pilot Welding Cell deployment.
  • Year 2: AMR integration for material feeding.
  • Year 3: Scaling to primary production lines.
  • Year 5: Full "Lights Out" capability for component machining.
