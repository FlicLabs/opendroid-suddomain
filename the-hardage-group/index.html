Executive Summary

Operational Pivot

The Hardage Group faces a critical junction in hospitality management. Increasing labor costs in housekeeping and dining require a shift toward automation. By implementing robotics, the group shifts from manual dependency to asset-light, tech-enabled operations.

Strategic Value

The dominant ROI driver is labor reallocation. Shifting repetitive tasks (floor care, delivery) to machines allows human staff to focus on high-touch guest interactions, protecting Net Operating Income (NOI) and increasing asset valuation.

Pathway A

Autonomous Cleaning

Deployment of commercial AMRs for vacuuming hallways and lobbies during night shifts.

20% Labor Reduction

Status: High Priority / Immediate

Pathway B

Service Delivery

Fleet of dispatchable robots for room service, linens, and amenities delivery.

15% Wait Time Drop

Status: Strategic / Brand Builder

Pathway C

Dining Automation

Robotic bussing and runner supports for high-volume breakfast and event dining.

1.5x Table Turnover

Status: Efficiency Enhancer
