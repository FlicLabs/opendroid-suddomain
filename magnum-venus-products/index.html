Executive Summary

Magnum Venus Products is positioned to transition from traditional discrete manufacturing to a connected Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport into the Knoxville facilities, MVP can resolve skilled labor shortages and significantly increase throughput for pumping systems and filament winders. This research outlines a roadmap to flexible, data-driven manufacturing.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of IP67-rated collaborative robots to tend CNC machines and assist in torque-critical valve assembly. Enables "lights-out" component machining.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Material Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy pump casings and raw stock between fabrication, coating, and assembly lines, reducing human transit time.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Focus: Quality & Palletizing

Vision systems for seal integrity inspection and vacuum-gripper arms for boxing spare parts, ensuring zero-defect shipping and reduced ergonomic strain.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 2

Year 3

Year 5
