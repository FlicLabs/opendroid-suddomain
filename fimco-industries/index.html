Fimco Industries

Report ID: OD-2023-FIM

Executive Summary

Strategic transition from legacy discrete manufacturing to an Industry 4.0 Flexible Manufacturing System. The focus is on integrating high-mix automation to handle seasonal demand spikes for sprayers and spreaders, reducing manual labor dependency while increasing throughput quality.

$60k+ Entry CAPEX
30% Target OEE Lift
14 Mo ROI Period
100% Yield Quality

5-Year Cumulative Savings

Throughput: Manual vs. Cobot

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Welding & Assembly

Deployment of 20kg payload Cobots for machine tending (tube bending) and precision nozzle assembly. Eliminates repetitive strain and ensures consistent torque specs.

PATHWAY B

AMR Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to bridge Rotational Molding and Assembly lines. Replaces forklift traffic, improving safety and enabling JIT tank delivery.

PATHWAY C

Automated Palletizing

End-of-Line robotic cells with integrated vision systems. Handles packing of spot sprayers and skid units while performing final visual QA checks.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 Pilot Phase: EOL Palletizing on High-Volume Spot Sprayer Line
  • Year 2 Mobility: Deploy AMR Fleet for Tank Transport
  • Year 3 Heavy Assembly: Frame Welding & Boom Assembly Cobots
  • Year 5 Full Autonomy: Centralized Data Dashboard & Predictive Maintenance
