Executive Vision

Polo Custom Products is positioned to transition from a high-mix manual job shop to a Flexible Hybrid Smart Factory. By integrating collaborative robotics into RF welding and thermoforming, and utilizing AMRs for material transport, the organization can mitigate labor shortages while ensuring the zero-defect quality required by medical and defense clients.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Target: RF Welding & Thermoforming.

Machine tending automation to remove operators from hazardous pinch points and ensure consistent cycle times.

Pathway B: AMRs

Target: Material Transport.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to move fabric rolls and WIP between cutting, sewing, and sealing stations.

Pathway C: QC Vision

Target: End-of-Line.

Automated visual inspection and palletizing to ensure 100% yield compliance for medical/defense contracts.
