Executive Summary

Healthy Partners Inc. is poised to transition from a labor-intensive administrative model to a technology-enabled care platform. By integrating Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for back-office operations and deploying physical service robotics within high-volume clinics, the organization can alleviate bottlenecks in referral processing and clinical logistics. The primary ROI driver is the optimization of provider time and administrative overhead, projected to yield significant margin expansion within 18 months.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Administrative Software Robotics

Scope: Deployment of RPA bots for referral authorization and claims coding.
Impact: Reduction in referral processing time from 3 days to instant approval for 60% of cases. Annual savings of £3M.

Pathway B: Clinic Logistics Robots

Scope: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for transporting samples and supplies.
Impact: Reduces clinical staff walking time by 4 miles/day. Enhances infection control.

Pathway C: Remote Medication Robotics

Scope: Table-top dispensing robots for high-risk chronic patients.
Impact: 15% reduction in hospital readmissions for targeted cohorts.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 2: Physical Deployment Roll out Service Robots in top 5 regional clinics.
