Keystone Health

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Keystone Health serves over 60,000 patients across Franklin County, PA. As a Federally Qualified Health Center (FQHC) with a unique statewide mandate for agricultural worker health, Keystone requires high-efficiency solutions to manage low-margin reimbursement models.

Key Insight: The dominant ROI driver is Automated Pharmacy Fulfillment, capable of generating millions in incremental margin to subsidize critical community outreach programs.
Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Pharmacy Automation

Focus: High-speed robotic dispensing & pouch packaging.

Impact: Increases script volume by 30-40%. Captures significant 340B revenue.

Highest ROI

Pathway B: Telepresence

Focus: Remote diagnostic robots for farmworker camps.

Impact: Extends physician reach to rural sites without travel time.

Strategic Value

Pathway C: Facility AMRs

Focus: Lab transport & UV-C disinfection robots.

Impact: Reclaims 15-20% of clinical staff time from logistics.

Operational Efficiency
