Gramercy Surgery Center Inc.

Robotics Opportunities & ROI Analysis Portfolio

Executive Summary

Gramercy Surgery Center, a leading multi-specialty ASC in NYC, is positioned to capture high-margin revenue by integrating robotics. The primary opportunity lies in Surgical Robotics to shift complex orthopedic cases (Total Joints) to the outpatient setting. Secondary opportunities include logistics automation for sterile processing and UVC disinfection for safety.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Surgical Robotics

Focus: Orthopedics & Soft Tissue

Deploying Mako/ROSA or Da Vinci systems to enable complex outpatient procedures.

Impact: £1.5M - £3M Incremental Revenue

Pathway B: SPD Logistics

Focus: Autonomous Transport

AMRs to move sterile trays between SPD and ORs, reducing manual labor and fatigue.

Impact: Saves 1.0 - 1.5 FTE labor

Pathway C: UVC Disinfection

Focus: Turnover & Safety

Robotic terminal cleaning to reduce infection risk and market safety standards.

Impact: Reduced Surgical Site Infections
