Andwell Health Partners

Healthcare Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Andwell Health Partners faces the unique challenge of delivering home health and hospice care across Maine's vast, rural geography. Unlike urban models, the primary efficiency killer is travel time. This analysis identifies Logistics AI and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) as the most critical levers. By automating routes and documentation, Andwell can reclaim thousands of clinical hours annually, offsetting the regional nursing shortage and ensuring financial sustainability.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
Pathway A • High Value
Rural Logistics AI

Scope: "Software robotics" for dynamic route optimization across rural counties.

Impact: Reduces windshield time by 20-30%. Increases visit capacity by ~0.5 visits/day per clinician.

ROI Driver: Maximizing billable time vs. drive time.

Pathway B • Retention
Admin RPA Bots

Scope: Automating OASIS intake forms and coding.

Impact: Reduces after-hours charting ("pajama time"). Improves staff retention.

ROI Driver: Lower turnover costs and faster billing cycles.

Pathway C • Innovation
Medication Robotics

Scope: In-home dispensing robots for palliative/chronic care.

Impact: 99% adherence; reduces emergency visits.

ROI Driver: Value-based care incentives and readmission avoidance.
