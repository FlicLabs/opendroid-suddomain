Executive Vision

Flexible Cleanroom Manufacturing

To solidify leadership in the semiconductor and biotech supply chains, Evans Components Inc. must transition from manual precision to automated reliability. The strategic goal is to implement Industry 4.0 standards to reduce contamination risks and ensure 100% traceability of high-purity valves and manifolds.

Pathway A

Cobots

Application: Machine Tending & Orbital Welding.

Focus on safety and precision to reduce operator fatigue and ensure perfect weld alignment.

Pathway B

AMRs

Application: Material Transport.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle stainless steel stock and finished goods, minimizing human particulate contamination.

Pathway C

Automated QC

Application: Vision Inspection.

Automated optical inspection for surface finish and weld penetration to maximize yield and throughput.
