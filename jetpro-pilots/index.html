Financial Model & Opportunity Pathways

The transition to robotics offers immediate operational cost reductions. By reallocating manual labor to higher-value tasks and utilizing automated systems for repetitive cleaning, JetPro Pilots can achieve significant savings.

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

  • Auto-vacuuming & disinfection
  • Reduces turnaround by 60%
  • Focus: Labor Efficiency

Pathway B: UV-C Sanitization

  • Deep clean overnight
  • 99.9% pathogen reduction
  • Focus: Health Standards

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

  • High-pressure, low-water
  • 60% Water conservation
  • Focus: Surface Protection

Pathway D: AI Inspection

  • Fuselage micro-crack detection
  • Predictive maintenance data
  • Focus: Safety
