Project: Resilient Health

Executive Summary

Resilient Health acts as a critical provider of community-based behavioral health and chronic care management. The integration of robotics and automation offers a transformative pathway to scale impact without proportional increases in headcount. The primary value drivers are AI-driven dynamic caseload management, telepresence robotics, and automated medication adherence systems.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics AI

Scope: Dynamic acuity-based routing and safety monitoring.

Impact: Increase daily visits from 4.5 to 6 (+33%). Reduces unbillable travel time and improves staff safety scores.

Pathway B: Telepresence

Scope: Remote support robots for high-risk/rural patients.

Impact: Allows senior clinician supervision of multiple sites. Reduces travel to remote locations for routine checks.

Pathway C: Med Automation

Scope: In-home smart dispensing units.

Impact: Drastic reduction in medication errors and readmissions. Real-time adherence data for payers.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation

Deploy AI scheduling app to all field staff. Establish central dispatch command. Initiate pilot of 50 telepresence units in rural zones.

Year 2: Scale

Expand telepresence to 20% of census. Integrate smart medication dispensers for high-risk chronic patients. Link data to EHR.

Year 3: Optimization

Full fleet automation and predictive health analytics. Move to risk-based contracts leveraging superior data capabilities.
