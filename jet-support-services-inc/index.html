JET SUPPORT SERVICES INC.

Strategic Robotics Implementation Report

Prepared by Open Droids Consultancy Division

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Jet Support Services Inc. stands at the precipice of an operational revolution. By integrating robotics and automation into cleaning and maintenance workflows, the airline can achieve unprecedented levels of efficiency, safety, and hygiene. This report outlines a strategic transition from labor-intensive manual procedures to a sophisticated, automated ecosystem. The core vision is to deploy a fleet of autonomous agents that operate in parallel with ground staff, reducing turnaround times, minimizing chemical usage, and ensuring a sterility standard that reassures passengers and crew alike.

ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

Jet Support Services Inc. operates a diverse fleet of narrow-body and wide-body aircraft, servicing key domestic hubs and international gateways. The current operational model relies heavily on shift-based manual labor for cabin cleaning, lavatory servicing, and exterior detailing. While effective, this model faces challenges regarding labor shortages, variable cleaning quality, and exposure to hazardous chemicals. The high-density hub operations necessitate rapid aircraft turnaround, where every minute of delay impacts profitability. Transitioning to an automated model will allow Jet Support to scale operations without linearly scaling labor costs.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots. Navigates aisles for vacuuming and misting, cutting cleaning time by 50%.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection Robots. Neutralizes pathogens autonomously, providing verifiable sterility logs.

Pathway C

Exterior Aircraft Washing. High-pressure, eco-friendly systems reducing drag and improving fuel efficiency.

Pathway D

AI-Powered Inspection Bots. Visual crawlers detecting fuselage micro-fractures for predictive maintenance.
