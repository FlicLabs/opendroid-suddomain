Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deploy 6-axis cobots with force feedback for handling fragile optical components. Improves repeatability in screwdriving and lens alignment while maintaining safe shared workspaces with technicians.

B. Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: Material Transport & WIP Flow

Replace manual forklifts for sub-assembly movement. Integrates with MES for JIT line-side replenishment, reducing technician walking time by ~40% and minimizing high-value component damage.

C. Automated End-of-Line

Focus: Palletizing & Quality Inspection

Gantry robots for heavy system crating combined with Machine Vision for final defect detection. Ensures shipping integrity and automates serial verification.
