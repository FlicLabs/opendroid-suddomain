One Senior Care

Robotics & Automation Opportunity Portfolio

Executive Summary

One Senior Care (One Homecare Solutions / CenterWell) operates on a value-based, capitated model integrating home health, infusion, and DME. The organization assumes full financial risk for patient episodes, making operational efficiency and readmission reduction paramount.

Key Insight: The dominant ROI drivers are Logistics Automation (DME supply chain) and Process Automation (Administrative efficiency). These levers directly expand margin within fixed capitated payments.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics AI

Automated Warehousing & Delivery

Implementation of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) in DME hubs and AI-driven fleet routing.

Impact: 20-30% Labor Savings Payback: ~18 Months

Pathway B: RPA

Admin & Claims Automation

Software robotics to handle prior authorizations, claims processing, and data entry.

Impact: 60-80% Process Speed Payback: < 9 Months

Pathway C: Clinical

Medication Adherence

Smart in-home dispensing robots for high-risk polypharmacy and infusion patients.

Impact: Reduced Readmissions Value: Star Rating Improvement

Financial Model & ROI (Pathway A)

Focusing on the high-volume DME logistics network:

  • Baseline: 50k+ orders per hub annually; high manual labor and fuel costs.
  • Investment: $2-3M CapEx per major hub for AMRs and Routing Software.
  • Returns: $1.5M - $2.5M annual savings per hub via reduced overtime, fuel efficiency, and asset loss prevention.
  • Strategic Gain: Faster equipment delivery reduces hospital length-of-stay, saving partner payers significant "hotel" costs.
