Executive Summary

Infinitum is positioned to revolutionize the electric motor sector with its air-core technology. To meet scaling demands for HVAC and EV sectors, the manufacturing process must transition from manual batching to a connected, automated ecosystem. This strategy outlines the deployment of Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS) to enhance throughput while protecting delicate PCB stators.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Stator Assembly

Goal: Eliminate manual handling of sensitive PCBs.

Utilization of force-limited 6-axis cobots with vacuum grippers allows for precision stacking of stator layers. This reduces oil contamination and misalignment errors, directly improving motor efficiency ratings.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Goal: Optimize WIP (Work-In-Progress) flow.

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for line-side replenishment creates a dynamic factory floor. Materials move autonomously between etching and housing stations, reducing technician travel time by 40%.

Pathway C: Automated Inspection

Goal: Zero-defect shipping.

End-of-line vision systems integrated with palletizing robots ensure 100% verification of VFD port alignment and housing integrity before the product is crated.
