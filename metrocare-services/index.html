Metrocare Services is the largest provider of community-based mental health and developmental disability services in Dallas County, Texas, serving over 55,000 individuals annually. The strongest robotics and automation levers are administrative automation, AI-driven triage, and logistics robotics to reduce administrative burden and improve staff efficiency.
