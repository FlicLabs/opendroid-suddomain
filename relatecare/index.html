Executive Summary

RelateCare operates at the intersection of clinical expertise and patient access logistics. The primary opportunity lies not in physical hardware, but in "Software Robotics" (RPA) and Conversational AI to alleviate bottlenecks in scheduling, referrals, and triage. Implementing these automation levers can shift the model from linear labor scaling to high-margin asset leverage, generating over $3M in annual incremental value.
