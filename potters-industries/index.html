Executive Vision

Potters Industries is positioned to transition from continuous process monitoring to active robotic intervention. By implementing flexible automation in material handling and packaging, the organization will reduce exposure to hazardous environments and stabilize production throughput.

Robotics Opportunities

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Lab Sampling

Use IP67 Cobots for hot glass bead sampling and sieve analysis to ensure safety and data integrity.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Deploy Autonomous Mobile Robots for "fork-free" transport of raw silica and finished super-sacks.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Palletizing

Automated vacuum gripping systems for heavy bag stacking and simultaneous visual quality inspection.
