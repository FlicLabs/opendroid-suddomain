Executive Vision

NovaLash stands at the precipice of a manufacturing evolution. By integrating collaborative robotics into the adhesive filling and kitting lines, the company can transition from a labor-intensive model to a precision-first "Smart Factory." This strategic shift prioritizes brand integrity through consistency, ensuring every bottle of adhesive and every lash tray meets the exacting standards of professional stylists worldwide.

Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Deployment of 6-axis arms for bottle capping and torque verification. Eliminates repetitive strain injuries and ensures airtight seals.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for material transport. Connects the chemical mixing room to filling lines without human manual handling.

Pathway C: Automated QC

Vision systems for label alignment and fill-level detection, ensuring zero defect leakage to customers.

Financial Impact

  • CAPEX: $60k - $120k per fully integrated cell.
  • OPEX Savings: Reduction in scrap materials by 15% and labor reallocation.
  • Strategy: Phased implementation starting with high-risk/high-repetition zones.
