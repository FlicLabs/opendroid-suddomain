Executive Summary

Docs Dermatology is a multi-specialty practice facing high-volume workflows. The strongest robotics levers are AI-driven administrative automation, robotic-assisted surgery, and automated skin imaging. The dominant ROI driver is increased physician throughput and reduced administrative burden, enabling higher patient volume and improved diagnostic accuracy.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A – Administrative Automation & Triage (Highest Value) AI scribes, scheduling, and triage. Impact: 15–25% throughput increase, £3.6M incremental revenue.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot AI Scribe and Scheduling Automation (1–2 locations). Scale to all locations within 6 months.

Years 2–3: Deploy automated skin imaging devices to all exam rooms.

Years 3–4: Pilot robotic-assisted surgery in one surgical suite.

Strategic Positioning

Executing this roadmap positions Docs Dermatology as a digitally enabled, precision dermatology practice, enhancing throughput, diagnostic accuracy, and patient satisfaction. This creates a competitive edge in a crowded market and supports value-based care initiatives.
