Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House Serving

Focus: Autonomous delivery of food to tables to increase table capacity by 15% during peak hours. Staff shifts to pre-bussing and guest engagement.

Pathway B: Back-of-House Frying/Grilling

Focus: Robotic arms for fry stations ensuring 100% consistency on burgers and fries. Reduces food waste by 20% and safety incidents.

Pathway C: AI Kiosks & Voice Agents

Focus: AI-driven upselling at drive-thrus and kiosks. NLP handles complex customization (e.g., "The Fieldworker") with near-zero errors.
