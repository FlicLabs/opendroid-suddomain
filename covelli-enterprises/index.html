Strategic Robotics Implementation for Covelli Enterprises
Executive Vision

The integration of robotics within the fast-casual sector is no longer theoretical; it is an operational imperative. For Covelli Enterprises, the strategic deployment of automation offers a dual-value proposition: mitigating labor volatility while elevating service consistency. The objective is to augment human staff, allowing them to focus on high-touch interactions while robotics manage repetitive, high-volume tasks. This roadmap projects a 15-25% reduction in FOH labor costs and a payback period of < 14 months.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots
Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for food delivery and bussing reduce walk-time for staff. This allows servers to manage 20% more tables during peak hours, directly increasing covers per hour without adding headcount.

Pathway B: BOH Automated Frying
Focus: Consistency & Safety

Automated frying units ensure precise cook times and batch consistency. Eliminates oil-handling hazards and guarantees product uniformity across all franchise locations, protecting brand integrity.

Pathway C: AI Kiosks & Voice
Focus: Upselling & Order Accuracy

AI-driven drive-thru agents and kiosks remove fatigue and human error. Algorithmic upselling increases average check size by 10-15% while ensuring 100% order accuracy during rush periods.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1

Pilot & Validation

Deploy AI Kiosks (Pathway C) in 3-5 locations. Launch FOH Serving Robot pilot in one high-volume flagship store to validate integration.

Year 2

Standardization & BOH Testing

Scale Pathway A (FOH Robots) to top 20% of revenue sites. Initiate Pathway B (Automated Frying) pilot in central kitchen or flagship.

Year 3

Aggressive Expansion

Portfolio-wide rollout of Pathway C (Voice/Kiosks). Gradual expansion of Pathway A to mid-tier locations. Target 40% labor hour automation support.

Year 4-5

Optimization & Full Ecosystem

Full integration of BOH automation (Pathway B). Data sync across POS/Inventory. Evaluation of next-gen hardware refresh and autonomous late-night models.
