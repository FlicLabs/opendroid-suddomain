Executive Summary

The Edgewater Madison operates in a high-friction environment where staff must navigate two distinct towers and extensive event spaces. By deploying automation, the property can shift the labor model from "logistics-heavy" to "guest-centric." The primary opportunities lie in autonomous guest room delivery and large-scale floor care, yielding direct margin improvements while enhancing the modern luxury aesthetic.
