Healthcare Robotics Portfolio

Strategic analysis for Helio Health Inc. focusing on high-impact automation in behavioral health, substance use recovery, and facility logistics. The proposed portfolio targets a net annual benefit of ~£160k with a payback period of under 12 months.

PATHWAY A

Medication Automation

Deployment of robotic dispensing cabinets and packaging automation to secure MAT (Medication Assisted Treatment) and psychiatric pharmaceuticals.

  • Impact: 40-60% reduction in med-pass time.
  • Safety: Biometric chain of custody.
  • ROI: ~£200k/year in labor value reallocation.
PATHWAY B

Facility & Logistics AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for floor scrubbing, UV disinfection, and linen transport within high-density residential centers.

  • Impact: Consistent hygiene in 24/7 facilities.
  • Efficiency: Reduces non-clinical staff movement.
  • Status: Low risk, immediate deployment.
PATHWAY C

Telepresence Network

Remote specialist access via robotic endpoints for safety rounds and psychiatric evaluations across distributed clinics.

  • Impact: Increases billable encounters.
  • Access: Specialist reach to rural clinics.
  • Integration: Seamless EHR connectivity.
