Executive Summary

Camco Manufacturing is positioned to transition from legacy mass production to a flexible, smart manufacturing model. By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), Camco can address labor shortages, improve safety in chemical handling, and increase throughput in high-mix assembly lines. This strategy ensures dominance in the RV aftermarket through superior operational agility.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending Cobots

Focus: Injection Molding & CNC Safety.
Deploying IP67-rated Cobots to tend molding machines removes operators from high-heat environments and reduces cycle time variance by 15%.

Pathway B: AMRs for Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency.
Autonomous Mobile Robots transport bins of molded parts to assembly, allowing staff to focus on quality assurance rather than pushing carts.

Pathway C: Automated Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield.
Heavy-duty arms for stacking chemical fluids and leveling blocks. Reduces ergonomic strain and increases end-of-line throughput by 25%.
