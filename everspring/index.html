Executive Summary

Everspring operates as a hybrid technology-enabled healthcare provider. The primary friction points are manual medication dispensing and administrative overhead. The highest ROI opportunity is Pathway A: Centralized Robotic Pharmacy Fulfillment, which shifts the cost curve of distribution, followed by Pathway B (RPA) for administrative scalability.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Pharmacy Robotics

High-throughput pill counting and verification. Highest ROI

Pathway B: Admin RPA

Software robotics for billing and scheduling. Quick Win

Pathway C: Lab Automation

Pre-analytic processing speed. Quality Focus
