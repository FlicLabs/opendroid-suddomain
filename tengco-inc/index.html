Executive Summary

Tengco Inc. operates at the critical intersection of global sourcing and custom distribution. The high-level vision for introducing robotics focuses on Heavy-Payload Automation and High-Density Retrieval. By transitioning from manual heavy-lifting to automated logistics, Tengco can mitigate ergonomic risks associated with fasteners while increasing accuracy for complex bills of materials.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

AMRs for Transport

Replacing manual forklifts with heavy-duty AMRs (1000kg+). Reduces travel time by 50% and improves safety in heavy-pallet zones.

PATHWAY B

Piece Picking & ASRS

Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for high-density fastener storage. Increases pick rates from 50 to 250+ lines per hour.

PATHWAY C

AI Logistics

Predictive slotting based on international lead times. AI positions inventory dynamically before pick shifts begin.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Foundation. Deploy WCS, 1 VLM, and 2 Heavy-Duty AMRs.
  • Year 2: Expansion. Pick-to-Light integration and fleet expansion.
  • Year 3: Optimization. Full AI dynamic slotting integration.
  • Year 4: Scaling. Replicating the model to secondary distribution centers (MO).
  • Year 5: Smart Warehouse. 90% automated material handling with robotic bin picking.
