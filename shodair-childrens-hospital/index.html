Executive Summary

Shodair Children's Hospital is uniquely positioned to leverage robotics not for surgical intervention, but for high-throughput genetics and psychiatric care enhancement. The primary financial driver is the modernization of the Medical Genetics Laboratory, where automation can unlock a 40-60% increase in capacity. Secondary drivers include social robotics for therapeutic outcomes and logistics automation to support the expanded facility.

Strategic Roadmap

  • Phase 1 (Year 1): Lab Modernization. Procure liquid handling robots and integrate LIMS to boost testing capacity immediately.
