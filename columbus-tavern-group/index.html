Strategic Opportunity Pathways

Three-pronged approach to modernizing the tavern experience without sacrificing atmosphere.

PATHWAY A

FOH Service Robots

Deployment of runners for heavy tray delivery and bussing. Allows human servers to remain in their sections, focusing on drink refills and guest interaction.

PATHWAY B

Automated Frying

Back-of-House integration of auto-basket fryers for wings and fries. Ensures perfect consistency, reduces food waste, and minimizes burn risks.

PATHWAY C

Voice AI Agents

Implementation of AI for phone orders and reservations. Captures 100% of inbound calls during loud service hours, ensuring zero lost revenue.
