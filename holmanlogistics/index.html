// Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Targeting high-friction operational zones within Holman facilities.

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Decoupling horizontal travel from vertical lifting. Replacing long-haul forklift runs with Autonomous Mobile Robots to keep operators in active picking zones.

High Priority
Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems for e-commerce clients. Increases pick density and reduces storage footprint by 30%.

Pathway C: AI Inbound/Outbound

Computer vision at docks and AI scheduling to predict loading sequences, reducing detention fees and dwell time by 20%.
