02. Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: AMR Integration

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace manual pallet jacks. Focuses on internal transport velocity and reducing employee walking time.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking

Implementation of ASRS (Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems) for high-density buffering and precision sorting of small parcels.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer vision at loading docks to optimize truck turnaround times and predictively route inbound freight to the correct zones.

03. Implementation Roadmap

YR 1
Pilot Program: Deploy 10 AMRs in primary hub; Install AI Vision at Inbound Docks.
YR 2
Scale & Evaluate: Expand fleet to secondary hubs; Introduce robotic sorting arms.
YR 3
Integration: Full WES (Warehouse Execution System) connectivity across all robotics.
YR 4
Optimization: Machine Learning application to refine routing logic and reduce idle time.
YR 5
Maturity: Fully automated "Dock-to-Van" ecosystem operational.
