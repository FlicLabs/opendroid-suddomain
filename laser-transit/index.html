Laser Transit is positioned to transition from a traditional 3PL to a tech-enabled fulfillment hub. By implementing a "Dark Warehouse" strategy for high-frequency zones, the organization can decouple revenue growth from labor market volatility, securing dominance in the Northeast logistics corridor.
