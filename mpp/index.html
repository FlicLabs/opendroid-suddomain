Executive Vision

MPP is positioned to lead the powder metallurgy sector by transitioning from batch processing to agile, data-driven manufacturing. By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), MPP will reduce scrap rates caused by green part fragility and bridge the data gap between compaction presses and sintering furnaces.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

PATHWAY A

Cobot Machine Tending

Focus: Green Part Integrity

Deploying force-sensitive Cobots to unload compaction presses. This eliminates manual handling of brittle unsintered parts, ensuring uniform tray loading for the furnace.

PATHWAY B

AMR Material Transport

Focus: Vibration Control

Replacing manual fork trucks with smooth-path AMRs. This reduces vibration-induced micro-cracks during the transport of green parts to sintering lines.

PATHWAY C

Automated Inspection

Focus: Throughput & Yield

High-speed vision systems for 100% dimensional analysis post-sintering, coupled with industrial palletizing robots to clear bottlenecks at the shipping dock.
