Executive Vision

Longwood Gardens represents the pinnacle of botanical preservation. Our strategic vision for the dining venues (1906, The Cafe, Beer Garden) is to implement "Invisible Automation." By utilizing robotics for transport and AI for ordering, we remove the friction of service, allowing the human staff to focus purely on the guest experience and the botanical atmosphere.

Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Front-of-House (1906 & Beer Garden)

Deployment of Tier-1 Food Runners to manage the long haul distances between the kitchen and the outdoor terraces. This reduces server fatigue and ensures food arrives hot, while heavy-payload robots manage bussing in the Beer Garden.

PATHWAY B Back-of-House (The Cafe)

Implementation of automated frying assistants to handle high-volume demands during the Christmas Display and Festival of Fountains. This ensures consistency and safety.

PATHWAY C AI & Kiosks

Installation of wood-paneled, aesthetic self-service kiosks in The Cafe to reduce line wait times during peak flows, utilizing AI to suggest pairings and increase average ticket size.
