EXECUTIVE VISION

Ethos Pet Brands is positioned to redefine the "Farm-to-Bowl" philosophy through Industry 4.0. By integrating IoT and robotics into the Brownwood facility, the organization can transition from reactive batch processing to a proactive, continuous flow model. This ensures traceability, safety, and agility in a premium market.

ROBOTICS PATHWAYS
PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Variety Packing & Kitting: Implementing IP67 washdown-ready cobots to manage the high-mix complexity of variety packs. Focus on reducing repetitive strain injuries.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Intralogistics: Replacing forklifts with Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) for ingredient transport from warehouse to mixers. Improves facility safety.

PATHWAY C: PALLETIZING

End-of-Line Automation: High-speed robotic palletizing coupled with vision inspection for bag seal integrity. Critical for yield and throughput.
