Children's Medical Group

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

OPEN DROIDS

1. Executive Summary

Children’s Medical Group (CMG) faces unique challenges: seasonal demand surges, high administrative burdens for immunization/school records, and the emotional complexity of pediatric care. This analysis validates robotics and automation not as a replacement for compassion, but as an infrastructure layer. The dominant ROI drivers are (1) AI-driven triage/scheduling, (2) Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for admin, and (3) Social Robotics for clinical throughput. Projected operational capacity lift is 20-25%.

2. Organizational Profile

  • Model: Multi-site independent pediatric primary and urgent care network.
  • Scale: 10–15 locations, ~150k–200k annual visits.
  • Economics: High volume, lower margin per visit. Dependent on throughput.
  • Bottlenecks: Call center overload, needle anxiety slowing room turnover, paperwork.

3. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A – Intelligent Triage & Scheduling (High Value)

Scope: AI/Algorithmic software to act as digital gatekeeper. Automates symptom checking and booking.

Impact: Reduces call volume by 30–40%. Recovers 5–10% of physician capacity lost to gaps.

Pathway B – Administrative RPA

Scope: Bots handling data tasks between EHR and payer portals (School forms, authorizations).

Impact: Reduces admin cost per visit by 15%. Decreases DSO by 5–7 days.

Pathway C – Clinical Social Robotics

Scope: Interactive social robots in exam rooms to manage patient anxiety during vaccinations.

Impact: Reduces procedure time by 3–5 mins/encounter. High marketing differentiation.

5. Implementation & Strategy

Phase 1 (Mo 1-6): Deploy Pathway A to relieve phone lines before flu season.

Phase 2 (Mo 6-12): Implement RPA for back-office and school forms.

Phase 3 (Year 2): Pilot Distraction Robotics in high-volume clinics.

Strategic Note: Adopting this portfolio transitions CMG from a reactive practice to a tech-enabled pediatric platform, securing long-term sustainability against retail competitors.
