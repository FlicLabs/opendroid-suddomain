01. Executive Summary

Wipaire is positioned to revolutionize its MRO and manufacturing throughput by adopting Industry 4.0 robotics. This strategy focuses on deploying autonomous systems for float washing, paint preparation, and cabin detailing. The primary goal is to shift skilled human technicians away from hazardous cleaning tasks toward high-value engineering and modification work, reinforcing Wipaire's status as the global leader in aircraft flotation.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Interior Detail

Autonomous vacuums and surface wipes for post-modification detailing on Cessna Caravans and Twin Otters. Ensures factory-finish consistency.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen and mold elimination for seaplane interiors. Critical for moisture management in aquatic aircraft environments.

Pathway C: Exterior Wash

Highest Priority: Automated gantries or drones for float/hull washing to remove corrosive salts and aquatic debris efficiently.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision bots for intake scanning. Detects rivet issues, corrosion, and hull stress fractures faster than human inspection.
