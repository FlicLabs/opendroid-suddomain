02 Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous BPH Resection High ROI

Deployment of image-guided robotic waterjet systems (Aquablation) to standardize prostate resection, reduce OR time, and improve sexual function outcomes.

Pathway B: Robotic Stone Management Ergonomics

Adoption of flexible robotic ureteroscopy to treat complex kidney stones, reducing surgeon fatigue and enabling treatment of larger burdens in the ASC.

Pathway C: AI & Workflow Automation Efficiency

Implementation of AI triage for patient routing and laboratory automation for high-volume diagnostics.
