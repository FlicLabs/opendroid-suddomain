01. Executive Summary

Technical Systems Inc. is positioned to lead the HVAC manufacturing sector by transitioning from rigid assembly lines to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By leveraging Industry 4.0 connectivity, the organization can decouple production throughput from linear labor scaling, ensuring operational agility and enhanced worker safety.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of Collaborative Robots for automated torque screwdriving and machine tending. Eliminates RSI and ensures quality consistency.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace manual forklifts. Enables JIT line-side replenishment and automated transport of chassis.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Automated palletizing paired with machine vision inspection to guarantee 100% visual QA before shipping.
