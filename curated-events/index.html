Executive Summary

Curated Events specializes in high-touch corporate gatherings and experiential marketing. Facing industry-wide labor shortages, the company's strongest automation levers are Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics and Automated F&B systems. The dominant ROI driver is the optimization of load-in/load-out workflows, reducing overtime and injury risk.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot & Logistics

Procure 4 heavy payload AMRs. Train technical leads. Validate 30% labor reduction across 20 pilot events.

Year 2: Service Integration

Lease robotic bartending units for holiday seasons. Measure pour-cost efficiency and guest engagement.

Year 3: Expansion

Full fleet rollout to regional teams. Investment in proprietary drone fleet for summer experiential events.
