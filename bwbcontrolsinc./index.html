Executive Summary

BWB Controls Inc. is positioned to transition from traditional manual assembly to a high-precision Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By integrating collaborative robotics into the assembly of pneumatic relays and level switches, the organization can overcome skilled labor shortages while ensuring the rigorous safety standards required for Oil & Gas certifications. This strategy prioritizes predictive workflows over reactive production.
