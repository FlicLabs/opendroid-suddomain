01. Executive Summary

The aviation industry is pivoting toward automated ground support equipment. For Avmax Group, integrating robotics offers a dual advantage: streamlining labor-intensive heavy maintenance cleaning and enhancing asset value through certified sanitization. This strategy targets a transition from manual dependency to an AI-driven fleet maintenance model.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Auto-Clean

LiDAR-mapped vacuuming robots for charter turnarounds. Reduces interior cleaning crew size by 66%.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sanitization for lease returns. Provides "Bio-Safety Certificate" for new lessees.

Pathway C: Exterior Systems

Automated washing gantries for MRO. Reduces wash time from 6 hours to 45 mins per Regional Jet.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Drone-based optical inspection for dent/buckle analysis during C/D checks.
