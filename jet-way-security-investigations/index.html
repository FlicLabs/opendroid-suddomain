AVIATION CLEANING AUTOMATION

Strategic Research for Jet Way Security & Investigations

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Jet Way Security & Investigations is positioned to revolutionize its operational footprint through the strategic integration of robotics. This report outlines a transition from traditional manual labor to a hybrid automated workforce, ensuring superior hygiene standards, reduced turnaround times, and significant cost efficiencies. The vision centers on a scalable ecosystem of autonomous units that operate seamlessly within secure aviation environments.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots
    AMRs designed for high-speed interior sanitization and debris removal during short turnaround windows.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots
    Autonomous units utilizing UV-C light to neutralize pathogens on high-touch surfaces, enhancing passenger safety.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing Systems
    Mechanized robotic arms for fuselage cleaning, reducing labor risks and water consumption.
  • Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection Bots
    Multi-functional bots performing cleaning tasks while simultaneously auditing structural integrity via computer vision.
