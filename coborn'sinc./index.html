Executive Summary

Coborn's Inc. stands at a critical juncture in the Upper Midwest grocery landscape. The proposed Hybrid Fulfillment Optimization strategy moves beyond traditional warehousing, leveraging automation to support both central distribution and the growing "CobornsDelivers" e-commerce platform.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Transport

Deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle heavy pallet transport, decoupling transit from picking tasks.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Utilizing high-density ASRS for "Goods-to-Person" picking, specifically targeting slow-moving dry goods and e-commerce fulfillment.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer vision for expiry date management and predictive slotting to reduce perishable shrinkage.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 WiFi Infrastructure Audit & Pilot AMR Deployment (10 Units).
Year 2 WCS Integration & Expansion into Robotic Piece Picking for HBC.
Year 3 Full ASRS rollout & AI Predictive Slotting for Cold Chain.
Year 4-5 Network-wide scaling & Urban Micro-Fulfillment.
