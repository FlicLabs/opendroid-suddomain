StepStone Hospitality

Robotics Opportunities & ROI Analysis Portfolio

Executive Summary

StepStone Hospitality faces a critical opportunity to offset industry-wide labor shortages and rising wages through targeted automation. The highest value robotics levers are Autonomous Floor Care (Housekeeping), Guest Delivery Robots (Logistics), and Culinary Automation (F&B). Implementation shifts the operational focus from low-value transport tasks to high-value guest interactions.
