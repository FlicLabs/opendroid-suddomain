01. Executive Summary

Rx HealthCare Services faces critical pressures in labor costs and reimbursement tightening. By leveraging Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and AI-driven field logistics, the organization can shift from a manual staffing model to a tech-enabled care platform. The dominant ROI driver is the optimization of field clinician throughput, capable of generating millions in incremental margin.

04. Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 (0-6 Mo)

Deploy RPA for billing/intake. Digitize forms. Establish baseline metrics.

Phase 2 (6-12 Mo)

Launch AI Logistics for field routing. Pilot Telepresence with heart failure patients.

Phase 3 (12-24 Mo)

Scale remote monitoring. Evaluate physical assistive robotics for rehab therapy.
