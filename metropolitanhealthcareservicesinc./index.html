Metropolitan Healthcare Services Inc.

Strategic Robotics & Automation Analysis Portfolio

Executive Summary

Metropolitan Healthcare Services Inc. (MHS) is positioned to pivot from a labor-centric support provider to a technology-enabled logistics partner. By integrating automation into Environmental Services (EVS) and Patient Transport, MHS can solve critical labor shortages and drive hospital throughput.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: EVS Automation

High Priority

Scope: Autonomous floor scrubbers and UV-C disinfection robots.

Impact: Offloads large-area cleaning, ensuring consistent infection control and freeing staff for high-touch disinfection.

Pathway B: Logistics AMRs

Strategic

Scope: Autonomous Mobile Robots (Tugs) for linen and waste transport.

Impact: Reduces workplace injury and eliminates "dead walking time" for staff, increasing time-on-floor.

Pathway C: AI Patient Dispatch

Software

Scope: Predictive algorithms integrated with hospital ADT feeds.

Impact: Optimizes transporter routing, reducing patient wait times by ~25% and increasing transports per labor hour.
