Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots

Compact autonomous vacuums and wipers designed for rapid turnarounds on Cessna Caravans and Twin Otters. Operates independently to ensure cabin readiness.

Pathway B UV-C Disinfection Robots

Mobile UV-C units for terminal and hangar use. Provides chemical-free sanitation of high-touch surfaces and cabin air between flights.

Pathway C Exterior Aircraft Washing

Automated systems targeting salt deposits critical for seaplane operations. Reduces water usage and prevents corrosion-induced airframe drag.

Pathway D AI Inspection Bots

Drone and crawling bot systems utilizing thermal imaging to detect structural anomalies, bird strikes, and corrosion before they become maintenance issues.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Strategy)

  • Year 1 (Pilot): Deployment of 2 Cabin Robots and 2 UV-C units at Fort Lauderdale (FXE). Baseline metrics establishment.
  • Year 2 (Expansion): Introduction of Exterior Washing Systems for seaplane fleet at Key West (EYW). AI inspection software beta testing.
  • Year 3 (Adoption): Fleet-wide coverage of 80% turboprops. Full integration of exterior washing for corrosion control.
  • Year 4 (Optimization): Data-driven route optimization and predictive maintenance via AI Bots. Achieve target OPEX reduction.
  • Year 5 (Maturity): Full fleet automation coverage. Integration of sustainable water recycling. Gen-2 hardware upgrades.
