Executive Summary

We propose a hybrid automation ecosystem for PSS Industrial Group to transition from manual, labor-intensive warehousing to intelligent fulfillment. By integrating heavy-duty Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for industrial components and Goods-to-Person systems for small parts, PSS can secure a decade of competitive advantage in the energy supply sector.

Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Heavy Goods Transport

Deploying heavy-payload AMRs to replace manual forklifts for valves, pipes, and palletized bulk. Decouples labor from transport, increasing throughput by 35% and significantly reducing safety incidents.

Pathway B: Piece Picking

Focus: Accuracy & Density

Utilization of Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for fittings and gaskets. Condenses 1,000 sqm of storage into 150 sqm, enabling high-speed, light-directed picking with 99.9% accuracy.
