02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport. Focus on cross-dock buffering to keep forklifts at the trailer.

Pathway B: Secure ASRS

Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems for high-value cage operations. Critical for electronics and pharmaceutical freight security.

Pathway C: AI Vision

Computer vision at dock doors for automated dimensioning, damage claims reduction, and trailer utilization optimization.
