01. Executive Summary

New Ocean Health Solutions is a digital health management platform provider. While primarily software-centric, the company is poised to leverage "Software Robotics" (RPA) and integration with home-health hardware to reduce operational bottlenecks. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of human capital costs per interaction (PMPM) and increased member adherence.
