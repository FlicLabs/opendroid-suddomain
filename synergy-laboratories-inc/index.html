Executive Summary

Synergy Laboratories Inc. operates in a high-volume diagnostic environment where turnaround time (TAT) and accuracy are paramount. Our analysis identifies that replacing manual pre-analytical workflows with automated sorting cells and deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for intra-lab transport will yield the highest immediate ROI.

The proposed "smart lab" infrastructure addresses critical bottlenecks in sample accessioning, reducing error rates by near 99% and liberating highly trained staff for analytical tasks.
