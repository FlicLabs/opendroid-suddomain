Executive Vision

CAI is positioned to transition from a labor-intensive model to a hybrid-automated ecosystem. By implementing robotics, we shift staff focus from "logistics" (carrying trays) to "hospitality" (guest engagement), ensuring sustainable growth amidst labor shortages.

Automation Pathways

  • Pathway A (FOH): Serving robots to handle food running and dish bussing. Reduces server travel by 60%.
  • Pathway B (BOH): Automated frying arms to ensure safety and consistency. Eliminates localized hazard pay.
  • Pathway C (AI): Voice agents and Kiosks. Expected to increase ticket size by 18% via algorithmic upselling.

Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1 Pilot FOH runners in 3 flagship locations.
YEAR 2 Regional expansion to 50% of stores + AI Kiosks.
YEAR 3 Full fleet integration & heavy BOH retrofits.
YEAR 5 Next-Gen Humanoid experimentation.
