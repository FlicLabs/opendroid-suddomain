Executive Summary

Strategic Vision

The shift toward robotics in hospitality is not about automation replacing humanity, but augmenting it. For Clique Hospitality, the goal is to deploy a Hybrid High-Tech, High-Touch model. By leveraging autonomous systems for logistics (FOH serving, BOH frying) and AI for order management, we liberate human talent to focus on guest connection and culinary creativity. This transition ensures operational consistency during high-volume surges while optimizing labor cost structures.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous runners for food delivery and dish collection. Reduces server steps by 40%, allowing focus on guest interaction and sales.

B. BOH Automated Frying

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Vision-guided robotic arms for fry stations. Ensures perfect cook times, reduces burn risks, and maintains hygiene standards during rushes.

C. AI Kiosks & Voice

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

Smart POS modules and voice agents. Drives check size via data-driven add-ons and eliminates order entry errors.
