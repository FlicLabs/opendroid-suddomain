Executive Summary

UnityBPO sits at the critical intersection of clinical workflows and IT support. The primary automation opportunity is not physical robotics, but "Digital Workers" (RPA & Virtual Agents). Transitioning from a labor-heavy help desk model to an AI-orchestrated platform can reduce Tier 1 operational costs by 30-40% while enhancing the human-centric service delivery that defines the brand.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A
RPA for Access Management Highest ROI

Deployment of unattended software robots to handle user provisioning, password resets, and termination access across disparate hospital systems.

  • Reduces onboarding time from days to minutes.
  • Eliminates 20-30% of total help desk volume.
  • High security compliance.
B
Intelligent Virtual Agents (Tier 0) Strategic

Conversational AI integrated into ITSM tools to handle routine clinical hardware issues (printers, carts) and basic triage.

  • 24/7 instant availability for clinicians.
  • Significant reduction in Average Handle Time (AHT).
  • Scalable without linear headcount growth.
C
Patient Scheduling Bots Volume Driver

Patient-facing robotic agents for appointment scheduling, refills, and intake prior to nurse routing.

  • Zero hold times during volume spikes.
  • Standardized EHR data entry.

Implementation Roadmap

MONTHS 1-6

Foundation & Quick Wins

Pilot RPA for Password Resets & Provisioning. Establish Digital Center of Excellence.

MONTHS 7-12

Expansion to Clinical Support

Roll out Tier 0 Virtual Agents for IT support. Integrate with Epic/Cerner for basic workflows.

MONTHS 13-24

Cognitive Automation

Deploy patient-facing bots and self-healing infrastructure robots. Reach 30-40% automation.
