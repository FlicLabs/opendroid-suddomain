Executive Vision

Weathermatic is poised to transition from legacy manufacturing to a flexible, data-driven Smart Factory model. By integrating collaborative robotics into high-mix, high-volume production lines, the company will secure its position as a leader in Smart Water Management. The strategy focuses on automating the "Dull, Dirty, and Dangerous" tasks—specifically machine tending for injection molding and precision assembly for IoT controllers.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly. Deployment of IP67-rated collaborative arms to unload injection molders and assist in screw-driving for SmartLink controllers. Focus on consistency and waterproofing.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Material Transport. Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle resin bin transport and finished goods movement. Eliminates manual forklift risks and optimizes WIP flow.

PATHWAY C: Vision & EOL

Inspection & Palletizing. Automated visual QA for seal integrity followed by robotic heavy-lifting for palletizing. Focus on yield improvement and reducing physical strain.
