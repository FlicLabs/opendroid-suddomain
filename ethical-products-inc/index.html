03 / Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMR Transport

Deployment of Goods-to-Person Autonomous Mobile Robots to eliminate non-value-added walking time. Targets a reduction in forklift reliance and increased safety.

Pathway B: Vertical ASRS

High-density automated storage for slow-moving inventory. Recovers 60% of floor space by utilizing vertical height up to 40ft.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive logic layers to optimize battery charging during off-peak hours and pre-position inventory before surge shifts.
