01 / Executive Summary

Operational Efficiency in Community Care

Morehouse Community Medical Centers Inc. (MCMC) stands to gain significant value through the adoption of "Software Robotics" (RPA), automated pharmacy dispensing, and telepresence solutions. Unlike tertiary care requiring heavy surgical robotics, MCMC's opportunity lies in reducing administrative overhead and extending specialist reach into rural Louisiana. The strategic implementation of these technologies can unlock over $2M in cumulative value over 5 years by optimizing workforce utilization and increasing patient throughput.
