Executive Vision

Grayhill Inc. is positioned to redefine HMI manufacturing through the adoption of Industry 4.0 Flexible Automation. By shifting from manual-heavy assembly to a hybrid model utilizing Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), Grayhill can secure its leadership in the HMLV (High-Mix Low-Volume) market. This strategy targets a reduction in lead times, consistency in haptic quality, and a modernized workforce structure.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Assembly & Testing.

Deployment of 6-axis arms for precision screwdriving and haptic life-cycle testing. Ensures consistent actuation force data logging.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Intralogistics.

Automated material transport for PCB trays and finished goods. Reduces non-value-added walking time for skilled technicians.

Pathway C: Vision

Focus: Quality Control.

Automated optical inspection for display bonding and surface defects, paired with end-of-line palletizing systems.
