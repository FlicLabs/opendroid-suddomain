Executive Summary

Glorious Cannabis Company, a leader in the Michigan cannabis sector, faces scaling challenges typical of high-growth vertical operators. By shifting from manual manufacturing to robotics-driven infusion and packaging, the company can address critical bottlenecks. The analysis identifies three key pathways, with Automated Infused Pre-Roll Manufacturing offering the highest immediate impact, potentially generating millions in incremental margin annually.

Opportunity Pathways Analysis

Pathway A: Infused Pre-Roll Automation

Scope: Deployment of robotic injection and twisting systems.
Impact: Reduces labor costs by 60-70% and triples production capacity.

Pathway B: Harvest & Trim Automation

Scope: Bladeless trimming and optical sorting.
Impact: Standardizes product consistency and reduces seasonal labor dependency.

Pathway C: Compliance & Vault Robotics

Scope: ASRS and RFID tracking for Metrc compliance.
Impact: Mitigates regulatory risk and eliminates inventory shrinkage.
