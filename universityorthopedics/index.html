UNIVERSITY ORTHOPEDICS

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

University Orthopedics (UOI) is positioned to transition from a regional academic practice to a tech-enabled musculoskeletal system. By leveraging robotics in ASC Surgery, Rehabilitation, and Logistics, UOI can migrate high-margin cases from hospitals to owned facilities, improve therapist utilization, and optimize overhead. The dominant ROI driver is the capture of facility fees via safe, robotic-assisted outpatient joint replacement.

$1.2M+ Annual Margin Uplift (Est)
14-18 Mo ROI Payback Period
300+ Incremental Cases/Year
Opportunity Pathways
HIGHEST VALUE

Pathway A: ASC Surgical Ecosystem

Deployment of next-gen robotic systems (Mako/VELYS) at Kettle Point ASC to enable same-day discharge for complex joints and spine.

Impact: Migrates 15-20% of volume from hospital to ASC. Reduces LOS to <4 hours.

EFFICIENCY DRIVER

Pathway B: Robotic Rehabilitation

Integration of robotic gait trainers and exoskeletons in therapy centers to allow concurrent patient treatment.

Impact: Doubles therapist capacity per hour. Gamified data improves adherence.

OPERATIONAL SUPPORT

Pathway C: Sterile Logistics

Automated sterile supply robots and tray management to reduce OR turnover times.

Impact: Adds +1 surgical case per room/day via faster turnover.
