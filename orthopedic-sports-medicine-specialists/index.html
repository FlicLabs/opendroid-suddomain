Executive Summary

Orthopedic and Sports Medicine Specialists (OSMS) is positioned at a critical juncture in the shift toward outpatient joint replacement. The integration of robotic-assisted surgical systems and automated rehabilitation technologies offers significant leverage. The dominant ROI driver is the migration of complex joint replacements to the ASC setting, enabled by precision robotics and increased throughput.
