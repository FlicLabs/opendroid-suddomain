Executive Summary

Mennekes faces a critical pivot point driven by the e-mobility boom. The transition from traditional push-manufacturing to high-velocity fulfillment requires a Hybrid Autonomous Logistics Environment. Our strategy integrates heavy-payload AMRs for manufacturing and high-density ASRS for component management to resolve labor scarcity and throughput bottlenecks.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A AMRs for Internal Transport

Replacement of manual forklifts with Lidar-guided units for 24/7 movement of raw granulate and finished palletized goods.

Pathway B High-Density ASRS

Cube storage implementation to compress small-part inventory by 60%, critical for expanding EV component stock without new construction.

Pathway C AI Predictive Logistics

Integration of Machine Learning to predict EV charger demand surges and pre-position inventory for rapid fulfillment.

5-Year Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot deployment of 3-5 AMRs and WMS integration.
  • Year 2: ASRS installation for top 20% high-velocity SKUs.
  • Year 3: Full machine-to-machine integration (Injection Molding -> AMR).
  • Year 4: AI Predictive Inventory Slotting activation.
  • Year 5: Digital Twin realization and "Dark Warehouse" capability.
