DORONI AEROSPACE

Strategic Automation & Robotics Integration Report

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Integrating robotic automation into Doroni Aerospace's operations represents a pivotal step in scaling eVTOL manufacturing and commercial readiness. This strategy proposes a 5-year phased deployment of autonomous cleaning and inspection systems, targeting a 35-45% reduction in OPEX and a payback period under 24 months. By shifting manual labor to robotic precision, Doroni ensures rigorous hygiene standards, aerodynamic efficiency, and operational agility required for Advanced Air Mobility (AAM).

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

PATHWAY A: CABIN CLEANING

SLAM-navigated bots for interior sanitation and seat wiping.

PATHWAY B: UV-C DISINFECTION

Autonomous UV arrays for pathogen elimination without chemicals.

PATHWAY C: EXTERIOR WASHING

Soft-touch gantry systems preserving composite aerodynamics.

PATHWAY D: AI INSPECTION

Computer vision crawlers detecting micro-damage on airframes.

IMPLEMENTATION ROADMAP

  • YEAR 1 Pilot Phase: Deploy 2 Cabin Robots & 1 UV-C unit. Validate safety protocols.
  • YEAR 2 Standardization: Install Exterior Washing System. Train internal staff.
  • YEAR 3 Integration: Introduce AI Inspection Bots. Link to digital logs.
  • YEAR 4 Scaling: Expand to secondary hubs. Full fleet deployment.
  • YEAR 5 Optimization: Predictive AI scheduling. Full OPEX reduction realized.
