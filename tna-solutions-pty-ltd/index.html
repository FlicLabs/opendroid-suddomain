Executive Vision

Tna Solutions is positioned to lead the sector by transitioning from traditional high-precision fabrication to an Industry 4.0 Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By adopting High-Mix Low-Volume (HMLV) robotics, Tna can reduce assembly lead times for flagship VFFS and processing equipment, ensuring agility in a volatile global supply chain.

Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A

Collaborative Welding

Deployment of Cobots for TIG welding sub-assemblies. Ensures food-grade hygiene consistency and relieves skilled welders for complex bespoke tasks.

Pathway B

AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport stainless steel sheets and parts. Creates a fork-free safety zone and reduces non-value-added transit time.

Pathway C

Automated Inspection

Vision-based robotic cells for end-of-line verification. Scans fabricated parts against CAD models to eliminate rework before final assembly.
