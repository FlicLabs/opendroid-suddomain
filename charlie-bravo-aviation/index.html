Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots
    Mobile units utilizing LIDAR for aisle navigation, performing vacuuming and surface sanitation to reduce turn-around times.
  • Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection Robots
    Non-chemical sanitization systems deployed post-cleaning to ensure pathogen-free environments and enhance passenger confidence.
  • Pathway C: Exterior Aircraft Washing Systems
    Robotic gantries reducing water usage by 50% while providing consistent fuselage cleaning without human fall-risk.
  • Pathway D: AI-Powered Inspection Bots
    Thermal and visual sensors on agile bots for predictive maintenance and FOD detection in hard-to-reach aircraft areas.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Strategy)

  • Year 1: Assessment & Pilot
    Deploy 4 pilot units (2 Cabin, 2 UV-C) at primary hubs. Establish baseline metrics.
  • Year 2-3: Interior Expansion
    Scale cabin/UV-C bots to 50% of fleet. Integrate fleet management software and staff training.
  • Year 3-4: Exterior & Inspection
    Install exterior washing systems in hangars. Deploy AI inspection bots for routine checks.
  • Year 4-5: Full Ecosystem
    100% interior coverage. Complete exterior rollout. Optimize resource consumption.
  • Year 5+: Optimization
    AI refinement for predictive maintenance. Full integration with airport tarmac systems.
