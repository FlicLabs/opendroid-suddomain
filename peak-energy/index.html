Executive Vision

Peak Energy is positioned to lead the sodium-ion revolution. By transitioning from rigid conveyor lines to a flexible, modular automated ecosystem, the company can address the high-variance needs of modern energy storage.

Primary Goal: Move from pilot-scale to Gigafactory readiness using smart robotics.

Robotics Opportunities

  • Pathway A (Cobots): Collaborative arms for battery module assembly and torque application. Enhances safety and precision.
  • Pathway B (AMRs): Autonomous transport for electrode rolls. Eliminates static conveyors and improves floor usage by 15%.
  • Pathway C (Inspection): AI-Vision for cell defects. Prevents costly formation of bad cells.
