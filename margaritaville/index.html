Executive Vision

The strategic objective is to transition Margaritaville to a hybrid hospitality model. By utilizing automation for logistics (runners) and repetitive culinary tasks (frying), human staff are liberated to focus on the "entertainment" aspect of the guest experience.

Robotics Opportunities

PATHWAY A FOH Serving Robots
Deploying shelf-based robots to handle heavy tray transport across large resort floor plans.

PATHWAY B BOH Automated Cooking
Automating fry stations for high-volume items (Volcano Nachos) to ensure safety and consistency.

PATHWAY C AI Kiosks
Tabletop ordering to capture impulse beverage sales and increase average ticket size.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program in flagship tourist hubs (Vegas/Orlando).
  • Year 3: Global standardization & POS integration.
  • Year 5: Full AI autonomy ecosystem.
