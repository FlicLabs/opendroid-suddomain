Executive Summary

Entek International is positioned to dominate the supply chain for EV battery separators. However, scaling requires decoupling production capacity from labor availability. This report outlines a shift from traditional manufacturing to a data-driven, automated ecosystem focusing on machinery assembly and material handling.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Machinery Assembly Assist

Focus: Safety & Precision.
Deployment of UR10e units to assist in gearbox assembly for twin-screw extruders, reducing technician fatigue and ensuring precise torque application.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Intralogistics & WIP Flow

Focus: Efficiency.
Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport PE resin and finished rolls, integrating with MES for JIT delivery.

PATHWAY C: QUALITY

Vision Inspection & Palletizing

Focus: Yield.
Automated handling of heavy separator rolls combined with micron-level vision inspection for pinhole defects.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Cobot deployment in Oregon & Vision System integration.
  • Year 2: SCADA integration and AMR pilot in UK facility.
  • Year 3: Global standardization across US, UK, and Indonesia.
  • Year 4: AI Predictive Maintenance for Extruder parts.
  • Year 5: Fully autonomous supply chain ecosystem.
