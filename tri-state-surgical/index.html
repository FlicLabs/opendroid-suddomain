Executive Summary

Tri State Surgical is positioned to transform from a regional logistics provider into a high-tech supply chain partner. By leveraging Automated Storage (ASRS) and Robotic Sterile Processing, the organization can eliminate critical bottlenecks in inventory shrinkage and labor shortages. The proposed automation roadmap addresses the immediate need for scalable throughput while ensuring 99.99% accuracy in surgical kit delivery.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: ASRS Logistics

Focus: Automated Storage & Retrieval.

Impact: Reduces warehouse footprint by 50% and eliminates picking errors. High-density storage for 40k+ SKUs.

Est. Benefit: £4.5M/Year

Pathway B: Robotic SPD

Focus: Sterile Processing Decontamination.

Impact: Reduces biohazard exposure and standardizes instrument cleaning. Critical for infection control.

Est. Benefit: £1.8M/Year

Pathway C: AMRs

Focus: Autonomous Transport.

Impact: Connects loading docks to sterile zones without human intervention. Reduces manual cart pushing.

Est. Benefit: £0.5M/Year
