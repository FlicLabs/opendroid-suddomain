SeneGence International

Industrial Automation & Smart Manufacturing Strategic Assessment

Executive Summary

The vision for SeneGence is to transition from traditional discrete manufacturing to Industry 4.0 enabled mass personalization. By integrating smart manufacturing solutions, SeneGence can achieve rapid changeovers for diverse product lines (cosmetics, skincare) while maintaining rigorous quality standards. The focus is on creating a digitally connected factory floor that supports direct-to-consumer demand fluctuations through flexible, data-driven automation.

Process Type

Discrete Manufacturing: Assembly of components, precise filling, and packaging.

Hybrid Elements: Liquid blending (Process) leading to discrete bottling lines.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment for machine tending and assembly (capping, labeling). Safe operation alongside humans with easy redeployment for SKU changes.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

Autonomous transport of raw materials and finished goods. Reduces forklift traffic and enables just-in-time line-side replenishment.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput & Yield

Robotic palletizing and AI vision inspection. Ensures perfect packaging and optimizes warehouse space utilization.

Output: Manual vs. Cobot-Assisted

Financial Model Overview

CAPEX Strategy

Modular deployment starting at $60k-$120k per cell. This minimizes initial risk while proving ROI before scaling. Includes robot, safety, and EOAT.

OPEX & ROI

Drivers: Labor reallocation, scrap reduction via vision systems, and higher throughput.

Target: Sub-18 month payback period driven by a 20-30% uplift in OEE.

Implementation Roadmap (Year 1 - 5)

Year 1: Foundation & Pilot

Audit, baseline OEE, and pilot deployment of Cobots (Pathway A) and End-of-Line robots (Pathway C) on the primary LipSense line.

Year 2: Scaling & Integration

Expand cobot cells to secondary lines. Deploy AMRs (Pathway B) for logistics. Integrate IoT sensors for basic machine health monitoring.

Year 3: Data Connectivity

Implement central Manufacturing Execution System (MES). Connect all robotic assets for real-time data visualization and reporting.

Year 4: Advanced AI & Flexibility

Deploy AI-driven vision inspection. Enable "Lights Out" manufacturing for specific shifts. Dynamic scheduling via AMR fleet management.

Year 5: Ecosystem Maturity

Full autonomous supply chain integration. Predictive maintenance suite achieving near-zero downtime. Digital twin implementation.
