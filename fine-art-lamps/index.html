01. Executive Vision

Fine Art Lamps must transition to "Craftsmanship at Scale." The objective is not to replace the artisan, but to deploy Industry 4.0 tools to handle non-value-added tasks. This allows skilled workers to focus entirely on the artistic integrity of the lighting fixtures while automation handles material transport and repetitive polishing.

02. Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: Cobot Finishing

Deployment of force-sensitive Cobots for metal buffing and polishing. Reduces repetitive strain injuries and ensures base-layer consistency before hand-finishing.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport fragile glass components between kilns and assembly lines, reducing breakage and WIP transit time.

Pathway C: Vision QC

Automated optical inspection for finish consistency and palletizing assistance for heavy shipping crates.
