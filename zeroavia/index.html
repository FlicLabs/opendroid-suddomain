ZeroAvia Strategic Automation

Prepared for the Senior Executive Team. Analyzing the integration of robotics into hydrogen-electric aviation operations.

Executive Summary

ZeroAvia is poised to revolutionize aviation with zero-emission powertrains. This report recommends a parallel revolution in ground operations. By implementing Open Droids' automated cleaning and maintenance solutions, ZeroAvia can reduce drag coefficients through precision washing, ensure passenger safety with UV-C disinfection, and drastically cut turnaround times to support high-frequency regional flight models.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Automation

LiDAR-guided vacuums and trash removal bots to streamline inter-flight cleaning cycles.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Robotic arms delivering 99.9% pathogen reduction without harsh chemicals.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantries to remove drag-inducing debris, extending hydrogen flight range.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Thermal imaging bots for predictive maintenance on airframes and fuel tanks.
