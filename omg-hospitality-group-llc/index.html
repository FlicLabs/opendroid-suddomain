Executive Summary

OMG Hospitality Group stands at a pivotal juncture where the adoption of robotics can significantly enhance the high-volume, atmosphere-driven dining experience. Our research suggests a "Hybrid-Service Model" where automation handles low-value logistics (bussing/running), allowing human staff to focus entirely on guest interaction and upselling. This approach preserves the brand's energetic vibe while stabilizing operational costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service Bots

  • Deploy: Multi-shelf runners (Keenon/Pudu).
  • Impact: Reduces server walking by 60%.
  • Result: Higher staff retention and faster food delivery.

Pathway B: BOH Auto-Frying

  • Deploy: Automated frying arms (Flippy).
  • Impact: Consistent cook times for high-volume bar food.
  • Result: Improved safety and zero food waste.
