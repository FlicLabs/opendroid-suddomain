Prelude Fertility: Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Prelude Fertility, as the largest network of fertility clinics in North America, faces the dual challenge of maximizing laboratory throughput and maintaining impeccable safety standards for biological assets. The proposed robotics portfolio shifts operations from artisan, high-variability manual processes to standardized automated workflows.

Key ROI drivers include Automated Cryostorage, Robotic ICSI, and AI-Driven Embryo Selection. This transformation enables capacity expansion without linear headcount growth.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 (Year 1): Safety Foundation
Deploy Automated Cryostorage in top 3 centralized facilities. Implement AI-grading software in Tier 1 markets.

Phase 2 (Years 2-3): Lab Automation Pilots
Partner with hardware vendors (e.g., Overture Life) to pilot Robotic ICSI in NY or SF hubs. Validate clinical non-inferiority.

Phase 3 (Years 3-5): Network Standard
Roll out robotic fleets to all major hubs. Shift embryologist roles from "operator" to "supervisor."
