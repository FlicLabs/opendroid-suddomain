Resort Logistics & Guest Service Automation

Mt. Hood Meadows faces complex operational challenges due to its high-altitude, weather-dependent environment. Robotics integration focuses on autonomous terrain analysis, intelligent fleet management, and guest service automation.

  • Autonomous Terrain Analysis: Deployment of LiDAR-equipped drones for snow safety reduces patrol exposure and optimizes demolition placement.
  • Intelligent Fleet Management: IoT integration for grooming and patrol vehicles ensures optimal routing, reducing fuel consumption by 10-15%.
  • Guest Service Automation: RFID rental tracking and automated F&B kiosks reduce wait times by 30-40% during peak holidays.

Lodge & Hospitality Operations

High-volume lodge operations require precision during short seasonal windows. Automation targets back-of-house efficiency and inventory control.

  • Kitchen Automation: Robotic fry stations and beverage dispensers handle peak lunch rushes, reducing waste and reallocating staff to guest interaction.
  • RFID Inventory: Automated cycle counts and rental tracking minimize shrinkage (2-5% reduction) and improve stock visibility.
  • Autonomous Sanitation: Floor scrubbers and UV-C bots maintain hygiene standards overnight, reducing janitorial labor costs.
