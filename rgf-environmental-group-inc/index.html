Executive Summary

RGF Environmental Group is positioned to revolutionize its Port of Palm Beach manufacturing hub by integrating Industry 4.0 standards. The shift from manual assembly to flexible, hybrid automation will secure supply chain resilience for critical IAQ products.

By leveraging cobots for delicate PHI-cell assembly and AMRs for logistics, RGF can scale production volume while maintaining its vertical integration strategy and "Made in USA" quality commitment.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway AAssembly Cobots

Utilization of force-sensing cobots (UR/Fanuc) for screw driving and PHI-cell insertion. Reduces repetitive strain and ensures IP-rating consistency.

Pathway BLogistics AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots to bridge injection molding and assembly lines. Eliminates manual cart pushing and optimizes WIP flow.

Pathway CSmart Palletizing

End-of-line automation with vision inspection. Ensures 100% packaging verification and consistent pallet stacking for shipping.
