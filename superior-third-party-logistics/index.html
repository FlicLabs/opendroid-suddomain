Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Internal Transport & Travel Reduction.

Associates spend 50% of time walking. Goods-to-Person AMRs bring inventory to the picker, tripling productivity without adding headcount.

Pathway B: ASRS

Focus: Density & Accuracy.

Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems utilize vertical height, compressing storage footprint by 85% and virtually eliminating pick errors.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Focus: Predictive Efficiency.

AI models analyze inbound/outbound flows to predict labor needs and position robotic fleets in high-demand zones pre-shift.
