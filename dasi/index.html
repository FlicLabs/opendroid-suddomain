EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Integration of robotic automation to modernize DASI's operations, focusing on efficiency, hygiene, and scalability. The strategy leverages four key robotic pathways to reduce operational costs and enhance safety standards.

ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

DASI operates a diverse fleet serving domestic and international routes. Current manual cleaning processes face challenges in cost and consistency. Automation offers a solution to maintain high-frequency operations while ensuring premium service quality.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

AI-driven robots for vacuuming and wiping. Reduces turnaround time.

B. UV-C Disinfection

Automated sanitization of high-touch surfaces. Enhances passenger safety.

C. Exterior Washing

Robotic fuselage washing. Improves aerodynamics and reduces water waste.

D. AI Inspection Bots

Drone-based visual inspections. Predictive maintenance analytics.
