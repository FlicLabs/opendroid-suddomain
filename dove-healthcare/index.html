Research Portfolio: Dove Healthcare

Executive Summary

Dove Healthcare is a key regional provider in skilled nursing and rehabilitation. This analysis pivots the organization from a traditional labor-heavy model to an AI-enabled care platform. The integration of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics and Robotic Rehabilitation systems offers the highest ROI by mitigating staffing shortages and enhancing clinical differentiation.
