Executive Vision

Wolf's Ridge Brewing defines the intersection of craft brewing and fine dining. The robotics strategy is not "replacement," but "elevation." By integrating assistive cobots for logistics (running/bussing), we protect the human element of the brandâ€”allowing servers to focus on beer education and guest connection while robots handle the heavy lifting across the sprawling Dining Room and Taproom layout.

Opportunity Pathways

  • Pathway A (FOH): Collaborative Serving Robots to bridge the distance between kitchen and taproom, focusing on running heavy trays and bussing dirty dishware.
  • Pathway B (BOH): Automated frying assistance for high-volume Taproom snacks (fries/wings) to ensure consistency during peak rushes.
  • Pathway C (AI): Smart Kiosk/QR ordering in the Taproom to reduce bar lines and increase second-round drink sales via AI upselling.

Financial Model

  • Est. CAPEX: $50,000 - $65,000 (2 Units + Integration).
  • OPEX Impact: Target 15-25% reduction in non-service floor time (running/bussing).
  • Payback Period: < 14 Months via increased table turnover and reduced support staff hours.
