Client Analysis

Hudson Valley Hospice

Hudson Valley Hospice faces the critical challenge of balancing high-touch end-of-life care with the logistical realities of serving two semi-rural counties. The strategic implementation of AI-driven logistics and therapeutic robotics offers a pathway to increase patient capacity by 15-20% while significantly reducing clinician burnout. This portfolio outlines a transition from manual operations to a tech-enabled palliative platform.
