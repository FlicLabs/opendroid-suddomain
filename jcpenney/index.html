Automated Logistics & Robotics Integration Roadmap

01. Executive Summary

JCPenney is navigating a critical pivot point. The strategic vision is to transform legacy distribution centers into high-velocity omnichannel fulfillment nodes. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven sorting, JCPenney can move from a wave-based, batch-processing model to a continuous flow model, essential for competing with Amazon and Walmart delivery standards.

03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Transport)

Focuses on eliminating non-value-added walking time. AMRs meet associates in zones.

  • Tech: Locus / Fetch Robotics
  • Goal: Increase picks from 60 to 180 UPH

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Targeting high-volume, small-item categories like cosmetics (Thirteen Lune partnership) and jewelry.

  • Tech: RightHand Robotics / Exotec
  • Goal: 99.9% Order Accuracy

05. Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: The Foundation

Audit of Cedar Hill, TX facility. Data cleansing. Pilot deployment of 50 AMRs for holiday peak.

Year 3: Heavy Automation

Retrofitting primary e-commerce facilities with ASRS. Reduction of manual conveyor dependence.

Year 5: Autonomous Chain

Full predictive shipping. 40% reduction in overall logistics cost per unit.
