Executive Vision

For 2941 Mediterranean Street Food, automation is the key to scaling the "Bazaar" experience without compromising quality. By integrating Pathways A, B, and C, we shift the labor focus from repetitive logistics to culinary consistency and guest hospitality.

The proposed model creates a hybrid service environment: an "Express Lane" for digital efficiency and a "Traditional Lane" for guided customization, both supported by robotic back-end logistics.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Front-of-House Robotics

Deployment of autonomous bussing units to patrol dining areas. This removes the burden of heavy lifting from staff and ensures rapid table clearing during the lunch rush.

PATHWAY B Automated Culinary Prep

Integration of smart-grills and automated vegetable processors. This standardizes the cooking of proteins (Chicken/Steak) across all franchises, ensuring safety and consistency.

PATHWAY C AI Kiosks & Voice

Implementation of recommendation-engine kiosks. These units are programmed to upsell sides and drinks, increasing the average ticket size while reducing line congestion.
