Meridian HealthCare

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model for Integrated Recovery Networks

Executive Summary

Meridian HealthCare faces operational challenges distinct from general acute care. The optimization levers involve high-volume medication dispensing for Medication-Assisted Treatment (MAT), facility hygiene, and workflow automation. The dominant ROI driver is Pathway A (Automated Dispensing), projecting a net positive financial impact within 14 months.

Pathway A: Automated Dispensing

HIGHEST VALUE TARGET

Implementation of robotic dispensing cabinets and packaging units for MAT clinics. Replaces manual pouring with biometric-secured dosing.

  • Precision dosing eliminates error.
  • Reduces patient wait times by 60%.
  • Near-zero inventory shrinkage.

Pathway B: Autonomous Sanitation

OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY

Deployment of UV-C Disinfection Robots and autonomous floor scrubbers in high-traffic waiting areas and inpatient detox units.

  • Consistent pathogen reduction.
  • Reduces EVS labor hours.
  • Visible commitment to safety.
