Executive Summary

A Place for Mom moves from a labor-intensive advisory model to a high-velocity, tech-enabled logistics platform. By leveraging Software Robotics (RPA) and Telepresence, the company can resolve its primary bottleneck: lead conversion time. The strategy focuses on automating the administrative friction of senior care placement.
