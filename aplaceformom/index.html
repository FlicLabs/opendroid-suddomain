Executive Summary

Mary Free Bed is a leader in rehabilitative care with a massive regional network. The next phase of innovation shifts from device adoption to Network-Wide Integration. By leveraging AI-driven therapeutic dosing and autonomous logistics, MFB can significantly reduce Length of Stay (LOS) and operational overhead.
