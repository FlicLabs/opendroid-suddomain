01. Executive Summary

Capital Pump & Equipment is positioned to lead the sector by transitioning from traditional manual-heavy workflows to a Smart Manufacturing ecosystem. This report details a strategy to leverage Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to reduce bottlenecks, enhance safety during heavy lifting, and increase overall throughput by 30%.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Fabrication

Deployment of IP67 Cobots for precise arc welding of pump skids and machine tending for CNC component creation. Focus on reducing welder fatigue and increasing consistency.

Pathway B: Logistics

Utilization of Heavy-Payload AMRs for moving pump casings and engines between welding, painting, and assembly. Eliminates non-value-added forklift travel.
