Strategic Analysis & ROI Projections

Executive Summary

Eden Health's hybrid care model—combining digital access with in-home primary care—relies on the efficiency of its mobile workforce. The primary robotics opportunity lies in AI-driven logistics and urban mobility optimization. By automating dispatch and routing, Eden can significantly increase clinician utilization and reduce idle time, resulting in immediate revenue growth without increasing headcount. Secondary opportunities include portable diagnostic automation to expand the scope of in-home care.
