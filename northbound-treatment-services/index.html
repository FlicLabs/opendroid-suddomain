Executive Summary

Northbound Treatment Services operates in a high-touch environment where clinical excellence is often bottlenecked by administrative complexity and logistics. Our analysis identifies Operational Support Automation as the primary value driver. By deploying Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for revenue cycles, automated medication dispensing for safety, and telematics for client transport, Northbound can unlock significant margin expansion while reducing clinical burnout.

Pathway A: RPA & Revenue Cycle

Software robotics to handle repetitive tasks such as insurance verification, prior authorization, and claims submission. This reduces the Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) and frees up staff for patient care.

Safety Critical

Pathway B: Auto-Dispensing

Secure medication dispensing cabinets in detox units. Automates inventory tracking, reduces diversion risk, and significantly cuts down nursing time spent on med-passes.

Logistics

Pathway C: Fleet Telematics

AI-driven routing for the vans transporting clients between sober living and clinical centers. Reduces fuel costs, improves punctuality, and monitors driver safety.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Digital Foundation

Launch RPA for Intake/Billing. Install Fleet Telematics.

Year 2: Clinical Support

Deploy Automated Medication Dispensing in Detox. Expand RPA scope.

Year 3: Advanced Integration

Fully automated logistics manifests. Evaluation of facility cleaning robots.
