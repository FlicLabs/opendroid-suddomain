VISION

Executive Summary

To maintain competitive advantage, Dibble must transition from static batch processing to an agile, data-driven manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating Industry 4.0 technologies, specifically Collaborative Robots (Cobots) and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs), Dibble will unlock 24/7 production capability. This strategy moves the organization from reactive maintenance to predictive optimization, significantly reducing WIP latency and increasing overall yield.

STRATEGY

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Integration of ISO/TS 15066 compliant Cobots for machine tending. Focus on safety and precision to stabilize cycle times.

Pathway B: AMRs

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for material transport, eliminating manual forklift dependency.

Pathway C: Palletizing

Automated end-of-line systems with vision inspection to maximize throughput and ensure 100% quality compliance.

FINANCE

Financial Analysis

  • CAPEX: $60k - $120k per fully integrated robotic cell.
  • OPEX Savings: 15% reduction in annual overtime costs.
  • OEE Impact: Target increase of 20-30% via downtime reduction.
  • Breakeven: Projected at 14-16 months based on labor reallocation and scrap reduction.
