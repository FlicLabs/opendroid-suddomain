VISION

Executive Summary

Manhattan Beachwear is positioned to transition from a manual legacy model to a high-velocity automated fulfillment center. By leveraging AMRs and goods-to-person logic, the company can mitigate seasonal labor volatility while increasing shipping accuracy for both B2B and DTC channels.

STRATEGY

Implementation Roadmap

YR 1 Pilot Launch: 15 AMRs in "High Velocity" Pick Zones.
YR 2 WMS Integration & Fleet Expansion to 100% coverage.
YR 3 Install High-Density ASRS for seasonal storage.
Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Replacing manual push-carts with autonomous transport units to eliminate 50% of non-productive walking time.

Pathway B: Piece Picking

Utilizing robotic arms for unit-level picking to ensure 99.9% accuracy during high-volume summer peaks.

Pathway C: AI Logic

Predictive slotting algorithms that move high-demand SKUs to front-of-house locations automatically.
