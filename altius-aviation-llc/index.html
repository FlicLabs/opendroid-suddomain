Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Mobile units for vacuuming and surface wiping during turnarounds. Reduces labor dependency.

B. UV-C Disinfection Robots

Post-clean pathogen neutralization. Enhances passenger safety perception.

C. Exterior Washing Systems

Robotic gantries for fuselage washing. Improves fuel efficiency via drag reduction.

D. AI Inspection Bots

LiDAR and Camera-based structural integrity checks. Predictive maintenance.
