To support rapid site deployment, the manufacturing arm (Tommy Car Wash Systems) must transition from a job-shop model to a connected, flexible manufacturing ecosystem. Our strategy focuses on automating the "Wash Kit" fabrication line to ensure equipment availability is never the bottleneck for franchise growth.
