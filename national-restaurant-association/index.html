Executive Vision

The National Restaurant Association is positioned to lead a technological renaissance. By shifting from labor-dependent models to technology-enabled service, the industry can solve chronic staffing shortages while improving consistency. This blueprint focuses on robotics not as replacement, but as augmentation for the 500,000+ member businesses.
