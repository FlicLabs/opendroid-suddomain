Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of Goods-to-Person robots. Eliminates 15km of daily walking per picker. Increases pick rates to 200 UPH.

Pathway B: ASRS & Density

Vertical shuttle systems for high-value SKUs. Increases storage density by 400% in metro hubs.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive stocking. Algorithms pre-position inventory based on outbound forecast, cutting cycle time by 30%.

Implementation Roadmap

  • YEAR 1 Foundation & Pilot: WMS Integration and 10-Unit AMR Pilot in single zone.
  • YEAR 2 Scaling: Expansion to 50% facility coverage and introduction of ASRS for top SKUs.
  • YEAR 3 Optimization: Full AI Analytics integration; reduction of seasonal temporary labor.
  • YEAR 4 Regional Rollout: Replication of model to satellite distribution centers.
  • YEAR 5 Full Autonomy: "Dark Warehouse" capabilities for unsupervised night shifts.
