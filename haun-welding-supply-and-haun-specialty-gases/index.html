1. Executive Summary: The Smart Fill Vision

Haun Welding Supply and Haun Specialty Gases are positioned to transition from traditional batch processing to a "Smart Fill & Logistics" ecosystem. This strategy prioritizes safety and precision in gas filling operations.

By integrating Collaborative Robots (Cobots) for valve manipulation and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for cylinder transport, Haun can reduce manual handling hours by 25% while ensuring pharmaceutical-grade consistency in specialty gas mixtures.

2. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Valving

Focus: Ergonomic Safety & Torque Precision

  • Deployment of IP65-rated Cobots (e.g., UR10e) for cylinder maintenance.
  • Automated de-valving and re-valving with force-torque sensing.
  • Reduction in Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI).

Pathway B: Heavy Duty AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

  • Autonomous transport of gas cylinder cages (1000kg+ capacity).
  • Automated dock-to-fill-plant logistics.
  • Reduction of forklift traffic in high-density areas.
