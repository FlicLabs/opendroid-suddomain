Executive Synthesis

Operational Bottleneck

New Narrative faces a critical bottleneck in clinical labor supply, exacerbated by a high administrative burden where clinicians spend ~25-30% of their time on documentation rather than patient care.

Robotics Solution

Implementation of Ambient Clinical Intelligence (ACI) for workflow automation and a fleet of Socially Assistive Robots (SAR) for cognitive engagement. This shifts the model from linear labor scaling to tech-enabled capacity.
