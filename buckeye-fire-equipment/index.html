Executive Vision

Buckeye Fire Equipment is positioned to transition from traditional manufacturing to a technology-enabled Smart Factory. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport, the organization will secure higher throughput, reduce ergonomic injuries associated with heavy pressure vessels, and ensure 100% quality compliance in a highly regulated industry.

Operational Profile

  • Hub: Kings Mountain, North Carolina
  • Type: Hybrid (Discrete & Process)
  • Key Bottlenecks: Manual cylinder transfer, Valve torque fatigue, EOL palletizing.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

01. Cobot Assembly

High-payload collaborative arms for precise valve torquing and cylinder loading. Reduces repetitive strain injuries.

02. AMR Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving heavy steel shells and chemical agents. Improves WIP flow efficiency.

03. EOL Palletizing

Automated stacking of finished goods with integrated vision systems for final quality gatekeeping.
