01 // EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) sector faces a critical inflection point defined by labor scarcity and consumer demand for hyper-personalization. The strategic imperative for the Consumer Brands Association is a shift from rigid, high-volume lines to agile, data-driven ecosystems. Our model predicts a substantial reduction in Cost Per Unit (CPU) through the integration of Collaborative Robotics and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs).

04 // ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision. Deployment of IP66-rated cobots for machine tending and assembly. Enables collaboration without physical caging in brownfield sites.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow. Autonomous Mobile Robots replace manual forklifts for line-side replenishment, decoupling labor from transit and improving floor safety.

Pathway C: EOL Auto

Focus: Throughput. Heavy-payload palletizing arms and vision systems ensure 99.9% label accuracy and eliminate end-of-line bottlenecks.
