XYMOGEN

Robotics Consultation & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

Xymogen is a leader in professional-grade functional medicine. As the company scales its WholeScripts direct-to-patient logistics and cGMP manufacturing, operational complexity increases.

The Opportunity: Implementing Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) in fulfillment and AGVs in manufacturing can yield over $1M in annual net operational savings while ensuring sterile compliance.

Operational Scale

  • Facility: 136,000+ sq. ft. HQ in Orlando.
  • Model: B2B2C (Practitioner to Patient).
  • Certifications: TGA, cGMP, NSF International.

The Bottleneck

  • High-mix, low-volume picking for individual patient orders.
  • Heavy manual material handling in sterile zones.
  • Strict audit trails required for every bottle.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics AMRs

Highest ROI

Scope: "Swarm" robots for WholeScripts fulfillment center.

Impact:

  • 2x-3x increase in picking speed.
  • Eliminates 50% of staff walking time.
  • ~$1M Net Annual Benefit.

Pathway B: Production AGVs

Safety & Compliance

Scope: Heavy-load transport for raw powder drums.

Impact:

  • Reduces cross-contamination risks.
  • Prevents workplace lifting injuries.
  • Digital chain of custody for ingredients.

Pathway C: Vision QC

Quality Assurance

Scope: Automated visual inspection on packaging lines.

Impact:

  • Detects cracks/dents at high speed.
  • Label verification (OCR) prevents recalls.
  • Automated audit documentation.
