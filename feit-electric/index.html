Executive Vision

Feit Electric stands at a pivotal junction between traditional manufacturing and Industry 4.0. To maintain dominance in the Smart Home sector, our research indicates a shift to Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS) is critical. This strategy leverages collaborative robotics to manage high-mix SKU complexity while securing margins against rising labor costs.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Assembly Precision.
Deploying collaborative arms for LED driver screwing and thermal paste application. Reduces RSI and guarantees thermal consistency.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Logistics Flow.
Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle material transport between injection molding and assembly, eliminating non-value-added walking time.

Pathway C: EOL

Focus: Throughput & QC.
Automated palletizing and vision inspection to meet strict retail display requirements and clear shipping bottlenecks.

Implementation Roadmap
