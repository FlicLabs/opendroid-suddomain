Executive Summary

Somafina, a contract manufacturer for nutraceuticals and supplements, stands to gain significant margin expansion through the deployment of end-of-line robotics. By shifting from manual case packing and material handling to automated systems, the company can resolve bottleneck issues, improve GMP compliance, and increase total facility throughput.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: End-of-Line Packaging (High ROI)

Implementation of robotic palletizers and case packers. This eliminates heavy lifting and speeds up the transition from bottle filling to warehousing.

Pathway B: Autonomous Logistics (AMRs)

Deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport raw ingredients and bulk mixtures between weighing rooms, blenders, and encapsulation suites, ensuring safety and traceability.

Pathway C: Automated Quality Inspection

Vision systems for high-speed detection of defects in softgels and gummies, ensuring premium quality control without slowing down production lines.
