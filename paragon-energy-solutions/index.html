01. Executive Summary

Paragon Energy Solutions is positioned to revolutionize its high-compliance manufacturing through Industry 4.0 adoption. By shifting to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS), Paragon will enhance traceability, safety, and precision. The strategy focuses on reallocating skilled labor from repetitive hazardous tasks to high-value QA roles, ensuring strict adherence to NRC standards while significantly reducing lead times.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: Cobots

Deployment of UR10e or Fanuc CRX units for CNC machine tending. Operates cage-free to ensure continuous production during shift changes.

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for WIP flow. Eliminates manual cart pushing, reducing fatigue and optimizing material replenishment.

PATHWAY C: Auto-QA

Vision-equipped robotic arms for end-of-line palletizing and inspection. Creates a digital audit trail for every component shipped.
