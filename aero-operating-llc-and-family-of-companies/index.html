Strategic Research Report

Open Droids presents a comprehensive analysis for the integration of autonomous robotic systems into the Aero Operating LLC ecosystem. This transition from manual-heavy processes to a collaborative human-robot workforce is designed to reduce ground time, lower injury rates, and ensure aviation compliance.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning
HEPA filtration and debris removal during turnaround windows.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection
Clinical-grade sterilization for lavatories and galleys.

Pathway C

Exterior Washing
Low-water robotic gantries to prevent corrosion.

Pathway D

AI Inspection
Computer vision bots for fuselage integrity checks.
