Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

KARE is a digital labor marketplace connecting post-acute care communities with qualified caregivers on a per-shift basis. The strongest automation levers are (1) Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for credentialing, (2) Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) integration for hybrid labor models, and (3) Telepresence for virtual clinical oversight.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Workforce AI

Scope: RPA for onboarding and AI for predictive shift matching.

Impact: Reduces time-to-fill and operational overhead. Increases shift fill rate to ~95%.

Pathway B: RaaS Partnership

Scope: Bundling physical service robots (cleaning/dining) with human labor shifts.

Impact: Differentiates KARE as a total workforce solution, not just a temp agency.

Pathway C: Telepresence

Scope: Virtual RN supervision via mobile robot kiosks.

Impact: unlocks high-margin "fractional nursing" revenue streams.

Financial Impact (Pathway A)

Implementing algorithmic orchestration and RPA offers immediate ROI through margin expansion.
