Open Droids Research: Punch Pizza

Strategic Automation Analysis & Implementation Plan

Executive Summary

Punch Pizza is positioned to adopt a Hybrid Hospitality Model. By automating the runner and busser functions, the human workforce can pivot entirely to the artisanal wood-fired cooking process. This strategy maximizes throughput during the high-velocity lunch window while preserving the authentic Neapolitan experience.

Organizational Profile

  • HQ: Minneapolis-St. Paul, MN
  • Model: Fast Casual (Counter Order / Table Delivery)
  • Core Challenge: FOH Bottlenecks during peak table turnover.
  • Tech Fit: High. Open floor plans facilitate LiDAR navigation.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service

Goal: Table Turnover & Support.

Deployment of autonomous food runners to deliver pizzas immediately from the oven. Includes "bus tub" mode for rapid table clearing.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Goal: Consistency & Prep.

Automated vegetable slicing and dough portioning. Focus on dish-pit automation to support high volume of reusable dishware.

Pathway C: AI Kiosks

Goal: Upselling & Accuracy.

Intelligent kiosks for the lunch rush. AI recommends wine pairings and add-ons, increasing average ticket size.

Implementation Roadmap

Y1
Pilot Program: Deploy 2 bots at high-volume locations (e.g., Grand Ave).
Y2
Expansion: Roll out to 50% of locations. Integrate POS with AI Kiosks.
Y3
Integration: Full fleet rollout. Begin BOH Dishwashing automation tests.
Y4
Optimization: Redesign floor plans based on 3 years of robot traffic data.
Y5
Maturity: Full Smart Restaurant ecosystem synchronization.
