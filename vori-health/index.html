Executive Summary: Vori Health

Vori Health is positioned to transition from a service-heavy MSK provider to a scalable platform via "Software Robotics." The primary opportunity is Pathway A: Computer Vision & AI Motion Tracking, which decouples clinician time from patient throughput. This enables a shift from 1:1 synchronous care to 1:Many asynchronous monitoring, driving massive margin improvements.

Pathway A: High Priority

AI Motion Tracking

Concept: Browser-based computer vision to track exercise form without wearables.

Impact: Increases therapist panel capacity from ~40 to 80+ active episodes.

ROI: Immediate payback; est. £8M+ incremental annual benefit.

Pathway B: Long Term

Robotic Rehab Hardware

Concept: Haptic resistance devices sent to post-op patients for controlled load management.

Impact: Captures high-acuity surgical recovery market.

ROI: 18-24 month payback due to logistics/hardware costs.

Pathway C: Operational

Admin RPA

Concept: Automating insurance verification and PROM reporting.

Impact: Reduces SG&A by 20-30%, ensuring scalability.

ROI: 6-month payback; foundational for growth.
