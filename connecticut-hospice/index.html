Executive Summary

Connecticut Hospice, the pioneer of US hospice care, faces operational challenges regarding nursing shortages and physical strain. Our analysis recommends a "Compassionate Automation" strategy: deploying assistive robotics for patient handling and autonomous logistics units. This protects the workforce, enhances dignity, and generates significant ROI through injury reduction.
