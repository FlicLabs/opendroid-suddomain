The Eye Institute of Utah

Executive Summary

The Eye Institute of Utah is positioned to leverage robotics not merely for clinical novelty, but as a primary driver of throughput and margin expansion. By integrating Surgical Precision Robotics (Pathway A) and Autonomous Sterile Processing (Pathway B), the institute can unlock over £2M in annual incremental net benefit.

The core strategy relies on increasing "Premium Conversion" (upselling high-margin IOLs) via robotic precision guarantees, while simultaneously reducing OR turnover times to process higher daily volumes.
