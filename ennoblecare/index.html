Ennoble Care: Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Executive Summary

Ennoble Care shifts the care paradigm from reactive dispatch to longitudinal, relationship-based home care. The greatest robotics opportunities lie not in replacing clinicians, but in optimizing their reach through AI-driven logistics and enhancing patient safety via automated medication dispensing and telepresence. The ROI is driven by a massive reduction in hospital readmissions and a ~24% increase in clinician capacity.

Organizational Profile

Core Model Continuum of care: HBPC, Palliative, and Hospice. Focus on preventing decline in chronic patients.
Operations Multi-state operation with interdisciplinary teams. High "windshield time" is the primary bottleneck.
Economics Value-Based Care & Shared Savings. Margin is created by keeping patients OUT of the hospital.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics AI

Software Robotics for Workforce Orchestration. Dynamic routing that respects continuity of care.

$9.5M Net Annual Benefit
Pathway B: Telepresence

Virtual Rounding & RPM. Physicians visit virtually; aides visit physically. Continuous vital monitoring.

$3M ER Avoidance Value
Pathway C: Med Robotics

Automated Dispensing. Smart locking pill dispensers to prevent overdose and ensure adherence.

Safety Primary Driver

Implementation Roadmap

1
Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)
Deploy Logistics AI to optimize routes and scheduling. Establish data baselines for travel time and visit capacity.
2
Phase 2: Targeted Hardware (Months 7-18)
Pilot medication robotics with top 50 high-risk patients. Deploy telepresence robots for weekend on-call coverage.
3
Phase 3: Scale & Integrate (Months 18+)
Full scale hardware rollout. Integration of RPM data directly into the logistics engine to auto-trigger visits.
