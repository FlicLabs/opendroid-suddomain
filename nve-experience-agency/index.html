Executive Summary

NVE Experience Agency: Robotics & ROI

NVE operates at the intersection of logistics and culture. Our analysis identifies a high-value opportunity to transition from renting to owning robotic assets. By deploying In-House Experiential Robotics and Warehouse AMRs, NVE can secure over £1.5M in cumulative savings over 5 years while increasing social media throughput by 40%.
