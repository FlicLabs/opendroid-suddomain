Executive Summary

The integration of robotic automation into aviation cleaning and maintenance operations represents a transformative opportunity for MidAmerica St. Louis Airport. By deploying a fleet of specialized autonomous units, the airport can achieve unprecedented levels of operational efficiency, hygiene standards, and cost predictability. This report outlines a strategic vision where Deep Space Blue and Electric Purple robotic systems seamlessly navigate the tarmac and terminal, reducing labor dependency while enhancing turnaround speed.
