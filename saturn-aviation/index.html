Automated Maintenance & Cleaning Integration

Executive Summary

Saturn Aviation stands at the precipice of a technological revolution. By shifting from manual labor to autonomous cleaning and inspection systems, the airline can expect drastic reductions in turnaround times and operational hazards. This report details the strategic integration of robotics into your fleet operations, ensuring compliance, safety, and efficiency.

PATHWAY C

Exterior Washing

Gantry and drone-based washing systems to improve aerodynamics and fuel efficiency.

PATHWAY D

AI Inspection

Computer vision bots for detecting micro-fractures and fuselage damage.
