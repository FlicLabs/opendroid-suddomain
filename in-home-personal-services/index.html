Executive Summary

In Home Personal Services operates in a high-touch, labor-intensive market. By pivoting to a technology-first model, the franchise can solve its most critical bottleneck: caregiver turnover and utilization. Our analysis identifies AI-driven logistics as the primary wealth generator, unlocking millions in uncaptured revenue. Secondary investments in assistive robotics will drastically reduce workers' compensation claims, solidifying the operational foundation.

Opportunity Deep Dive: Pathway A

Logic: The franchise model bleeds revenue through inefficient scheduling (gaps) and administrative overhead.

  • Scope: Deploy "Software Robotics" to automate dispatch and matching.
  • Impact: Reduces admin time by 30-40%, allowing franchise owners to focus on sales rather than rostering.
  • Financials: Estimated $2.5M top-line growth network-wide simply by filling shifts that are currently declined due to manual scheduling friction.
