The logistics landscape is shifting from static storage to dynamic, high-velocity flow. For G&P, the next frontier is the digitization of the warehouse floor. This research outlines a strategic pivot toward an Automated Distribution Ecosystem. 02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Transport

Replacing manual forklifts for long-haul internal transport. Expect a 40% increase in pallets moved per hour compared to manual operation during peak fatigue shifts.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of Goods-to-Person (GTP) systems. Increases pick accuracy from 98.5% to 99.9% while utilizing vertical warehouse space to reduce footprint by 30%.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer Vision at bay doors to automate damage scanning and predict outbound truck loading patterns, maximizing trailer fill rates.

03. Implementation Roadmap

YEAR 1: FOUNDATION & PILOT Network infrastructure upgrades (5G/Wi-Fi 6) and pilot AMR launch in primary SC/GA hub.
YEAR 2: EXPANSION Deployment of High-Density ASRS and integration of Robotic Fleet Management with WMS.
YEAR 3: OPTIMIZATION Full AI Vision rollout at docking bays and "Dark Warehouse" capability for night shifts.
YEAR 5: AUTONOMY Predictive AI for labor scheduling and network-wide fleet autonomy.
