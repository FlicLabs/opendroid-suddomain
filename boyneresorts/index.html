Boyne Resorts

Robotics & Automation Strategy Portfolio

Executive Summary

Boyne Resorts, North America's third-largest mountain operator, faces critical challenges in labor seasonality and operational bottlenecks. Our research identifies Autonomous Snow Operations and High-Volume Hospitality Automation as the primary levers for value creation. The strategy shifts the operational model from labor-dependent to asset-optimized.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: Snow Ops

High Value Industrial

Scope: Autonomous grooming vehicles & AI snowmaking.

Labor reduction: 30-40%

Fuel savings: 10-15%

Targets the most difficult overnight shifts to recruit.

Pathway B: F&B Bots

High Speed Service

Scope: Robotic burger stations & beverage dispensers.

Throughput lift: 20-30%

Payback: 1.5 Years

Solves the critical "Lunch Rush" revenue bottleneck.

Pathway C: Logistics

Guest Exp Storage

Scope: Gantry-based ski valet & rental storage.

Wait time cut: >50%

Space saved: Vertical Stack

Premium differentiation for flagship properties.
