01. Executive Summary

The HVAC/R distribution market is evolving. Young Supply Company is positioned to leverage automation to solve critical labor shortages and SKU complexity issues. By moving to a hybrid automated fulfillment model, Young Supply can secure its leadership in the Michigan/Ohio market.

02. Robotics Pathways

A. AMRs for Heavy Transport: Automated movement of compressors and bulk units to shipping docks, reducing manual forklift travel by 50%.

B. Piece Picking (ASRS): Vertical Lift Modules for small parts (fittings/controls). Increases pick accuracy to 99.9% and recovers floor space.

C. AI Logistics: Predictive stocking based on weather patterns and seasonal contractor demand.
