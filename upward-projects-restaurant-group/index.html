Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House Serving

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) handle food running and bussing. Allows servers to cover 25% more tables by eliminating travel time.

Pathway B: Back-of-House Automation

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Robotic arms for frying and grilling. Ensures identical cook times, reduces food waste (COGS), and minimizes burns/slips.

Pathway C: AI Order Interfaces

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

AI Kiosks and Voice Agents drive average check size up via data-driven recommendations (12-18% increase) and eliminate order errors.

Recommendation: Start with Pathway A (FOH) for immediate labor relief, followed by Pathway C (AI) for revenue growth.
