Executive Summary

Infinity Rehab faces critical pressures from staffing shortages and value-based payment models (PDPM). Our analysis identifies a high-ROI opportunity to transition from a manual labor model to a technology-enabled platform. By deploying clinical robotics (exoskeletons) and AI-driven workforce orchestration, Infinity Rehab can unlock significant margin expansion while improving patient outcomes.
