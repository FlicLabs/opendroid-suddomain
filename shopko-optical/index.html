01. Executive Summary

Shopko Optical stands at a critical juncture where high-precision optical manufacturing meets high-velocity retail distribution. Our vision is to implement a Seamless Optical Fulfillment Loop.

By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for delicate lens transport and ASRS for high-density frame storage, Shopko Optical can reduce material breakage, eliminate non-value-added walking time, and achieve a 40% reduction in order-to-delivery time.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Deployment of conveyor-top runners to transport lens trays between blocking, generating, and coating stations. This keeps skilled technicians at their stations, boosting lab throughput by approx. 35%.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking (ASRS)

Utilization of Shuttle ASRS for frame inventory. This consolidates storage footprint by 85% and utilizes Pick-to-Light technology to virtually eliminate prescription-to-frame mismatch errors.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

Predictive batching software that routes work dynamically based on coating chamber bottlenecks and lens complexity.
