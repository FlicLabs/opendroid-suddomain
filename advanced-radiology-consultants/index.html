Executive Summary

Advanced Radiology Consultants (ARC) is positioned to transform from a diagnostic service provider to an automated intelligence hub. By leveraging Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for administrative friction and AI-driven software robotics for image acquisition, ARC can decouple revenue growth from labor constraints. The projected outcome is a 20% increase in network throughput and a massive reduction in administrative overhead.

Current Profile

  • Volume: ~350,000 Annual Exams
  • Bottlenecks: Staffing shortages, Scheduling gaps, Authorization denials
  • Opportunity: High-margin throughput expansion

Pathway A

Admin RPA

Implementation of software robots to handle insurance verification, coding, and scheduling.

Impact: £2M/yr Savings

Pathway B

Acquisition AI

AI interfacing with MRI/CT scanners to reconstruct images with fewer samples, reducing scan time.

Impact: +30% Capacity

Pathway C

Tele-Robotics

Robotic ultrasound arms allowing remote expert manipulation and local patient support.

Impact: Labor Solution
