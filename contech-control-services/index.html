Executive Summary

Contech Control Services is positioned to transition from manual craftsmanship to a High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) flexible manufacturing model. By integrating collaborative robotics, the company can augment skilled technicians, reduce lead times for custom control panels, and capture a larger market share in the Gulf Coast region.

Robotic Pathways

Pathway A: COBOTS

Automated backplane drilling and terminal torqueing to ensure precision and reduce fatigue.

Pathway B: AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving wire spools and components, optimizing technician time.

Pathway C: VISION

Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) for schematic verification and end-of-line quality checks.
