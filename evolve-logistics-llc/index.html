02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deploying Lidar-guided Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport. Focus on "Goods-to-Person" to eliminate 60% of picker walking time.

Pathway B: Piece Picking & ASRS

High-density automated storage systems paired with robotic arms. Increases order accuracy to 99.9% and reduces storage footprint by 50%.

Pathway C: AI & Predictive Logistics

Integration of Machine Learning to predict order surges and optimize dock scheduling, preventing bottlenecks before they occur.
