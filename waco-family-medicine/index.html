Robotics Opportunity Pathways

1. Administrative Workflow Automation (Highest Value)

Scope: RPA for billing/authorizations, ambient scribing, automated scheduling.

  • Impact: Reduces no-shows from 20% to 12% (adding ~20k visits).
  • Efficiency: Saves providers ~1-2 hours daily on documentation.
  • Value: $2.5M - $5M annual revenue/collection impact.

2. Supply Chain & Facility Logistics

Scope: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for clinic interconnects, automated inventory tracking.

  • Impact: Reduces manual transport time and stockouts.
  • Value: Labor savings (0.5-1 FTE per hub) + 5-10% inventory waste reduction.

3. Patient Triage & Diagnostic Support

Scope: Automated vital stations, AI-assisted visual triage kiosks.

  • Impact: Standardizes intake, saves 3-5 mins per patient.
  • Value: Supports nurse-led care models in school-based clinics.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Administrative Automation

Pilot ambient scribing (3 clinics) -> Network-wide RPA deployment -> Full administrative rollout.

Year 2: Logistics Optimization

AMR pilot for central hub delivery -> Automated inventory in 5 locations.

Year 3: Clinical Support Expansion

Deploy triage kiosks in school-based health centers and high-volume primary care clinics.
