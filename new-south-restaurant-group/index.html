Executive Vision

New South Restaurant Group represents the pinnacle of Southern hospitality. Our strategic research indicates that integrating collaborative robotics will not replace this hospitality, but elevate it. By automating low-skill logistics (running and busing), we empower your waitstaff to remain in their sections, increasing guest interaction and average ticket size.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Reducing server fatigue and increasing table turns.

  • Automated food running from kitchen to zone.
  • Bulk busing capabilities for faster table reset.

Pathway B: AI Voice Agents

Focus: Reservation accuracy.

  • Zero missed calls during dinner rush.
  • Allows host to focus on greeting guests.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1 Pilot Phase: Deploy 2 units at 'Table 100'. Staff integration training.
Year 2 Standardization: Rollout to all full-service locations. AI Voice integration.
Year 3 BOH Optimization: Automated frying assistants for appetizer stations.
Year 5 Full Autonomy: Integrated FOH/BOH telemetry for predictive cooking.
