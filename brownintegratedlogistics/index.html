Executive Vision

Brown Integrated Logistics is positioned to transition from traditional warehousing to a high-velocity, automated fulfillment model. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for internal transport and leveraging AI for predictive inbound logistics, Brown can decouple throughput from labor market volatility.

The Opportunity: Replace long-haul forklift travel with autonomous units, allowing human operators to focus on high-value supervisory tasks.
