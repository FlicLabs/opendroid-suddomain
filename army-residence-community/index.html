Executive Summary

Army Residence Community (ARC) faces critical challenges in labor availability and campus logistics. By implementing a tri-phased robotics strategy—focusing on Dining Automation, Logistics Tugs, and AI Safety—ARC can shift staff focus from low-value transport tasks to high-value resident care. The projected financial impact includes a net margin recovery of over $130,000 annually in the initial phase alone.
