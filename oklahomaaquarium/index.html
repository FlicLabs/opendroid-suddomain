Oklahoma Aquarium

Robotics Consultation & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

The Oklahoma Aquarium faces a unique operational challenge: maintaining the world's largest captive collection of bull sharks. The current model relies on human divers, which incurs high risk and insurance costs. Our analysis identifies Automated Underwater Tank Maintenance as the primary value driver, capable of reducing hazardous dive hours by up to 80% while ensuring pristine visitor experiences.
