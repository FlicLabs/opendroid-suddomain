Client: Gulfport-Biloxi Intl Airport

Executive Summary

Gulfport-Biloxi International Airport is positioned to become a Tier-1 Smart Hub prototype. By integrating autonomous cleaning and maintenance robotics, the airport can drastically reduce operational friction, improve passenger safety, and offer superior ground handling services to partner carriers. This report outlines a shift from reactive manual labor to predictive, automated workflows.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Cabin Cleaning

High-volume suction and electrostatic spraying to reduce turnaround time by 40%.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous pathogen neutralization for terminals and galleys (99.9% efficacy).

Pathway C

Exterior Washing

Gantry and drone systems for fuselage cleaning, reducing water usage by 70%.

Pathway D

AI Inspection

LIDAR-equipped rovers for continuous FOD and runway pavement monitoring.
