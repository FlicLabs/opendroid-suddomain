Iris Telehealth

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

01 / Executive Summary

The Opportunity: Moving from a staffing model to a tech-enabled logistics platform.

Iris Telehealth is uniquely positioned to leverage Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to solve the industry's biggest bottleneck: credentialing lag. While physical robotics (telepresence) offers a strategic differentiator in ERs, the dominant ROI lies in "software robotics" that automate administrative friction, projected to yield ~£1.8M in annual net benefit with rapid payback.

Model B2B Telepsychiatry Partnerships
Scale 200+ Health Systems / ~500 Clinicians
Key Friction Admin Burden & Credentialing (90+ Days)
02 / Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA & Admin

Highest ROI

Software robots to automate credentialing, licensing, and EMR data entry.

  • Reduces credentialing lag by 30-40%
  • Saves 3-5 admin hours/clinician/week
  • Accelerates time-to-revenue

Pathway B: Telepresence

Strategic

Mobile, self-driving robots for Emergency Departments (EDs).

  • Psychiatrists "drive" into rooms independently
  • Reduces burden on ED nursing staff
  • Premium service differentiator

Pathway C: Clinical AI

Workflow

NLP agents for automated documentation and risk scoring.

  • Reduces charting time by 50%
  • Increases throughput (1-2 more patients/shift)
  • Improves clinician retention
