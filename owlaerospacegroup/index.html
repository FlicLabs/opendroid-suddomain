01. Executive Vision

OWL Aerospace is positioned to transition from manual legacy cleaning to a fully autonomous ground support ecosystem. By integrating robotics, we target a drastic reduction in Turnaround Time (TAT) and Variable Operating Costs (VOC), moving the company towards a "Lights Out" maintenance model.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Goal: TAT Efficiency. Deploying AMRs for vacuuming and trash collection can reduce cabin prep time by 8-12 minutes per flight.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Goal: Fuel Savings. Automated dry-washing reduces aerodynamic drag, potentially saving 0.5% - 1.0% in annual fuel burn while conserving 90% water.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Goal: Safety. Drone-based fuselage scanning reduces inspection times from 6 hours to 45 minutes.
