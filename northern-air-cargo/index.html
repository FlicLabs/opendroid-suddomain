OPEN DROIDS

Strategic Automation Research

Client: Northern Air Cargo

Executive Summary

Northern Air Cargo is positioned to revolutionize its operational efficiency through strategic robotics integration. By addressing the unique challenges of the Anchorage hub—specifically cold weather turnaround and cargo hold maintenance—Open Droids proposes a hybrid workforce model. This automation strategy aims to reduce aircraft downtime, enhance fuel efficiency via aerodynamic optimization, and ensure consistent biosecurity across the fleet.

Profile & Fleet

Hub: Ted Stevens Anchorage (ANC)

Fleet: Boeing 737-300/400/800, B767-300

Focus: Critical cargo logistics for Alaska & Caribbean.

Operational challenges include extreme weather de-icing support and heavy labor requirements for large-body exterior washing.

Robotics Pathways

A. Interior: Cargo hold debris removal & cockpit sanitization.
B. Disinfection: UV-C towers for livestock/perishable transport hygiene.
C. Exterior: Gantry-free washing & de-icing assistance.
D. Inspection: AI drones for stress fracture & icing detection.
