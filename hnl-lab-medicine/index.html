HNL Lab Medicine Opportunities

Executive Summary

HNL Lab Medicine operates as a high-volume diagnostic network requiring precision, speed, and reliability. The integration of robotics focuses on automating the pre-analytical and post-analytical workflow to reduce bottlenecks. The primary opportunity lies in Total Lab Automation (TLA) which allows for a 50% increase in capacity while reducing manual touchpoints by 75%. The secondary focus is on logistics and internal transport, ensuring sample integrity from collection to result.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

1. Central Laboratory Automation (TLA)

High-value automation of the sample processing track. Eliminates manual sorting and centrifugation.

  • Scope: Automated track systems, robotic arms for aliquoting.
  • Impact: 30-50% throughput increase; 15-25% TAT improvement.
  • Value Driver: Capacity expansion without real estate growth.

2. Logistics Automation (AMRs)

Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport within the lab and hospital network.

  • Scope: Courier robots for ER-to-Lab transport.
  • Impact: 10-15% reduction in transport labor; 24/7 availability.
  • Value Driver: Reliability and cost avoidance.

3. Point-of-Care Robotics

Automated dispensing and micro-sampling to standardize patient intake.

  • Scope: Automated dispensing cabinets; robotic phlebotomy guides.
  • Impact: Reduced medication errors; standardized sample volumes.
  • Value Driver: Patient safety and liability reduction.
