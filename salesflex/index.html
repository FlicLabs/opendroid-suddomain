The transition from manual pick-and-pack to a Hybrid Automated Fulfillment ecosystem is critical. SalesFlex must leverage robotics not just for cost reduction, but to enable late-cutoff same-day shipping and 99.9% inventory accuracy.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Predictive engines for smart inventory placement based on real-time SKU velocity.
