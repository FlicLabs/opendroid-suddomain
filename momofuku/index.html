Organizational Profile

Brand Overview

Founded by Chef David Chang, Momofuku is a culinary leader blending fine dining with fast-casual innovation. The brand focuses on ingredient-driven simplicity and bold flavors.

Locations: Concentrated in NYC, Las Vegas, Toronto.
Cuisine: Modern Asian-American, Noodles, Buns, BBQ.

Service Models

  • Fine Dining (Ko): Full-service, high-touch, tasting menus.
  • Fast-Casual (Noodle Bar, Ssäm Bar): Counter-service/QR, high-volume, rapid turnover.
  • Hybrid Operations: Complex kitchens requiring consistency across distributed locations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A. Front-of-House (FOH)

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Automated runners for heavy tray carrying and bussing. Allows servers to focus on hospitality rather than logistics. Increases covers per shift.

B. Back-of-House (BOH)

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Automated frying and grilling stations. Ensures precise execution of signature items (fried chicken, buns) and reduces burn risks in high-heat zones.

C. Order & Voice AI

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

AI Kiosks and Voice Agents for off-premise orders. Drives average check size through algorithmic suggestions and reduces remake waste.

Financial Model

Investment & Savings

  • CAPEX: $70k - $90k estimated for pilot (2 FOH + 1 BOH).
  • OPEX Reduction: Target 15-25% labor cost savings.
  • Waste Reduction: 5-10% reduction in food waste via precision cooking.

ROI Projection

Revenue Impact: +10-15% daily covers via improved turnover.
Payback Logic: Combination of labor displacement (2-3 FTE) and incremental revenue.
