Executive Summary

The strategic vision for Simon Pearce is "Invisible Automation." By utilizing robotics for heavy lifting (bussing stoneware/glass) and back-of-house precision, we reduce physical strain on staff and extend longevity, allowing servers to remain guest-focused. This is a hybrid service model where technology supports artisanal excellence.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A: FOH Serving & Bussing (Priority)

Rationale: Simon Pearce's signature heavy stoneware creates ergonomic risks.
Application: Deployment of autonomous shelf runners to transport dishes from kitchen to station.
Outcome: Servers stay at tables; robots do the heavy hauling.

PATHWAY B: BOH Automated Prep

Rationale: Consistency in high-volume items like Vermont Cheddar Soup.
Application: Precision automated sous-vide and slicing systems.

PATHWAY C: AI Voice Agents

Rationale: High volume of tourist/reservation calls.
Application: AI agents handling FAQs to free up host staff.
