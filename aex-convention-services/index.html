01. Executive Summary

AEX Convention Services operates in an industry defined by rigid deadlines and fluctuating labor availability. By shifting from manual material handling to an AI-orchestrated logistics model, AEX can address its primary bottlenecks: drayage inefficiency and overtime costs.

The dominant ROI driver is Pathway A (Autonomous Material Handling), capable of reducing temporary labor dependance and accelerating event turnaround times.

02. Operational Profile

  • Core Model:
    General Service Contractor (GSC) for trade shows, handling drayage, install/dismantle, and decoration.
  • Critical Bottlenecks:
    Tight move-in windows (12-48 hours), high reliance on union/temp labor, freight tracking gaps.
  • Strategic Fit:
    Ideal for logistics-focused AMRs and large-scale floor cleaning robotics.
