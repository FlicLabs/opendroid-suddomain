Executive Vision

Havertys faces a unique challenge: the "Big and Bulky" logistics sector. Standard e-commerce robots cannot lift a sectional sofa. Our strategy pivots to Heavy-Payload Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs).

By automating the long-haul transport across distribution centers, we reduce workman's comp claims and increase dock-to-stock velocity.

Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: Heavy AMRs

Deployment of autonomous tuggers (e.g., OTTO/Fetch) to move palletized furniture without human guidance.

PATHWAY B: VNA Automation

Automated turret trucks for accessories (rugs/lamps), reclaiming floor space for high-margin furniture.

PATHWAY C: AI Logistics

Predictive yard management to sync inbound trucks with outbound delivery routes.
