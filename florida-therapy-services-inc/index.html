Executive Summary

Florida Therapy Services Inc. (FTS) stands at a pivotal transition point. As a leader in community-based behavioral health across Florida, the primary constraints are not clinical expertise but administrative burden and logistical friction. This analysis identifies Software Robotics (RPA) as the immediate high-yield lever to recapture lost clinician hours, with Socially Assistive Robotics offering a secondary strategic advantage in pediatric and foster care outcomes.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Admin & Clinical Software Robotics

Focus: Automated intake, AI-driven progress note generation, and predictive scheduling.

  • Eliminates 40% of manual documentation time.
  • Enables +3 billable patient hours per clinician/week.
  • Low capital expenditure; high immediate impact.

Pathway B: Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR)

Focus: Therapeutic robots (e.g., Moxie) for foster care and pediatric autism therapy.

  • Differentiation factor for state RFP bidding.
  • Enhances emotional regulation training between human sessions.
  • Strategic brand builder for pediatric services.

Pathway C: Remote Presence & Safety

Focus: Telepresence robots for rural psychiatry and smart dispensers for medication adherence.

  • Reduces travel time for high-cost psychiatrists.
  • Improves safety for home-visit case managers.
  • Reduces readmission risks for severe psychiatric cases.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Immediate

Phase 2: Innovation
