02. Opportunity Pathways

A. Internal Transport (AMR)

Deployment of heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace forklift travel. Focus on receiving dock to reserve storage transit.

B. Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of Shuttle ASRS for slow-moving SKUs (Health/Beauty). Vision-guided arms for break-pack accuracy.

C. AI Predictive Logistics

Synchronization of inbound vendor data with outbound replenishment to minimize perishable dock time and spoilage.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1 Pilot Launch: Deploy AMRs for dunnage/pallets in one high-volume FL DC. Install single-aisle ASRS test.
  • Year 2 Integration: Connect robotic streams to WMS. Refine AI algorithms for cold-chain route optimization.
  • Year 3 Expansion: Roll out successful modules to 5 regional DCs (GA, NC). Retrofit frozen food sections.
  • Year 5 Autonomy: Full "Lights-Out" capabilities in storage zones. Store inventory automatically triggers robotic retrieval.
