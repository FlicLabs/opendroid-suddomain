Gas South District • Robotics Portfolio • 2024-2025

Executive Summary

Gas South District represents a complex operational ecosystem blending a 13,000-seat arena, a convention center, and a theater. The primary operational bottlenecks are labor-intensive facility changeovers and vast perimeter security needs. Our analysis identifies Industrial Floor Care and Autonomous Security Robots as the highest-yield automation levers, capable of driving hundreds of thousands in operational savings while modernizing the guest experience.

Pathway A: Floor Care

Scope: Autonomous scrubbers for Arena concourses & Convention halls.

Impact: 40-60% reduction in cleaning labor hours. Consistent sanitation standards.

Status: High Priority

Pathway B: Security (ASR)

Scope: Outdoor parking patrol & indoor night sentries.

Impact: 24/7 force multiplication, license plate recognition, and theft deterrence.

Status: Strategic

Pathway C: Logistics

Scope: Concession runners and suite delivery bots.

Impact: Increased revenue uptime and premium guest experience.

Status: Future Phase
