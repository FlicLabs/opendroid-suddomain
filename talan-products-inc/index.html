Executive Vision

Talan Products Inc. is positioned to transition from a high-efficiency traditional manufacturer to a flexible, data-driven Smart Factory. The primary strategic objective is to integrate cyber-physical systems into existing high-speed stamping and aluminum extrusion lines. This shift will mitigate labor risks, enhance safety, and ensure 100% quality compliance in the high-growth EV and Solar sectors.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Machine Tending (Cobots)

Deployment of IP54-rated Cobots for press brake tending. Removes operators from repetitive, high-risk zones.

Pathway B: Intralogistics (AMRs)

Autonomous Mobile Robots for heavy coil and WIP transport, reducing fork-truck congestion.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Vision-guided palletizing for heavy metal components to ensure consistent stacking and throughput.
