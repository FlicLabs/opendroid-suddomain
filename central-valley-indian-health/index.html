Executive Summary

CVIH is uniquely positioned to leverage automation not to replace staff, but to extend reach. By implementing Patient Flow AI and Remote Telepresence Robotics, the network can recover thousands of lost operational hours annually. The projected financial impact involves a net incremental benefit of roughly $3.75M per year, primarily driven by optimized scheduling and retained revenue.
