SHONEYS AUTOMATION STRATEGY

Operational Transformation & Robotics Integration

Executive Summary

The integration of robotics into Shoneys' legacy full-service model represents a strategic pivot from labor-intensive operations to a hybrid autonomous ecosystem. By offloading repetitive tasks—such as food transit and frying—to intelligent systems, Shoneys can mitigate rising labor costs while elevating the guest experience. The core vision is Human-Robot Collaboration: robots handle logistics, humans handle hospitality. This approach targets a 15-25% reduction in front-of-house labor costs and a sub-14-month payback period through increased table turnover and algorithmic upselling.

Operational Context

Shoneys operates in a high-volume, mid-market segment. The primary operational friction point is the Front-of-House server role, which is high-turnover and labor-intensive. Breakfast operations mimic QSR volume, while lunch/dinner require table service, creating staffing complexity.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for food delivery and bussing. Allows servers to manage 40% more tables by eliminating transit time.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Robotic frying stations for high-volume items (fries, tenders). Ensures precise cook times and reduces oil-burn safety incidents.

Pathway C: AI Voice/Kiosks

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

AI Drive-thru agents and lobby kiosks. Algorithmic upselling increases check size by 8-12% while reducing order errors.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase. Deploy FOH Robots & AI Voice in 10 flagship locations. Staff training and workflow integration.
  • Year 2: Optimization. Analyze data streams. Deploy BOH Frying automation in pilot units. Refine maintenance protocols.
  • Year 3: Regional Rollout. Expand to 25% of franchise network. Bulk procurement lowers CAPEX. Centralized monitoring dashboard live.
  • Year 4: Brand Standardization. Tech becomes mandatory for new franchisees. Integration of predictive maintenance AI.
  • Year 5: Full Ecosystem. 80%+ network penetration. Lights-out kitchen prep shifts. Fully autonomous P&L forecasting.
