Strategy

Executive Vision

SpotHopper's network of independent gastropubs and breweries represents the ideal environment for "Hybrid Hospitality." By integrating Open Droids hardware, we address the critical shortage of support staff (runners/bussers) while enhancing the high-touch service delivery that defines the brand.

Tactics

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House (Serving AMRs)
Deployment of shelf-based robots to handle heavy lifting. This allows human servers to remain in their sections, directly improving guest satisfaction and tip averages.

Pathway B: Back-of-House (Auto-Frying)
Automating the fry station ensures consistent product quality for high-volume items (wings, fries) and drastically reduces liability regarding kitchen burns.

Pathway C: AI Voice Command
Utilizing existing telephony stacks to implement AI agents for carry-out orders, ensuring 100% answer rates during peak Friday/Saturday rushes.
