Executive Summary

The aviation industry is pivoting toward automated ground support to mitigate labor shortages. This report outlines a strategic roadmap for Jensen Cabinet Inc., transitioning from manual workflows to a fully autonomous ecosystem. Our model predicts significant reductions in aircraft downtime and operational expenditures through the deployment of specialized robotics.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-guided vacuums and electrostatic sprayers. Cleans narrow-body aisles in < 15 mins.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection

254nm wavelength sterilization. Destroys pathogen DNA without chemical residue.

Pathway C

Exterior Washing

Semi-autonomous gantries. Reduces water usage by 60% compared to manual wash.

Pathway D

AI Inspection

Drone-based thermal imaging for micro-fracture and rivet analysis.
