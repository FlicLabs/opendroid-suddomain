Waco Family Medicine

Robotics & Automation Strategic Portfolio

Executive Summary

Waco Family Medicine (WFM) is a critical FQHC and teaching health center. The most potent automation levers are not surgical, but operational: Pharmacy Automation, Administrative RPA, and Education Simulation. Implementing these technologies addresses burnout, increases 340B revenue, and modernizes resident training.
