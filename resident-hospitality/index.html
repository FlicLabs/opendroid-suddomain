Executive Vision

Automation at Resident Hospitality represents a shift to a "High-Touch, High-Tech" model. By offloading low-value logistical tasks (running, bussing, frying) to robotics, we liberate human staff to focus purely on guest experience and revenue-generating upsells.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pilot

Deploy FOH Runners at 2 Flagship Locations.

Year 2: Expansion

Rollout to all units; BOH Fryer Pilot.

Year 3: Maturity

Full AI Voice Integration & Data Optimization.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A

FOH Serving Robots

Focus on reducing server steps. Robots handle food running and bussing, keeping servers in their sections.

Pathway B

Automated BOH

Robotic fry arms ensure perfect consistency, reduce waste by 12%, and improve kitchen safety.

Pathway C

Voice AI

Drive-thru and phone AI agents to guarantee 100% call capture and consistent upselling scripts.
