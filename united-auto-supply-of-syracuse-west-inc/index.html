Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Throughput Efficiency

Deployment of deck-load AMRs to handle the transport of totes between picking zones and packing stations. This eliminates manual cart pushing and increases active picking time from 40% to 75%.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking (ASRS)

Density Accuracy

Implementation of Goods-to-Person (GTP) or Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for high-velocity SKUs like spark plugs and filters. Utilizing vertical space to free up floor capacity.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

AI Predictive

Predictive AI to pre-sort orders into staging lanes based on specific truck routes and stop orders, reducing driver loading time significantly.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: WMS Middleware deployment & Pilot of 5 AMRs in small-parts zone.
  • Year 2: Scale fleet to 20 units; install first Vertical Lift Module.
  • Year 3: Robotic arm integration for bin picking & AI route planning.
  • Year 4: Data-driven layout restructuring based on heatmaps.
  • Year 5: "Lights Out" capability for night shift replenishment.
