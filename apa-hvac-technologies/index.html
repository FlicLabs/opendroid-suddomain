Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Machine Tending & Assembly. High-payload cobots for press brake loading and torque-controlled screwdriving on AHU casings.

Pathway B: AMRs

Material Transport. Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving sheet metal kits and sub-assemblies, reducing forklift reliance.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Palletizing & QC. Automated stacking of motors and compressors with integrated vision systems for label verification.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Outlook)

Year 1
Pilot Phase: Deploy 2 machine-tending Cobot cells and establish vision system protocols.
Year 2
Logistics Integration: Roll out AMR fleet for internal logistics and ERP integration.
Year 3
Expansion: Scale screwdriving automation and automated palletizing for spare parts.
Year 5
Smart Factory: Full Digital Twin simulation and AI-driven predictive maintenance.
