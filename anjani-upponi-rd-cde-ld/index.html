Executive Summary

Anjani Upponi RD CDE LD represents a specialized clinical practice where the primary constraint is clinician time. This research proposes shifting from a manual operational model to an "AI-Augmented Practice." By implementing software robotics for administrative tasks (RPA) and AI for clinical data synthesis (CGM analysis), the practice can unlock 30-40% more capacity. The financial model projects a net benefit of £33k+ annually with a payback period of under 8 weeks.
