Robotics & AI Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: TLA Scaling (Highest ROI)

Expanding Total Laboratory Automation (TLA) to Chemistry and Hematology. By integrating middleware for "AI Delta Checks" (automated verification of results), TriCore can handle 50% more volume without increasing FTEs.

Pathway B: AI Clinical Intelligence Commercialization

TriCore is uniquely positioned to transition to a SaaS (Software as a Service) model. By packaging their validated algorithms (like the prenatal model) for national health systems and payers, TriCore can diversify revenue away from fee-for-service reimbursement.
