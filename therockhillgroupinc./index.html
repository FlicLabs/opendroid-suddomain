01. Executive Summary

The Rockhill Group Inc. (TRG) is positioned to modernize its aviation support services through the integration of robotics. Transitioning from manual maintenance to an AI-driven ecosystem will enhance mission readiness, extend airframe lifecycles, and ensure predictive fleet availability for critical training sorties.

02. Organizational Profile

Fleet Scope Est. 50-80 Active Aircraft (Fixed & Rotary Wing)
Hub Presence Molino, FL (HQ) & Contract Support Sites
Daily Volume High-Frequency Training Sorties (4-6 Turns/Day)

03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Rapid FOD removal for tight flight schedules. Reduces cleaning time from 20 to 5 mins.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection Chemical-free sterilization of high-touch cockpit avionics. 99.9% pathogen elimination.

Pathway C

Exterior Wash Systems Automated gantries reduce water usage by 60% and improve aerodynamic efficiency.

Pathway D

AI Inspection Bots Thermal imaging drones for early detection of micro-cracks and predictive maintenance.

04. Financial Analysis

  • CAPEX: $75k - $150k per robotic unit.
  • OPEX: 35-45% reduction in labor & chemical costs.
  • Turnaround Cost: Reduced from $125 (Manual) to $65 (Robotic).

The target ROI is driven by labor reallocation to high-value maintenance tasks and increased sortie generation rates. We project a break-even point within 18 months.
