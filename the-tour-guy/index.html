Executive Summary

The Tour Guy is a high-volume travel provider facing critical bottlenecks in inventory management and logistics. The primary automation opportunity is Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for ticket procurement and AI-driven guide dispatch. This shift from manual operations to an automated ecosystem will secure scarce inventory faster than competitors and optimize guide utilization. The dominant ROI driver is the automated procurement of tickets, directly correlating to revenue recognition.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA Inventory Procurement (High Value)

Deployment of software robotics to monitor and secure timed-entry tickets from major attraction portals instant they are released.

  • High-frequency API monitoring and headless browser automation.
  • Automated purchasing scripts to secure blocks of inventory.
  • Impact: £5-8M annual incremental revenue retention.

Pathway B: AI-Driven Guide Logistics

Algorithmic logistics platform optimizing guide assignment based on language, rating, and location.

  • Dynamic routing for meeting points.
  • Real-time reallocation of guides for no-shows.
  • Impact: Significant NPS score improvement and payroll automation.
