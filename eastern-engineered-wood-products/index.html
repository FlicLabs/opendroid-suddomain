Logistics Automation Strategy

Transforming Heavy-Load Distribution through Robotics & AI Integration

Executive Vision

For Eastern Engineered Wood Products (EEWP), the strategic pivot is shifting from manual "search-and-pick" operations to a semi-autonomous "goods-to-saw" workflow. By integrating heavy-payload AMRs and AI-driven load building, EEWP targets a drastic reduction in material damage, increased picking safety, and optimized flatbed loading density for I-joists and LVL inventory.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A

AMRs for Internal Transport

Deploying heavy-payload "Tugger" AMRs to handle 40ft I-joists, decoupling transport from the picking process.

B

Auto-Feed Saw Lines

Automated material retrieval systems feeding directly into precision trim saws to ensure 99.9% dimensional accuracy.

C

AI Logistics

Reverse-stop logic algorithms to optimize flatbed truck loading, ensuring stability and faster site drop-offs.
