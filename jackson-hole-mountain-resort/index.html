CLIENT: JACKSON HOLE MOUNTAIN RESORT

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Jackson Hole Mountain Resort (JHMR) operates in a high-stakes environment requiring precision in safety, logistics, and guest experience. The integration of robotics shifts the operational focus from labor-heavy maintenance to tech-enabled optimization. The dominant ROI drivers are Autonomous Snow Management, Drone-Based Safety, and Automated Hospitality.

Pathway A

Autonomous Grooming

Deploying LiDAR-equipped snow groomers to automate night shifts on intermediate terrain.

Impact: 20-30% labor cost reduction and optimized fuel usage.

Pathway B

Drone Safety & SAR

Heavy-lift drones for avalanche mitigation and thermal drones for search and rescue operations.

Impact: drastic reduction in insurance liability and faster terrain opening.

Pathway C

Hospitality Bots

Automated back-of-house food prep to increase throughput during peak lunch hours.

Impact: increased revenue velocity and reduced food waste.
