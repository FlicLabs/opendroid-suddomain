01. Executive Vision

Fehr Bros. Industries stands at a pivotal junction. As a leader in industrial rigging and hardware, the transition from manual bulk handling to a "Smart Industrial Hub" is essential to secure future market dominance. Our strategy integrates high-payload robotics to manage heavy wire rope inventory while utilizing vertical automation for high-density hardware. This approach targets a 99.9% accuracy rate and a significant reduction in physical labor strain.
