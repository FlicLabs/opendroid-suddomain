Executive Summary

Buckhead America Hospitality faces critical pressures in labor retention and wage inflation within the select-service hotel sector. Our analysis identifies Housekeeping Automation (Pathway A) as the primary value driver, shifting floor care from manual labor to autonomous systems. This transition solves for high turnover rates and physical strain while delivering a projected annual network savings of over $230,000.

Pathway A: Floor Care Automation

Scope: Deployment of autonomous vacuum sweepers and scrubbers in lobbies and corridors.

Impact: Reduction of 3 labor hours per day per hotel. Consistent cleanliness standards independent of staffing levels.

Pathway B: Guest Logistics

Scope: "Relay" delivery bots for room service and amenities.

Impact: Front desk staff remain at post during check-in peaks. Increased guest satisfaction scores via novel technology interaction.
