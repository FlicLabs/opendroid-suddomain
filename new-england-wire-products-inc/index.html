01. Executive Vision

New England Wire Products is positioned to dominate the retail fixture market by pivoting from manual fabrication to Human-Centric Automation. This strategy addresses labor shortages in skilled welding while enhancing the flexibility required for rapid Point-of-Purchase (POP) display rollouts.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Welding

Implementation of IP67-rated Cobots to handle repetitive wire grid "stitch" welds. This relieves skilled labor bottlenecks and ensures consistent structural integrity.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for line-side replenishment. Moving wire spools and welded frames to powder coating without forklift intervention.

Pathway C: Vision & Palletizing

End-of-line automation utilizing Machine Vision for paint inspection and heavy-payload arms for palletizing odd-shaped display racks.
