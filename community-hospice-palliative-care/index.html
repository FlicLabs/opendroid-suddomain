Executive Overview

Community Hospice & Palliative Care operates a complex network of home-based care requiring high-efficiency logistics and fleet management. This analysis identifies three primary robotics pathways: Fleet Logistics Optimization, Internal AMRs, and Telepresence RPM. The immediate opportunity lies in AI-driven fleet optimization to reduce operational overhead and increase clinician capacity.
