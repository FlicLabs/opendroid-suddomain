01. Executive Summary

Liberty Pumps is positioned to transition from isolated automation to a connected Smart Factory ecosystem. As an ESOP entity, the strategy prioritizes labor augmentation over reduction. By integrating robotics into casting, machining, and assembly, Liberty can secure supply chain resilience and handle high-mix production efficiently.

02. Robotics Pathways

PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Machine Tending & Assembly: Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for loading CNC lathes and torque-sensing arms for star-pattern bolt fastening. Focus on reducing repetitive strain injuries (RSI).

PATHWAY B: AMRs

Material Transport: Autonomous Mobile Robots connecting the powder coat line to assembly stations. Reduces operator walk-time by approx. 40%.

PATHWAY C: END-OF-LINE

Palletizing & Inspection: Automated heavy lifting of boxed pumps (20-100 lbs) and machine vision systems for paint and thread inspection.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Network infrastructure & Pilot of EOL Palletizing.
  • Year 2: AMR Fleet rollout connecting Production to Paint.
  • Year 3: Cobot integration in CNC & Assembly. Digital Twin initiation.
  • Year 4: AI-driven scheduling & Vision Quality loops.
  • Year 5: Full autonomy in heavy transport. Workforce transition to supervision.
