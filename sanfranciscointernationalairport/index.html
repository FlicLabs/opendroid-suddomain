Robotics Opportunity Pathways

A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

High-speed vacuuming and trash removal bots to reduce critical path cleaning time. Aiming for a 12-minute reduction per narrow-body turn.

B

UV-C Disinfection

Medical-grade sanitization for terminals and cabins. Ensures 99.9% pathogen elimination without chemical residue, supporting "SFO Healthy Building" goals.

C

Eco-Exterior Washing

Robotic dry-wash and misting gantries designed for California's drought conditions. Reduces water usage from 1,000 gallons to <50 gallons per wash.

D

AI Inspection Bots

Automated drones and rovers for fuselage checks and ramp FOD (Foreign Object Debris) detection, enhancing safety and digital record keeping.
