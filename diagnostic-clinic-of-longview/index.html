Diagnostic Clinic of Longview

Executive Summary

Diagnostic Clinic of Longview (DCOL) faces the optimization challenge of managing patient flow and diagnostic turnaround times without the centralized resources of a massive health system. The strongest automation levers are Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for revenue cycle, Automated Laboratory Systems, and Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for internal logistics. These initiatives can unlock millions in retained earnings through operational efficiency and accelerated diagnostic throughput.
