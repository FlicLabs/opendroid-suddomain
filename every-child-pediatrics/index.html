Executive Summary

Every Child Pediatrics serves over 24,000 children annually through a safety-net model. The primary opportunity lies in "Tech-Enabled Capacity Expansion." By deploying RPA for administrative tasks and Telepresence Robotics for School-Based Health Centers (SBHCs), the organization can bridge the gap between high patient demand and provider scarcity. The projected ROI focuses on reclaiming 30% of administrative time and converting travel time into clinical billable hours.
