Executive Summary

K18 Hair operates at the intersection of biotechnology and consumer goods. To maintain market leadership, the manufacturing philosophy must mirror the precision of the laboratory. Our strategic vision is to transition from batch processing to an Agile Smart Manufacturing Ecosystem, prioritizing modular production lines capable of rapid changeovers while maintaining pharmaceutical-grade quality.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Safety & Precision. Deployment of IP66-rated Cobots for bottle feeding and machine tending. This eliminates repetitive strain injuries and ensures zero human contact with the interior of the packaging.

PATHWAY B

AMRs for Material Transport

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency. Autonomous Mobile Robots will transport raw ingredients (lipids/peptides) to mixing suites, reducing floor traffic and forklift risks.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield. High-speed robotic palletizing combined with inline Vision Systems to scan serialization codes, ensuring 100% traceability for anti-counterfeit measures.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot End-of-Line Palletizing & Digital OEE Baseline.
  • Year 2: Cobot integration on Filling Lines.
  • Year 3: Logistics Automation (AMRs) for warehouse transport.
  • Year 5: AI Integration & Dark Factory capability.
