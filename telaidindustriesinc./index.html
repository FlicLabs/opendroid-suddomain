01. Executive Vision

Telaid's transition to a Zero-Touch Configuration Center represents a pivotal shift in logistics strategy. By deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for material transport and Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs) for inventory density, Telaid will decouple high-value technical labor from low-value material movement. This automation layer ensures the Norcross, GA facility scales efficiently with client project volume.
