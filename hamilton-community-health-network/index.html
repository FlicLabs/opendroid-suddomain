Executive Summary: Hamilton Community Health Network

HCHN faces the dual challenge of managing high patient volumes typical of FQHCs while navigating staffing constraints. The primary robotics opportunities lie in Pharmacy Automation, Administrative RPA, and Telepresence. These technologies optimize workforce utilization, reduce medication errors, and significantly increase patient throughput without expanding physical footprints.
