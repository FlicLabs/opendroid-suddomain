EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report details a strategic vision for integrating robotic automation into Paradise Helicopters' cleaning and maintenance operations. The focus is on leveraging Autonomous Cabin Cleaning, UV-C Disinfection, Exterior Washing, and AI Inspection technologies to reduce operational costs by 35-45%, decrease aircraft downtime, and ensure superior hygiene standards. The proposed roadmap outlines a 5-year phased deployment designed for rapid ROI and seamless operational integration.

ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

1. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Micro-robots for compact rotorcraft interiors. Reduces turnaround time by 30% with LiDAR navigation for obstacle avoidance.

2. UV-C Disinfection Units

Automated sanitation cycles. Zero chemical residue. Safety sensors ensure crew protection during overnight ops.

3. Exterior Washing Systems

Soft-touch robotic rails. Reduces water usage by 40% and eliminates scaffolding risks for fuselage and blades.

4. AI Inspection Bots

Thermal & visual crawlers. Shifts maintenance from reactive to predictive with computer vision defect detection.
