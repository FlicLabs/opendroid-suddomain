Executive Summary

Claiborne Senior Living is positioned to transition from traditional dining logistics to a technology-assisted hospitality model. By integrating Collaborative Robotics (Cobots), Claiborne can alleviate the physical burden of tray running, allowing staff to pivot towards "Resident Concierge" roles.

This strategy addresses critical labor shortages while simultaneously elevating the high-touch service required in independent and assisted living environments.

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Deployment of shelf-style serving robots to manage long-distance kitchen-to-table transit. Increases face time between staff and residents.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Automated frying and grilling stations to ensure dietary texture consistency and safety, crucial for senior health standards.

Pathway C: AI Concierge

Voice-activated in-room ordering systems to empower resident autonomy and reduce front-desk call volume.
