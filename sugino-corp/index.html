01. Executive Vision

Sugino Corp is positioned to transition from a high-precision discrete manufacturer to a connected Smart Factory leader. By integrating robotics into the production of JCC (Jet Clean Center) and Selfeeder units, Sugino can secure its competitive advantage in reliability while dramatically increasing throughput. This strategy leverages Cobots for precision assembly and AMRs for "forklift-free" logistics.
