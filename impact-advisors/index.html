Executive Summary

The "Cyber-Physical" Integrator Opportunity

Impact Advisors is uniquely positioned to expand beyond traditional EHR and IT consulting into the high-growth sector of physical automation. The recommended strategy involves establishing a Robotics & Automation Advisory Practice. This new service line will guide health systems through the selection, implementation, and integration of physical robotics (AMRs, Surgical, Pharmacy), bridging the critical gap between hardware and the Electronic Health Record.
