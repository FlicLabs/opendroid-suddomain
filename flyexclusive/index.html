Executive Summary

For flyExclusive, the 2nd largest private charter operator in the US, integrating robotics offers a dual advantage: ensuring consistent white-glove presentation across a floating fleet and optimizing turnaround times at the Kinston, NC MRO. This report outlines a transition to a hybrid automated model, utilizing compact robotics for repetitive tasks to free up skilled labor for high-value detailing.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • PATHWAY A: Compact Interior Bots

    Fleet Specific: Tailored for Cessna Citation/Challenger narrow fuselages. Low-profile vacuum/wipe rovers.

    Impact: 30% reduction in cabin turnaround time.

  • PATHWAY B: UV-C Disinfection

    Health & Hygiene: Portable UV-C robotics for 99.9% pathogen elimination without chemical damage to leather veneers.

  • PATHWAY C: Exterior Automation

    Sustainability: Automated dry-wash systems at paint facilities to reduce water usage by 90% and extend livery life.

  • PATHWAY D: Drone Inspection

    Maintenance: AI-powered visual inspection drones for Kinston hangars to detect micro-cracks and reduce downtime.
