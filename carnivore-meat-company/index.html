01. Executive Summary

Carnivore Meat Company is positioned to dominate the ultra-premium raw pet food sector. However, relying on manual labor in sub-zero environments creates a scalability ceiling. This report outlines a pivot to Industry 4.0, integrating collaborative robotics to ensure food safety, traceability, and continuous flow in the Green Bay facilities.

02. Opportunity Pathways

PATH A Cobot Tray Loading

Focus: Safety & Precision. Automating the "Forming to Drying" transition. Reduces ergonomic strain and improves hygiene by minimizing human contact with raw proteins.

PATH B AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow. Replacing manual forklifts with Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy racks into freeze-dryers, reducing accidents and transit time.

PATH C End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput. High-speed palletizing and vision-based seal inspection to guarantee oxidation prevention for raw products.
