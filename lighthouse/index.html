Executive Insight

The Robotics Opportunity

Lighthouse stands at a pivotal moment. By transitioning from manual picking to an AMR-integrated ecosystem, the organization can bypass current labor shortages and real estate constraints. The recommended Pathway A (AMR Transport) offers the lowest barrier to entry with the highest immediate impact on non-value-added travel time.
