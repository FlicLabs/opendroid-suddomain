Executive Summary

Bluestem Health, an FQHC in Lincoln, Nebraska, faces bottlenecks in administrative capacity and pharmacy throughput. The proposed robotics portfolio focuses on Pharmacy Automation (ScriptPro/Parata), RPA for Billing, and Intake Kiosks. These initiatives directly target 340B revenue expansion and administrative efficiency, offering a high-impact, low-risk path to financial sustainability.

Financial Impact Statement

Implementing Pathway A (Pharmacy Automation) alone creates a self-funding mechanism for subsequent technologies. By increasing daily prescription capacity from 200 to 300, Bluestem Health captures significant incremental 340B revenue. Combined with RPA operational savings, the network achieves over £1.4M in cumulative value over 5 years.
