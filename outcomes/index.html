OUTCOMES

Robotics & Automation Analysis Portfolio

Executive Summary: Outcomes is the market leader in Medication Therapy Management (MTM), connecting 60,000+ pharmacies to payers. The primary opportunity is shifting from manual human outreach to Software Robotics (RPA) and Conversational AI. This transition transforms high-cost labor into high-leverage clinical decision making.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA & Workflow

Automated software bots to handle patient identification, claims queueing, and documentation pre-filling.

40% Reduction in Admin Time

Pathway B: Digital Robotics

AI-driven Voice/Text agents to handle initial patient outreach and scheduling, filtering out non-contacts.

30% → 40% Completion Rate Uplift

Pathway C: Hardware Integration

API integration with retail pharmacy robots (Parata/ScriptPro) to trigger alerts at the physical point of dispense.
