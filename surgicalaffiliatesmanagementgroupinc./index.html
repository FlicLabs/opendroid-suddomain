Executive Summary

Surgical Affiliates Management Group Inc. (SAMGI) manages surgical teams across US hospitals. Unlike facility owners, SAMGI's core "robotics" opportunity lies in logistics and process automation rather than hardware manufacturing. The strategy focuses on AI-driven scheduling to maximize OR throughput and standardizing clinical robotics programs to ensure partner hospitals achieve ROI on their existing Da Vinci/Mako assets.

Key Insight: By shifting from a staffing agency model to a tech-enabled operations partner, SAMGI can unlock tens of millions in incremental margin for hospital partners via improved block utilization.
