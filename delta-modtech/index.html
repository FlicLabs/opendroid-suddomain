Executive Summary

Delta ModTech is positioned to transition from standard discrete manufacturing to a Smart Factory Model. By automating internal machine tending and intralogistics, the company can accelerate the production of its flagship Crusader converters while reducing operational overhead.

OPPORTUNITIES

Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots. Deploying UR/Fanuc arms to tend CNC mills. Enables "lights out" fabrication of precision rollers and die stations.

Pathway B: AMRs. Utilizing Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy WIP materials between the stockroom and assembly bays, eliminating forklift traffic.

Pathway C: Inspection. Automated metrology cells to validate part tolerances before they reach the assembly line.
