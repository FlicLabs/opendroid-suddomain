Project: Serenity Healthcare

Executive Strategy

The operational shift from manual logistics to an AI-orchestrated robotic ecosystem addresses Serenity Healthcare's critical staffing bottlenecks. By deploying Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and automated dispensing, the network can recapture over 18,000 clinical hours annually, shifting focus from transport to patient outcomes.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Logistics Pilot (Year 1)

Deploy AMRs in 3 flagship facilities. Establish baseline for "Clinical Hours Returned."

Phase 2: Scale & Safety (Year 2)

Rollout AMRs to 45 sites. Pilot Passive Monitoring AI in memory care wings to reduce fall risks.

Phase 3: Integration (Years 3-5)

Central Command Center integration. Full pharmacy automation. Data utilization for payer negotiations.
