01. Executive Vision

The Hybrid Workforce Model

One Source Staffing is positioned to lead the logistics sector by transitioning from a traditional labor supplier to a "Managed Productivity Partner." By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven logic, we aim to reduce the volatility of human capital while guaranteeing throughput for industrial clients. This strategy moves beyond headcount to focus on outcome-based service delivery.

03. Opportunity Pathways

A

AMR Transport

Goal: Reduce walking time.

Deployment of goods-to-person AMRs to handle internal transport. Allows temp staff to remain in active zones, reducing training time and increasing safety.

B

Robotic Picking

Goal: 99.9% Accuracy.

Integration of lightweight ASRS and robotic picking arms for high-velocity SKUs. Drastically reduces miss-pick chargebacks from clients.

C

AI Logistics

Goal: Predictive Staffing.

AI layers analyzing client volume to predict staffing needs before spikes occur, optimizing the ratio of human to robot resources.
