Executive Summary

Advanced Dermatology and the Center for Skin Research faces critical bottlenecks in procedural throughput and diagnostic wait times. By integrating robotic hair restoration, automated whole-body imaging, and digital pathology, the organization can shift from a labor-intensive model to a scalable, technology-augmented platform. The dominant ROI driver is the Robotic Aesthetics pathway, offering high margins and reduced clinician fatigue.
