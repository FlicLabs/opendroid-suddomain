Organizational Profile

Fleet Composition: Mid-size business jets and regional turboprops. High utilization rates requiring rapid turnaround.

Operational Model: Currently manual-heavy for cabin detailing and exterior wash. Hubs located at high-traffic regional airports.

Challenge: Labor volatility, hygiene standards, and tight turnaround windows necessitate high-efficiency automation.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

  • A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning
    Self-navigating units for vacuuming and wiping. Reduces ground time.
  • B. UV-C Disinfection
    Chemical-free sanitation for high-touch surfaces. Enhances passenger trust.
  • C. Exterior Washing
    Gantry/Robotic systems reducing water usage and manual labor.
  • D. AI Inspection Bots
    Predictive maintenance via visual/thermal anomaly detection.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Strategy)

Year 1: Pilot Phase
Deploy 2 Cabin Cleaners & 1 UV-C unit at primary hub. Focus on SOP development.

Year 2: Expansion
Integrate Exterior Washing Systems. Connect data streams to central maintenance software.

Year 3: Scaling
Roll out to secondary hubs. Deploy AI Inspection Bots. 50% automation coverage.

Year 4: Automation
85% cleaning automation. Predictive maintenance workflows active. Reduce AOG time.

Year 5: Optimization
Full ROI achieved. Swarm logic integration. Focus on energy efficiency upgrades.
