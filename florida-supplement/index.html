Florida Supplement Robotics Analysis

Florida Supplement is a leading contract manufacturer facing operational bottlenecks in material handling and packaging. The implementation of a robotics portfolio focused on End-of-Line automation and Intralogistics will shift the facility from a manual labor-heavy model to a high-velocity smart factory.

Strategic Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1 (Year 1)

Deploy End-of-Line robotic palletizers on top 2 volume lines. Integrate with WMS.

Phase 2 (Year 2)

Launch AMR fleet (3-5 units) for raw material transport. Pilot Vision QC systems.

Phase 3 (Year 3)

Full fleet integration. Expansion of automation to liquid filling lines.
