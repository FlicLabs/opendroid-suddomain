GMP Laboratories of America Inc.

Executive Summary

GMP Laboratories of America Inc. faces a pivotal opportunity to transition from labor-intensive material handling to an automated, high-throughput manufacturing model. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for intralogistics and Collaborative Robots (Cobots) for packaging, the facility can unlock over $2.9M in annual net benefit. The strategy shifts focus from manual carting and lifting to process optimization, directly improving margins and scalability.

Operational Efficiency

Automation stabilizes the "heartbeat" of the manufacturing line. While manual processes suffer from fatigue, breaks, and shift changes, robotic systems maintain consistent cycle times.

Key Insight: The primary value is not just replacing labor, but ensuring the high-speed encapsulation machines are never idle waiting for materials or clearing output.
