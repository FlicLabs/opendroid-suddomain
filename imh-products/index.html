01. Executive Vision

IMH Products is positioned to transition from a traditional metal fabrication job shop to a Contract Manufacturing 4.0 leader. By decoupling revenue growth from labor availability, IMH can leverage data-driven flexible manufacturing to handle High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) orders with the efficiency typically reserved for mass production.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for Press Brakes and CNC Lathes. This transforms the operator role from manual loader to cell manager, overseeing three machines simultaneously to reduce idle time.

Pathway B: Autonomous Material Transport (AMRs)

Utilization of heavy-payload AMRs (500kg+) to automate the flow of stamped parts from the Press Room to Welding. This reduces forklift traffic and minimizes WIP wait times.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Automation

Implementation of automated palletizing cells with integrated vision systems for "Go/No-Go" quality inspection, ensuring 100% yield consistency before shipping.
