ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

1. AUTONOMOUS CABIN CLEANING

Compact robots navigating aisles to vacuum and collect trash, allowing crews to focus on high-touch sanitation.

2. UV-C DISINFECTION ROBOTS

Chemical-free pathogen neutralization post-cleaning, ensuring medical-grade cabin hygiene in under 15 minutes.

3. EXTERIOR WASHING SYSTEMS

Articulated arms for fuselage cleaning, reducing water usage and preventing micro-scratches from manual washing.

4. AI INSPECTION BOTS

High-res cameras and LiDAR for identifying seat damage and spills, feeding data into maintenance dashboards.
