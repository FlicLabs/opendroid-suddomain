Beltone Alliance

Healthcare Robotics Opportunities & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

Beltone Alliance operates as a leading network of hearing care providers with over 1500 locations in North America. The traditional hearing care model relies heavily on manual audiometry, physical silicone ear impressions, and administrative-heavy patient management.

The primary robotics and automation opportunities lie in three specific areas: (1) Deployment of 3D Digital Otoscopy and Scanning Robotics, (2) Automated Audiometry Kiosks, and (3) Robotic Process Automation (RPA). The dominant ROI driver is the increase in daily patient throughput and the reduction of material waste and shipping logistics.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Pilot & Scanning (Year 1)

Deploy 3D Digital Scanners in top 10% performing clinics to validate conversion rate uplift. Integrate scanners with manufacturing ordering software.

Phase 2: Automation Expansion (Year 2)

Roll out Automated Audiometry kiosks to high-traffic clinics to relieve schedule congestion. Begin RPA integration for backend scheduling.

Phase 3: Network Standard (Year 3)

Full deployment of scanning and automation tools as brand standard. Development of satellite remote clinics using fully robotic diagnostic suites.
