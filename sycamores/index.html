Sycamores Robotics Portfolio

Behavioral Health Automation, Logistics AI & Social Robotics

Executive Summary

Sycamores is a behavioral health leader serving the Greater Los Angeles area. The operational shift is moving from static residential care to dynamic, community-based mobile crisis intervention. The robotic opportunity lies in AI-driven logistics for mobile teams and therapeutic robotics for residential care.

Logistics AI

Optimization of Mobile Crisis Outreach Teams (MCOT) to reduce response times.

Social Robotics

Therapeutic robots (SAR) for trauma and ASD support in residential settings.

Telepresence

Remote psychiatry access for group homes and foster facilities.
