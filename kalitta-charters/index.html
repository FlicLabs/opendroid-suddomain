KALITTA CHARTERS: AUTOMATION STRATEGY

Executive Summary

This strategic roadmap envisions a future where Kalitta Charters leads in aviation support automation. By integrating autonomous cleaning and inspection technologies, we target a 35-45% reduction in operational overhead while enhancing aircraft safety and readiness. The proposed 5-year phased approach ensures minimal disruption and maximized ROI, transitioning manual labor toward complex technical tasks.
