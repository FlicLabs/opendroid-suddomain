OPEN DROIDS
Client: Think Hospitality

Executive Summary

Think Hospitality is positioned to revolutionize its operational model through strategic robotics integration. By addressing critical labor shortages and elevating the guest experience, the proposed roadmap leverages Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for service, industrial automation for cleanliness, and culinary robotics for back-of-house efficiency. The dominant ROI driver is the reallocation of labor hours from low-value transport tasks to high-value guest interactions.

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Pathway A Pilot

Launch flagship pilot for Service AMRs. Integrate with Property Management System (PMS) and elevator controls. Establish staff training protocols.

Year 2: Expansion & Pathway B

Scale service robots to 3-5 properties. Introduce cleaning robotics for high-traffic public areas. Launch guest-facing "Tech-Forward" marketing.

Year 3: Optimization & Culinary

Deploy automated food runners in high-volume dining venues. Utilize accumulated data to optimize fleet routing and charging cycles.
