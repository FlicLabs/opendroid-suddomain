01. Executive Summary

Ziglift is positioned to pivot from a traditional equipment provider to a tech-enabled smart logistics entity. By automating the custom fabrication and refurbishment divisions, Ziglift will increase inventory yield and reduce lead times for complex racking systems.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Fabrication Cobots

Deploying IP67-rated welding cobots to assist in the manufacturing of custom mezzanines and cantilever racks. Focus on reducing welder fatigue and increasing arc-on time.

Pathway B: Heavy Transport AMRs

Utilizing Autonomous Mobile Robots to move heavy steel uprights between offloading, paint booths, and staging areas. This mitigates forklift traffic in high-density pedestrian zones.

Pathway C: Automated Inspection

Vision-guided systems to inspect used racking for structural integrity before refurbishment, ensuring 100% quality control.
