Executive Summary

BestCo is positioned to transition from rigid, manual processes to a flexible, data-driven Smart Factory ecosystem. By integrating Cobots and AMRs, we will decouple labor from throughput, ensuring resilience against labor shortages.

Opportunity Pathways

A. Collaborative Robots

Deploying IP67-rated Cobots for machine tending to reduce operator idle time by 40%.

B. AMRs (Mobile Robots)

Replacing forklifts with autonomous units for line-side replenishment, reducing safety incidents.

C. Automated Palletizing

Vision-guided robotic arms for final quality checks and palletizing to reduce lifting strain.
