Executive Summary

Unified Supply stands at the threshold of a technological revolution. By integrating Open Droids' autonomous cleaning and inspection solutions, the airline can pivot from a labor-heavy operational model to a streamlined, tech-forward strategy. This transition promises to enhance fleet uptime, ensure hospital-grade hygiene, and secure a competitive advantage in the aviation logistics sector.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

LIDAR-navigated vacuums and debris collection for rapid turnaround times between flights.

Pathway B

UV-C Disinfection

Pathogen neutralization using ultraviolet light to ensure sterile environments for passengers.

Pathway C

Exterior Washing

Automated gantries reducing water waste and eliminating dangerous high-altitude manual labor.

Pathway D

AI Inspection Bots

Computer vision drones for detecting fuselage micro-cracks and structural anomalies.
