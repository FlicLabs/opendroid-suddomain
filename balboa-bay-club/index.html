Consultation Report: Balboa Bay Club

Balboa Bay Club: Robotics Portfolio

A strategic analysis for integrating autonomous systems into luxury hospitality. The focus is on maintaining high-touch service while automating high-volume logistics, cleaning, and marine maintenance to combat labor costs and enhance the member experience.

$1M+ 5-Year Projected Savings
30% Reduction in Logistics Time
< 9 Mo ROI Payback Period
3 Key Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics & Service

Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Deployment of fleet for room service, linen transport, and housekeeping logistics.

  • Reduces room service delivery time by 30%.
  • Saves 2-3 FTE equivalent in annual labor.
  • Reduces workplace injury from heavy cart transport.
Highest Impact

Pathway B: Automated Cleaning

Commercial Floor Care

Autonomous scrubbing and vacuuming for Grand Ballrooms and public corridors.

  • Ensures consistent overnight cleaning standards.
  • Eliminates overtime costs for event turnover.
  • Allows staff to focus on vertical detail work.
High Efficiency
