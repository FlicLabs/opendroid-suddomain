Executive Summary

Goal: Zero-AOG Latency. Boeing Distribution Services manages a High-Mix, Low-Volume ecosystem where speed and accuracy are critical. Our analysis suggests shifting from manual bin-searching to a Goods-to-Person (GTP) model can reduce AOG (Aircraft on Ground) fulfillment times by 60%, drastically improving airline fleet uptime.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATH A AMRs & Transport

Deploying Lidar-guided Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle long-haul transport of totes within mega-hubs. This decouples picking from walking, tripling associate efficiency.

PATH B Robotic Piece Picking

Utilization of Cube Storage ASRS for high-density storage of small fasteners. Combined with computer vision arms for 99.99% picking accuracy.

PATH C AI Predictive Logistics

Machine learning models that sync with global flight data to pre-stage maintenance parts before orders are even placed.
