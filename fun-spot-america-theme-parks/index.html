Fun Spot America Theme Parks

Robotics Analysis & ROI Model

Fun Spot America is a premier family-owned theme park operator facing high-volume, labor-intensive challenges. The strongest robotics levers are Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for sanitation, Culinary Automation for throughput, and Drone Inspections for safety. This strategy shifts the operational focus from manual labor dependency to asset-optimized guest experiences.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Pilot & Calibration (Months 1-3) — Deploy 2 autonomous scrubbers at Orlando. Map safety zones and retrain staff as fleet managers.
  • Phase 2: Network Rollout (Months 4-12) — Expand janitorial fleet to Kissimmee and Atlanta. Introduce beverage kiosks at choke points.
  • Phase 3: Advanced Integration (Year 2+) — Pilot drone inspection for SkyCoasters. Integrate all data into a central operations dashboard.
