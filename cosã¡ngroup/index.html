Healthcare Ops Automation ROI Analysis
Executive Summary

Cosán Group operates at the intersection of technology and chronic care, providing the workforce for Chronic Care Management (CCM) and Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM). The primary opportunity is shifting from a labor-heavy model to an AI-orchestrated ecosystem. Dominant levers include Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for data triage and billing, and Automated Logistics for device fulfillment.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Automated Logistics

Immediate Cost Reduction

Automated fulfillment systems for deploying, retrieving, and sanitizing RPM devices. Reduces churn costs by 20% and accelerates time-to-revenue.

Pathway B: RPA & AI Triage

High Scale / Margin Expansion

Software robotics to ingest vitals and draft clinical notes. Increases staff capacity from 250 to 500+ patients per coordinator.

Pathway C: Social Robotics

Strategic Differentiation

Deployment of companion robots for high-risk seniors to improve retention and capture behavioral health data.
