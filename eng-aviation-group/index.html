Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

SLAM-enabled robots for vacuuming and wiping. Reduces crew size and ensures consistency.

UV-C Disinfection

Post-cleaning pathogen neutralization. Chemical-free safety assurance.

Exterior Washing

Robotic crawlers for fuselage cleaning. 40% water reduction and faster AOG recovery.

AI Inspection Bots

Thermal & visual imaging for micro-detection. Automated compliance reporting.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year Phased Strategy)

Year 1: Pilot Phase

Deploy 5 Cabin + 3 UV-C units at primary hub. Data tracking and staff training initiation.

Year 2: Expansion

Scale to 20 Cabin + 10 UV-C units. Begin Exterior Washing pilots on narrow-body fleet.

Year 3: Fleet Adoption

Full narrow-body coverage. Deploy AI Inspection Bots for routine visual checks.

Year 4: Optimization

Wide-body integration. Predictive maintenance algorithms active. Labor reallocation.

Year 5: Maturity

Full autonomous ecosystem. Continuous software optimization. Secondary hub expansion.
