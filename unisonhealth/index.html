Robotics & Automation Research Portfolio

Executive Summary

Unison Health is a regional leader in integrated behavioral health and substance abuse treatment in Northwest Ohio. The most significant automation opportunities are not physical robots, but Process Robotics (RPA) and Automated Dispensing. By shifting administrative burdens to AI agents and securing medication management with hardware robotics, Unison can unlock over $2M in annual incremental value while addressing critical staff shortages.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: RPA & Intake AI

Type: Software Robotics

Deployment of AI agents for insurance verification, claims processing, and automated clinical documentation (AI scribes). Targets the high admin burden of behavioral health.

Impact: High ($2M+ / year)

Pathway B: Automated Dispensing

Type: Hardware Robotics

Secure automated dispensing cabinets (ADCs) for residential detox units and smart packaging for outpatient MAT compliance.

Impact: High Safety / Moderate Financial

Pathway C: Telepresence

Type: Mobile Robotics

Mobile robots for remote rounding in residential facilities, maximizing the reach of scarce psychiatrists.

Impact: Access Expansion
