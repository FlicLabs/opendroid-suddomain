Executive Summary

1st Choice Healthcare is positioned to transition from a labor-intensive community health model to a tech-enabled regional network. By leveraging Robotic Process Automation (RPA), Centralized Pharmacy Robotics, and Telepresence systems, the organization can address critical staffing shortages and administrative overhead. The dominant ROI drivers are administrative efficiency and increased pharmacy throughput, with projected break-even points on major hardware within 18 months.
