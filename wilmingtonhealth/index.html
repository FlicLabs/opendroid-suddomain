Organizational Profile

Model: Physician-owned multi-specialty group with 23+ locations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways
Pathway A: Ambulatory Surgical Robotics Highest Strategic Value

Deployment of orthopedic (Mako/Rosa) and soft-tissue robotics within ASCs to compete with regional hospitals.

  • Key Lever: Retain complex joint replacements in lower-cost outpatient settings.
  • Impact: ~$2.25M incremental annual revenue via case retention.
  • Strategic: Attracts top-tier surgeons and tech-savvy patients.
Pathway B: Admin Process Automation (RPA) High ROI / Low Risk

Software robotics to automate scheduling, billing, and prior authorizations.

  • Key Lever: 30-40% reduction in labor hours for routine admin tasks.
  • Impact: Faster revenue cycle and reduced staff burnout.
  • Payback: Immediate operational savings (< 6 months).
