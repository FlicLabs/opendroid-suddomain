FOOTHILL FAMILY

ROBOTICS & AUTOMATION STRATEGIC PORTFOLIO

01. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Foothill Family is a critical pillar in Southern California's social safety net. However, administrative friction and staff burnout threaten scalability. The strategic integration of Social Assistive Robotics (SAR) and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) offers a path to shift resources from paperwork to people. This strategy targets a potential $4M+ annual value creation through efficiency gains and staff retention.

02. OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

PATHWAY A: Therapeutic Robotics

Focus: Clinical Quality

Deployment of social robots (e.g., Moxie) in pediatric autism and trauma therapy to accelerate rapport building and improve social responsiveness.

IMPACT: +15-20% Client Engagement

PATHWAY B: Process Automation

Focus: Operational Efficiency

"Software Robotics" and NLP to automate case notes, billing compliance, and intake, reducing clinician administrative load.

IMPACT: +2.5 Billable Hours/Week

PATHWAY C: Logistics AI

Focus: Service Delivery

AI-driven routing for home visitors and telepresence robots for school consultations to minimize travel downtime.

IMPACT: -15% Travel Costs

03. FINANCIAL PROJECTION (PATHWAY B)

Basis: 300 Staff Members | $80k Avg Cost

Efficiency Gain: 5 hrs/week saved = 2.5 new billable hrs

Retention Gain: Reduced turnover (25% → 15%)
