The Blood Connection: Strategic Overview

Executive Summary: The Blood Connection is a non-profit community blood center operating across South Carolina, North Carolina, and Georgia. The organization faces critical labor shortages and donor engagement volatility. Robotics and automation offer significant potential to stabilize operations, reduce reliance on manual labor, and ensure consistent blood availability for over 70 partner hospitals.

Primary Opportunity: Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for donor recruitment and data management is the highest-value, lowest-risk pathway. It leverages AI to optimize scheduling, reduce no-shows, and increase donor conversion rates.
