Executive Vision

Kaplan Development Group stands at the precipice of a hospitality revolution. By integrating collaborative robotics into your senior living and dining facilities, we move beyond simple automation. We propose a "Human-in-the-Loop" strategy where robots handle the logistics of heavy lifting and transport, allowing your staff to focus entirely on resident connection, dietary safety, and the emotional aspects of hospitality.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A FOH Serving Robots

Deployment of autonomous shelves to runners. Reduces staff walking distance by 60%, drastically lowering fatigue and injury risk while ensuring hot food arrives hot.

PATHWAY B BOH Consistency

Automated beverage dispensing and smart-combi ovens to ensure strict adherence to dietary restrictions (sodium/sugar) with zero variance.

PATHWAY C AI Voice/Kiosks

Bistro and Café integration. AI instantly cross-references resident ID with health records to prevent allergen conflicts at the point of order.
