Strategic Pathways

1. Tele-Presence (Highest Value)

Deployment of mobile tele-robotic platforms to connect ED physicians with remote neurologists and intensivists. Reduces transfer rates by 15-20% and keeps revenue local.

2. Environmental Automation

UVC disinfection robots to reduce HAI risks and speed up room turnover during high-volume tourist seasons, alleviating pressure on limited environmental services staff.

3. Robotic Rehabilitation

Differentiation of the Skilled Nursing Facility using robotic gait training, attracting patients for "swing bed" recovery instead of out-migration to urban centers.
