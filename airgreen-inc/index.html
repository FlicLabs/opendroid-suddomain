Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous vacuum systems and debris manipulation arms designed for narrow aisle navigation and rapid turnaround.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Robotic arms emitting hospital-grade UV-C light to neutralize 99.9% of pathogens on tray tables and bins.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Gantry and crawler systems that adhere to the fuselage, reducing water usage and eliminating fall risks.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision drones scanning for micro-cracks, lightning strikes, and paint erosion.
