Executive Summary

One Community Health (Sacramento) faces margin pressure and high patient volumes typical of FQHCs. The strongest automation levers are Pharmacy Robotics for medication adherence and revenue, Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for billing efficiency, and Lab Automation for safety.
