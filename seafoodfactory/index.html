Executive Summary

Seafood Factory stands at a pivotal juncture. The integration of IP65-rated Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and Automated Storage Systems (ASRS) offers a definitive solution to cold-chain operational fatigue. This strategy moves beyond simple automation to create a "Smart Cold Chain" ensuring 99.9% accuracy and a 35% increase in throughput within sub-zero environments.

Strategic Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Focus: Transport & Hygiene.

Deployment of stainless steel, washdown-ready AMRs to automate movement between wet processing floors and blast freezers, eliminating human fatigue in extreme cold.

Pathway B: ASRS

Focus: Density & Picking.

Shuttle-based storage systems to maximize vertical cold storage. Vacuum grippers ensure delicate handling of fish to prevent tissue damage.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Focus: Predictive Flow.

Algorithmic inventory slotting based on "expiration urgency" and catch volume, automating FIFO compliance physically.

Implementation Roadmap

Y1
The Pilot: Digital Twin modeling of flagship facility & deployment of AMRs for dock-to-freezer transport.
Y2
The Core: Integration of high-density ASRS shuttles & WMS updates with AI predictive modules.
Y3
The Scale: Rollout of successful architecture to satellite metro hubs. Labor force transitions to supervisory roles.
Y5
Optimization: Full "Lights-Out" autonomy in deep-freeze zones and continuous route optimization.
