01. Executive Summary

This strategic research outlines the integration of robotics for Bobby’s Burgers. The objective is to scale the "Ultimate Burger Experience" by automating high-repetition tasks (frying, grilling, running). This protects brand consistency across franchise expansion while significantly widening profit margins through labor optimization.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

FOH: Serving Robots

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support.
Deployment of shelf-based delivery bots to handle food running and clearing. Allows human servers to focus on guest interaction and hospitality "vibe".

PATHWAY B

BOH: Automated Kitchen

Focus: Consistency & Safety.
Utilization of robotic fry arms (e.g., Flippy) and smart clamshell grills. Ensures the signature "Crunchified" texture is identical in every location.

PATHWAY C

AI: Voice & Kiosks

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy.
"Chef’s Voice" AI at kiosks to suggest pairings (e.g., specific shakes with burgers), driving an estimated 8% increase in ticket size.
