Executive Vision

The strategic imperative for CliqStudios is to decouple labor dependency from production capacity. By implementing Flexible Manufacturing, we transition from purely manual craftsmanship to Human-Robot Collaboration.

Primary Goal: Reduce lead times by 15-20% while stabilizing product quality in sanding and finishing.

Robotics Pathways

  • Pathway A (Cobots): Collaborative Sanding. Uses force-torque sensors for consistent finish on wood doors, removing the most labor-intensive bottleneck.
  • Pathway B (AMRs): Material Transport. Autonomous units moving flat-packs between cutting and assembly to reduce non-value-added walking time.
  • Pathway C (Vision): End-of-Line Inspection. Automated quality checks for paint defects and robotic palletizing for heavy lifting safety.
