** Dorai Home: Aviation Robotics Strategic Report

DORAI HOME

Strategic Aviation Robotics Integration Report

Executive Summary

The future of aviation maintenance at Dorai Home lies in the integration of robotic automation. This report outlines a strategic vision to transition from manual processes to a hybrid automated model. By deploying autonomous systems for cleaning, disinfection, and inspection, Dorai Home can achieve significant operational efficiency, superior hygiene standards, and a substantial reduction in long-term operational expenditures. This shift will position Dorai Home as a leader in aviation innovation.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Automates vacuuming and tray table sanitization. Reduces turnaround time and ensures consistent hygiene standards regardless of crew availability.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Deploys UV-C light post-cleaning to eliminate pathogens. Provides verifiable safety standards for passengers and crew.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing Systems

Automated gantry systems reduce water usage by 60% and eliminate height-safety risks while maintaining aerodynamic efficiency.

Pathway D: AI Inspection Bots

Thermal and visual imaging for structural detection. Enables predictive maintenance and reduces unscheduled downtime.
