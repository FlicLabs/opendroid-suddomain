Executive Summary

USG is positioned to transition from high-labor dependency to a technology-augmented service model. By integrating Flexible Manufacturing Systems into the Charlotte fabrication hub, USG can secure consistent weld quality on pressure vessels while significantly reducing lead times. The strategy prioritizes human-robot collaboration over replacement.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Welding

Deployment of Cobots (Fanuc CRX/UR16e) for orbital welding and root pass consistency. Reduces operator fatigue and stabilizes arc-time.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots for moving pipe spools and consumables from warehouse to welding bays, reducing non-productive walking time.

PATHWAY C

Automated QA & Staging

Vision-equipped robotic arms for geometric inspection and automated crating of field-ready components.
