Hudini Healthcare Robotics

Hudini is positioned to become the "Central Nervous System" for hospital robotics. By leveraging its existing integration with EHR and bedside tablets, Hudini can shift from a digital engagement platform to a physical orchestration engine. The primary opportunity lies in Patient-Initiated Robotic Concierge services, reducing nurse burnout by offloading non-clinical fetching tasks to autonomous mobile robots (AMRs).
