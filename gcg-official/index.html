Aviation Cleaning & Maintenance Automation Analysis

Executive Vision

The integration of robotic cleaning automation represents a strategic leap for GCG. By transitioning from manual-heavy workflows to a hybrid automated ecosystem, GCG can secure consistent hygiene standards, drastically reduce aircraft turnaround times, and mitigate labor shortages. This report outlines the financial and operational impact of deploying autonomous units across the GCG Caribbean and Latin American network.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

High-suction vacuum bots and electrostatic sprayers to reduce turn times by 30%.

PATHWAY B UV-C Disinfection

99.9% pathogen reduction in <10 mins. Premium health-safety add-on service.

PATHWAY C Exterior Aircraft Wash

Semi-autonomous rigs reducing water usage by 60% and improving fuel efficiency.

PATHWAY D AI Inspection Bots

Computer vision drones for preemptive GSE and fuselage damage detection.
