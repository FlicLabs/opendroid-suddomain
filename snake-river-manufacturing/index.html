01. Executive Vision

Snake River Manufacturing is positioned to dominate the heavy fabrication sector by adopting a flexible High-Mix/Low-Volume (HMLV) automation strategy. By pivoting from purely manual processes to a hybrid "Cobot" (Collaborative Robot) ecosystem, the organization will solve critical labor shortages while enhancing product consistency across agricultural and industrial lines.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Welding Cobots

Deployment of IP67-rated cobots for MIG/TIG welding. Allows human welders to focus on complex joins while robots handle repetitive sub-assemblies.

Pathway B: Heavy AMRs

Autonomous Mobile Robots (1000kg payload) to replace forklifts for moving steel plates and WIP carts, improving shop floor safety.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Automated palletizing and machine vision inspection to increase throughput yield and reduce ergonomic strain before shipping.
