Executive Summary

Erie County Medical Center Corporation (ECMCC) serves as the regional center for trauma, burn, behavioral health, and rehabilitation in Western New York. The proposed robotics portfolio targets three critical operational levers: (1) Surgical robotics expansion to decrease Length of Stay (LOS), (2) Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics within the long-term care and inpatient towers, and (3) Robotic pharmacy automation.

The dominant ROI driver is the reallocation of labor through logistics automation and the increase in surgical throughput capacity.
