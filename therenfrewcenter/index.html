Consultation Report

The Renfrew Center

Robotics & Automation Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

The Renfrew Center is the nation's pioneer in residential eating disorder treatment. The primary automation opportunity shifts from physical robotics to "Digital Robotics" and Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM). By deploying connected devices for blind weighing and vitals, Renfrew can significantly expand its virtual census (Renfrew@Home) to higher-acuity patients, driving an estimated £2.5M - £4.5M in annual net incremental benefit.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A - Highest Value

Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)

Scope: Deployment of blinded connected scales and vital monitoring for the Renfrew@Home virtual track.

Impact: Safely treats higher acuity patients at home. Reduces nursing admin time by 30%.

Exp. ROI: High
Pathway B - Efficiency

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Scope: Automating insurance verification, authorization, and intake workflows.

Impact: Reduces time-to-admission and denial rates. Improves clinician retention.

Exp. ROI: Medium
Pathway C - Clinical

Therapeutic VR & Biofeedback

Scope: VR for exposure therapy (body image/dining) and biofeedback wearables.

Impact: Market differentiation and standardized clinical exposure protocols.

Exp. ROI: Intangible

Financial Model: Pathway A (RPM Focus)

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation & Safety

Deploy RPA for central intake to speed admissions. Pilot blinded RPM devices with 50 high-acuity virtual patients to validate safety protocols.

Year 1.5 - 2: Scale Virtual

Roll out RPM hardware kits as standard for Renfrew@Home. Integrate data flows directly into Unified Treatment Model dashboards.

Year 2 - 3: Clinical Innovation

Introduce VR exposure therapy labs in residential campuses. Evaluate backend food-service automation.
