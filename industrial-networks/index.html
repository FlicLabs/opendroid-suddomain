Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Yard Transport

Deployment of ruggedized outdoor Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport heavy maintenance parts (couplers, brakes) across rail yards, reducing mechanic travel time by 25%.

Pathway B: Robotic MRO Picking

Implementation of ASRS (Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems) for parts inventory, ensuring 99.9% stock accuracy and condensing storage footprint by 60%.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Logistics

Utilization of fixed-position vision systems and drones for automated inbound railcar inspection, predicting maintenance needs before failures occur.
