Xsolis: Robotics & Automation Portfolio

Executive Summary: Xsolis leverages Cognitive Automation and AI-driven utilization review rather than physical robotics. By automating the friction between payers and providers via the CORTEX platform, Xsolis offers a high-ROI "software robotics" opportunity that eliminates administrative waste and optimizes revenue integrity.

Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Integration & Pilot (Months 1-6)

Establish live EHR feeds to CORTEX. Train AI models on historical data. Go-live with concurrent review automation.

Phase 2: Network Scale (Months 7-12)

Roll out to all network facilities. Activate automated "Precision Utilization Management" links with payers.

Phase 3: Advanced Automation (Year 2)

Deploy RPA bots for denial appeals and predictive logistics agents for bed capacity management.
