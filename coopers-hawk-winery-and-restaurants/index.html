01. Executive Summary

Cooper's Hawk represents a unique intersection of fine dining, retail, and membership logistics. Our strategic analysis suggests a "Hybrid Service Model." By deploying automation for low-value logistics (food running and bussing), we liberate human staff to focus on high-value interactions: Wine Club conversions and Tableside Sommelier services.

03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service

Tech: Autonomous Runners (Servi Plus)
Goal: Reduce kitchen trips.
Impact: Servers stay on the floor to sell more wine.

Pathway B: BOH Culinary

Tech: Smart Grills / Auto-Fryers
Goal: Consistency in appetizers.
Impact: Standardized quality during peak rushes.

Pathway C: AI Voice

Tech: AI Reservation Agents
Goal: Zero missed calls.
Impact: Hosts focus on seating, not answering phones.
