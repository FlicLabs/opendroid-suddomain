Strategic Vision

The vision for Artistic Tile is a "Zero-Breakage, High-Velocity" distribution model. By shifting heavy transport to Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and intricate picking to Vertical Lift Modules (VLMs), we mitigate the risk of damaging high-value stone while optimizing labor for white-glove quality assurance.

Robotics Pathways
  • Pathway A (Heavy AMRs): Decouple travel time from picking. Automated transport of pallets from Receiving to Storage.
  • Pathway B (VLMs): High-density storage for mosaics and samples. Reduces floor footprint by 85%.
  • Pathway C (AI Logistics): Predictive lot management to ensure stone color consistency in order fulfillment.
