Executive Strategy

The transformation of Express Logistics Inc. focuses on bridging the gap between current throughput constraints and the rising demands of E-commerce. By leveraging Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI Orchestration, we will eliminate the inefficiencies of manual transport and maximize the utility of high-cost urban real estate.
