Executive Summary

Automating Care Infrastructure

LifeSkills Inc. faces a critical turning point where labor shortages in Direct Support Professionals (DSPs) threaten operational sustainability. By pivoting from a pure labor-based supervision model to a sensor-augmented robotics model, the organization can unlock significant margin while improving client safety. The strategy focuses on high-impact, low-dexterity automation: smart home monitoring and automated medication dispensing.
