01. Executive Vision

PurposeBuilt Brands stands at the precipice of an Industry 4.0 transformation. Moving from traditional batch processing to a Flexible Manufacturing Ecosystem will allow the facility to handle high SKU proliferation with minimal changeover downtime. The strategy prioritizes the integration of collaborative robotics to enhance safety and agility, positioning the Gurnee, IL facility as a beacon of modern CPG manufacturing.

02. Robotics Opportunities

PATHWAY A: COBOTS

Deployment of IP66 washdown-ready cobots for trigger-sprayer insertion. This addresses the highest ergonomic risk factor on the line while ensuring 99.9% insertion accuracy.

PATHWAY B: AMR LOGISTICS

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots for "Kanban-on-wheels." AMRs will transport raw packaging materials from storage to filling lines, decoupling human traffic from forklifts.

PATHWAY C: PALLETIZING

Heavy-payload collaborative palletizers at the End-of-Line. This removes the primary throughput bottleneck, allowing for continuous operation during breaks and shift changes.
