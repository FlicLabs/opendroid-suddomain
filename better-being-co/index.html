Executive Vision

Better Being Co. requires a strategic pivot toward Industry 4.0 to handle high-mix, high-volume demands while maintaining strict GMP compliance. Our proposal transitions your facility from static processing to a flexible manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating Cobots and AMRs, we project a 30% increase in space efficiency and rapid adaptability for diverse packaging formats.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision
Washdown-ready collaborative robots for capping and bottle unscrambling. Ensures consistent torque application and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency
Autonomous Mobile Robots replacing manual forklifts. Triggered by fleet management software to prevent line starvation of caps, labels, and bottles.

Pathway C: Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield
High-payload arms with vision systems for end-of-line stacking. Includes OCR for lot code verification to ensure 100% traceability.
