Executive Vision

MFP Automation Engineering is positioned to transition from a traditional systems integrator to a Smart Manufacturing ecosystem leader. The strategy focuses on flexible manufacturing for high-mix, low-volume hydraulic power units, leveraging data-driven insights to optimize throughput.

Organizational Profile

  • HQ: Grand Rapids, Michigan
  • Type: Discrete Manufacturing & Integration
  • Core Product: Hydraulic Power Units (HPUs), Manifolds, Lubrication Systems.
  • Challenge: High manual labor in material handling and repetitive torque assembly.

01. Cobot Assembly

Focus: Safety & Precision

Deployment of collaborative robots for manifold assembly. Ensures 100% torque compliance on valve stacks and reduces repetitive strain injuries for skilled technicians.

02. AMR Logistics

Focus: WIP Flow

Heavy-duty Autonomous Mobile Robots (500kg+) to transport motors and reservoirs between warehouse and assembly. Eliminates non-value-added walking time.

03. Auto-Inspection

Focus: Quality Yield

End-of-line robotic palletizing with integrated vision systems. Automatically inspects port plugs and label placement before crating.
