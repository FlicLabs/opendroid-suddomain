Executive Summary

Juice Beauty is positioned to redefine "Clean Beauty" through "Clean Manufacturing." Our strategy focuses on reducing contamination risks through non-contact robotics and increasing throughput via automated material handling.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Assembly

Deploying IP66 hygienic Cobots for filling and capping. Ensures precise torque and reduces repetitive strain injuries.

PATHWAY B

AMR Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots to move organic ingredients from cold storage to mixing tanks, maintaining temperature integrity.

PATHWAY C

Vision & Palletizing

Automated end-of-line systems using computer vision for label compliance and robotic arms for shipping palletization.
