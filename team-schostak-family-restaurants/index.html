Executive Summary

This report outlines a strategic transition for Team Schostak Family Restaurants towards a "High-Tech, High-Touch" service model. By leveraging the hybrid portfolio of TSFR (Casual Dining, Fast Casual, and QSR), we have identified opportunities to reduce operational friction through robotics. The goal is to alleviate labor shortages in the Michigan market while targeting a 15-25% reduction in FOH labor costs and improving guest throughput.
