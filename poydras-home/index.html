RESEARCH PORTFOLIO: POYDRAS HOME

Executive Summary

Poydras Home is transitioning to a "Green House" decentralized care model. While this improves resident outcomes, it creates significant logistical inefficiencies due to increased travel distances for staff. The integration of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for dining and laundry logistics represents the highest ROI opportunity, capable of reclaiming thousands of nursing hours annually.

Strategic Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Logistics (Priority)

Deployment of AMRs to handle food/linen transport between central hubs and Green House units. Solves the distance inefficiency of the new campus layout.

Pathway B: Robotic Rehab

Installation of body-weight support systems in the Therapy Gym to differentiate Poydras as the premier regional stroke/fall recovery center.

Pathway C: Cognitive Care

Utilizing PARO social robots in memory care units to reduce resident anxiety and pharmaceutical reliance.
