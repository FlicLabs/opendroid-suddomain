Executive Summary

MainStreet Family Care is a rapidly expanding urgent care and primary care network focused on delivering accessible medical services to rural and suburban communities. The strongest robotics and automation levers are (1) Process Automation and Intelligent Triage to reduce front desk friction, (2) Automated Lab Processing to accelerate throughput, and (3) Autonomous Sanitization to maximize room utilization. The dominant ROI driver is the increase in patient velocity per hour.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Intelligent Triage & Process Automation

Deployment of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and AI-driven kiosk inputs to handle 90% of intake, insurance verification, and initial triage data entry.

  • Key Lever: Automated insurance scrubbing before the patient sees a provider.
  • Key Lever: AI-driven queuing that routes patients based on acuity.
  • Impact: Reduction in front desk labor hours by 30%.

Pathway B: Lab Automation

Implementation of compact, benchtop automated liquid handling systems and rapid diagnostic readers that integrate directly with the EMR.

  • Key Lever: Automated aliquoting of samples to reduce technician time.
  • Impact: Reduction in lab processing time by 40%.
