Strategic Pathways

Saint Luke's Health System is positioned to leverage robotics across three primary vectors:

  • Surgical Robotics Expansion: Focus on high-margin orthopedic and soft tissue procedures using Mako and da Vinci systems to reduce Length of Stay (LOS) and capture market share.
  • Logistics Automation (AMRs): Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for EVS, Linen, and Pharmacy transport to reduce non-clinical labor strain and improve nurse availability.
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA): Software bots to handle Revenue Cycle Management, prior authorizations, and claims processing, reducing administrative overhead.
