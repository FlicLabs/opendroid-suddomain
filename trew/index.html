01. Executive Summary

Trew is positioned to revolutionize its manufacturing capabilities by transitioning from traditional fabrication to a smart, flexible ecosystem. By integrating robotics into the production of MDR conveyors and sortation solutions, Trew can decouple revenue growth from labor headcount linearities, ensuring scalability to meet explosive e-commerce demands.

02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Assembly

Focus: Safety & Precision
Deployment of collaborative robots for high-mix welding of conveyor frames and force-sensitive roller insertion, reducing repetitive strain injuries.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow
Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to transport heavy steel components between fabrication, powder coating, and assembly lines.

Pathway C: End-of-Line

Focus: Throughput
Automated visual inspection of cabling and robotic palletizing of finished chute sections to streamline shipping operations.
