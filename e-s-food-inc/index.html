02. Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Internal Transport

Deployment of AMRs to handle dock-to-stock movements. This eliminates manual forklift travel, allowing staff to focus on high-value quality control and picking tasks.

Implementation of Goods-to-Person stations for high-velocity dry goods. This densifies storage by 40% and ensures near-perfect picking accuracy for mixed-case pallets.

Computer vision integration for cold-chain management. Automated expiration scanning ensures strict FEFO adherence and reduces spoilage costs.
