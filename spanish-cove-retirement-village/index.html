Strategic Analysis

Executive Summary: Spanish Cove is positioned to solve critical labor bottlenecks in dining and housekeeping through targeted automation. The introduction of autonomous runners allows human staff to focus on resident interaction, transforming the dining experience from a logistical challenge to a hospitality triumph.

Implementation Strategy: We recommend a phased approach starting with a 3-month pilot in the main dining hall using a Robotics-as-a-Service (RaaS) model to minimize upfront capital risk. Success will be measured by steps saved, table turnover rates, and employee satisfaction scores.
