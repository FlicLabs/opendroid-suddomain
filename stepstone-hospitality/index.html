Executive Strategy Summary

StepStone Hospitality is uniquely positioned to leverage automation across its diverse Marriott, Hilton, and IHG portfolio. By focusing on Pathway A (FOH Service) and Pathway B (BOH Consistency), the organization can mitigate the rising costs of labor while simultaneously elevating the guest service standard. The integration of AI-driven kiosks further ensures revenue optimization through precision upselling.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot food runners at 3 flagship convention hotels.
  • Year 2: 30% portfolio penetration & AI Room Service agents.
  • Year 3: Automated BOH modules for high-volume sites.
  • Year 5: Full enterprise-scale autonomous operations.
