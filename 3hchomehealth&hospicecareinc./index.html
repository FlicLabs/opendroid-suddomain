3HC Home Health & Hospice

Robotics & Automation Opportunity Portfolio

Executive Summary

The Core Opportunity: 3HC faces the classic rural healthcare logistics challenge: high windshield time relative to patient care time. By shifting from manual logistics to AI-driven "Software Robotics" and introducing Telerobotic Presence, 3HC can unlock tens of thousands of incremental visits annually without increasing headcount.

Primary ROI Driver: Converting non-productive driving hours across Eastern NC into reimbursable clinical episodes.

Organizational Profile

Geography: 20-25 counties in Eastern North Carolina (Triangle to Coast).
Model: Non-profit skilled nursing, rehabilitation, hospice, and private duty.
Operational Constraint: High mileage costs and rural staffing shortages.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Intelligent Logistics

Type: Software Robotics / AI

Automated, dynamic route optimization to compress the travel schedule of ~150 field clinicians.

Impact: +15% Capacity Lift Status: Immediate Priority

Pathway B: Telerobotics

Type: Telepresence Hardware

Remote presence robots and smart hubs for high-acuity chronic care and remote hospice support.

Impact: Reduced Readmissions Status: Strategic / 6-12 Months

Pathway C: Assistive Robotics

Type: Therapeutic / Social

Robotic companions (e.g., Paro) for dementia and palliative care to reduce patient anxiety.

Impact: Market Differentiation Status: Innovation Pilot
Financial Model: Pathway A (Logistics)

Modeling a 150-clinician fleet operating in a rural environment with current inefficiencies.
