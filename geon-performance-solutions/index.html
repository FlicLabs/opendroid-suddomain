01. Executive Summary

Geon Performance Solutions is positioned to lead the polymer sector by transitioning from batch processing to a Material-as-a-Service (MaaS) ecosystem. By integrating collaborative robotics and AMRs, Geon can address labor shortages, improve compounding consistency, and achieve a flexible manufacturing state capable of High-Mix/Low-Volume production efficiently.

02. Organizational Profile

Sector: Specialty Polymer Formulations & Compounding

Footprint: Global (12+ Facilities including Westlake, OH HQ)

Process Type: Hybrid (Process Compounding + Discrete Finishing)

Current Bottlenecks:

  • Manual additive weighing creating formulation variance.
  • Forklift dependency for internal resin logistics.
  • End-of-line palletizing acting as a throughput cap.

03. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Focus: Lab Automation & Precision Dosing

  • Automated tensile testing and sample preparation in QA labs.
  • Micro-dosing of hazardous powder additives to ensure 99.99% accuracy.

Pathway B: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focus: WIP Flow Efficiency

  • Transporting raw resin from warehouse to extruder hoppers.
  • Moving gaylords of finished pellets to shipping zones without forklift traffic.

Pathway C: Automated Palletizing

Focus: Throughput & Yield

  • High-speed palletizing of 25kg bags and bulk boxes.
  • Integrated vision systems for seal inspection and label verification.
