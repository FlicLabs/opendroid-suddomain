Executive Summary: Experiential Automation

For The Maggiore Group, robotics represents more than efficiency; it is a tool to amplify the high-energy atmosphere of concepts like Hash Kitchen and The Sicilian Butcher. By deploying automation for heavy lifting (5ft charcuterie boards) and high-volume bussing, we protect staff physical health while accelerating table turns during peak brunch hours.

Strategic Goal: Create a hybrid service model where robots handle the "Heavy & Repetitive" tasks, allowing human staff to focus exclusively on the "Vibe & Upsell."

Pathway A: FOH

Serving & Bussing Bots

  • Transport heavy Meatball Buckets/Boards.
  • Roam as mobile bussing stations.
  • Reduce server fatigue and back injuries.

Pathway B: BOH

Consistency Automation

  • Auto-lift fryers for Cannoli/Brunch items.
  • Precision pasta boiling stations.
  • Standardized cooking times for quality.
