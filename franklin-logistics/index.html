Franklin Logistics

Strategic Automation & Robotics Roadmap

Executive Vision

Franklin Logistics is poised to transition from a manual Person-to-Goods model to a high-velocity Automated Smart Hub. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI-driven predictive logistics, we project a 250% increase in throughput and a significant reduction in operational overhead within 24 months.

Pathway A: AMRs

Internal Transport Focus

Decouples travel from picking. Operators stay in zones while robots handle transit. Expected to reduce non-value-added travel by 40%.

Pathway B: ASRS

Picking & Density Focus

Utilization of vertical space (up to 40ft) and robotic picking arms. Targets 99.9% pick accuracy and reclaim 45% floor space.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive Efficiency

AI-driven WES to optimize dock scheduling and pre-position inventory based on real-time demand signals.
