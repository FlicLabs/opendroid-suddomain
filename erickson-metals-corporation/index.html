Strategic Robotics Pathways

Pathway A

Internal AMRs

Deployment of heavy-duty autonomous mobile robots (1,500kg payload) to move skids and long-stock, decoupling transport from saw operators.

Pathway B

ASRS / Storage

Vertical Lift Modules and Honeycomb storage for sheet/bar stock. Increases facility storage density by 40-60%.

Pathway C

AI Logistics

Predictive nesting algorithms to reduce scrap and AI-driven inventory replenishment based on mill lead times.
