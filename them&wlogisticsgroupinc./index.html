01. Executive Summary

The M&W Logistics Group Inc. is positioned to pivot from manual-heavy logistics to a human-centric automated model. By integrating Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) and AI logic, M&W can secure a future-proof position in the 3PL market.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs (Transport)

Replacing manual forklifts for long-haul internal travel. Targets a 25% increase in pallet movement per man-hour.

Pathway B: Robotic Picking

High-velocity SKU handling for food & beverage. Increases storage density by 40% via vertical utilization.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Computer vision at bay doors and TMS integration to predict truck arrival, reducing dwell time by 30%.
