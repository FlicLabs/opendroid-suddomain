Executive Summary

The hospitality landscape is undergoing a tectonic shift toward automation. For Ditka's Restaurant Group, the strategic vision is "Invisible Automation." We propose integrating robotics to handle low-value logistics—heavy lifting and dish transport—freeing human servers to focus entirely on guest rapport and wine service. This hybrid model mitigates labor shortages while maintaining the "Iron Mike" standard of excellence.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House (Runners)

HIGH PRIORITY

Utilizing tiered serving robots (e.g., Bear Robotics Servi) to transport sizzle-platters and dirty dishes. This reduces server fatigue and allows them to remain in their section.

Pathway B: Back-of-House (Auto-Grill)

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Implementation of automated fryer arms for appetizers to ensure consistency and improve kitchen safety, allowing chefs to focus on steak preparation.

Pathway C: AI Concierge

IMMEDIATE DEPLOYMENT

24/7 AI Voice Agents to handle reservations and FAQs, ensuring zero missed calls during peak service hours.
