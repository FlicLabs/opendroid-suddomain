Executive Summary

Buffalo Rock Company stands at a critical juncture for logistics modernization. The beverage distribution industry faces unique challenges: high SKU proliferation, extreme physical demands on labor, and tight Direct Store Delivery (DSD) windows.

Our vision is the transition to an Intelligent Distribution Ecosystem. By shifting from manual case-picking to a hybrid automated model, Buffalo Rock can secure its supply chain against labor volatility while significantly increasing throughput capacity.
