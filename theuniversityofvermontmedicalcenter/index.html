Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Surgical

Advanced Surgical Fleet

Scope: Expansion of Da Vinci & Mako systems for urology, gyn, and orthopedics.

Impact: Reduces Length of Stay (LOS) by 0.5–1.5 days. Retains high-margin regional referrals.

ROI: ~$1.7M net annual benefit via volume retention and bed capacity release.

Pathway B: Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots

Scope: Deploy AMRs (Tugs) for linen, waste, meds, and meal transport.

Impact: Recovers 15k-20k clinical labor hours annually. Reduces staff injury and burnout.

ROI: $1.2M–$1.8M in labor efficiency savings. Payback < 24 months.

Pathway C: Rehab

Robotic Rehabilitation

Scope: Gait training robotics (Vector/ZeroG) for stroke and TBI recovery.

Impact: Superior functional outcomes, staff safety, and regional brand differentiation.

ROI: Driven by donor funding and cost-avoidance (readmission reduction).
