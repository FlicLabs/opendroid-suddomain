Executive Summary

The integration of robotics for Down the Street Eats focuses on a Hybrid Service Model. By automating low-value logistics (running, bussing, repetitive frying), human staff are liberated to focus on high-value hospitality.

This strategy aims to mitigate labor volatility while enhancing the guest experience through faster service and consistent food quality.

Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House

Focus: Logistics & Turnover

  • Deployment of "Runner Bots" for food delivery and bussing.
  • Reduces server steps-per-shift by up to 40%.
  • Allows servers to remain in their sections, increasing upsell opportunities.

Pathway B: Back-of-House

Focus: Consistency & Safety

  • Automated frying baskets for fries and wings.
  • Ensures 100% texture consistency and reduces oil burns/accidents.
