Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: FOH Service

Deployment: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

Focuses on dining room cleanliness and food running. Relieves counter staff to focus on register speed and guest interaction. Reduces perception of wait times.

Pathway B: BOH Automation

Deployment: Robotic Fry Arms & Auto-Grills

The highest ROI pathway. Eliminates oil burn risks and guarantees perfect cook times for high-volume items (fries, chips). Redeploys 1 FTE to assembly.

Pathway C: Drive-Thru AI

Deployment: Voice AI Agents

Never misses an upsell. Filters background noise for 99.9% order accuracy. Critical for maximizing peak hour throughput in the drive-thru lane.

Implementation Roadmap

  • YEAR 1
    Pilot & Calibration: Deploy Pathway C (Voice) and B (Fryers) in 3 high-volume test sites.
  • YEAR 2
    Regional Expansion: Rollout to 30% of locations. Centralized tech support established.
  • YEAR 3-4
    System Integration: Inventory telemetry integration. Predictive labor modeling based on robot data.
  • YEAR 5
    Autonomous Shift: Testing fully automated late-night operations with minimal supervision.
