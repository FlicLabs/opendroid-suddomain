Executive Summary

The North Texas Food Bank is positioned to transition from traditional volunteer-heavy logistics to a High-Velocity Automated Ecosystem. This shift will decouple food volume from labor availability, ensuring nutritional value retention and scalable distribution across the 13-county service area.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for dock-to-stock transport.

  • Eliminates 12+ miles of daily forklift travel.
  • Reduces safety incidents in volunteer zones.
  • 25% increase in pallet velocity.

Pathway B: Auto-Kitting

Goods-to-Person ASRS for specialized food box creation.

  • 40% increase in vertical storage density.
  • Near-zero packing error rate.
  • Accessible for volunteers with mobility limits.
