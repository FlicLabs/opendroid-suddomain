TACO MAC AUTOMATION STRATEGY

Strategic Robotics Integration & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

The strategic vision for Taco Mac focuses on a Hybrid Human-Centric Automation model. This approach integrates robotics to augment staff capabilities, specifically targeting logistical burdens in the front-of-house and consistency challenges in the back-of-house. By deploying autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for food running and automated fry stations for product consistency, Taco Mac aims to increase table turnover rates and reduce labor costs by 15-25% while maintaining the social, sports-centric atmosphere of the brand.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Front-of-House Serving

Focus: Table Turnover & Staff Support

Deployment of autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for heavy food and beverage delivery. Reduces server physical fatigue, allowing staff to manage 6-8 tables instead of 4-5. Ideal for high-volume "game day" rushes.

Pathway B: Back-of-House Automation

Focus: Consistency & Safety

Automated fry stations for wing preparation. Ensures precise cook times for brand consistency and reduces safety risks associated with hot oil handling and slip hazards.

Pathway C: AI-Driven Ordering

Focus: Upselling & Accuracy

AI Voice Agents for phone catering orders and counter kiosks. Programmed for 100% consistency in upselling (e.g., beer flights, sides), increasing average check size without customer friction.

Financial Model & Impact Analysis

CAPEX Breakdown: $15k-$25k for FOH units; $30k-$40k for BOH automation.

OPEX Impact: Direct reduction in labor scheduling costs (15-25%) and minimized food waste (3-5%).

Payback Strategy: Driven by labor savings and increased revenue via higher table turnover rates.

Implementation Roadmap (5-Year)

Year 1: Pilot & Proof of Concept

Deploy FOH Serving Robots at 2 high-volume locations. Establish cleaning/charging protocols. Measure baseline metrics for table turn time and labor hours.

Year 2: BOH Integration

Introduce Back-of-House automated frying in pilot locations. Roll out FOH robots to 50% of the estate. Optimize navigation and staff training (ETL).

Year 3: Full Estate & AI Upselling

100% FOH robot deployment across all Taco Mac locations. Implement AI Voice Agents for catering and large party reservations to capture incremental revenue.

Year 4: Advanced Analytics

Integrate robot data with POS for predictive staffing based on sports schedules. Implement predictive maintenance for BOH equipment to eliminate downtime.

Year 5: Sustainability & Innovation

Upgrade to next-gen eco-friendly models. Review 5-year ROI audit. Explore autonomous bussing and sanitization systems for enhanced hygiene.
