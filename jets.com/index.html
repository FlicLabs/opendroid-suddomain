01. Executive Vision

The private aviation sector demands a synthesis of luxury, speed, and absolute safety. For Jets.com, the integration of robotics into cleaning and maintenance operations represents a shift from labor-intensive manual turnover to precision-engineered automation. This strategy aims to standardize the "Jets.com Clean" guarantee while maximizing fleet utilization.

02. Robotics Pathways

Pathway A: Autonomous Cabin Cleaning

Deployment of Compact Articulated Vacuums (CAVs) designed for the narrow aisles of Learjets and Gulfstreams. Focus on carpet restoration and debris removal.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Hospital-grade sterilization for VIP peace of mind. 5-minute cycles to inactivate pathogens on armrests and tray tables without chemical residue.

Pathway C: Exterior Dry Wash

Hangar-based robots utilizing microfiber technology to clean fuselages without water runoff, ensuring compliance with strict airport environmental regulations.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Thermal-equipped drones for pre-flight checks, identifying airframe anomalies faster than human visual inspection.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase at Teterboro (TEB). Deployment of 3 Interior / 1 Exterior unit.
  • Year 2: Expansion to Van Nuys (VNY) & Opa-locka (OPF).
  • Year 3: Full Fleet Standardization & "Robotic Sterile" Certification.
  • Year 5: Predictive Maintenance Integration (15% reduction in AOG).
