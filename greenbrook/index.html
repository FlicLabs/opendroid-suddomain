Executive Summary

Greenbrook TMS operates in a high-touch, labor-intensive environment where clinical technicians manually position magnetic coils for depression treatment. The dominant ROI driver for robotics is the decoupling of labor from chair time via robotic-assisted positioning and AI-driven logistics. This shift enables a multi-chair supervisory model, significantly enhancing margins and capacity.
