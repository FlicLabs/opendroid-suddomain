01. Executive Vision

Ventrac's transition to Industry 4.0 centers on Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS). By integrating collaborative robotics into the Orrville facility, the objective is to decouple labor hours from production volume, allowing for rapid scaling during peak seasonal demand for snow and mowing attachments.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobot Welding

Deployment of collaborative arms for high-mix arc welding tasks. Focus on reducing fatigue and increasing weld consistency on sub-assemblies.

Pathway B: AMR Logistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots handling WIP transport between Fabrication, Paint, and Assembly. Reduces forklift dependency and traffic risks.

Pathway C: Auto-Palletizing

End-of-line automation using vision systems to inspect and palletize diverse attachment SKUs for shipping.
