ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

A. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning Robots

Compact AMRs utilizing Lidar for navigation. Performs vacuuming, waste collection, and surface sanitization of tray tables and armrests.

B. UV-C Disinfection Robots

Zero-contact units delivering rapid pathogen elimination. Eliminates chemical residue and reduces biohazard risks during short layovers.

C. Exterior Aircraft Washing

Automated gantry or mobile robotic systems. Reduces water usage by 30% and cuts wash time from hours to minutes.

D. AI-Powered Inspection Bots

Thermal imaging and high-res cameras. Automated fuselage integrity checks and bird strike damage detection.
