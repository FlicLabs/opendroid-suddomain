Midstate Radiology Associates LLC

Robotics Research Portfolio & ROI Analysis

Executive Summary

MRA is a dominant imaging provider in Connecticut. The primary opportunity lies in decoupling labor from geography to solve staffing shortages.

Top Priority
Remote Scanning "Cockpits" (Telerobotics)

Key Drivers: Staffing shortages, asset utilization, and administrative friction.

Organizational Profile
  • Scale: 25+ Locations in CT
  • Partners: Hartford HealthCare
  • Volume: High-throughput outpatient imaging
  • Bottleneck: Technologist availability & Scheduling
Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Remote Scanning

Technology: Virtual Cockpits allowing 1 senior tech to manage 3 scanners remotely.

Impact: +15-20k annual exams, extended operating hours.

ROI: High

Pathway B: Admin RPA

Technology: Software robotics for prior-auth and scheduling.

Impact: Immediate cash flow, reduced denials.

Payback: 3 Months

Pathway C: Interventional

Technology: Robotic needle guidance & Auto-positioning.

Impact: Clinical differentiation & safety.
