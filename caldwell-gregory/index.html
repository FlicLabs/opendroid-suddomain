01. Executive Vision

Transitioning from a traditional service model to a Smart Micro-Factory ecosystem. By integrating Industry 4.0 automation into regional distribution hubs, Caldwell & Gregory can revolutionize the refurbishment and staging of commercial laundry assets, ensuring faster deployment and higher cosmetic standards.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Machine Tending & Assembly

Assisting technicians with heavy drum lifting and precision torque application to reduce strain injuries and warranty leaks.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: Material Transport

Autonomous Mobile Robots handling heavy commercial units from dock-to-staging, eliminating manual forklift dependencies.

Pathway C: Quality Control

Focus: Automated Inspection

Vision-guided systems for end-of-line aesthetic validation and automated palletizing for shipment.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot launch at single high-volume hub (1 AMR + 1 Cobot).
  • Year 2: ERP Integration and automated parts reordering.
  • Year 3: Vision System rollout for QC; 30% throughput gain.
  • Year 5: "Lights Out" staging capabilities.
