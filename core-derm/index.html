Executive Summary

Core Derm is positioned to transition from a labor-constrained model to a tech-enabled platform. By deploying Automated Total Body Photography (ATBP), Robotic Hair Restoration, and AI Workflow Automation, the network can uncouple revenue from manual provider hours. The dominant ROI driver is increased throughput and the creation of high-margin cosmetic procedural lines that do not increase provider burnout.
