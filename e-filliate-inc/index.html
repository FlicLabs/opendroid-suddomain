Automated Logistics Strategy & Feasibility Study

Executive Vision: High-Velocity Accessory Distribution

E-Filliate Inc. requires a transition from traditional manual warehousing to a Goods-to-Person (GTP) architecture. Given the high-density, small-form-factor nature of mobile accessories (cables, audio, charging), automation offers a critical advantage in shrinkage control, accuracy, and replenishment speed.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A

AMR Internal Transport

Replacing manual pallet jacks with Autonomous Mobile Robots. Focus on "Dock-to-Stock" efficiency to reduce non-value-added travel time.

Pathway B

Robotic Piece Picking

Implementation of bin-based ASRS (AutoStore or Shuttle). Ideal for small SKUs like earbuds and cables, maximizing vertical storage density.

Pathway C

AI Logistics

Predictive inbound/outbound logic. Using retail data to pre-position high-velocity SKUs near packing stations during peak seasons.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program & WMS Audit (3-5 AMRs)
  • Year 2: Scaled Automation (ASRS for Top 20% SKUs)
  • Year 3-4: AI Integration & Optimization
  • Year 5: Dark Warehouse Capability
