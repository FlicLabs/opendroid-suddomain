Executive Summary

This research outlines a strategic vision for integrating robotic automation into Dreamline Aviation's workflows. The primary objective is to transition to a hybrid automated ecosystem, reducing Turn-Around-Time (TAT) and minimizing long-term operational expenditures (OPEX) while ensuring consistent hygiene standards.

  • Fleet: Narrow Body (45), Wide Body (12), Regional (18).
  • Hubs: Centralized major transport hubs.
  • Current Model: High reliance on outsourced labor.
