Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

LiDAR-mapped autonomous vacuums designed for single-aisle Airbus configurations. Ensures consistent debris removal during 45-minute turnarounds.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Clinical-grade UV-C robots aligning with "Mood Lighting" aesthetics to eliminate 99.9% of pathogens on tray tables and lavatories.

Pathway C: Exterior Wash

Gantry and drone-based washing systems that recycle water, ensuring compliance with California environmental regulations.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

Computer vision crawlers for fuselage inspection, detecting lightning strikes and rivet stress ahead of AOG delays.

Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Program at SFO (5 Cabin Units, 2 UV-C Units).
  • Year 2: Expansion to LAX and introduction of Exterior Washing.
  • Year 3: Fleet standardization across all major hubs.
  • Year 4: Deep AI integration with maintenance tracking software.
  • Year 5: Full Autonomy (80% automated cleaning workforce).
