Executive Summary

Carefluence is positioned to pivot from purely digital data interoperability to physical workflow orchestration. By becoming the "Central Nervous System" for hospital robotics, the company can address the fragmentation between Electronic Health Records (EHR) and autonomous fleets.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Intelligent Process Automation

Scope: High-volume administrative "Software Robotics" for claims and referrals.
Impact: 40-60% reduction in processing costs; $5M-8M annual savings.

Pathway B: Robotic Fleet Orchestration

Scope: Middleware to connect physical robots (Tugs, Delivery) to EHR data triggers.
Impact: 30% utilization uplift; elimination of manual dispatching.
