02. Robotics Opportunities

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of heavy-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to automate the movement of roll cages and pallets to loading docks, eliminating non-value-added walking time.

Pathway B: Auto-Picking

Utilization of ASRS (Automated Storage & Retrieval) and computer vision arms for handling high-mix ambient SKUs with superior density and accuracy.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Orchestration layers that synchronize robot traffic with delivery route planning, ensuring Last-In-First-Out (LIFO) loading optimization.

03. Implementation Roadmap

  • Year 1: Pilot Phase in Mid-Atlantic DC (AMR Transport focus).
  • Year 2: Expansion to 3 major hubs & Vision Picking Pilot.
  • Year 3: Network Standardization & AI Orchestration.
  • Year 5: Full Autonomy Integration & Dark Warehouse capability.
