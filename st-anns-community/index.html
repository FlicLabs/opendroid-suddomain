Executive Summary

St. Ann's Community is a premier senior living provider in Rochester, NY, serving thousands across independent living, assisted living, and skilled nursing. The organization faces critical pressure from labor shortages and fixed reimbursement rates. This analysis identifies Automation of Dining Hospitality and Logistics Transport as the primary levers to reclaim over $1.2M in cumulative value over 5 years. By shifting staff from conveyance to care, St. Ann's can improve resident safety and operational margins.
