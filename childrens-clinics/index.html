Executive Summary

Children's Clinics (Tucson, AZ) is a critical medical home for children with complex needs. The proposed robotics strategy shifts focus from manual administrative coordination and physically intensive therapy to an AI-orchestrated, robotic-assisted model. Key drivers are Pediatric Rehab Robotics (Pathway A) and Administrative RPA (Pathway B).
