01. Rocky Mountain Poison & Drug Safety

Focus: Software Robotics (RPA) & AI Triage

Rocky Mountain Poison & Drug Safety (RMPDS) is a premier public health and toxicology center. The primary opportunity lies in shifting from high-cost manual clinical data entry to an AI-augmented workflow. Implementation of Software Robotics for compliance reporting and AI agents for low-acuity triage offers massive ROI by freeing up toxicologists for complex case management.
