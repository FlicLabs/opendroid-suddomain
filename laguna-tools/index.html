01. Executive Summary

Laguna Tools is positioned to transition from semi-automated batch assembly to a fully connected Industry 4.0 ecosystem. By integrating Cobots for precision gantry assembly and AMRs for heavy casting transport, Laguna can reduce lead times, mitigate skilled labor shortages in Texas and California, and create a scalable "Smart Assembly Hub" model.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Application: Machine Tending & Gantry Assembly.

Deploy IP54 Cobots to assist with heavy lifting of cast iron tables and precision screwdriving, ensuring identical torque specs on every CNC unit.

Pathway B: AMRs

Application: Material Transport.

Autonomous Mobile Robots to replace forklifts for moving WIP (Work In Progress) from welding to assembly lines, improving floor safety.

Pathway C: Quality & EOL

Application: Inspection & Palletizing.

Vision-systems for geometric verification of router beds and vacuum-grippers for boxing accessories.
