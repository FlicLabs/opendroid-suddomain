Executive Summary

Aeroseal is positioned at a critical juncture in the HVAC sector. To meet decarbonization goals and rising demand, the company must transition from batch-assembly to a Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS). By integrating Industry 4.0 automation, Aeroseal can reduce lead times, improve inventory turnover, and achieve a net productivity gain of 25% within 24 months.

Organizational Profile

HQ: Miamisburg, Ohio

Operations: Hybrid manufacturing environment managing chemical processing (proprietary sealants) and discrete assembly (electromechanical injection units).

Challenge: Reliance on manual assembly limits surge capacity during peak HVAC seasons. High ergonomic risk in end-of-line packaging.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Cobots

Focus: Safety & Precision

Automating torque-critical screwdriving and PCB insertion for HomeSeal units. Ensures consistent seal pressure and reduces warranty claims.

Pathway B: AMRs

Focus: WIP Flow

Autonomous Mobile Robots for transporting sealant canisters and motor assemblies. Decouples material handling from skilled technician labor.

Pathway C: EOL Automation

Focus: Throughput

Robotic palletizing for heavy sealing units and buckets. Integrated vision systems for label verification and accessory kit inspection.
