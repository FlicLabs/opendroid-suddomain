Executive Summary

Stantec is positioned to bridge the gap between traditional AEC and advanced manufacturing. The strategic focus is on Modular Construction and Infrastructure Component Fabrication.


Industry 4.0 Vision: Transition from project-based site assembly to factory-controlled, scalable modular manufacturing. This leverages Digital Twins for design validation and robotics for physical production.

Organizational Profile

  • Production Scale: Low-volume, High-mix (Bespoke Modules).
  • Core Process: Discrete Manufacturing (Steel frames, MEP integration).
  • Key Locations: Western Canada, UK, Australia, US (Water Infra).
  • Challenge: Skilled labor shortage & site safety risks.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A Cobots: Machine Tending & Assembly

Deployment of collaborative arms for precision welding and structural assembly. Focus on Safety (handling heavy loads) and Precision (consistent weld quality).


Pathway B AMRs: Material Transport

Large-payload Autonomous Mobile Robots to move raw materials (steel, drywall) and WIP components across bays. Focus on WIP Flow Efficiency and reducing forklift accidents.


Pathway C Automated End-of-Line

Robotic 3D vision inspection and heavy-load palletizing. Focus on Throughput and Yield by automating QC and load-out.
