EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The strategic integration of robotic cleaning automation aims to revolutionize Pacific Aviation's operational efficiency. By transitioning to a hybrid human-robotic workforce, we ensure superior hygiene standards, optimize turnaround times, and achieve significant OPEX reductions while maintaining the highest safety protocols.


ORGANIZATIONAL PROFILE

Fleet Composition: Narrow & Wide-body aircraft
Operational Model: Hub-and-spoke with heavy reliance on manual ground crews.
Opportunity: High volume hubs allow for rapid data collection and deployment scalability.

ROBOTICS PATHWAYS

  • 1. Autonomous Cabin Cleaning: Trash collection & vacuuming bots.
  • 2. UV-C Disinfection: Chemical-free interior sanitization.
  • 3. Exterior Washing: Water-efficient robotic gantry systems.
  • 4. AI Inspection: Visual crawlers for predictive maintenance.
