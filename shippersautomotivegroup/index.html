Executive Vision

Shippers Automotive Group is positioned to transition from a standard 3PL to a Tech-Enabled Logistics Partner. By leveraging AMRs and AI, we decouple throughput from labor volatility, aiming for "Dark Warehouse" capabilities in sorting and sequencing operations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: AMRs for Internal Transport

Replacing forklifts for long-haul transport. AMRs move pallets from receiving to sequencing lanes, improving safety and allowing labor to focus on value-add inspection.

Pathway B: Robotic Piece Picking

Utilization of Cube Storage (ASRS) for small parts. Increases density by 400% and eliminates costly sequencing errors.

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive efficiency using vision systems at dock doors to instantly verify ASNs and detect freight damage.
