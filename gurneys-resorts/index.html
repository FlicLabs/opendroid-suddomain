Client: Gurney's Resorts

Executive Summary

Gurney's Resorts operates in high-demand, seasonal luxury markets where labor availability and service consistency are critical challenges. This analysis identifies an asset-light robotics strategy focusing on Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) for logistics and commercial cleaning automation. The primary goal is to reallocate luxury staff from low-value transport tasks to high-value guest interactions.

Implementing this roadmap allows Gurney's Resorts to navigate seasonal labor shortages while elevating the guest experience.
