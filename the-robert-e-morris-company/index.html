02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A

Collaborative Machine Tending

Deployment of IP67 Cobots on mobile pedestals to automate Okuma/Tsugami machine loading during engineering runoffs. Increases safety and frees up Application Engineers.

PATHWAY B

AMR Intralogistics

Autonomous Mobile Robots to transport tooling and raw stock from the warehouse to the engineering floor, reducing forklift traffic in showroom areas.

PATHWAY C

End-of-Line Automation

Automated palletizing and boxing for the Parts Distribution division, ensuring consistent packaging quality and reducing manual lifting strain.
