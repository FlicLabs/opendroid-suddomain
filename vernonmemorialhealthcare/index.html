Vernon Memorial Healthcare

Robotics Consultation & Opportunity Portfolio

Executive Summary

Vernon Memorial Healthcare (VMH) is a vital Critical Access Hospital in Wisconsin's Driftless Region. Facing the rural healthcare trilemma of staffing shortages, geographic dispersion, and regional competition, VMH is uniquely positioned to leverage robotics to secure independence. The primary opportunity lies in Orthopedic Surgical Robotics to retain high-margin cases, supported by operational automation to stabilize the workforce.

Strategic Positioning

Adopting this portfolio positions Vernon Memorial Healthcare not merely as a rural access point, but as a technologic leader in the Driftless Region. This "High-Tech, High-Touch" strategy ensures the hospital remains independent, financially viable, and capable of delivering modern care locally.
