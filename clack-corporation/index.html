01. Executive Vision

Clack Corporation is positioned to transition from traditional semi-automated processes to a flexible, intelligent manufacturing ecosystem. By integrating robotics into water treatment component assembly and blow molding, the corporation will address labor volatility while increasing throughput capacity for high-demand pressure tanks and control valves.

02. Robotics Opportunities

PATHWAY A

Cobot Assembly

Deployment of IP67-rated Cobots for machine tending and precise valve assembly. Focuses on reducing ergonomic strain and ensuring consistent torque application.

PATHWAY B

AMR Logistics

Fleet deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) to move heavy pressure tanks and raw resin. Creates a fork-free zone to enhance safety.

PATHWAY C

Auto-Palletizing

End-of-line industrial arms for heavy tank stacking combined with vision systems for zero-defect quality assurance on valve threading.
