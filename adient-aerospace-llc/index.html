Strategic Automation Report

Adient Aerospace LLC | Aviation Cleaning & Maintenance
01 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report proposes a comprehensive robotics integration strategy designed to modernize the cleaning and maintenance operations of Adient Aerospace LLC. By shifting from manual processes to an autonomous ecosystem, Adient aims to achieve higher hygiene standards, operational efficiency, and significant cost reductions. The core vision involves deploying specialized robotic units for cabin care, disinfection, exterior washing, and AI-driven inspections, creating a data-rich environment for continuous improvement.

03 ROBOTICS OPPORTUNITY PATHWAYS

Pathway A: Cabin Cleaning

Autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) for vacuuming seats and floor tracks. Reduces cleaning time by 40%.

Pathway B: UV-C Disinfection

Autonomous UV-C units for overnight sanitization of high-touch surfaces. Enhances hygiene standards.

Pathway C: Exterior Washing

Automated gantry systems for fuselage washing. Reduces water usage by 60% and improves aerodynamics.

Pathway D: AI Inspection

LiDAR-enabled bots for visual QA. Creates digital twins and detects micro-defects.
