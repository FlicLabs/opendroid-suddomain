Executive Summary

Emergency Medicine Specialists operates in a high-pressure environment where minutes equate to margin and patient safety. Our analysis indicates that shifting from a pure labor model to a Tech-Enabled Care Platform via Process Automation and Telepresence Robotics is not just viable, but necessary for future scalability.
