Executive Vision

Thrust Flight stands at the forefront of aviation training. The integration of robotic cleaning and maintenance automation represents a transformative leap. By deploying autonomous systems, Thrust Flight can reduce aircraft downtime, ensure hospital-grade sanitization, and standardize preventative maintenance.

  • Fleet: High-frequency Cessna 172 & Piper Seminole training units.
  • Goal: Maximizing billable flight hours through automated turnaround.
