CMCBrands Logistics
Automation Strategy

Prepared for Senior Stakeholders

Executive Vision

To transition CMCBrands from a labor-intensive fulfillment model to a high-velocity, automated ecosystem. By leveraging robotics, we address the specific challenges of apparel seasonality and SKU density, transforming logistics from a cost center into a competitive advantage.

Pathway A: AMRs

Deployment of Autonomous Mobile Robots for internal transport. Eliminates manual cart pushing.

Impact: 30-40% Walking Reduction

Pathway B: ASRS

Automated Storage & Retrieval Systems for high-density uniform storage and piece picking.

Impact: 99.9% Pick Accuracy

Pathway C: AI Logistics

Predictive modeling for inbound flow and "Back-to-School" rush demand forecasting.

Impact: 0 Bottlenecks
