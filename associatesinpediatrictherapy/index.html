Associates in Pediatric Therapy

Robotics Analysis & ROI Opportunities Portfolio

Executive Summary

Associates in Pediatric Therapy (APT) is a regional leader in pediatric care. Our research indicates that integrating robotics shifts the focus from simple labor substitution to therapist augmentation. By deploying Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) and gamified rehabilitation tools, APT can solve critical engagement challenges in pediatrics, reduce cancellation rates, and drive roughly £800k–£1.2M in annual incremental value.

Organizational Profile

  • Model: Multi-site outpatient (PT/OT/Speech) & School Partnerships.
  • Scale: 20+ Clinics in KY/IN region.
  • Philosophy: "Play with a Purpose."
  • Challenge: Therapist burnout & patient engagement.

Strategic Goal

To transition from a standard service provider to a Tech-Enabled Pediatric Health Platform, using robotics to gamify therapy and automate administration.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Social Robotics

Target: Speech & Autism Therapy

Deploying interactive humanoid robots (e.g., Moxie/NAO) to act as social bridges. Robots provide infinite patience for repetition and lower social anxiety for ASD children.

Impact: +15% Goal Efficiency

Pathway B: Gamified Rehab

Target: PT & OT (Clinic & Home)

Wearable sensors and smart-boards that turn repetitive exercises into video games. Increases compliance and creates a "fun factor" that reduces child resistance to therapy.

Impact: -10% Cancellations

Pathway C: Admin Automation

Target: Operations & Documentation

AI scribes to listen and draft notes, and RPA to auto-fill cancellations from waitlists. Directly combats provider burnout and lost revenue.

Impact: +2-3 Billable Hrs/Week
