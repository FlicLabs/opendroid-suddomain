EXECUTIVE VISION

Waffle House represents a unique challenge in automation due to its "High-Touch, High-Speed" culture. Our strategic implementation utilizes robotics not to replace the Grill Operator, but to optimize logistics. By automating the "Drudgery Loop" (dish transport and phone orders), we empower staff to focus on the customer experience.
