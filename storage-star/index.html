01. Executive Vision

Storage Star requires a pivot from traditional batch processing to a flexible, data-driven Industry 4.0 model. By integrating collaborative robotics and autonomous transport, the organization aims to reduce lead times by 40% and stabilize labor volatility.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Machine TendingSafety

Deploying IP66-rated Cobots to man press brakes. The cobot picks raw sheet metal, loads the press brake, and triggers the cycle. This removes operators from dangerous machinery and ensures 100% repeatability.

Pathway B: Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs)

LogisticsWIP Flow

Implementing AMRs to replace manual forklifts for WIP transport. AMRs will autonomously move stamped parts to welding cells, reducing traffic accidents and optimizing flow.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Automation

PalletizingQuality Control

Heavy-payload robotic arms with vision systems to stack flat-packs and inspect labels. This improves yield and eliminates ergonomic injuries.
