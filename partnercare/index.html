Pathway A: RPA Focus

Scope: Automating prior authorizations, eligibility, and denial management.

  • Reduction in cost-to-collect by 20-30%
  • Decrease in Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) by 10-15 days
  • Auto-correction of coding errors before submission

Pathway B: Clinical Focus

Scope: Robotic navigation for spinal cord stimulator (SCS) placement.

  • Increased precision in lead placement
  • Reduction in radiation exposure (fluoro) for staff
  • Marketing differentiation for partner ASCs

Implementation Roadmap

Year 1: Foundation

Deploy RPA bots for eligibility and prior authorization in Florida region. Establish Automation Center of Excellence.

Year 2: Pilot & Expand

Pilot robotic navigation in top volume ASC. Implement AI scheduling logic to optimize block time.

Year 3-5: Scale

Network-wide roll out of interventional robotics. Full automation of backend billing and collections.
