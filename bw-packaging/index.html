01. Executive Summary

BW Packaging is positioned to transition from traditional machine building to a digitally integrated Smart Manufacturing ecosystem. The core vision aligns with "People-Centric Automation," aiming to remove repetitive tasks from skilled artisans to focus on high-value engineering.

This report outlines the shift toward a flexible manufacturing floor, utilizing robotics to bridge labor gaps and accelerate the lead time for engineered-to-order packaging solutions.

02. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Collaborative Robots (Cobots)

Deployment: Machine Tending & Assembly.
Utilization of UR10e or Fanuc CRX units to automate CNC loading cycles. This allows skilled machinists to manage multiple cells, enhancing safety and precision in sub-assembly.

Pathway B: AMRs (Autonomous Mobile Robots)

Deployment: Material Transport.
Implementation of fleet-based AMRs for "Just-in-Time" kit delivery from warehouse to assembly bays, significantly reducing non-value-added walking time for technicians.

Pathway C: End-of-Line Automation

Deployment: Quality & Logistics.
Automated optical inspection for dimensional accuracy of frames and robotic crating solutions for spare parts to increase throughput.
