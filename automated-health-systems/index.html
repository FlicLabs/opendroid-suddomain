Executive Summary

The strongest levers for automation are not physical mechanics, but "Software Robotics" (RPA) and Conversational AI. Transforming the enrollment broker model via these technologies presents a $10M+ annual margin opportunity per major contract by decoupling revenue growth from headcount linearity.
