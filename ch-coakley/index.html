Executive Summary

CH Coakley is positioned to transition from a traditional 3PL and storage provider to a high-velocity, tech-enabled fulfillment hub. By integrating AMRs and ASRS technology, the company can address the dual challenge of labor shortages and increased consumer demand for speed, specifically in their Milwaukee operations.

Robotics Opportunity Pathways

PATHWAY A AMRs for Transport

Utilization of Autonomous Mobile Robots to handle long-distance transport within the warehouse. This eliminates non-value-added walking time, focusing human workers on complex packing and quality control tasks.

PATHWAY B Robotic Piece Picking & ASRS

Implementation of vertical lift modules for the Records Management division. This ensures high-density storage for documents and rapid retrieval times, significantly reducing the square footage cost per file.

PATHWAY C AI Logistics

Deploying computer vision at receiving docks to automate dimensioning and condition reporting, vital for the Commercial Moving sector to mitigate liability.
