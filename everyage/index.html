Pathway A: Service Automation

Deployment of autonomous mobile robots in dining venues at Abernethy Laurels and Piedmont Crossing. By offloading heavy tray carrying and "busing" duties to robots, dining staff can remain on the floor, increasing resident interaction time and reducing physical strain.

Strategic Impact: Reduces agency labor usage during peak dining hours and serves as a powerful marketing tool for prospective residents seeking tech-forward communities.
