Executive Summary

CorneaGen's opportunity lies in shifting from manual artistry to microrobotic precision. By automating the delicate process of DMEK/DSAEK graft preparation, the company can virtually eliminate the 3% industry-standard tissue wastage, standardize optical quality, and create a scalable "Bio-Robotics" platform. The financial model suggests a rapid ROI based on yield retention alone.
