1. Executive Summary

Gulf Coast Hotel Management Inc. (GCHM) operates a robust portfolio of extended stay and limited-service hotels. The strongest robotics levers are (1) Automated Floor Care, (2) Service Delivery, and (3) Laundry Automation. The dominant ROI driver is the reduction of non-value-added labor hours, recovering hundreds of thousands in labor leakage annually.
