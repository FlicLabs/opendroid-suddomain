1. Executive Summary

Catalyst Behavioral Services is positioned to transform from a labor-intensive ABA provider to a tech-enabled care platform. By integrating Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR) for therapy and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for operations, the organization can reclaim ~20% of clinician time currently lost to administration. The dominant ROI driver is the conversion of these hours into billable revenue, generating over £1M in incremental annual revenue across a 50-clinician baseline.

3. Robotics Opportunity Pathways

Pathway A: Socially Assistive Robotics (SAR)

Scope: Deployment of humanoid robots (e.g., NAO, Moxie) for consistent social skills instruction.

Impact: Improves data accuracy, standardizes therapy delivery, and increases session engagement.


Pathway B: Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Scope: "Software Robotics" handling intake, scheduling, and clinical note generation.

Impact: The primary financial engine. Recovers 5-8 hours per week per clinician, directly increasing billable capacity.


Pathway C: Telepresence Robotics

Scope: Remote mobile presence for senior supervision.

Impact: Reduces travel costs by 30% and expands service radius to rural areas.
