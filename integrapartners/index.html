Executive Summary

Integra Partners operates as a critical gateway in the post-acute care supply chain, managing DMEPOS benefits for over 15 million lives. The company stands at a pivotal point where software robotics (RPA) can resolve high-volume administrative bottlenecks, while the development of a specialized physical robotics network can capture the emerging market for high-tech rehabilitation devices.
